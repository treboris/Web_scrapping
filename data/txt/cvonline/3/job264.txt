DevOps Engineer 
Your new company
Because of the rapidly changing environment, they had set their aim to become the most data & technology-driven airline in the world. Changing our way of working, modernizing our technology stack and architecture and the right positioning of people is key to achieving this ambition. We have organized our product teams around business processes, where together business and IT are end-to-end responsible for the digital products and services that support our operation.  We are looking for a software engineer with a specialization on DevSecOps, who collaboratively organize all necessary  on the automation and configuration management of environments tasks to help maximize the flow of value. As a software engineer, you will work shoulder to shoulder with other software engineers, like backend specialists, data scientists, domain experts, and frontline staff to shape the future of them and the aviation industry.
Your new role
As a DevSecOps specialist, you will apply your skills to change the way KLM works, driving innovation 
transforming us into a truly data-driven organization. We value both technical expertise (analysis, design & architecture, engineering, quality assurance and DevSecOps of course) as well as the interpersonal parts of the job (coaching, consulting, coordination, and support).  As a software engineer DevSecOps your key result areas are:
Make use of right technology and govern architecture for the environments in line with the organizational objectives, ensuring that the right technologies are chosen to strike an optimal balance between achieving business goals, supporting the realization of functional and technical requirements, environments and continuity costs 
Build, migrate, implement and maintain environments and key technologies for COTS/SAAS apps by using the IaC principles, showing leadership towards OPS- & Security teams, and collaborate with the development team on the continual process improvement.
Build, implement and maintain CI/CD pipelines and its processes to support development teams with automated testing and security scanning while keeping an eye on FinOps.
Support the migration of our critical-, but also legacy landscape from on-premises to the Azure and Google Cloud environment in line with our strategical objectives. Experience with migrating COTS to SAAS is a pre.
Design and implement the overall architecture aligned with other self-engineered products to deliver
Translate development team needs into compelling solutions and deliver them to end users so that business value is created
Take care of specification, design, engineering, testing, delivery, maintenance, monitoring, and support of the IaC landscape
Foster a culture where state-of-the-art approaches, tooling and technologies are applied as fits with the mission of the department, the KPI’s and desired business impact
What you'll need to succeed
We are looking for passionate and talented DevSecOps engineers, for curious builders who are biased towards action, communication and are thriving in a fast-paced and international environment.   We have found that people who match the following requirements are the ones who fit us best:  
Preferably Bachelor degree or higher in Computer Science, Software Engineering or other relevant fields
Minimum of 5 years of professional experience with engineering CI/CD pipelines on-premise and/or Azure/GCP environments. 
Minimum of 5 years of professional experience in performing and automate COTS/SAAS environments by using the IaC and monitor everything principles, while industrializing environments
Knowledge of CI/CD techniques and tooling (e.g., k8s, GitHub Actions, Bamboo, Jenkins, Git, etc.)
Proficient with automation tooling, configuration management and problem finding
Experience with bringing enterprise applications to production and supporting development teams 
Experience in coaching and knowledge sharing to familiarize teams with DevSecOps best practices
Knowledge with and experience in agile way of working (e.g., SAFe, Scrum, Agile)
Great communication skills, written and spoken (English) and team spirit 
This role requires participation in our on-call support rotation
Available to come to the office 3 days a week, there are no formal rules about work from home yet, but most teams have a hybrid way of working
In addition, the following requirements are considered nice to have:  
Preferable being a certified Cloud DevOps Engineer for Azure / GCP / AWS
Experience in the developer- / infra related role in previous jobs

10% of your time set aside for personal learning and development 
An excellent pension scheme, profit sharing, and yearly bonus.
Substantial discounts on flight tickets (after 6 months of employment)
A travel allowance
Discount on health insurance
A learning budget
