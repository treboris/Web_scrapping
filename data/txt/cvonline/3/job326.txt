Pozíció leírása / Job description
With key markets in the Netherlands, the UK, and the US, as well as growth markets in China, Brazil, and Iberia, our international partner supports one of the largest financial services groups, by offering IT, finance, risk, model validation, actuarial, audit, and asset management services.A diverse workforce and an inclusive culture are fostered by our partners. They value diversity as a whole, including but not limited to, racial, gender, and ability diversity as well as diversity in language, culture, beliefs, age, origin, background, perspectives, and experiences.
This position will provide the company's SAP organization with an experienced Ariba functional admin supporting our Ariba hosted instance. The selected candidate will be an excellent communicator with proven technical and analytical skills. This position is involved in functional support and day to day maintenance of our Ariba instance. This includes working on request and incident tickets and supporting the agile team way of working. Functions for this include running agile ceremonies, updating the Jira board and creating new backlogs.
Responsibilities:
Work in a multi-national environment
Work on user requests and incidents as they are submitted
Work with the Product Owner on enhancements and vendor changes
Work in agile environment, including working on backlogs, and provide support to PO
Communication with various business units on request and issues
Be able to use problem solving skills in providing solutions
Work with business on analysis of requirements
Provide support for regulatory and compliance issues/requests
Work within documented change management process
Provide project support and oversight as needed
