 Tasks / Feladatok
The development of solutions for our future PLM / ALM landscape based on the Siemens PLM application suite is your primary goal. You will take part in developing concepts and in the technical realization.
You will configure our PLM system landscape and adapt it from a technical perspective. Furthermore you will help in coordinating our external partners as part of the project activities. 
In building up our future engineering landscape you will get in touch with different topics like PLM, ALM, MBSE (Model Based System Engineering), Big Data and Advanced Analytics and you will work jointly together with colleagues from the engineering departments as well as from IT.
You will always keep an eye on technological developments in order to be able to assess the beneficial use of digital technologies in the field of engineering applications.
