Networking background

Plan, build and execute technical solution designs for the delivery of both high-performance and resilience information security technologies to support the company’s Low Latency Electronic Trading environments.
Produce high quality documentation, schematics and presentation materials as needed
Participate in various reporting functions, analysis of metrics, key performance indicators and vulnerability remediation initiatives
Ensure the continued stability and resilience of the infrastructure by adhering to strict change management guidelines
Provide individual leadership in the realm of change, incident, and problem management

Troubleshoot complex connectivity issues often involving multiple support teams
Adhere to corporate and Information Security and other functional standards and controls
