Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur client is a global technology company supplying systems for passenger cars, commercial vehicles and industrial technology, enabling the next generation of mobility. With its comprehensive technology portfolio, the company offers integrated solutions for established vehicle manufacturers, mobility providers and start-up companies in the fields of transportation and mobility.Elvárások / Requirements
Degree in Information technology/Engineering/Economics or similar 
3-5 years of relevant experience in a similar position and in-depth knowledge of the SAP applications CO/PS/IM
Open mindedness for new solutions and interest in international teamwork
Sound knowledge of the SAP applications CO/PS/IM
Process and reporting knowledge in the field of controlling and knowledge of the SAP development process, ABAP, LSMW, FIORI
Good English (to successfully communicate in an international work environment) 
Amit kínálunk / Offer
Support business partners in identifying optimal solutions for ZF in the CO/PS/IM modules in various ERP systems.
Assessment, control, and implementation of business/functional requirements from an IT technical point of view
Cooperation in national and international projects (BMS roll-outs, reporting unit splits/mergers, S/4 HANA transformation projects).
Creation and maintenance of documentation, e.g. specifications, concepts, documentation of customizing settings.
IT Support for CO/PS/IM in various ERP systems 
Advantages:
ABAP programming and debugging skills 
Basic understanding of End-to-End processes (effect on Finance) and cost engineering 
Knowledge of German language

Stable company background and long-term working possibility
Full of challenges and high-level professional activities at an international company

Career opportunities, continuous training program and language courses
