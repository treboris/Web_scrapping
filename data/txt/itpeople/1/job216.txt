Looking to strengthen the IT-Support team right away or by agreement, BOSCHUNG MECATRONIC is recruiting a:DevOps Engineer 100%(Remote work & periodic on-site visits. English required; Workplace in Hungary)Your tasksSet up new IT infrastructure services including documentation of the standard systemConfigure and install various IT infrastructure components and services (e.g. servers, blades, compute fabric, network fabric, virtualization platform)Automation of maintenance and installation processesParticipation in the observability-stack to monitor our platform and identify improvementsReporting on the status of specific IT infrastructure projects by collecting/prioritizing information; actively participating in projectsDaily use of databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL, Scylla)Your profileBachelor of Science in Informatics or equivalent education/professional experience.Organized and structured way of working both autonomously and as part of a teamFluent in English, French and/or German is a plusExperience in working with networks and virtualized servicesExperience setting up and maintaining secondary data centersExperience participating in projects related to the network design & solutions architectureGood understanding / experience in virtualization technologies and containerization (Proxmox, Open Stack, K8s)Understanding of / experience with GNU/Linux systems, network administration (Debian-based) and network technologies (VPN, L2, Access, Port Channel, Trunk, VLANs, BGP)Programming in Shell and PythonGood understanding of storage technologies and protocols (Ceph, iSCSI, NFS)Hands-on experience with monitoring, diagnostic, and analysis tools such as Prometheus and NagiosKnowledge of modern configuration management tools (e.g. Git, Ansible).What we have to offerYou will be able to carry out a variety of activities in a modern infrastructure and a prosperous businessYou will be familiarised with your new task with the support of a talented and motivated team, working with de latest technologies!We offer attractive terms and conditions and will be happy to support you in your professional developmentAre you interested in working for Boschung Mecatronic?
