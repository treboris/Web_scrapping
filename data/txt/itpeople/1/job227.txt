Junior Digital Executive állás Budapest
Egyéb IT pozik állások 
Elvárások What you bring to the Team: You have university/college degree as a fresh graduate and/or you are a student but able to work full time OR 6 – 12 months of experience within a media agency environment or practical experience with Google Ads and Facebook ads is an advantage Fluent Hungarian and English language knowledge are essential You have a strong affinity towards digital media channels Knowledge of the following platforms is an advantage, but not must-have: Google Ads, Facebook Ads Manager, Google Analytics You enjoy handling large sets of data while relying on your strong analytical & system thinking skills You are very competent with Microsoft Office applications, especially Excel You are highly organised, punctual, and carry out tasks in a timely manner  Monotonous tasks never frighten you and you are eager to learn fast to accomplish more complex tasks You are proactive and a great team player Feladatok Are you interested in digital media channels?  Would you like to start your career in a highly analytical and data-driven industry?  Are you fuelled by thirst for knowledge and ready to gain experience on the agency side?  If your answers are YES, then join our super team and start your career with us! Your mission and responsibilities:Executing digital media campaigns, e.g. Google, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok Campaign platform setup (creatives, keywords and any additional campaign assets) Conducting keyword research Monitoring and fine-tuning PPC campaigns’ performance to ensure objectives & KPI’s are met Creating post-buy reports and conducting financial administration at the end of the campaign Preparing regular & ad-hoc KPI and competitive reports Supporting PPC & Display Managers. Előnyök What’s in it for You? Home office opportunity after probation period Diverse benefit package: cafeteria, mobile phone, plus BKK pass Fruit Day on Monday and Sandwich Day on Friday Structured onboarding program and trainings Fantastic opportunity to learn and grow in the field of online media Open and helpful team welcomes you with regular team building activities Employee loyalty program Modern and innovative environment
