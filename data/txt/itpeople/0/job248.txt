Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentGlobal Technology Services (GTS) partners with the Aegon businesses to provide global IT standards and governance, global IT procurement services, and IT infrastructure, application, and cloud support services.
From 6 locations in the US, the Netherlands, UK, and Hungary, the company support 20+ country units and 24000+ employees in the world. All of these teams work closely together to provide innovative and digital solutions as well as critical technology support.This is essential for enabling businesses around the globe to be innovative, digital and competitive, efficient and effective and to provide the best solutions and customer experience for our customers and shareholders.
The Intermediate Service Management Analyst is responsible for supporting the delivery of critical services for each team that comprises Production Support. Service Management practices include Problem Management; Knowledge Management, Incident Management, Change Enablement, Capacity, Availability and Performance Management and Service Introduction.Elvárások / Requirements
Bachelor’s degree in computer science or related degree or equivalent work experience and minimum of 2-4 years technical enterprise work experience.
Experience in change management in multinational environment
Excellent time management skills including the ability to juggle multiple priorities simultaneously.
Strong decision making and problem-solving skills.
Ability to stay committed to the task even during times of frequent change or information delays.
Comfortable working with multiple teams.
Strong verbal and written communication skills.
Accurate and precise attention to detail.
Able to build strong interpersonal relationships with teammates, leadership, senior management, and internal and external stakeholders.
Proficient computer skills, Microsoft Office Suite; working knowledge of software development.
