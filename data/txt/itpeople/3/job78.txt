SAP Ariba IT Expert - remote (11452)Our client is a global, family-owned German company that develops market-leading solutions for the solar and automotive industries. They are looking for a SAP Ariba IT Expert to their Budapest-based office.The work can be managed full remotely – with time-to-time office presence – from any location in Hungary.Main tasks, roles and responsibilities:• Detailing the solution design in close cooperation with our solution architects• Customizing in SAP ERP and Ariba to fulfill business requirements• Creation of programming requests for our internal development team or for external programmers• Steering of external consultants and / or developers• Testing• User support, incident handling and standard service fulfillment• Participation in the S/4 rollout• Support of processes in the area of purchasingWork experiences:• 3 or more years of experience• Experienced working in an international, virtual teamTechnical/ IT knowledge:• Deep SAP knowledge (S/4 preferred) in SAP purchasing processes and in SAP Ariba including the interfaces between both• Knowledge in SAP BTP as basis for our new supplier portalLanguage:​​​​​​​• Good English skills (spoken and written)
