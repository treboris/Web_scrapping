System Administrator állás Országos
Tesztelés állások 
Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur partner has more than 40 years of research and development in the field of lottery systems. They are the worldwide leading supplier of lottery products, with more than 8,500 employees. Currently they are looking for a system administrator at their office in Budapest.Elvárások / Requirements
Have knowledge of Linux systems
Confidence working with Microsoft systems (Active Directory, DHCP, DNS, GPO)
Have experience with IP networking, virtualization, backup systems, and server hardware
Excellent English language skills
Minimum of 1-2 years of relevant experience (Juniors are also welcome to apply)
Advantageous for the position:
Experience in MS SQL Servers and SCCM
Experience in Confluence, SharePoint, Jira administration
 Amit kínálunk / Offer
Level 1 and Level 2 client side support.
Provide office IT support, completing tasks based on JIRA tickets
Collaborate with the Vienna team to resolve IT-related incidents
Manage systems on computers within the premises
Offer hardware and software support for users and servers (Windows and Linux systems)
Assist and provide technical support for integrated systems deliveries
Prepare complete workspaces as needed (assemble table/chair, prepare PC)
Execute backups according to backup procedures using VEEAM backup softwareManage hardware-software inventory
Monitor and manage switchboards and phones, and provide reporting
Perform current upkeep, care, maintenance, and upgrades
Kapcsolattartó / Information
Competitive payment package (13. and 14. months salaries)
Cafeteria and Health insurance
Mentoring Program, Trainings
Flexible work schedule
Downtown office, in the heart of Budapest, at the foot of Buda castle (easily accessible by public transport)
Indoor bike and motorcycle storage
Recreation room: table soccer, darts and sauna
Team buildings
Excellent working atmosphere
Open company culture
Economically stable and growing enterprise
Non-conventional domain: lottery and sports games
