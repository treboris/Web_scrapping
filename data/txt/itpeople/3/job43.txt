Network Engineer állás Országos
Rendszer / Hálózat, DevOps állások 
Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur partner is a well known technology company, with almost 2,000 employees worldwide, our client's Controls and Digitalization teams has a global presence. Their team focuses on providing value-added services and solutions to our customers in the areas of control, digitization, automation and electrical equipment. Elvárások / Requirements
Several years of professional experience of network OR IT security
Very good knowledge of LAN/WAN, TCP/IP, switching, routing, VPN and RF technologies as well as IT-Security concepts, standards, and hardening measures 
Knowledge in configuration of the hardware components related to switching/routing/firewall (Cisco, Ruggedcom, Checkpoint, Scalance switches and firewalls, etc.) 
Willingness for business trips (in total <30% per year) 
Fluent language skills in English
Amit kínálunk / Offer
Supporting our client's automation control system development and closely collaborating with the sales & bid manager
Configuration of switches/routers/firewalls under consideration of IT security requirements, commissioning of these as well as ensuring error-free functioning of the network during unit and system tests  
Preparation of technical specifications and documentation regarding communication and IT security in the projects 
Kapcsolattartó / Information
Opportunity to have an active engineering role on future handling complex projects with a high digitalization level
Working with an international teams and business environments
Excellent opportunity for professional development on a new tailored organization
Competitive benefit package (basic salary, bonus, daily allowance for travelling periods)
