They allow vehicles to see, think and act. In the four technology domains of Vehicle Motion Control, Integrated Safety, Automated Driving, and Electric Mobility, the company offers comprehensive product and software solutions for established vehicle manufacturers and newly emerging transport and mobility service providers. Our client electrifies a wide range of vehicle types.
