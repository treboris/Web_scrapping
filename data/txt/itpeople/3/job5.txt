Solution Architect - FORCAM állás Országos
Rendszer / Hálózat, DevOps állások 
Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur Business Service Center is a German family-owned automotive company, and they would like to represent themselves also in the Hungarian market. They are planning to hire 350 new employees, and they will have a brand new office in Budapest. New colleagues will have a chance to join a new company and to make decisions on professional topics. These employees who add their own ideas would be prioritized by the management of the company. Fluent English is necessary to use for everyday work.Elvárások / Requirements
Three or more years of relevant technical and business experience in shop floor applications, preferably in FORCAM FORCE
Experience in the design, implementation and operation of manufacturing IT solutions
Practical experience in XSLT, Java Script and SQL, and experiences in RabbitMQ middleware are welcome.
Ideally experience in project and requirements management as well as agile methods.
Good English skills (spoken and written).
Positive and integrative collaboration skills, establishing strong supportive relationships across all departments.
Experienced working in an international, virtual team.
Basic willingness to travel (approx. 5-10%)
Amit kínálunk / Offer
Translate business requirements into a solution design and align it with business analysts, business stakeholders and the other solution architects of the manufacturing digitalization portfolio.
Apply proof of concepts and solutions by performing the required system configuration and customizing in FORCAM.
Implementation of necessary process and system adaptations in FORCAM OEE and other manufacturing IT systems.
Perform the initial testing before handing the solution to the business team
Manage external consultants or developers.
Advice the customer on both operational and strategic matters related to FORCAM OEE.
Support the community of users in their daily work by solving incidents and handling service requests.
Ensure the global FORCAM landscape is up-to-date and confirms company security guidelines.
Participate in international projects including the S/4 HANA initiatives.
Kapcsolattartó / Information· Flexible working conditions with UNLIMITED remote work possibility, you can have a real work-life balance.
· Modern, newly built sustainable office in the heart of Budapest.
· Competitive salary, benefits and compensation packages (Medicover, bonuses, extra day off on your birthday).
· Supportive, diverse and highly skilled community.
· Ability to really make a difference and build new processes from scratch, you can participate in the formation and shape of the BSC processes.
· You can enjoy our value-oriented work culture: we are down-to-earth, appreciative, inspiring and innovative.
·Dynamically developing company (10-20% yearly growth in the automotive and solar industry), an independent, family-run business which provides much flexibility.
