SAP-PP IT Expert - remote (11470)Our client is a global, family-owned German company that develops market-leading solutions for the solar and automotive industries. They are looking for a SAP-PP IT Expert to their Budapest-based office.The work can be managed full remotely – with time-to-time office presence – from any location in Hungary.Main tasks, roles and responsibilities:• Detailing the solution design in close cooperation with our SAP-PP solution architects• Customizing in SAP to fulfill business requirements• Creation of programming requests for our internal development team or for external programmers• TestingSpecial Tasks/ Key challenges:• Support of processes in the area of SAP-PP• Participation in the S/4 rolloutRequirements• 3 or more years of experience• Experienced working in an international, virtual team• Deep SAP knowledge in the area of SAP-PP and in the surrounding SAP modules• Beside SAP-PP we are running SAP-ME and MPDV/HYDRA in some areas. Knowledge in SAP-ME and/or HYDRA, especially on the interfaces to and from S/4, would be beneficial, but it is not a mustLanguage:• Good English skills (spoken and written)
