Egyéb IT pozik állások 
Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur partner is one of the leading global automotive suppliers. As an internationally positioned IT team, they support the Client's product development by providing applications and solutions for mechanical and software development and for the management of development data. In this process, they count more than 6,000 users among their internal customers.They operate in a very heterogeneous environment of applications, such as CATIA Mechanical Design, MS Project Server, JIRA, GIT Software Management, Doors NG Requirements Management, and their PLM system CIM Database.To strengthen their team they are looking for a motivated IT specialist at their location in Budapest.They offer an exciting work environment in a globally operating enterprise with many various opportunities for your own personal and professional growth.Elvárások / Requirements
At least 1 year of experience in SharePoint
General IT and SharePoint knowledge
Fluency in English, medium-level German skills (spoken and written)
Experience working in an international, virtual team
Good communication skills
Amit kínálunk / OfferOverall Purpose of Job:
Our partner is running a huge MS-SharePoint farm to support the communication needs of the business. Besides SharePoint, some additional tools for collaboration (i.e. Concept Board, Staffbase, Sitecore, etc.) are in use. In this position you will be Consulting the business users in case of questions about how to design their SharePoint pages in the best way, your customers will mainly be located in Europe.
Main tasks, roles and responsibilities:
Incident and problem management
Monitoring system operation and proactive error detection and solving
Ownership of the services or a set of additional tools besides SharePoint
Fulfill service requests, documentation, and training needs
Kapcsolattartó / Information
Flexible working conditions with unlimited remote work possibility, you can have a real work-life balance
Modern, newly built sustainable office in the heart of Budapest
Competitive salary, benefits and compensation packages (Medicover, bonuses, an extra day off on your birthday)
A supportive, diverse and highly skilled community
Ability to really make a difference and build new processes from scratch
