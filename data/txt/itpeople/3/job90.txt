Ariba Functional Admin állás Budapest
Rendszer / Hálózat, DevOps állások 
Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentAegon is a multinational financial services group headquartered in The Hague, the Netherlands. The company has multiple locations across the globe, with core markets in The Netherlands, UK and the US, growth markets in China, Brazil and Iberia and an Asset Management organization with locations in UK, US, NL and Hungary. Aegon Global Business Solution Center is supporting these businesses with IT, Finance, Risk, Model Validation, Actuarial, Audit and Asset management services with a growing team of approx. 300 people, based in Budapest, Hungary.
At Aegon, we foster a diverse workforce and an inclusive culture. We are a people business, and we recognize that delivering excellent client outcomes comes from harnessing distinct perspectives. The successful candidate will be self-motivated; service oriented and possesses excellent interpersonal and analytical skills. Candidate must have the ability to develop, coordinate, communicate, document, execute testing and implementation plans according to project requirements with minimal supervision. In addition to the skills listed above, candidates must also demonstrate the following behaviors: team work; communication; tolerance of ambiguity; commitment to task; continuous learning; attention to detail; quality.Amit kínálunk / OfferThis position will provide the GTS SPA organization with an experienced Ariba functional admin supporting our Ariba hosted instance. The selected candidate will be an excellent communicator with proven technical and analytical skills. This position is involved in functional support and day to daymaintenance of our Ariba instance. This includes working on request and incident tickets and supporting the agile team way of working. Functions for this include running agile ceremonies, updating the Jira board and creating new backlogs.
Responsibilities:
Work in a multi-national environment
Work on user requests and incidents as they are submitted
Work with the Product Owner on enhancements and vendor changes
Work in agile environment, including working on backlogs, and provide support to PO
Communication with various business units on request and issues
Be able to use problem solving skills in providing solutions
Work with business on analysis of requirements
Provide support for regulatory and compliance issues/requests
Work within documented change management process
Provide project support and oversight as needed
