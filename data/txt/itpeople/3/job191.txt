Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentJoin our client's being engaged in the development of new bio-based technologies.
The Onsite IT Operations Specialist is responsible for device management and automation as well as various IT projects. The successful candidate will work closely with the IT Manager toensure the smooth running of all IT systems and services within the organisation.
 Elvárások / Requirements
3+ years of experience in IT operations, device management and automation.
Relevant studies in system administration
Experience with endpoint management tools.
Knowledge of cloud technologies such as AWS or Azure.
Experience in managing IT projects.
Knowledge of scripting languages such as PowerShell or Python is an advantage
Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to troubleshoot complex technical issues.
Upper intermediate written and verbal communication skills in English.
Amit kínálunk / Offer
Responsible for managing and maintaining all hardware and software assets acrossthe organisation.
Develop, implement and maintain automation solutions to streamline IT operationsand reduce manual effort.
Manage endpoint security and enforce compliance with IT policies and procedures.Troubleshoot and resolve hardware and software issues.
Create and manage IT project plans and timelines, ensuring projects are delivered ontime, within budget, and to a high standard.
Assist with the deployment of new IT systems and technologies.
Provide IT support to staff as required.
Monitor and maintain IT systems and infrastructure to ensure maximum uptime andavailability.
Perform regular backups and disaster recovery testing.
Stay up-to-date with emerging IT trends and technologies.
Kapcsolattartó / Information
Being part of a global team
Excellent compensation package
Wide range of responsibilities in local system administration
