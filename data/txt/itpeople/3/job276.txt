Elvárások REQUIREMENTSHard skills:If you would like to go for programming direction, you should have strong basics in OOP, in language independent programming and in databases (preferably in SQL)If you would rather like to go towards the business functions, strong logical thinking but also SQL knowledge is a requirementFluent English is overall expectedSoft skills:Ability to collaborate with different teams and colleaguesDiligenceSocial thinkingApplications are welcome from the following fields: Information Technology and IT relevant specializations, Engineering, Business InformaticsPlace of work: Budapest, Hungary. The internship is in hybrid mode, but at least 50% from our office – Graphisoft Park FeladatokTHE STRUCTURE OF THE VOCATIONAL / ROTATION BASED TRAINING PROGRAMWhat is important?You should have minimum 3 active semesters left from your (BSc or MSc) studies as of September 2023. At the beginning of the program the participants get an intense, 2-weeks-long technical training (Cloud Technologies, ABAP). In each following semester 2-4 days of technical or soft-skills training will be provided. Beside the trainings the students are working as VT trainees:If you join us for 3 semesters, in the first semester you should work in 16 h / week and from the 2nd semester 20h / weekIf you join us for 4 semesters, in the first year 16 h / week and from the 2nd year 20h / week working hours will be requiredHowever in special cases also the 32h will be allowed – according to agreement How does the program work?In each semester the participants could go to different projects in a rotational way which ensures to get to know many aspects of software development or any other business area (see above)During the summertime, in July you are asked to work in 40h / week, in return in the first three weeks of August you will be freeYou can also do your mandatory practice or diploma work under the umbrella of the VT programThe program can be very well combined with your university schedule. At the end of the program the participants get a certificateThe next batch will start in August 2023 ElőnyökThe Vocational / Rotation based Training Program at SAP Hungary offers a range of possibilities that allows you to continuously broaden your knowledge, skills and pave the way for a successful career. This wide-ranging program of studies provides for students an opportunity to develop themselves personally and professionally. Upon completion of your studies, you can consider employment in almost any area of the company. Many graduates of the program find their ideal job in the areas of software development, services & sales, product support, cloud operations or in the consulting. If you are in your Bachelor studies during your VT time and would like to continue your Master studies afterwards, the VT program offers you also a solution, the Master VT program.  Success is what you make it. At SAP, we help you make it your own. A career at SAP can open many doors for you. If you’re searching for a company that’s dedicated to your ideas and individual growth, recognizes you for your unique contributions, fills you with a strong sense of purpose and provides a fun, flexible and inclusive work environment.There is an endless opportunity for learning, participating in many techie and non-techie events, doing sports with us! You will get a continuous mentoring for accompanying You in your first steps within the company.
