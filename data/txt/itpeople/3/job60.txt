Come and work at their company, where a cheerful atmosphere leads you on the path of development and professional knowledge! Thanks to the continuous expansion, they are looking for a new leader for their IT Customer Support team in the following position:German-speaking Service Desk Team LeaderYour main tasks will be:-to watch over the IT service desk team-to coordinate operational tasks daily, like magnifying unresolved cases to relevant teams-to motivate the team to achieve the target KPIs-to run, analyse and make reports-to perform technical training and keep the training material up to date-to register the best practices and to share your knowledge with your colleaguesThe key requirements are:-High school diploma-Fluency (on a business level) in German and English-Knowledge gained using the Microsoft Operating System-Minimum 2 years of practice as an IT Service desk analyst-Minimum 1 year of experience in people managementThe main advantages:-Having a background in team leading-Advanced knowledge in some of the following applications and business technology: Windows, Office, SFB, Outlook, O365, ActDir, SAP, CRM, ERP, etc.The benefits:-developing soft and hard skills with internal training-attractive salary and benefits, private health insurance-amiable work atmosphere-career growth opportunities
