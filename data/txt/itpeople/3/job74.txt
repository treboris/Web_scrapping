SAP SD Expert – remote (11545)Our client is a global, family-owned German company that develops market-leading solutions for the solar and automotive industries. They are looking for a SAP SD Expert to their Budapest-based office.The work can be managed full remotely – with time-to-time office presence – from any location in Hungary.Main tasks, roles and responsibilities:• Detailing the solution design in close cooperation with our SAP-SD solution architects• Customizing in SAP to fulfill business requirements• Customizing in CAS genersis world to fulfill business requirements/Customizing in SAP C4C to fulfill businessrequirements• Steering of external consultants• Creation of programming requests for our internal development team or for external programmers• Testing• Support of processes in the area of automatic warehouses and the interface to SAP ERP• Participation in the S/4 rolloutWork experiences:• 3 or more years of experience• Experienced working in an international, virtual teamTechnical/ IT knowledge:• Deep SAP knowledge in the area of SAP-SD and in the surrounding SAP modules• Experience with processes of an automotive supplier• Knowledge in SAP C4C/CAS would be beneficial, but is not a must, it can be provided via trainingLanguage:• Good English skills (spoken and written)
