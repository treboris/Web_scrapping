Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentAegon is a multinational financial services group headquartered in The Hague, the Netherlands. The company has multiple locations across the globe, with core markets in The Netherlands, the UK, and the US, growth markets in China, Brazil, and Iberia, and an Asset Management organization with locations in the UK, US, NL, and Hungary. Aegon Global Business Solution Center is supporting these businesses with IT, Finance, Risk, Model Validation, Actuarial, Audit, and Asset management services with a growing team of approx. 300 people, based in Budapest, Hungary.
At Aegon, they foster a diverse workforce and an inclusive culture. They are a people business, and they recognize that delivering excellent client outcomes comes from harnessing distinct perspectives. They value diversity in the full range of human uniqueness without limitation, including race, gender, ability, language, culture, beliefs, age, origin, background, perspectives, and experiences.Elvárások / RequirementsQualifications
Two years of related work experience.
Working knowledge of project management methodologies and principles (e.g., Prince 2, PMP, PMBOK).
Proficiency using MS office project (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
Analytical and organizational skills to plan, prioritize and analyze project data.
Interpersonal and communication skills to work effectively with all levels of staff and management and proactively resolve issues to complete assignments on time.
Preferred Qualifications
Associates Degree in computer science, business, or relative field.
Amit kínálunk / Offer
Support the development, maintenance, and analysis of reporting at the portfolio and project level, including exception reporting of portfolio issues.
Work closely with the PMO leadership team, portfolio, and project managers to ensure that project portfolio information is maintained and available for planning and forecasting.
Create project plans and team spaces within the planning tool.
Support the time capture function within the planning tool.
Process work and project requests received into the intake tool.
Develop, maintain, and distribute reporting metrics.
Apply company PMO project standards and methodology, including quality assurance analysis.
Support TA PMO best practices by seeking opportunities to make efficiencies and improvements for recommendations to the PMO leadership team.
Kapcsolattartó / Information
Competitive salary
Comprehensive health package
 Brand new office at an excellent location
 Hybrid working
Opportunities for growth and advancement
 A highly people centric, positive and supportive work environment
