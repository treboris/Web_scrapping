Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur partner is one of the world's leading automotive technology companies, with headquarters located in Veszprém. The factory produces high-quality sensors for vehicle safety systems for all car manufacturers in the world.
We are looking for a Software Development Engineer for their growing team. Elvárások / RequirementsThe requirements you should fulfill:
Bachelor or Master’s degree in any field of engineering or computer science
Good knowledge of C programming language
Problem solving, practical thinking, engineering mindset
Confident English communication
Collaborative, open-minded, team player attitude
It's an advantage if you also have:
Knowledge of programing languages like C++, C#, Python, Java, etc.
Experience in embedded programming, real-time systems
Knowledge of relevant automotive standards (ASPICE, AUTOSAR)
Automotive tool knowledge (e.g.: Vector, Lauterbach)
Experience with agile development methods
Amit kínálunk / OfferThe following challenges await their new colleague:
Software Development of Advanced Driver Assistance System projects in embedded environment
Analyze internal and customer requirements
Develop new solutions according to software requirement specifications and relevant standards
Following and applying software development processes (ASPICE, AUTOSAR)
Analyzing root causes of detected failures
Evaluate test results and resolve the detected issues
