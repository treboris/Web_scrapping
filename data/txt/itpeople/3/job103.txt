System Installation Specialist (German speaking) állás Budapest
AWS
remote
Szoftverfejlesztő állások 
Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentJoin our global leading company in the power plant industry!
We are looking for a german speaking installation specialist for their remote expert center. You will perform the installation and configuration of the automated data collection system. You will also have the opportunity to interact with customers from all over the world.Elvárások / Requirements
Solid technical skills in IT system administration, network topology & scripting
Fluency in German and English
Interest in working in the field of power plant computer architectures, consisting of servers, clients and network devices such as firewalls, routers, switches, etc.
Strong communication, collaboration, active listening, and problem-solving skills
Amit kínálunk / Offer
Execution of software installation on a distributed control system
Ensuring the collection and upload of plant data to an AWS cloud which is performed automatically on a daily basis via Data Agents installed on the customer architecture
Managing patches and upgrades
Responsible for the remote data acquisition for a growing fleet of over 400 power plants
You will be the first point of contact for defined services for our customers on a global scale
You will help make energy systems up to date, less vulnerable and more reliable
Kapcsolattartó / Information
Be part of a global team
Internal training opportunities
Gain special knowledge in the power plants industry
Competitive compensation package
