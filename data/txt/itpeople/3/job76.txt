SAP QM Consultant (11621)We are looking for an SAP QM Consultant on behalf of our prestigious multinational client with worldwide presence.Responsibilities:• Provide analysis and support for IT projects relative to quality management (SAP QM) including supporting data migration and risk management.• Provide support of SAP QM module including support and testing of incidents, continuous improvements, rollouts, releases, enhancement packages, and upgrades while following Global CAB processes.• Develop functional specifications for ABAP programs.• Interface with business users and analyze business processes and specify/configure application to efficiently support those needs.• Coordination and moderation of solution design workshops, recording of requirements and preparation of decision templates• Provide 2nd level and 3rd level support of Incidents in compliance with defined SLA’s• Analysis of errors and implementation of troubleshooting and documentation• Support the training and knowledge management of the key user organization.• Participate in defining and integrating with other ERP modulesProfessional Skills:• Strong Quality Management business process and system knowledge. Ability to recognize problems and implement solutions.• Knowledge of quality management concepts and transactions, particularly within the automotive and rail industries.• Minimum 3 years SAP QM experience including experience configuring SAP QM• Experience in full-cycle implementation of SAP QM.• Basic cross-functional SAP knowledge in logistics modules• Experience with IT Ticketing system “ServiceNow” desirable• Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or other related discipline.• English for daily usage on proficient level B2 (C1 desirable)Nice to have:• Experience in S/4 Hana a plus.
