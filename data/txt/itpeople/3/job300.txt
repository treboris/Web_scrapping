Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur client is a global software engineering and IT outsourcing services supplier, looking for a Junior Golang Developer. You’ll be part of a team of professionals who are ready to find the best tailor-made IT solutions for their multinational clients in various industries and solve complex problems. You’ll create high-quality software products that move digital marketing business forward. Our client believes in agile methods, quick hypothesis testing, and shining eyes. They need you, a Golang developer, to help them make all of their plans come to fruition!Elvárások / RequirementsWhat our client expects from you
At least 1-year work experience in the IT sphere
Strong knowledge of Golang and understanding of the concurrency model of the language;
Excellent knowledge of HTTP protocol;
Experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and aspiration for perfection;
Good knowledge of SQL, experience with one of the relational databases;
skills in working with Git;
Strong technical English (conversational, written)
Will be a plus:
Experience with one of the frameworks Vue, React, Angular;
Experience with NoSQL;
Experience using Docker.
Amit kínálunk / Offer
Together with the mentor, a professional development plan will be developed specifically for you;
You will hone your skills in developing web applications and using modern frameworks;
Learn the intricacies of working in a team of responsive and professional colleagues;
You will improve your knowledge of English at an incredible pace;
You will have a chance to test your skills in a real project.
Kapcsolattartó / Information
Stable long-term work environment
Competitive salary with a flexible system of revisions

Comfortable A+ office in Budapest
