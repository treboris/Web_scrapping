SAP QM IT Expert - remote (11471)Our client is a global, family-owned German company that develops market-leading solutions for the solar and automotive industries. They are looking for a SAP-QM IT Expert to their Budapest-based office.The work can be managed full remotely – with time-to-time office presence – from any location in Hungary.Main tasks, roles and responsibilities:• Participate in worldwide projects to rollout and extend the current CAQ template within SAP PM and Syncos.• Supporting the CAQ IT team in migrating processes from the CAQ environment used today into SAP QM.• Collection of business requirements with the business stakeholders and creation of a solution design in close cooperation with the team of CAQ solution architects and external partners.• Customizing in SAP–QM and Syncos and deep collaboration with the experts of the surrounding modules• Creation of programming requests for our internal development team or for external programmers• Steering of external consultants and / or developers• Testing• User support, incident handling and standard service fulfillmentSpecial Tasks/ Key challenges:• Support of processes in the area of CAQ• Participation in the S/4 rolloutRequirements• 3 or more years of experience• Experienced working in an international, virtual team• Deep SAP knowledge in the area of SAP-QM and in the surrounding SAP modules• Beside SAP-QM we are using modules from Syncos. Knowledge in the Syncos modules would be beneficial, but it is not a mustLanguage:• Good English skills (spoken and written)
