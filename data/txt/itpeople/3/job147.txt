MEDICAL REVIEWERIf you have always dreamed of working within a multinational company, here is an excellent opportunity to try it! Our partner company performs its daily work in the IT services and business solutions fields with nearly 3000 employees. Thanks to the continuous expansion, we are looking for a new colleague for the following position:Medical ReviewerTasks:• Handle event reports about medical and drug adverse cases in the safety database.• Ensuring compliance with global and local procedural documents and local implementation of clients and the company’s policies and procedures.• Maintaining knowledge of the marketed drug currently under litigation.• Resolving queries relating to drug coding and AE coding in MedDRA.• Taking responsibility to provide solutions for medical queries during the case handling by case handlers.• Identifying medically important eventsRequirements:• University Medical Degree (or Alternate physician degree)• 0-2 years of experience in the field of Pharmacovigilance (advantage)• Good knowledge of pharmacovigilance regulatory requirements (both the US and EU are advantageous)• Excellent communication skills (verbal and written)• Being able to present clinical data and having a critical approach• Being able to interpret, and evaluate scientific data• Having an analytical mind• User-level IT skillsBenefits:Wide range of benefits, including a private health care packageCompetitive salary with Cafeteria benefitsCompany events, hobby clubs, and sporting initiativesContract type: full-time (40 hours/week), indefinite, hybridLegal requirements: you need to be currently eligible to work in Hungary
