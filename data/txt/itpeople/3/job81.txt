SAP GTS IT Expert - remote (11454)Our client is a global, family-owned German company that develops market-leading solutions for the solar and automotive industries. They are looking for a SAP GTS IT Expert to their Budapest-based office.The work can be managed full remotely – with time-to-time office presence – from any location in Hungary.Main tasks, roles and responsibilities:• Detailing the solution design in close cooperation with our solution architects• Customizing in SAP and in the corresponding foreign trade systems (AEB) to fulfill business requirements• Creation of programming requests for our internal development team or for external programmers• Steering of external consultants and / or developers• Testing• User support, incident handling and standard service fulfillment• Support of processes in the area of foreign trade• Participation in the S/4 rolloutWork experiences:• 3 or more years of experience• Experienced working in an international, virtual teamTechnical/ IT knowledge:• Deep SAP knowledge in the area of SAP-GTS, in the surrounding SAP modules and the interface technology between those• Beside SAP-GTS we are using AEB tools in some areas. Knowledge in the AEB tools for foreign trade and on the interface to SAP would be beneficial, but it is not a mustLanguage:• Good English skills (spoken and written)
