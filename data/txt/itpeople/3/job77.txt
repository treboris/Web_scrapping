SAP IT Senior Specialist for Master Data Processes – remote (11472)Our client is a global, family-owned German company that develops market-leading solutions for the solar and automotive industries. They are looking for a SAP IT Senior Specialist for Master Data Processes to their Budapest-based office.The work can be managed full remotely – with time-to-time office presence – from any location in Hungary.Main tasks, roles and responsibilities:• Close cooperation with our solution architects.• Customizing in SAP to fulfil business requirements.• Creation of programming requests.• Testing.• Support processes in the area of engineering changes, runout management and samples.• User support, incident handling and standard service fulfilment.• Participation in the S/4 rollout.Also, right now there is an opportunity of getting involved in the implementation of SAP S/4 HANA!Special Tasks/ Key challenges:• Support of processes in the area of SAP-PP• Participation in the S/4 rolloutRequirements• Deep SAP knowledge (S/4 preferred) in the area of material master data, BOMs, routings.• 3+ years of experience.• Good understanding of ALE technology.• Knowledge in iPoint- or comparable tools for sustainability and/or ILC add-on’s would be beneficial.• Good overview of the main processes in a producing company.• Experienced working in an international, virtual team.Language:​​​​​​​• Good English skills (spoken and written)
