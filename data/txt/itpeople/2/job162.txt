Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur partner is an Australian technology company creating the future of mobility with software-defined driverless transport. To build this new future and respond to growing demand, we are on the hunt to employ fresh thinking, technical minds to join their dynamic team, where you will continue to innovate and thrive together.
The company develops safety-rated, autonomous-ready, digital control systems (Digital Backbone) and modular vehicle platforms (Blanc Robot), for global commercial applications. They are developing the next generation of mobility, where vehicles run entirely on software and are programmable, improving efficiency & sustainability.
It creates an inclusive environment that empowers employees and embraces diversity. They welcome and encourage everyone who has a passion and curiosity for technology, automotive and innovation to explore the open positions and join this growing team.Elvárások / Requirements
An Engineering or Computer Science degree, or equivalent experience
A passionate interest in electric transport and an interest in developing new generations of electric vehicles
Proven experience in building and releasing production software packages in ECU's or controllers in relevant engineering projects
Proficiency in embedded software development in C/C++, and model-based development in Simulink
Proficiency with automotive or industrial communications such as CAN and Ethernet
Experience with the v-model automotive software development lifecycle
Experience with software functional safety implementations and preferably, ISO26262 certifications
Strong understanding of vehicle dynamics, control theory and sensors
Enjoy working in a fast-paced and growing global team environment
Ability to work independently with a strong team ethos
Capable of mentoring engineers in the software engineering domain
Minimum of 15 years of experience in software development in industry
Proficient in Hungarian and English
Amit kínálunk / Offer
Lead a software team developing safety-critical and mobility-focused embedded software
Lead new strategies and develop adaptions to existing algorithms for vehicle motion control, user interfaces, API's, actuator and energy management with the software development team
Manage implementation of systems tests, debugging and diagnostics and analyze resulting data
Manage a team planning, designing, and optimizing automated test cases and CI/CD pipelines with the software test team
Participate in design and code reviews within the software development lifecycle
Generate and analyze requirements and specifications from stakeholders
Collaborate with other software engineers, electrical, mechanical, systems, and product teams to deliver solutions
Kapcsolattartó / Information
Work at an exciting technology company within Hungary delivering globally in a multi-national group
Enjoy a clear sense of ownership in your field, with the satisfaction of working on developments that create the future of a new category of autonomous vehicles
Work alongside a friendly and passionate team on cutting edge technology
Have the opportunity to travel to other company offices including Australia and the UK
Enjoy modern office space and great work environment
