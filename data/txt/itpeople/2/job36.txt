											Our multinational client looks for a new colleague asSystems Design EngineerIn the Interchange MFT team we are responsible for the file transfer tools, used for both internal and external connections.Our multinational client looks for a new colleague asSystems Design EngineerIn the Interchange MFT team we are responsible for the file transfer tools, used for both internal and external connections.  On the one hand, we support the products Connect: Direct and UDM (Universal Data Mover) on both Mainframe and Open Systems. This makes us the point of contact for the support teams when more complex file transfer problems arise. At the same time, we manage file transfers to and from external parties via the Synchrony Gateway and Seeburger.  After a short training and introduction period you will be responsible for setting up, installing and maintaining the middleware components on Windows, Unix and / or Linux servers and zOS. This brings you into contact with all possible aspects of file transfer.  A selection from the range of tasks: Manage and update the current file transfer frameworks Assisting the other ICT teams in setting up new transfers Establish and maintain external connections In terms of internal, and external file transfer  Qualifications Advanced knowledge of Linux Knowledge of file transfer concepts, ranging from certificates, codepages, protocols, ... Scripting skills are a plus Sense for a very varied and broad deployment due to the integration atmosphere: deployment on both Open Systems and mainframe, packaging, maintaining scripted frameworks, installations  You are assertive, convincing, diplomatic and a self-starter at the same time. You like to take on responsibilities and you are able to work independently and in a problem-solving way.  At the same time, you have strong communication skills, as you regularly come into contact with internal customers or external parties. You enjoy working on a project basis and you can work constructively and professionally with colleagues within the team and throughout the organization in a sometimes time-critical environment.
