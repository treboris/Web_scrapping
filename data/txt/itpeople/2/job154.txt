Senior Business Application Support Analyst állás Országos
Mobil fejlesztő állások 
Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentAegon is a multinational financial services group headquartered in The Hague, the Netherlands. The company has multiple locations across the globe, with core markets in The Netherlands, the UK, and the US, growth markets in China, Brazil, and Iberia, and an Asset Management organization with locations in the UK, US, NL, and Hungary. Aegon Global Business Solution Center is supporting these businesses with IT, Finance, Risk, Model Validation, Actuarial, Audit, and Asset management services with a growing team of approx. 300 people, based in Budapest, Hungary.
At Aegon, they foster a diverse workforce and an inclusive culture. They are a people business, and they recognize that delivering excellent client outcomes comes from harnessing distinct perspectives. They value diversity in the full range of human uniqueness without limitation, including race, gender, ability, language, culture, beliefs, age, origin, background, perspectives, and experiences.Elvárások / Requirements

Amit kínálunk / OfferPrimary Purpose of the Position:
The Business Analyst Pool is within GTS Service Delivery which has knowledge of businessfunctions and GTS services. The Business Analyst team partners with internal clients, externalapplication vendors and GTS team members across the world to provide business requirementsdefinition, software quality assurance, development and on-going maintenance of globalapplications that are used to support the business needs of Aegon.
Business Analysiso Define and document business requirements related to system functionality, performance, security, and internal controls; document current state and future state processes for purposes of gap analysis, process improvement opportunities and training; obtain approvals from the business sponsor.o Communicate effectively with external and internal teams to ensure the delivery of the solutions and/or project’s functional requirements.o Participate or coordinate technology evaluations by assisting business areas with changes or selection of systems. Participate or coordinate Request for Proposal processes, impact analysis and feasibility studies. Maintain relationships with IT application vendors during selection processes and implementation projects. Provideconsultancy and research possibilities with respect to solutions and cost (support business case preparation).o Interact with the business to know and understand their processes and (IT) needs. Be the liaison between the business units, technology teams and support teams.o Liaise with peers in country-units and external parties in the field of Business Analysis, Project Management, Business Process Management, Application Management and Test Management, to exchange best practices, knowledge, and processes and to mature and improve Business Analyst role and expertise/skills.
Quality Assuranceo Prepare test cases based on knowledge of how the system is designed, requirements and expected results. Execution of detailed test cases/scripts for functional testing and regression testing. Track any issues and follow up on the related resolution.o Coordinate user acceptance testing with business end-users of application.o Ensure the quality of documents and other deliverables meets quality standards, appropriate methodology and acceptance criteria.
Operational Supporto Preparation of application administration documents, end-user documentation, and/or application training to end-users.o Assist in troubleshooting/resolution of issues. Ensure appropriate tickets are created to record and assign issues and document resolution.o Utilize change management process to ensure modifications or upgrades are authorized, tested when applicable, approved, and implemented appropriately.o Act as point of contact with application vendor and customer to coordinate new releases, updates, or changes.o Ensure proper application monitoring is in place and reviewed. Coordinate with technical staff if there is an issue.o Understanding risk and compliance controls and working with GTS Compliance team to ensure controls are met and/or mitigated.
 Conforms with and abides by all regulations, policies, work procedures, instruction, and all health and safety rules.
Exhibits regular, reliable, punctual, and predictable attendance.
