We are looking for a
Junior cloud and devops engineer
Component Soft, an official Kubernetes Services Provider and Training Partner as well as an Openstack Consulting Partner and Training Partner, provides consulting, support and training services for global and midsized telecom and IT services companies throughout Europe and North America. Our international team works mainly online from several European countries on complex projects and instructs the professionals of our clients using the most popular and cutting edge cloud and devops technologies such as Kubernetes, Istio, Openstack, Ansible, CI/CD tools etc. Currently we are looking for a junior cloud and devops engineer for our expanding consulting team.
Task:
Building, upgrading, supporting and later planning mission critical cloud systems using containers, Kubernetes, Istio and other cloud-native technologiesAutomating the installation and operation of these systems with Ansible, CI/CD tools, Python and GolangInstructing other project team members about technologies you are an expert inWriting technical documents about the systems you’ve built and problems you’ve solved
Expectation:
BSc degreeLinux sys.admin, networking, and virtualization skillsExperience in operating mission critical serversGood oral and written communication skills in EnglishAble to work independently and in teams, on multiple projects in the same period, even under stress and with unexpected problems causing quick switching among tasksWillingness to engage in continuous lifelong learning
Other:
Experience with the following technologies: Docker, Kubernetes, Istio, Gitlab, GithubExperience with AWS, MS Azure or Google CloudExperience with the following automation and programming tools: Ansible, any CI/CD tool, Python, GolangExperience at working in large international IT projectsExcellent oral and written communication skills in English
What we offer:
- Excellent salary for excellent performers- Working on complex international projects in multinational teams.- Onboarding trainings on the cloud and devops technologies used in our projects (Kubernetes, Istio, Ansible, CI/CD tools, Python, Go etc.)- Participating in European cloud and devops conferences after one year of successful work- Hybrid work, mainly from home with 1-2 days per week in the office- Permanently practicing and enhancing your English language skills- Family friendly workplace- Free lunch, coffee and refreshments in the office- Laptop and mobile phone

How to apply/ contact information:
Please include your CV to your application. The next steps of the selection process will be:1. A short video call to discuss your CV and motivation2. Solving a few real life IT problems to prove your technical skills3. Final personal meeting to discuss your and our expectations about the position
