We are searching for a
CSP Test Development Engineer
Task:
Description and Requirements A Cloud Service Provider (CSP) Test Development Engineer is responsible for the definition, development and management of test code and utilities that are incorporated into the manufacturing test process for Hyperscale servers(L10) and rack(L11) products manufactured by Lenovo. Job duties include: designing, writing, validating, and maintaining processes and code for automating the manufacturing test process. In this role you will be responsible for integrating these solutions into our test platform application for deployment into production. In addition, the expectation is that you will define test strategies for these products to ensure maximum commonality and  optimal performance on the manufacturing line.As a CSP Test Development Engineer will also work with Hardware and Software Development Engineers and external partners to build custom processes as required. Tactically you must be ready to react to any production issues as they arise and support worldwide manufacturing operations. Strategically, the expectation is that the successful candidate will be constantly looking for ways to optimize and improve the overall process for manufacturing efficiency. Responsible Tasks include:Interfacing with product development and partner teams to establish test requirements for products and solutions.Design and manage manufacturing test processes for server productsDevelop automated scripts and software for product test and ensure it is compatible with our manufacturing test platformInterface with multiple fulfilment sites to enable and manage product fulfilment processes and successful product deliveryParticipate in product code reviewsOpen and manage product defects that impact fulfilmentProvide Level3 production support for WW fulfilment sitesIssue regular status reports to Customer Account and WW Engineering teams.Root cause analysis, product debug, and problem resolutionInterface with fulfilment and manufacturing sites to provide critical order management supportManage and optimize cycle time to drive manufacturing efficiencies
Expectation:
Requirements:BS or equivalent Degree in Engineering (Computer Engineering, Computer Science, etc)Extensive scripting experience with Python (3.x preferred), Java and Rest APIExperience with code versioning and tracking systems (CVS, git, SVN)Experience with server and storage solutionsExperience with manufacturing test process development and deploymentSelf-motivated team playerAble to apply engineering conceptsEffective communicatorExperience with root cause analysis & problem resolution
