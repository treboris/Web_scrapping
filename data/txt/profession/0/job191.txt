Scrum Master (DWH & BI Solutions) (100-16-42887/PR)
Feladatok:
The company is the international shared service company and has its registered office in Munich. As a Scrum Master, you will be part of an agile, cross-functional team and you will be allocated to development or production activities and will be responsible for 2-3 teams.Together with your international team, you will take over end-to-end ownership for various business applications, contribute to world-wide rollouts and guarantee stable solutions.Responsibilities: - Align with respective product owners and team members to understand the business requirements of the customers - Ensure teams follow the defined Agile processes and methodology while actively managing the scrum process- Measure team delivery, maturity, and performance and review metrics with team to identify improvement opportunities- Act as a safe-guard to team by facilitating dependency management/risk management/impediment removal - Facilitate retrospective meetings and dailies as well as other scrum ceremonies - Participate in agile governance committees for whole program (cross-project stream)- Ensure team has a healthy product and Sprint backlog (in collaboration with Product Owner)- Utilize Jira and Confluence tools to manage day-to-day work
Elvárások:
- Practical Experience working in a scaled environment, across multiple scrum teams at a time- Practical Experience with Agile tool suites (e.g. Jira and Confluence)- Good understanding of DWH & BI solutions- Excellent communication skills in English
Amit a megbízónk kínál:
- Competitive salary package- Annual financial bonus – based on individual targets- Possibility to develop existing and new skills- Support of work-life balance- Comfortable working environment
