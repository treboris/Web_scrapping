IT Project Manager (Veszprém) (100-16-42992/PR)
Feladatok:
Our manufacturing partner company, in Veszprém county, is looking for software project manager for the following tasks: - Managing the product development and directing the disciplinary project team- Direct and manage project development, coordinate the development activities- Planning of the project (schedule, budget) in collaboration with the project team- Manage issues, participate and lead problem solving which are relevant for the product- Create and maintain project related documentation, reporting the project state to the management- Prepares and handles product and project maturity releases
Elvárások:
- At least 3 years working experience in project management- Specific experience (e.g. IT, software development, electrical)- Fluent English
Amit a megbízónk kínál:
- Cafeteria- Private Healthcare support- Flexible working hours and home office policy- Modern and innovative work environment- Career path model- Loyalty program- Learning opportunities- International engineering projects
