OTTO FUCHS Hungary Kft.
Inhouse Consultant SAP PP (remote)
OTTO FUCHS - We shape the futureExperience. Knowledge. Progress.Flights into space, worldwide transportation of people and international transport of goods, high technology in mechanical engineering, the shaping of large cities by modern architecture ...OTTO FUCHS is represented everywhere with ideas, products, and solutions.
Task:
As an in-house consultant, you will be responsible for the comprehensive support and further development of the SAP PP module and connected sub-systems.The implementation of adjustments in the SAP PP environment is part of your day-to-day businessYou act as a consultant between the individual plants and are a competent contact person for module-related issuesYou will be involved in national and international SAP projects and will be responsible for planning and implementing various sub-projects in the SAP PP environment, including project management. Your tasks include the analysis of processes in the area of production as well as their further development and optimization You will implement the design in the planned S4/HANA implementation project
Expectation:
You have successfully completed a degree with a focus on (business) informatics or comparable trainingYou have several years of SAP experience as a logistics consultant in the PP module; ideally APO-PP/DS as a foundationYou have very good knowledge in the conception and implementation of SAP logistics solutionsYou have knowledge in development (ABAP; Fiory)You have know-how and experience in planning, conception and implementation of SAP projectsYou are characterized by a business and customer-oriented way of thinking as well as a professional appearanceYou have strong teamwork and communication skillsBusiness fluent German and/or good English skills complete your profile
What we offer:
future-oriented training according to SAP Business Suite on HANA, SAP S/4HANA Collaboration in the migration to SAP S/4 HANAWork-life balance (mobile working, remote working, flexible flexitime model)Security (permanent position with an unlimited contract)
