System Engineer for Manufacturing Execution (f/m /div)*
Job description
Did you ever wish to leave your fingerprint on tomorrow’s manufacturing IT? State-ofthe-art technology is being created daily at our site in Cegléd and you can be an essential part of it. Embrace a stimulating challenge by joining us as a System Engineer for Manufacturing Execution. As a System Engineer for the Manufacturing Execution of our existing CAMSTAR MES (Manufactory execution system) module extension, you will be responsible for all new MES version upgrades, including end-to-end integration testing to sustain production from any downtime and stabilize operations. In your new role, you will: Support deep analysis and troubleshooting of complex topics and present overall root cause analysis, as well as long-term preventive measures.Be the key interface for existing local MES Solutions and take over the ownership to drive migration Projects for Global standard solutions inside Infineon.Manage and support various project initiatives related to Industry 4.0, Digitalization, and Workflow Automation inside the Semiconductor IndustryLeading project management as part of global team with internal and external staff.Be an interface to local stakeholders from the production line, global Core Team, Development Department, and external Vendors.
Profile
You are best equipped for this task if you have: A University Degree in IT, Computer Science, or a similar field.At least 3 years of working experience in the semiconductor or similar industries in a manufacturing environment.Leading and hands-on knowledge in Software development Lifecycle processes and best practice sharing.Strong solution or technical knowledge in Manufacturing Execution systems (ex: Camstar, Shopfloor Workflow) or equivalent would be beneficial.Project Management, Agile development and, ITIL certification would be added advantage.Fluency in English. Hungarian is a plus. Please send us your CV in English
Benefits
Cegléd: Wide range of training offers & planning of career development; International assignments; Different career paths: Project Management, Technical Ladder, Management & Individual Contributor; Holiday child care; Onsite social counselling and works doctor; On-site canteen; Corporate pension benefits; Performance bonus; Reduced price for public transport
Why Us
Part of your life. Part of tomorrow.We make life easier, safer and greener – with technology that achieves more, consumes less and is accessible to everyone. Microelectronics from Infineon is the key to a better future. Efficient use of energy, environmentally-friendly mobility and security in a connected world – we solve some of the most critical challenges that our society faces while taking a conscientious approach to the use of natural resources.The backend segments enable the development of comprehensive innovation across the entire value chain: starting with chip manufacturing, to wafer testing, preassembly, package development and development of new materials. The backend cluster combines the competencies for planning, productivity, innovation and quality, assembly and testing. * The term gender in the sense of the General Equal Treatment Act (GETA) or other national legislation refers to the biological assignment to a gender group. At Infineon we are proud to embrace (gender) diversity, including female, male and diverse.
