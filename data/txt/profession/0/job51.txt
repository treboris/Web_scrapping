ServiceNow Developer

Understanding of requirements received from business analystAnalyze incidents and identify correct resolutionsPerform small enhancements, incident resolutionsUnit Test all changes and bug fixesSupport Functional testing and perform bug resolutionsSupport implementation and rollout tasksWork in an Agile environment and communicate actively in Scrum meetingsUpdate JIRA tasks with correct progress information and report issues and obstacles in a timely mannerAdhere to standards for coding and documentationDocumentation, both in-line and offlineContribute to innovations and improvement initiatives
Job requirements:
3+ years of relevant experience in ServiceNowKnowledge of JavaScript language, server and client side coding, use of APIsExperience in server and client side development and debuggingFamiliarity with SCRUM / Agile working practicesAdherence to produce clean and easy-to-understand code and documentationsFamiliarity with REST APIs and SOAP would be a plusGood understanding of relational database structure, familiarity with SQL queriesAbility to design data models is good to haveEU passport or permanent residence in Hungary (no VISA support)
