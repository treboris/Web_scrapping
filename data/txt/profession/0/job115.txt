Scrum Master
At Loxon we build world-class software covering the entire lifecycle of lending and risk management and deliver them as integrated solutions for the financial services industry. We have major banking groups among our customers from 25+ countries in Europe and other global regions. We have a unique blend of competence of domain knowledge and technical skills with business professionals having strong background in banking and consulting, and software experts skilled in system & software design, data science and analytics.
About the job
We are building an environment where continuous improvement of the development process is in focus and where everyone’s common goal is to deliver outstanding software as fast as possible while having fun. We are looking for an experienced and results-driven Scrum Master who will actively contribute to the formation of our working culture. What you'll dosupport teams in the change management process;guide the team on how to use Agile/Scrum practices and principles;actively support teams to deliver value more effectively;collaborate with internal and external stakeholders to remove team impediments;design and facilitate the Scrum and Agile ceremonies;facilitate estimation, forecasting and outward coordination and communication on behalf of your teams;provide guidance to teams on the user story and acceptance criteria development and management, including story slicing, story mapping and backlog management;track and communicate agile metrics;coach teams and individuals facing challenges related to how they work together;grow a healthy, collaborative, continuously improving engineering culture;support and educate the Product Owner, especially with respect to grooming and maintaining the product backlog;collaborate with peers to spread effective ways of working across the company.
What we expect from you
3-4 years of experience as a Scrum Master;eagerness to work closely with your teams;understanding and experience with a variety of Agile approaches, including Scrum, Kanban and Extreme Programming (optional);extensive experience using and coaching on estimation, user story writing and sizing, retrospectives, and other Agile techniques;strong understanding of the Scrum/Agile events: Sprint Planning, Daily Stand-ups, Sprint Review, and Sprint Retrospective;strong sense of ownership over the facilitation of the team and its performance;deep understanding of communication, group dynamics and coaching;ability to help teams develop trust and safety;commitment to learning new things and improving upon existing challenges;experience with the use and configuration of one or more Agile management tools, such as Jira;excellent facilitating, coaching, and mentoring skills;Bachelor degree;fluency in Hungarian and in English.
Sector(s):
IT Development

We value if you have
demonstrated experience at implementing different scaled Agile methodologies;technical background;Scrum Master certificate.
What you can expect
an environment where you can continuously learn and grow;as a member of the Scrum Master community you will actively contribute to the formation of our working culture;competitive salary and career opportunities;recreational activities (wide range of sport opportunities: fitness pass, AYCM);diverse and flexible cafeteria (among others SZÉP Card, Edenred Card, sport and cultural event pass);Employee Assistance Program (EAP);health insurance;30 days of paid vacation;regular team building activities;in-house meetups and innovation competitions;learning opportunities (Udemy courses, workshops);professional working environment;opportunity to work from home.
