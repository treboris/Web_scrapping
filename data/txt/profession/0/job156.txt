Technical Specialist, M365
Hydro Global Business Services (GBS) is an organizational area that operates as an internal service provider for the Hydro group. Its ultimate purpose is to deliver relevant IT, financial and HR business services to all business areas within the company.
Role purpose:
Technical management of the M365 platform in Hydro (NHY).
Responsibilities:
Provide technical support in a global enterprise environment for Office 365 end-to-end servicesHandle requests, incidents, changes, and problem processes for the platform (3rd level support)Automate tasks and solutions in Office 365 by using eg. Powershell, contribute to the efforts managing the infrastructure from codeCollaborate closely with Microsoft and other suppliers; ensure good dialogue and exchange knowledge about business needs and product developmentUnderstand changes in the Microsoft platforms and manage these changes in Hydro Manage integration between M365 and other infrastructure applications and IT services in Hydro applying an automation focused approachProactively evaluate, test and verify new cloud tools and features based on business relevance. Handle testing regime of customizations, updates and integrations Document and maintain solution architecture Ensure that solution architecture and security for Office 365 comply with Hydro IS governance and enterprise architectureMaintain and contribute to Office 365 knowledge articles in ServiceNow
Requirements:
3-5 years of experience with Microsoft cloud technologies and Office 365Minimum BA degree (Information Technologies)Microsoft Office 365 certificatesStrong skills in Powershell or other scripting languages and commitment to grow in this areaIn-depth knowledge of all applications in the Office 365 platform is required. Knowledge & experience of ServiceNow is also preferredFluent English language knowledgeExperience in Office 365-related projects (eg. migration project)
Hydro offers:
Working at the world’s only fully integrated aluminum and leading renewable energy companyDiverse, global teamsFlexible work environment/home officeWe provide you the freedom to be creative and to learn from expertsPossibility to grow with the company, gain new certificatesAttractive benefit package
