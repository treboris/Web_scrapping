Service Delivery Specialist
Hospitality and Retail SystemsHRS (Hospitality & Retail Systems) is an international company with an unrivaled reputation for service and is a leading supplier of IT solutions for the HoReCa and retail sectors.HRS is Oracle's largest hospitality partner worldwide, providing coverage in over 75 countries to more than 3000 customers. Recognized as an ORACLE platinum partner, HRS offers a wide range of innovative solutions to its customers, these include: Property Management, POS, Spa & Guest Activities, Business Analytics, Payment Gateway, Mobile Applications, Channel Manager, Online Distribution Services, Finance and HR Systems, Passport Scanning & Recognition, plus much more.  
Task:
Main responsibilities:Installing, configuring and supporting Hospitality products as well as their associated interfaces.Ensuring that our PMS/POS solutions meet all the unique specifications and functions that are required by our Hospitality clients.Training clients in the use of application(s) and be able to demonstrate an understanding of current hotel management techniques, industry best practices and requirements.Be familiar with and adhere to the latest training and installation standards together with all project implementation procedures.
Expectation:
Main requirements:General technical IT experience in relation to installing hardware and the troubleshooting of that hardware.Good interpersonal skills, both with customers and cross-functional teams.Ability to work unsupervised or as a team member, this includes both the local office and wider company teams.Able to travel frequently within your region.Fluency in written and spoken English, as this is our primary language of communication.Hungarian native.
Other:
Experience in installing/configuring/training Hospitality PMS and/or POS products.Familiarity with PMS and/or POS systems (ORACLE or any other).Familiarity with Oracle PL/SQL is an advantage.
What we offer:
What we offer:International company.Young, enthusiastic environment.Excellent remuneration package.Wide possibilities for training and professional growth.
