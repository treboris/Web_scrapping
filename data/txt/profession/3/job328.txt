Expectation:
Bachelor's Degree or above in Computer Technical or IT-related discipline.1 or 2 years of experience as an IT engineer.Knowledge with Network: TCP/IP, Router, Switch, etc. Prefer with CCNA/CCNP certification or dedicated training.Knowledge with OS: WIN10/Windows Server.Knowledge of software for working: Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.Knowledge of the script: Python, JavaScript, etc.Familiarity with computer peripherals and applications.Fluent written and oral skills in English. Second language must be Chinese and/or Hungarian.Motivated, active, service-conscious, able to work in a team and independently.Accept the challenge and be willing to learn.Be able to accept business trips (occasional) and support for important meetingsovertime, occasional)Ability to solve problems quickly
