Senior Accounts Payable Representative - German Speaker
A Thermo Fisher Senior Accounts Payable Representative interacts with internal customers and suppliers via email or call to address requests regarding process, transactions – requisitions, purchase orders, invoices or vendor requests - and to provide solutions to any issues related to any areas of the end to end Purchasing process. They are responsible for resolving all assigned incoming cases quickly and accurately, demonstrating knowledge of the end to end process with focus on customer service and end user experience.
Key Responsibilities:
Resolves 80% or more of assigned internal and external customer cases without escalationAddresses process and training questions, requisitions and invoices status, approvals, how to update billing, or locate payment terms, as well as how to do order changes or resolve out of tolerance issuesEnsure outstanding customer experience by leveraging technologies like live chat & phone support in EnglishEngages with specialists (tier 2) after due diligence with the user, and after accurately documenting all the information within the case management toolEngages with category leaders, process owners or IT based on documented processesWorks with other departments within Thermo Fisher Scientific and with our suppliers to find solutions for both internal and external customers.Follows established service quality standards and meets established processing times and follow-up actions in accordance with departmental standardsStays abreast of new processes and initiatives within the company
Minimum Requirements/Qualifications:
Minimum of 1-2 years’ experience in a call center, customer service or AP Queries environmentWorking knowledge of Coupa or ServiceNow is a plusExcellent interpersonal skills, friendly and outgoing personality who enjoys assisting others.Demonstrates superior communication skills including probing, active listening and written skills.Demonstrates customer service mentality.Excellent problem solving skills and ‘can do’ attitudeAbility to exhibit patience and empathy and diffuse customer dissatisfaction.Demonstrates attention to detail and quality, and sense of urgency for quick resolutionAbility to work through and adapt to change, work both independently and within a team. Demonstrates resourcefulness.Ability to learn and apply new information.Ability to adapt to (frequently) changing priorities
Non-Negotiable Hiring Criteria:
1-2 year’s customer service experience requiredHigh School education required; college degree preferredFluent English and GermanExcellent listening, oral, and written communication skillsSolid understanding of computer basics (Windows, MS Office tools)
