i-Campaign Hungary Ltd, a Hungarian service provider specialized in Business Process Outsourcing and IT solutions, is currently looking for talented junior and experienced programmers to join our team. 
Task:
i-Campaign Hungary Ltd, a Hungarian service provider specialized in IT solutions, is currently looking for talented programmers to join our team.Our company is working with German companies to jointly develop their IT systems. For this we are looking for junior as well as senior software engineers. 
Expectation:
Knowledge in at least one of the following areas:- SAP ABAP experience, ideally in FI and CO- SAP Data Services- SAP Master Data Governance- Solid communication skills in English (written and oral)- Willingness to work with a multitude of technologies- You organize yourself with your team, also within agile frameworks, like Scrum or Kanban- Willingness to travel
Other:
- Bachelor’s or advanced degree in Computer Science or a related discipline- Ability to understand and apply business requirements quickly - Strong analytical-reasoning and problem-solving skills- Good debugging skills and solid work ethic- You enjoy passing on your knowledge to your colleagues and learning from them- Communication skills in German not required but preferred- Ideally experience in building and deploying applications- Multi talented Software Developer
What we offer:
- Competitive salary plus fringe benefits- Possible progression to a senior role- Possibility to gain experience in an interesting and challenging IT field- Brilliant working culture in a dynamic and ambitious environment- You work in a great team with international colleagues.
