Your place in the team 
To proactively increase revenue and penetration of IoT product categories by using product, process and pricing elements of the marketing-mix and to develop and manage services and portfolio over their entire lifecycle. 
You will be involved in the following tasks
Product Leadership and Knowledge: End-to-end responsibility for IoT products: Smart Parking, Smart Classroom, Smart bench products, -developments and portfolioInput to developing and implementing IoT Product Portfolio Strategy and PlanningIoT Product’s Lifecycle ManagementEnsure products deliver most recommended customer experienceCross functional teamworkEstablish strong relationship with Global and External partners 
We are looking for you if you have
Product development experience (2+ years) in the telecommunications/IT/ISP industry.Sound working knowledge of Mobile or Fixed or IoT solutions markets.A successful track record in product/segment marketing.Strategic thinking.Project management and E2E execution skills.Experience in the development of business cases, product and marketing plans, proposals and solution packaging.Experience in working with a consumer and enterprise sales force/sales channels and corporate clients.
