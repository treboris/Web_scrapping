As an IT System Analyst you will fulfil a crucial role in providing technical support related to our Customer Data Management system, either directly to users of the system or to service delivery functions. You will work closely with other IT System Analysts, Software Developers, Architects, Business Analysts, and IT operations experts to ensure reliable delivery of system modifications and ongoing operations. An ideal candidate shall demonstrate proven track record of specifying requirements and/or design, test IT technical solutions in business information systems and manage their implementation. The role also requires participating in the ongoing IT system support handling: handling incoming requests, resolving technical problems, identifying root causes and initiating IT solutions to enhance the stability and consistent, improved capability of his/her owned system
