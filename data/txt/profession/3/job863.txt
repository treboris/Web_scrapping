Currently we are looking for
ENGLISH SPEAKING SOFTWARE TESTER – BERLIN (E-T01)
Location: Berlin, Germany (onsite)
Salary: competitive, minimum 50.000 EUR gross per year, depends on experience
Type of employment: employee
About the position:
Our client provides graphics software that performs most of the painstaking work of creating data-driven slides for professional Excel and PowerPoint users. As part of our technical support team, the testing department checks for usability and understandability of new features. It is up to the testing department to find out if features are available for all use cases.Since the software is seamlessly integrated into Microsoft PowerPoint and Excel, they have to pay close attention to it's compatibility with the supported Microsoft Office products and Microsoft Operating Systems, and their respective updates. It is up to the testing department to review those updates, install and test them in detail. As a software tester, you will help improve products that over 1,000,000 professionals world-wide rely on.
Tasks:
As the software is constantly being developed you get to ensure the proper functioning of all new featuresEvery time the company issues a new version, it is up to you to perform a final release testMaintain, develop and enhance these automatic testsNew hardware testingChecking for faults and also for logical consistency and intelligibility of the company's productsDetailed and reliable reproduction of the error to fully understand, and subsequently resolve, the problem (every time an error occurs within the software, regardless of whether you, a customer or a colleague found it)After the error has been resolved by the developers, it is up to you to check if the problem has really disappeared
Requirements:
Fluent in English, German is a plusAt least 3 years of testing experienceAffinity with softwareStrong written communication skillsAttentive and detail-orientedPrecise, systematic approach to workHigh level of self-motivation (as the testers independently structure their work).
What we offer:
Family-friendly, highly flexible working hoursOpportunity to improve our software by giving direct feedback to our developer teamOpportunity to enhance existing processesDirect contact with our global customer baseVery competitive salaryA modern office located in the central historic district of BerlinWe will support with the relocation and work permit if needed
