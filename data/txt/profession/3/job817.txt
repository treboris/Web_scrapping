Master Data Specialist (with German) (1-1-40711/PR)
Feladatok:
Level up in your career, and make an impact by shaping and developing a new Business Support Center in Budapest!This opportunity is for you if you want to be an active part of a leading multinational company, and work within a modern, inspiring environment, with the latest technologies and processes. As a Master Data Specialist, you will be supporting financial services with adminstration and data management. You will be responsible for:- Handle financial data in the ERP system- Enter and maintain related documents in connection with suppliers, customers and invoicing- Review data entry periodically and ensure accuracy- Cooperate with Finance and Accounting teams and provide adminstrative support for them- Prepare reports- Manage change requests form external and internal partners
Elvárások:
What makes you the best candidate:- University degree- 1+ years of experience in Master Data / Excel reporting or Adminsitrative roles- Advanced Excel skills- Experience with an ERP system is an advantage- Fluent English language skills- Internediat German language skills (written only)
Amit a megbízónk kínál:
Our partner offers:- Fair working conditions and competitive payment- Regular working hours to support work-life balance- Health-conscious working environment with health insurance and leisure activities- Training and education programs to support personal and professional development
