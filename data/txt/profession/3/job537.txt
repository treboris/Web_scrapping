Network Operations specialist – L2/ L3
Our Client is a company with 35,000 employees in 40 countries on all continents, combining local expertise, worldwide reach and unmatched capabilities in R&D.
Our Client a Global Business Services (GBS) is an organizational area that operates as an internal service provider for the company group. Its ultimate purpose is to deliver relevant IT, financial and HR business services to all business areas within the company.
Role Purpose
As part of the Company Network Operations Team, the L2/L3 Specialist main responsibility is the management of the:
WAN (SDWAN)
LAN (local area network)/WLAN
DDI services (DHCP and IP address management)
Firewall management
and any additional services which might be added to the future.
Responsibilities
  Operation of corporate network / Incident and change management (3rd level support)  Vendor management  Manage and operate LAN and WLAN of all remote company sites globally  Handling daily mid and high level escalations  Support architectural decision  Create and maintain network documentation  Support the team members in their daily technical questions  Participate in internal network & security projects
Requirements
  3+ years’ experience in network operations / project team in a multinational environment  experience with WAN, LAN, WLAN, Firewall, VPN, DHCP, IPAM, Cloud proxies  Bsc. degree in Computer Science or a related area is preferred  CCNP Enterprise certification or equivalent knowledge is required  Fluent English is a must (verbal, written)  Strong knowledge of Cisco Wireless Solutions is an advantage.
We offer:
Working at the world’s only fully integrated aluminum and leading renewable energy companyDevelop yourself – specialists joining our team will have the opportunity to:Develop their technical knowledge on Azure (and evt. on AWS) platformsBuild strong automation skillsUnderstand how cloud platform governance and operational processes work at an enterprise scaleBe creative with platform modernization ideasDefine and improve processesWork with DevOps methodologyDevelop your language skills in EnglishModern office environmentUnlimited access to LinkedIn learning solutionsBenefits and 13th month salary, Christmas benefit, Cafeteria,Private Health Care InsuranceBonus (maximum 7% of the yearly salary)Company pension scheme membershipWork from home to protect your health,Multinational work environment with a solid background,In-career or out-of-position career development,Attractive salary, performance-related financial and moral recognition,Positive atmosphere, high quality, supportive work environment.
