MOL Group is an independent and international oil and gas company, headquartered in Budapest, Hungary. It is active in over 40 countries with a dynamic international workforce of 26,000 people and a track record of more than 100 years in the industry.
Join us in transforming MOL Group Consumer Services, one of the largest retailers in the CEE region. We, as Digital Factory has embarked on a journey to take this vast business to a new, digital era, both in terms of customer experience but also the way the organization is working.  We are delivering and operating two main platforms: Digital Data & AI platform to make internal operations more efficient and Digital Loyalty & CRM platform to turn the digital experience of our customers upside down. This generates immense impact the way people move around in the region, how the fuel, shop and eat at service stations.
We are searching for an enthusiastic talent to join the Digital Factory as a
Digital Business Analyst
The Digital Business Analyst is responsible for the advancement of digital business and their supporting technologies and contribution towards key business decisions on both a functional and technical level. He/She will demonstrate a good understanding on all digital Loyalty and e-commerce solutions and will map functional requirements to technical system features and functionality, furthermore responsible for contributing to all aspects of the analysis, design, development, building and testing of solutions within any Digital Factory project work.
