IT Administrator / Support
Gramont is a Swiss based SAP consulting company which provides innovative SAP solutions and products for our international customers in the field of PLM (Product Lifecycle Management), MDG (Master Data Governance) and EHS (Environment Health and Safety). We have offices in Switzerland, Germany, the USA, Hungary and India. 
Task:
Welcome to the leading SAP consulting company for PLM, MDG and EHS. We are an international team with unique professional expertise, technical know-how and next generation EHS and PLM solutions.As an IT Administrator you will take on an important key position in the company and ensure the smooth operation of the hardware and software. In addition, you will be the contact person for all IT service issues and advise our employees locally and worldwide.For our IT department, we are looking for a dedicated employee (m/f/d) in the area of IT support at the Budapest site as soon as possible.Above all, the following tasks are in focus:You will be the first point of contact for IT support and will supervise and support colleagues in all relevant IT issues.You are responsible for setting up and maintaining workstations and employee accesses.You will take care of the documentation and monitoring of the IT systems.Administration of our Microsoft 365 environment, Sophos VPN, etc.IT support for our Windows 10/11 clients.Installation and configuration of in-house hardware and software.Documentation of the IT landscape and IT processes.1st level support as well as onboarding and offboarding with the HR team.You will independently carry out your own projects.
Expectation:
RequirementYou have sound IT knowledge, based on an apprenticeship, a degree or a comparable qualificationYou have already gained experience in the IT sectorYou are willing to constantly develop yourself further and to open up new areas of responsibility for yourselfYour written and spoken English skills are goodProactive thinking, ability to work in a team and self-motivated action characterize youYour way of working is thorough, systematic and quality-conscious.
What we offer:
As part of the GRAMONT Group the employee will be:Part of an international team of world-class expertsFlat hierarchies with open doors and short decision-making paths in a relaxed but professional working atmosphereIndividual training opportunitiesEngage with innovative customers and partnersOpportunity to gain international work experience
