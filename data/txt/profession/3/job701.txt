Egészségbiztosítás
AVL is the leading supplier for automation and measurement devices for automotive companies worldwide. We believe that our innovations and products can lead to a better mobility solutions and for a sustainable future. We offer leading measurement and testing technologies for the entire powertrain system, based on a consistent, integrated, and open platform. We accelerate development increase flexibility and efficiency, and minimize our impact on the environment: AVL stands for the next generation of testing solutions.

Budapest, XI. district
