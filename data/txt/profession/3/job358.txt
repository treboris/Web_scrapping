.NET Developer (Salesforce)
As a .NET Developer with a Salesforce Field Services module focus, you will develop and implements software in support of a complex multi-platform application providing world-class Field Services scheduling and mobility to leaders in computer hardware and services. This is a dynamic, 24x7 global application suite used by the Unisys Digital Workplace Services Field Engineering business with applications and B2B interfaces to external customers and service providers.
Within this role, you will be responsible for the development, testing, and deployment of solutions for the application portfolio. You will have the chance to work closely with various functional and technical teams to translate business requirements into optimized, highly available, high-performing solutions. In this position, you will perform all phases of the software development life cycle; resolution of issues with existing systems; maintenance and deep support for solutions developed.
In this role, you will:
Have the possibility to work on an exciting migration project, learn a lot and work on Salesforce modules - mostly Field Services, and later on other business modules.Work effectively with architects and leads, including the ability to present technical solutions while showing a strong understanding of business activities and objectives.Develops high-level and detailed designs for Salesforce application integration.Develop, customize and/or configure software and the environment for the implementation project.Create system documentation and conduct training for support and deployment engineers when applicable.Develop system test plans and test cases.Provide deep-level technical support for the software developed.Be involved in release management and maintain a knowledge base of deployed environments.Complete all assigned activities within schedule; take ownership of assigned modules and work toward Quality & Timely delivery with continuous improvement.Provide on-call support (1 week/month), and usually work within the 9-18:00 or 10-19:00 timeframe.
You will be successful in this role if you have:
3+ years of experience with Salesforce development (Field Service or Service Cloud are preferred).Strong knowledge of the Force. com platform. You should be able to create complex workflows, create custom actions using apex and work with lightning web components etc.Been a part of at least 1 Salesforce service cloud implementation.3+ years experience with JavaScript, Visual Studio 2017 or above, ASP.NET MVC4 or above, C#, Web Services, REST API, XML/HTTP.Basic knowledge of node.js and REACT desiredA good understanding of product development lifecycle and version control systems is preferred.Strong basics in object-oriented programming concepts.Database knowledge and Developer tools (T-SQL, SQL Server Management Studio, TOAD, etc.) for SQL Server 2017, and experience with SQL and PL/SQL are desired.Excellent communication skills.Strong analytic and problem-solving skills.
