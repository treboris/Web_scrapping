ZF Hungária Kft. is searching for experienced colleague for the following position:
SAP Application Developer
Your Tasks:
Proactively gather, understand and manage requirements and expectations
Contributing to the entire cycle of product development, from first ideas and brainstorming sessions over creation of functional and technical specifications to continuously delivering solutions in an agile approach, based on business requirements and user stories
Implementation of applications in SAP ECC 6.0 and S/4 HANA environments including respective interfaces in collaboration with other teams across the internal organization and external developers
Responsible for end-to-end service delivery and operations (development, test management, deployment, maintenance, incident management)
Participation in corporate projects and orders, self-responsible performing of work packages
Your profile:
Degree in (Business) Information Technology or related disciplines
3+ years of ABAP development experience with good knowledge of Core ABAP, OOP, Enhancements, system integration, SAPUI5, oData and CDS
Experience and knowledge in agile software development as well as excellent analytical skills and process thinking
Fluent spoken and written English (Further language skills e.g. German are an advantage)
Flexibility, good teamwork experience and strong communication skills in an international environment
We offer:
Stable company background and long-term working possibility
Full of challenges and high-level professional activities at an international company
Participation in international projects and commission to foreign countries
Career opportunities, continuous training program and language courses
