AVL is the world's largest independent company for the development, simulation and testing of powertrain systems (hybrid, combustion engine, transmission, electric drive, batteries, fuel cell and control technology) for passenger cars, commercial vehicles, construction, large engines and their integration into the vehicle.
Fullstack Developer
Budapest, XI. District 
Your responsibilities:
You will join the development of our product which supports hundreds of AVL’s worldwide software development projects for the biggest car manufacturers, helping our Engineers and Project Leads to work according to the process and assure efficiency and quality of servicesThe product is an ecosystem for Automotive software development consisting of an Application Lifecycle Management software and our Custom softwareYou will participate in the product development from concept definition to deployment
Your profile:
Degree in Computer Science, IT Engineering or equivalentKnowledge of Java language and SpringBootKnowledge of Angular (v14+)Knowledge of relational databases (MS SQL Server)Experience with Python is an advantageFluency in written and spoken EnglishGood communication, presentation skills, strong team player
What we offer:
Health-oriented working environment in Budapest SciencePark (height-adjustable desks, 3–screen workstations, Private medical care, Free fruits, Sport & game nights, etc.),Work in a young group with supportive, friendly atmosphere with dedicated induction-training period, mentor program and further technical, language & soft-skill trainings,Flexible working time, possibility to work from home,International working environment and experience,Develop solutions for the largest car manufacturers and their suppliers.
