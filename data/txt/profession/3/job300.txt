ZF Hungária Kft. is searching for experienced colleague for the following position:
Development Engineer/ Fejlesztő mérnök
Tasks & Responsibilities:
Participation in R&D projects of electric driveline and conventional gearbox developments;Design of driveline-, gearbox housings (EV, MT, AMT);Design and detail design of mechanical components of complete drivelines;Performing packaging studies;Technical support of project engineers, in-house production and suppliers;Performing technical calculations;Recommendation and elaboration of design changes;Active contribution in D-FMEA sessions;Support the development team in technical risk analysis with standardized checklists and create reports;
Requirements:
BSC or MSC degree in Mechanical Engineering;Strong (at least 2-5 years) experience in casting and component modelling; (Tool: Creo preferable);Analytical capability, capable to abstract a functional analysis and give an objective rating of the remaining risks;Negotiation-level language knowledge of English and/or German;Excellent negotiation and communication skills;Self-confident and reliable;Independent and responsible work;Ability to take initiatives,Creativity, innovative approach;
Advantages:
Knowledge of drivelines and gearboxes in Commercial vehicle technology;User-level knowledge of PDM-systems (Intralink - Windchill);FMEA basic knowledge (Technical Risk Analysis Tools);
What offer:
Stable company background and long-term working possibilityFull of challenges and high-level professional activities at an international companyParticipation in international projects and commission to foreign countriesCareer opportunities, continuous training program and language coursesCompetitive salary and performance-related bonusCafeteria with optional elementsSchool start support
