Customer Service Representative with English
As a constantly growing company, Danfoss is looking for motivated employees who want to work with us on our success. We are looking for a Customer Service Representative with English to join our diverse team.
The primary function in this role is to provide commercial support such as order management, order entry, price checking, order clearing, compliance, complaint handling, coordination of delivery and billing.
Location: Budapest
The ideal candidate possesses these skills:
Bachelor’s degreeMinimum 1-3 years of relevant experienceStrong English knowledgeIn depth knowledge of computerized order management processes and software programsCustomer focused mindsetTeam-oriented problem-solving skills and corrective action methodologyOrganization and time management skills
