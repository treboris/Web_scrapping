Amway is a large multinational family-owned consumer goods company and the leading direct selling company worldwide. Amway offers more than 450 high-quality products and innovative solutions in the categories of nutrition, beauty, and home care exclusively through independent distributors and the own webshop. The company was founded in 1959 in Ada, United States, and operates in more than 100 countries generating revenue of 8.9 billion USD in 2021. Amway’s top-selling brands are Nutrilite™ vitamin, mineral, and dietary supplements, ARTISTRY™ skincare and color cosmetics, and eSpring™ water treatment systems.
IT Support Specialist
Purpose of the role:
Provide Technical Support for all hard and software in both the offices and the warehouse. Manage the local server room and provide First Level Support to Users regarding all the IT related issues in the Offices and the Warehouse.
What you’ll do:
Facility:Contact with building owner in case there are issues with heating/water etc.Ordering office equipment if neededMaintaining building surveillance system and building access cards   IT:Purchase of PC/Server/LAN equipment in accordance with I&O EUROPE Infrastructure specification and CER approval.Budget preparation together with I&OConfiguration and Implementation of PC´s/Servers/LAN Equipment according to I&O Europe Installation Guidelines. The provision of First Line Support for all PC/Server/LAN Hardware and Software Supporting the pickline employees when any issues arise (Printer not working, Packages not being scanned, Pickcarts not working)Repairing small issues with Warehouse devices or contacting the required supplier to have it fixed.Application support for various systems (Red Prairie, Jaspersoft, Metapack, Hybris)WMS support (Order Maintenance ‘address issues’, account creation)Maintenance on printers in both office and warehouse (Defective printer replacement, Toner replacement, Paper Jams, Updating address books)Programming of the terminals on the forklifts or hand scannersThe provision of First Line Support for Affiliate PBXThe provision of Network Administration Functions for existing and future LAN equipment (patching for new Desktops, Printers or Phones)Minimize Downtimes in case of required Recovery Assist I&O Europe Telecommunication Specialist if required, to analyze and fix Router problems
What you’ll need:
University degree in Information TechnologyUp to 3 years of related experienceGood knowledge of Windows Server 2003 – 2022 and Microsoft Windows 7/10Understanding Microsoft Active DirectoryUnderstanding and experience with AzureMicrosoft Office 2016, 365Knowledge of the ServiceNow tool would be an additional advantageAble to communicate in English and Hungarian (verbal & written)General understanding of Network Technologies (LAN, WAN, TCP/IP)Familiar with License ManagementBasic knowledge of Network SecurityAble to work in an international teamProject-oriented method of workCustomer Service oriented approachKnowledge of WMS systems (Red Prairie, Blue Yonder)
What can you expect from us?
Online recruitment process The chance to become part of a dynamic, international working environmentThe opportunity to work for a global Fast-Moving Consumer Goods companyCompetitive salary & great bonus potentialAdditional day off for your BirthdayFlexible Working Hours Cafeteria systemEmployee discount for high-quality Amway products Various events for employees during the yearPossibility of growth inside of the organization
