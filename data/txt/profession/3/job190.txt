SAP PM Expert
Job purpose:
As our new colleague as SAP Domain Expert, you will configure/design, deploy and support SAP modules used by EUROAPI. You will be the custodian of the right usage of ITS standards and solutions to our entities in Europe, US and Japan. You will work in close collaboration with Business Process Owners and other ITS Business & Process Analysts to aid the functional users and ensure the right usage of ITS & Industrial standards for her/his domain(s). This includes gathering business requirements, translating them into functional requirements, recommending and implementing solutions by configuring SAP and working with ABAP developers and other technical teams to meet ongoing business needs and fix issues.
In this role you will be able to utilize your ability to challenge processes and practices, to impact and influence, as well as your change management and negotiation skills.
About the Business Unit:
Digitalization, business processes simplification and harmonization are key pillars of the EUROAPI strategy. To reach those ambitions, and boost operational excellence, the company has consolidated all existing SAP solutions into one ERP/SAP solution.
In this context, we are looking for talents to deploy and support its current and future core model solutions based on SAP technology.
Main Responsibilities:
The SAP Domain Expert deploys SAP core models around EUROAPI industrial plants and ensures the good usage of the module in scope.For this, in close collaboration with key users and project leaders, you willBe responsible for the below tasks related to the following SAP module: Plant Maintenance (PM)Work in collaboration with business to translate business requirements into functional specificationsProduce appropriate documentation and actively participate in development of technical and workflow solutionsIdentify impacts, define actions/remediations and prioritize them with stakeholdersWork closely with the SAP ABAP team to build technical solutions as per the business requirementConfigure the system and perform system integrated test with site dataSupport key-users in data migration from legacy system to the new solutionExecute the Cut-Over activities and support the site during the hyper-care phase after the Go-LiveTrain the key-users to the core model solutionTroubleshoot SAP system issues reported by the business users via Service Now
We expect the following skills/qualifications from our ideal candidate:
Academic background: Computer Sciences, System Analysis, Engineering, Business school or similar areasMaster’s degree or equivalentAt least 3 years work experience as internal or external SAP consultantSuccessful and considerable experience in SAP deployment in industrial plantsKnowledge on business processes related to the SAP moduleFluent in English (writing, speaking and effective communication internationally)Autonomy within agreed projects, milestones
Nice to have:
Experience in Life Science industriesProject Management knowledgeSkills to read ABAP code
Terms of employment:
Based in Budapest, this job requires to collaborate in a global and matrixial environment with international business and ITS teamsThis job requires to work within structured environments with formal procedures and documented deliverables, compliance rules and reporting dutiesThis job can require some business trips to ITS and industrial locationsEnglish is the usual working language
