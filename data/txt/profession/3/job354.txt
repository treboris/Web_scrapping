IT Business Analyst (Sales & Commerce)
Would you like to be part of a familiar, international team? Would you like to enjoy our passion, we share with our consumers, during work? Join us, if you are ready to work on tasks and projects with high responsibility in an exciting, innovative market environment, where we appreciate and support you to realise your personal ambition!HEINEKEN wants to become the Best Connected Brewer.That includes digitalizing and integrating all of our processes using best-in-class technology, and embedding a data-driven culture.Do you want to be part of HEINEKEN’s digital transformation? Then check it out our open position in the Digital & Technology department in Budapest: IT Business Analyst (Sales & Commerce)Joining us at D&T, you will be part of one of the most dynamic and innovative departments, and will have a direct impact in building the HEINEKEN Company of the future.Purpose of the jobTranslate business process requirements into IT solutions for our Sales and Commerce departments.Initiate development projects based on business need and be the single point of contact, to give optimal IT support to the business within the following business areas: Sales & Commerce.
Task:
 - Regularly engage with business process owners and listen to their needs. Understand business processes, speak the business language  - Support business with defining their functional requirements, challenge them against best business practices and application capabilities. Support business with business case preparation for new requirements  - Assess the requirements and translate them into IT solution designs  - Seek for best business process, application solution practices and new technologies outside of the company  - Prioritize business demands on new requirements with regards to company objectives.Pro-actively suggest business systems improvements based on latest developments in the field.  - Help the business function to implement changes as consequence of new solutions / new function. Provide day-to-day support to key users on specific solutions  - Map as-is processes to our new reference architecture  - Manage/engage the project team and delivery of defined project  - Assures proper data governance and adherence to existing data standards with the help of the Data Manager  - Ensure we digitalize our route to customers and consumers by developing/implementing best in class solutions  - Helping the future sales force to achieve our ambitions on the market and make them more competitive with IT solutions
Expectation:
 - Bachelor/Master degree in IT professional, Business Administration  - Hands-on experience with sales execution, salesforce automation and CRM systems  - Experience with Microsoft Dynamics 365 CRM  - Experience with SAP SD module is an advantage  - Understanding the concept of integration (with Dell Boomi or SAP PO) is an advantage  - Experience with master data management (tools, policies and processes) is an advantage  - Project Management experience is an advantage, hands-on experience with Agile/SCRUM is a plus  - Understanding of Business processes (especially sales and commerce) and Technology  - Excellent Communication and Presentation skills: ability to speak non-IT business language to all levels (peer, superior, reports)  - Team player
What we offer:
- You will be a part of a truly inclusive, diverse and open company- You will be part of a young and dynamic team- Inspiring working environment- Ability to learn and shape our application landscape- Self-development opportunities and continuous feedback We are a proud family with rich heritage and history. We are passionate about the beer and cider that we sell. At our core we are pioneers, mavericks, and entrepreneurs. We connect people by creating meaningful experiences and memorable moments. Every day you have the opportunity to be extraordinary and leave your mark. At HEINEKEN, we don’t just accept difference - we believe in it, we support it, and we thrive on it for the benefit of our employees, our products and our community.
