European Molecular Biology Laboratory
Network Engineer (f/m/d)
The IT Services team operates and supports the IT infrastructure and services at EMBL headquarters in Heidelberg and at the laboratory’s sites in Barcelona and Rome.
As part of IT Services, the Network team is responsible for managing and developing the network infrastructure in our data centres, on campus, and to our external network providers. As a leading scientific institution with highly data-intensive research, extensive data flows at and between the laboratory’s six sites and to the Internet, EMBL is connected to national and international scientific networks using state-of-the-art technologies from vendors including Cisco, Extreme Networks and Fortinet.
We are looking for an enthusiastic Network Engineer to join our team on-site at the headquarters in Heidelberg.
Your role
Operate and monitor the network infrastructure at multiple EMBL sitesManage routers, switches and other network componentsParticipate in the decision making regarding network technologies and their lifecycleWork closely with suppliers and providers to support the operation and further development of our network infrastructureEvaluate the latest networking technologies Collaborate with the IT teams across all EMBL sitesMaintain technical documentation
You have
A degree in computer science and/or equivalent professional experienceThe ability to work in a self-motivated team and participate in technical decision makingGood understanding of networking technologies regarding OSI layers 1-4A customer-oriented personality and excellent communication skillsExcellent knowledge of English. Proficiency in German is desirable.Willingness to travel occasionally, mostly to other EMBL sites
You might also have
Relevant technical certifications such as CCNPExperience in topics such as virtual networking, network access control and zero-trust security model, software defined networking
