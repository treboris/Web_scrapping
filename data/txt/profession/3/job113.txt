IT Services Manager
Task:
The IT Services Manager plays a critical leading role in our digital team, part of Coca-Cola HBC IT function. In this high visibility role, you will have the chance to work with cross-functional senior stakeholders supporting the production and our colleagues with IT solutions. You will have the chance for further career development and with your innovative ideas to make this position your own! You will be managing a team of five people and will be responsible for their development. The position reports directly to the Digital & Technology Platform Director. YOUR NEW KEY RESPONSIBILITIES The IT Services Manager makes strategic impact in the following areas: 1. Projects Execution of projects considering costs, quality, and deadlines;Conducting tender procedures, providing technical support, managing supply contracts;Continuous contact with internal business functions and external IT suppliers;Participation in the development, documentation, and support of Digital Technology and Platform services/IT processes; Knowledge and compliance with GDPR, audit and IT standards.  2. Providing technical support Management of the national telecommunications network, servers, server room, which are not under the supervision of the central Digital Technology and Platform services;Preparation and maintenance of system documentation; Working closely with the local On-Site IT Support team as well as the central Digital Technology and Platform services; Organization to increase service levels; Develop, monitor and periodically test Disaster Recovery & Backup processes; Instant transmission of system errors to our central Digital Technology and Platform services, ATOS, IT Services Manager, and local service providers; Detect and remediate information security vulnerabilities.  3. Business support Liaising with IT service providers and SLA management; Providing technical support to Digital Technology and Platform services Functional Leaders and business users; Innovation: support for prototypes, concepts, tests at local level; Preparation and accurate implementation of the annual infrastructure budget (CAPEX, OPEX); Full support for the implementation of business systems from the infrastructure side in cooperation with Project Management. 
Expectation:
ARE THESE YOUR SECRET INGREDIENTS? Bachelor’s degree in IT, Engineering or Economics or equivalent experience; 5 + years of experience in the IT Services Industry and Vendor/Service Management; Experience in Service Integration & Management;  Strong vendor management skills and the ability to identify risks (time risks, resource risks, interdependencies, etc.) and to manage the risk mitigation actions in the area of responsibility; Agile/SCRUM certification and experience will be highly valued but is not a must; Understanding how business goals aligns with IT; Solid communication skills and ability to build relationships with diverse stakeholders; Fluency English (written and verbal).
