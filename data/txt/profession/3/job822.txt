Currently we are looking for
DEVELOPER EXPERIENCE / PLATFORM ENGINEER (JTD)
Location: Budapest, V. district, hybrid (2 day in office, 3 days HO)
Salary: competitive
Type of employment: employee position
Tasks:
Select and introduce new tools and teach developers how to work with them. Interpret the results and implement new workflows in development teams, together with your teammates. Support programmers by scripting and configuring all kinds of tools. Introduce new tools and processes to colleagues.Maintain and update of the tools and infrastructure that are implemented by the team. Provide excellent support to other developers who have issues or ask questions regarding these tools.
Requirements:
2 to 5 years of relevant work experience.  Ability to solve or improve technically complex development bottlenecks.  Being interested in building and testing infrastructure.  Have an affinity with programming and operations. Being a strong communicator and know how to stimulate change. Always look ahead and to the next step of the Development department.  
Experiences:
Delivery/deployment pipelines, such as Jenkins and GitLab CI.Docker and Helm.  Linux.  VM Management.  Configuration management tools, such as Puppet and/or Ansible.  Dependency management tools, such as Gradle, Maven, Renovatebot, Sonatype Nexus or npm.Monitoring and alerting tools, such as Prometheus and Grafana.
What we offer:
Freedom in your workAn innovative work environment, where your opinion is importantRoom for your personal improvement: one day every two weeks, which you can spend on your personal improvement, you have opportunity to visit conferences (we also organize our own conference every year)An international work environment: lots of interaction with colleagues from other teams, departments in our Dutch, German and Hungarian branches.Open company culture, familiar atmosphere, helpful colleaguesOur core values: trust, freedom, and responsibility
