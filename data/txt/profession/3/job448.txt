Task:
As member of the divisional controlling team the project controller is responsible for the overall success and profitability of various customer projects.► Requests related to customer acquisitions and serial projects► Transfer prices and material surcharges including reporting to the plants► Tooling (orders regarding machine tooling) including initial and additional acquisition of manufacturing components and machines needed for production► Kick-off & Review meetings► Cost plausibility check and deviation analysis► Invoice discrepancies► Special topics (on demand)► Alignment with AE including review of open topics and priorities, high level topics, escalations► Documentation of requests► Special topics, e.g. portfolio clean-up
Expectation:
What distinguishes you?► Completed university degree in commercial studies or industrial engineering► 3-5 years professional experience in controlling preferably in an international automotive environment► Good communication and negotiation skills► English language skills, German is an advantage► High stress resistance, flexibility, high level of proactivity and being a good team player.► Good understanding of new and complex topics, good learning skills
