About TCS Hungary’s IT Unit:
TCS Hungary, in its IT services area, implements best-in-class solutions for the Business 4.0 era, enabling strong digital foundations for our customers worldwide. Our work includes hybrid cloud solutions, hand-in-hand with cloud native architectures,building scalable and efficient solutions. Our scope of work covers agile, cloud-based and fullstack application development, DevOps automation and many more.
You are eligible for the internship if you are:
A full-time BSc student in your last year enrolled at a Hungarian university studying IT.Required to complete the mandatory internship during your studies which is no more than 320 hours.Additional Information: Internship start date: 26th June, 2023 Duration: 8 weeks Deadline to apply: 21st May, 2023 
The key requirements for the role are:
Fluent English skills and interest in international experience.Have some experience in high level programming languages, such as Java or Python or Scala.Interest in data engineering, data science and business intelligence.
Advantages would be the knowledge of:
Linux basics and shell scripting.Stream and batch processing concepts, ETL and corporate data architectures.Cloud platforms in any nature.SQL or no-SQL databases.During your internship you will have the opportunity to:Learn data engineering principle.Learn to design and implement ETL processes and pipelines.Work with Linux, Bash, Python and Scala languages.Learn the Apache Kafka ecosystem and various ETL tools.Gain experience in Azure-based hybrid cloud solutions for data engineering.Gain experience working at a fast-growing, truly colorful company.Work closely with senior engineer colleagues and mentors.
What we offer:
Possibility to start your IT career in a global and multicultural environment.Digital internship program focused on constant innovation and application of emerging technology.Possibility to work with clients leading in their industries.Internship induction & mentoring programme.Comprehensive opportunities for additional training and development through our Talent Development programme and online learning platforms.A successful employee engagement & community volunteering platform.
