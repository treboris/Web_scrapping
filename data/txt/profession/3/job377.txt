Global Contracting Support and Data Expert
Location: Budapest – Sanofi Business Services, Váci Greens
Flexible working: 60% home office / week
Job type: Permanent, Full time
About the job
The purpose of this role is to: Provide global functional support across Contracting COE systemsAct as functional platform specialist and be the primary point of contact for supporting enhancementsUnderstand change requirements at global and regional levels and translate them into actionable process and system changesHelp L2 support teams to improve their SLAs.Participate in monitoring of appropriate contracting COE metrics and support the performance of data analyses About Growing with us
In this role you will…
Monitor & handle tickets escalated from L2 Support groupsProvide functional support not provided by L2 Support groupsMonitor global incidents and escalate to appropriate Support level and/or system providerCoordinate Global Master Data maintenancePropose improvements of process/solution/technology etc. in collaboration with Digital/L2 teams & Contracting COE global/regional networkDiscuss and provide business requirements specifications to Digital/L2 teams and/or external lifecycle contract management solution providerSupport internal testing and release communicationSupport lifecycle contract management tool implementation and contract migrationEnsure quality of the data in the lifecycle contract management systemTrain all relevant stakeholders and Contracting COE members and maintain tool documentationProvide centralized reporting on metrics and perform on-demand data analysis/reports
About you
Education: at least bachelor’s degree in business information and management or equivalentExperience & knowledge: 1-3 years of experience in digital support and data analysisExcellent English language skills both verbal and writtenAbility to interact with internal and external clients in a professional manner (essential)Team player, self-driver and able to work collaboratively transversally with an end-to-end mindset (essential)Organized and structured to execute tasks as per deadlines (essential)Experience with delivering the service reporting – KPIs, SLAs definition and executionExperience with ServiceNow or other case management tool and digital solutionsAdvanced level knowledge of MS Office programs, Excel, Word, Power Point
When joining our team, you will experience:
An international work environment, in which you can develop your talent and realize ideas and innovations within a competent teamAn attractive, market-oriented salary and cafeteria benefitsFlexible home office policy, with a possibility to work up to 60% of time from home / where you can easily schedule your office daysWork from an "Office of the Year 2020" finalist officeCollective life and accident insuranceYearly medical check-upAn individual and well-structured introduction and training of new employees and we will dedicate a Buddy for you to better navigate in your first weeksYour own career path within Sanofi. Your professional and personal development will be supported purposefullyJoin a great community & special events (Monthly Board Game Nights, Summer Events, Well-Being Lectures & Sport Clubs)
