Associate Product Manager
Shoptet is the largest e-commerce platform in the CEE region. Thanks to Shoptet, thousands of people are fulfilling their dreams of starting and running businesses. We help small retailers and well-known brands succeed in the world of e-commerce. We have a meaningful product and superior customer care.Our mission is to help people achieve their dream of starting a business and help them be successful.The Associate Product Manager position is a new role in Hungary, you will be the one who is responsible for the product in the Hungarian market, with strong support from the Czech product team. You will be part of our Hungarian market team represented by our Country Manager Ivan Redey. In 2021, we doubled the company to 200 people in Czech Rebublic and celebrated the fair employer of the year award. We have a healthy company culture, a meaningful product and the ambition to become an e-commerce leader in CEE. Do you want to be there with us and move Shoptet forward? In the role of Associate Product Manager, you will have the opportunity to join our company and use your potential to improve our product together with us.Your missionMission of Associate Product Manager is contributing to initiatives for achieving product / market fit for our solution in Hungarian market.
Task:
As Associate Product Manager this is a huge adventure to be here at the beginning when we are building the Shoptet in the Hungarian market.You will be part of the Czech product team who will be responsible for your onboarding, your trainings and who will support you during the future projects. Also you will be a very important member of the Hungarian team. As a Product Manager Associate you will be the representative of our product towards our merchants, partner agencies.You will interview merchants and partner agencies to know their biggest pain points.Your tasks will gather product requirements towards product localisation, improvements and legal compliance.Also collecting quantitative data and evaluating which initiatives works and what don't.Collaboration with various teams from customer support, through marketing and other product teams for identifying opportunities and solutions.Reporting to Country Lead, collaborating with CPO, local team and product teams.You will work closely with other product managers, data team and development team, as well as external partners who cooperates with Shoptet and are tied to our success.
Expectation:
Native Hungarian with good English (your daily communication will be mainly in English with the Czech team).Having 2-4 years experience in product management or digital project management (who is interested in product management) in similar sectors (IT, digital, ecommerce, SaaS).Excellent prioritisation and commmunication skills.Proactive, self-driven, flexible, open-minded and able to work independently.Based in Budapest or nearby with possibility to visit office (we have a hibrid working model with home office opportunities)Willingness to go for business trips to Prague occasionally (part of training/onboarding will be in Prague)E-commerce background, local knowledge of the webshop market is a big plus. Having experience with SaaS business model is advantage.
What we offer:
We create inspiring and challenging environment, and support people to be successful. Our focus is to provide the organisation with a sustained competitive advantage to full-fill our great mission and vision.A very supportive and strong background from the Czech Shoptet.Continuous learning and developing.Great colleagues with experience from industry leading companies.You will be here at the beginning where we are building the Hungarian Shoptet with your participation. Cool office in a very premium location (Millenáris Park).Great community events - B-day, Teambuildings in Prague and Budapest.Fruits, vegetable and healthy snacks every day. Enterprise tariff for your own e-shop for free.Hibrid working model.
