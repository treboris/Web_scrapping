Hello there! We’re Vodafone Intelligent Solutions (also known as _VOIS) and we’re looking for a
Demand Management Specialist – Senior
Your role in a nutshell:
Provide overall governance of demand management, to ensure the quality of end to end demand management process. Liaise between customers and the solution groups. Act as the single point of contact until the end of the demand lifecycle. Track and push the approval of the demands and the solution of the customer requests. Investigate standard solutions and potential external solutions. Manage customer demand projects fully or partially and make status reports to the Steering Committee.
What you’ll do:
Drive the demand management process and ensures that demands are captured, assessed, costed and are implementable/ scalable with agreements on delivery commitments made by the implementation and operations teams.May coordinate transition and implementation projects as well.Drive the prioritization of demands.Highlight any arising risks, provide mitigation plan, also bring any issues up and applies problem solving techniques to resolve them.Chair the Demand Planning meetings.Examine local standard solutions, and also look for solutions externally. Make proposal for 3-4 alternative technical solutions.Engage with 3rd parties  (internal and external) including, but not limited to Vendors, Sales team , Local Markets and other stakeholdersTrack the demand process from the idea of a request until the functionality is delivered to the business.Identify and communicate any deviation from the demand management process, defines and implements corrective actions.Track capacity (resource, budget) planning and their use during delivery.Promote the Demand management function across all regions.Create and manage measurement systems to track utility and profitability of changes. Create weekly/ monthly reports on the changes and demands.
What will you bring to the team:
Fluent English knowledge (both written and oral)Experience of working with 3PL/4PL logistics service providers Demand management experienceExperience in project/ process/ change managementTelecom and SSC experienceCommunication and negotiation skillsDecision-making and problem-solving skillsTime management skills
In return we provide you with:
A dynamic environment where innovative ideas are always welcome A collaborative community where your professional goals and work are supported by a diverse team Access to internal trainings through Vodafone University and external trainings via other providers A supportive internal coaching and mentoring culture Opportunities to participate in company activities and Vodafone Foundation events Corporate assets including a laptop and mobile phone with Vodafone RED subscription (unlimited voice, text message, and data within the EU) Cafeteria package Private Medical Services Employee Assistance Program to support your wellbeing and mental health Life and accident insurance You can work remotely overseas for 20 days a year 16 weeks fully paid parental leave. This applies to all non-birthing partners who become parents due to birth or adoption You and your family members can enjoy several discounts from our partners You will be working in a hybrid model, which means 3 days working from home with 2 days office visit per week 
What you need to know about us
We are the engine that powers Vodafone around the world: our work impacts millions of people around the globe every day. If you want to experiment and learn fast, integrated into an international, multicultural and inclusive team, then _VOIS is the place for you.  #movewithus #_VOIS #VOISHU #LI-Hybrid
