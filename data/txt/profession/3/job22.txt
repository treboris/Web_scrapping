Hello there! We’re Vodafone Intelligent Solutions (also known as _VOIS) and we’re looking for a
German speaking Demand Management Specialist  (IT Project Coordinator - Business Analyst)
Your role in a nutshell:
Provide overall governance of demand management, to ensure the quality of end to end demand management process. Liaise between customers and the solution groups. Act as the single point of contact until the end of the demand lifecycle. Track and push the approval of the demands and the solution of the customer requests. Investigate standard solutions and potential external solutions. Manage customer demand projects fully or partially and make status reports to the Steering Committee. What you’ll do:Be the key interface for capturing demand requests. Manage and keep track of incoming requests, process them via the automated Demand Management Process.Assure the quality of information provided by Demand Requestors.Capture the requirements.Create assessment documentation for every demand request.Evaluate potential risks and report them to the immediate supervisor.Ensure all new demands are suitable for decision making and have a clear scope. Create Demand Board documentation (agenda, minutes), present all demand assessment forms to stakeholders and gather approval. Assure the quality of deliverables and improvements.Escalate issues that require intervention.Provide regular status update to the management, keep all stakeholders appropriately informed.Document the processes, create process descriptions and work instructions.Give input to process improvement, to increase efficiency and effectiveness of all Demand Management process activities.
What will you bring to the team:
Fluent German and English language skills Demand management or business analysis experienceCommunication and time management skillsDecision-making and problem-solving skillsCustomer focusTelecom and SSC experience (desired)Experience of an IT methodology or process model (e.g. ITIL) (desired)
In return we provide you with:
A dynamic environment where innovative ideas are always welcome A collaborative community where your professional goals and work are supported by a diverse team Access to internal trainings through Vodafone University and external trainings via other providers A supportive internal coaching and mentoring culture Opportunities to participate in company activities and Vodafone Foundation events Corporate assets including a laptop and mobile phone with Vodafone RED subscription (unlimited voice, text message, and data within the EU) Cafeteria package Private Medical Services Employee Assistance Program to support your wellbeing and mental health Life and accident insurance You can work remotely overseas for 20 days a year 16 weeks fully paid parental leave. This applies to all non-birthing partners who become parents due to birth or adoption You and your family members can enjoy several discounts from our partners You will be working in a hybrid model, which means 3 days working from home with 2 days office visit per week 
What you need to know about us
We are the engine that powers Vodafone around the world: our work impacts millions of people around the globe every day. If you want to experiment and learn fast, integrated into an international, multicultural and inclusive team, then _VOIS is the place for you.  #movewithus #_VOIS #VOISHU #LI-Hybrid
