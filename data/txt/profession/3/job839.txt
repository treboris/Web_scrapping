Illuminance Labs Kft
Service Desk (Level 3)
Our team are curious, collaborative and customer obsessed. We're endlessly focused on delivering for our customers and our Service Desk is the point where we make our first impression. Join Illuminance Labs and help us to deliver world class applications and infrastructure for our clients.
Task:
Provide Tier 3 technical support for end-usersManage and monitor client IT systems and infrastructure, including servers, networks, and applicationsRespond to and resolve complex technical issues and incidentsInstall, configure, and maintain hardware and softwareCollaborate with other IT staff to design and implement new systems and infrastructureMonitor and manage the IT ticket queue, ensuring prompt resolution of issues and effective communication with end-users via chat, phone and email.Develop and maintain IT documentation, including system configurations and troubleshooting guides
Expectation:
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or related field3+ years of experience in IT support or system administrationIn-depth knowledge of Windows and Linux operating systems, virtualization, and cloud technologiesExcellent problem-solving and analytical skillsStrong communication and customer service skillsFluency in English
Other:
Experience with ITSM tools, such as Jira, IT Glue and Datto RMM.
What we offer:
We offer a competitive salary, remote and hybrid working to meet your preference, a beautiful new office, and a dynamic work environment. If you are a morning person with a desire to support our friendly Australian customer base from 8am to 4pm each day then this is the position and company for you.
