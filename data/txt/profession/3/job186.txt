IT Business Analyst
How does a professional banking system work in the background? How can data be stored in a database? How to define the best solution for a calculation which is performing well and flexible enough? How does working in a Scrum team helps you to align technology and business aspects?
Are you ready to dive into the world of software design? If you are curious about how the agile approach improves the collaborative relationship with clients and also your personal growth, don’t hesitate to apply!
As an IT Business Analyst you will:
participate in defining and designing the best solution complying with the business requirements;support joint design meetings and workshops with the client;formulate use cases for IT systems;parametrise and model functionalities in a low-code framework;participate proactively in scrum ceremonies;cooperate with developers and clients in line with Agile values;define test scenarios in order to ensure successful delivery of the project (UAT and release support) in line with QA principles;perform testing of designed functionalities;support users in getting started with the application (user guide, e-learning material preparation).
What you will need:
interest in software development, information technology and banking;1 - 3 years of experience as a system analyst / business analyst;degree in economics or information technology;experience with SQL, MySQL on basic level;IT, banking or consulting experience is a plus;knowledge of visual modeling software is a plus (e.g: Rational Rose, MS Visio);working proficiency in English and fluent Hungarian;analytical thinking, attention to detail;good communication skills and a team player attitude;enthusiasm about Agile. 
Sector(s):
IT Development
IT Consultant, Analyst, Auditor
What you can expect from us:
supportive mentoring program;continous professional development while participating in international banking projects;to be part of an energetic, supportive, highly qualified team;competitive salary and career opportunities;recreational activities (wide range of sport opportunities: fitness pass, AYCM);diverse and flexible cafeteria (among others SZÉP Card, Edenred Card, sport and cultural event pass);Employee Assistance Program (EAP);health insurance;30 days of paid vacation;regular team building activities;in-house meetups and innovation competitions;learning opportunities (Udemy courses, workshops);professional working environment;opportunity to work from home.
