We are looking for a dedicated and knowledgeable
Integration Project Management Expert
to support the delivery of large integration programs and to become part of an exciting success story.
Take your next career step at MOL Group with our Retail team that is going through a huge transformation of its operations to become the first choice of customers. To support this long-term goal, MOL is heavily investing in acquisitions to expand its Service Station network and provide the opportunity for future growth across its existing and new markets. The integration of the newly acquired companies is of key importance which is managed via post-merger integration (PMI) programs.
Currently MOL Group Retail runs two major PMI projects: one is about the integration of the Polish LOTOS Paliwa company with 417 Service Stations which is our largest acquisition in Retail’s history and another one is about the integration of the to-be-acquired OMV subsidiary in Slovenia with 120 Service Stations.
YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES:
Reporting to the Group Integration Program Manager, you will be a key contributor involved in the integration of the newly acquired companies and responsible for the following activities:Manage and govern the project team of ~20 workstreams incl. all business and functional teams such as retail, wholesale, finance, HR, legal, etc.; encourage cross-functional alignment between the team membersCoordinate the definition of integration strategies and manage the preparation of the detailed integration program planEnsure the delivery of the integration program plan: structuring tasks, organising meetings, etc.Review the integration plans to confirm that they still support the goalsSupport status tracking and reporting on key achievements and risksPrepare decision-making materials for managementAct as a focal communication point for the project team, closely cooperate with internal stakeholders both on Group and local level
