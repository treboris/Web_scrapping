Your place in the team 
Conversational AI bot/virtual assistant designer works collaboratively to design and develop conversational AI / virtual assistant solutions, like chatbot and voicebot solutions supporting customer operations strategy implementation. This position holder is expected to develop virtual assistant solutions by leveraging conversational AI design methodologies and technologies, through conceptualization and design of virtual assistant conversation flows, based on AI design methods (e.g. conversational design, AI trainer, conversational copywriting methodologies.)
You will be involved in the following tasks
Develop virtual assistant that understand what humans say and respond appropriatelyAssist with application prototyping and recommend solutions around implementationDevelop & refine business requirements through user stories to drive the development of Virtual Assistants that deliver self-serve capabilitiesLearn and drive the usage of conversational AI in an enterprise environmentDesign the dialogs, dialog flow and logicDesign conversation and virtual assistant personaDefined Intent-Entities, design context in conversation flows.Mapping and design related business process and user journeysParticipating in AI conversation KPI target setting & measurement (precision, F1 scope, Recall, Confidence)Interact with Business Partners and customers on a regular basis to understand business needs and expectationsBe part of a Scrum Agile squad & work daily with the teamCollaborate with Technology and Business areas to drive prioritization decisions, data requirements, implementations, testing and maintenance
We are looking for you if you have
University or College degree Telecom or Service sector experienceStrong English and strong Hungarian as well.Conversational design practice and certification is preferred (CDI Academy)NLU /NLU software training/configurationAgile /Scrum tool usage MIRO board, OFFICE, JIRA, CONFLUENCE, Figma, DRAW.IOExperience in training of cognitive services: MS Composer, QNA maker, Question and answer, CLU.Driven by a digital mindsetExperience in cross-functional and agile program delivery and workingUtilize proactive problem-solving and innovative thinking to address challenges
