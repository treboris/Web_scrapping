Job Purpose:
As an Application Consultant you define, establish and support our technical documentation platform within our Product Information Management area. Furthermore you participate in planning, definition, and high-level design of the solution and exploration of solution alternatives for editing and structuring technical documentation including distributions solutions and translation engined.
Your tasks will be:
Working with stakeholders, and suppliers to establish high-level solution intent.Participating in planning, definition, and high-level design of the solution and exploration of solution alternatives editing and structuring technical documentationParticipating in planning, definition, and high-level design of the solution and exploration of solution alternatives for distribution of technical documentationParticipating in planning, definition, and high-level design of the translation management solutionBeing accountable for the Architecture to support the future workflow and process within Technical documentationEnsuring the system architecture have the optimal interface and alignment across the Enterprise systemsDefining systems and their interfaces, understand solution deployment, and communicate requirements for interactions to system developers/suppliersBuilding a scalable infrastructure for automating our pipelinePlanning and developing the Architectural Runway in support of new business Features and Capabilities
Your ideal profile:
Holding min. Bachelor’s Degree  Having experience defining architecture integrating information’s between several data workflows and systems.Having broad End to End understanding how to create efficient future architecture for data flowsHaving experience setting up automating workflows between sub systemsHaving experience with several of the following:Frontend development (React, TypeScript, JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, CSS)Backend development (Java, Java Content Repository (JCR)/CRX, Apache Sling, Lucene, Tomcat/JBoss, Apache Web Server)API/Web services creation and consumptionYou are preferably familiar with several of the following:Component Content Management Systems (CCMS)DITA, DITA-OT (Ant), XSL/XSL-FO, and XML Authoring Tools,Localization/translation toolsMasterdata and product information SAP PLMAdditional skillsSelf starterCan communicate complicated information’s to management and business stakeholdersAbility to quickly understand cross functional business needs and translate into technical requirementsAbility to interact closely with technical peers across a global enterprise set-up
