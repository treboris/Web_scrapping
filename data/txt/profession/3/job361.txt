Our client is an international banking group which provides services in 5 European countries.
The international IT solutions of the group, covering several European countries, takes place in Hungary and in another western European country with Hungarian and western European colleagues, therefore not only the IT services are international but also the teams.
We are looking for a junior/medior person who is interested in the recruitment field and has some relevant IT knowledge to able to help the selection process:
Recruitment specialist/support (IT area)
What we offer:
You can make sure your future career is steady in a long runLife-long learning and plenty of opportunities to challenge yourself, or get to know different IT fields if interestedCompetitive salary as well as other benefits such as:100% customizable cafeteria,annual performance-based reward,company phone with mobile internet, that you can use for private purposes as wellemployee discount at the bankYour English language skills won’t be worn out as you can use it on a daily basisFlexible work scheduleThe focus is on teamwork therefore it is important to hang out in the "open office" together, but you have the option to spend half of your working hours in home officeYour insight is valued and your suggestions will be heardInternational environment with a happy work atmosphereGreen office, equipped with:bicycle storage and showersrestaurant with panoramic viewbuffets with snacks and coffee and many dining options in the area
What we expect from You:
Fluency in English both written and verbal as testing candidates in English will be part of your daily jobSome knowledge of IT, so you can pre-screen efficiently the CVs for around 20 different profilesRecruitment experience is a plus but not a must, if you are interested to work on this field on a long run, we are happy to teach you all necesarry soft skills.Good knowledge of Excel, and PowerPoint.Strong communication skills as you will be the first contact point of all applicants and with the managers of different IT areas.Being proactive is something you feel comfortable with as advancing in your role you will have more and more responsibilitiesGood time management skills will be required as in this fast-paced enviroment.
Some of your tasks will be:
Pre-screening CVs, checking salary request,sharing CVs with the relevant IT managers,making pre-screen calls with candidates,following up with the selection process,setting up HR interviews,preparing reports, statistics,preparing presentations about recruitment for monthly, quarterly meetings,writing and posting job ads on various channels.
