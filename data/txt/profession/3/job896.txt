IT Manager
Are you an experienced, Service focussed IT manager with exceptional interpersonal skills, seeking a challenging new opportunity? If so, you will be interested in this fantastic opportunity to take our Leading international school through our next stage of development.
Expectations:
Serving as the first point of contact for IT support within the schoolSupporting students, teachers, whenever they encounter challenges with computer printers, projectors, interactive boardDiagnosing and troubleshooting software and hardware issuesWorking with suppliers to get the best support and service of key technologies for the schoolDeveloping and maintaining local networksEnsuring security and privacy of networks and computer systemsDriving the Implementation and adoption of new technologies in the SchoolManaging the IT service request function
Requirements:
Previous working experience as IT Manager and a degree in Computer ScienceKnowledge of Apple device management, Cisco system or experience with similar network technologyA competent knowledge and understanding of Microsoft/ Windows based systems (preferably within a school environment) having in depth knowledge of Windows client operating systems and architectureIT skills to include: Windows 10, Active Directory, DHCP & DNS, Microsoft office 365, printingConfident working alone, as well as part of a wider team and if needed outside of core hours to ensure services are ready for use for the key practices of the school
Person Specification:
Proactive, enthusiastic, ambitious and energetic personDemonstrate high levels of honesty and integrityFlexibility to work outside of office hours when requiredProblem solver and attention to detailsExcellent communication and interpersonal skillsOutstanding organisational and time-management skillsGood command of English
What we offer:
A secure job in an exponentially growing international schoolTeam spirit and a great working atmosphere in a friendly teamCompany laptop, mobile phoneSecured bike storageContract: Full-time, initially for 2 years, mutually renewableHours: 40 hours per week, 8am start (Mon-Fri)Due to the nature of the role only LOCAL APPLICANTS will be considered.
