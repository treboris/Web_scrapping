Csatlakozz jó hangulatú csapatunkhoz!
KBC Bank, the parent company of the Hungarian K&H Bank, provides banking services in 5 European countries.The international IT solutions of the banking group, covering several European countries, takes place with Hungarian and Belgian colleagues in Hungary and Belgium, therefore not only our services are international, but also our teams.Our responsibilities range from day-to-day operation to application development and its operation.We are constantly expanding the Hungarian side of the team, which is why we are looking for enthusiastic, motivated new employees with junior, medior or possibly senior work experience. Your focus will be twofold: management of the DDI (DNS, DHCP, IPAM) environment and performance analysis of end-to-end application dataflows. You not only contribute to the daily management of components, but are also involved in numerous projects. This way you can immediately use your expertise and you can quickly deepen it further.Together with business stakeholders (your internal customers), IT architects and system engineers from other teams, you play an important role in infrastructure and security projects. Your contribution ranges from analysis and design to implementation and testing; you are involved in the day-to-day management of various telecommunication and security components. Think of setup, configuration, capacity management, following up on new versions or functionalities, resolving security risks or integrating components into our current infrastructure.You will explore new technological possibilities. Is there an opportunity to improve or automate the way we work? You spotted it.You analyze problems so that incidents are solved and we continuously improve our domain.Together with your colleagues, you work in a rotational role to provide support outside office hours.
