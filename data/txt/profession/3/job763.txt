CLOUD DEVOPS ENGINEER (JD)
Location: Budapest XIII. district, partly remoter
Salary: competitive
Type of employment: employee
ABOUT THE POSITION:
A future-proof microservices architecture based on Spring Boot and Kafka that processes millions of securities transactions for our customers in the German banking industry in a stable and high-performance manner. Our partner's specially created Linux-based development environment offers the team and you all the possibilities to pursue modern software development.
Together in a cross-functional team you will drive the development and operation of our new cloud-based platform.You will jointly rely on scalable cloud technologies with Kubernetes as a central component, complemented by a number of other open source components as well.In addition to automating deployments and builds, you'll also be responsible for designing efficient processes and working with us to develop a modern development pipeline.In addition, you enjoy supporting other implementation and development teams with your accumulated experience on their way to the cloud and providing them with advice.
