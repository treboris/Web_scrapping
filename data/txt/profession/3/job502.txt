Task:
Sales Assistant - NO PHONE Job - Great Pay -- Work from Home!About us:Used Conex is a shipping container company based in Tampa Florida. Used Conex offers a wide range of new and used shipping containers that can be used for storage, cargo and freight.Remote position anywhere in the world  . This is a NO phone job! We are looking for part-time, remote sales assistants to post 1-3 ads per day, Monday-Friday on your Facebook Marketplace (each ad takes 5 minutes or less to post). You will respond to customers that message you, follow up with them often, and daily training will be provided to help you be the most successful you can be.The sales position that we have available can be done from your phone or computer.You can close the sale directly on Facebook messenger with an invoice that you will make with our system. Or, on the phone.The pay is 25 percent of the profit per container that you sell. That’s an average of $70-150 US dollars per container that you sell. Many people sell 20 or 30 containers a month here.This position is perfect for someone looking for a side job with potential to become full-time.
