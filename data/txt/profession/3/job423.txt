About NTT Hungary:
Hungarian subsidiary of a Japanese multinational company is looking for a new colleague in the position of Junior IT Engineer. The new colleague will join to the IT Services business unite located in Budapest and performs the Local IT tasks with the support of Hungarian and international teams.NTT Ltd. provides services to Hungarian and also international partners, the Hungarian branch is consist of 120 employees, which needs internal IT support.The position includes two separate responsibilities: primarily end-user support should be given to Hungarian employees in cooperation with the International IT team, secondly a data center lab should be supplied with maintenance and security upgrades. The data center tasks will be supported by Hungarian Architect colleagues.There is a possibility to work from home, however, certain tasks also require office presence in fixed time slots, these will be mostly predictable.Necessary devices will be provided by the company.
Responsibilities/tasks:
This position is a split role, both working on regional end user support and local servers Regional European end user support:IT asset responsibility, handover of devices, cooperation with HR and FinanceCoordinating IT asset procurement in cooperation with the Logistics Team -Manage IT ticketing system, participating in solution and escalation, cooperation with international IT teams - (EU IT) not needed as no time to manage tickets in time and due to being only available a limited time. To much risk for breaching the SLAsActive participation in on- and off-boarding process with the HR teamOnsite Support for basic network troubleshootingOnsite Support for Office Desk EquipmentOnsite Support assisting with Meeting room setup/testing before an event and VC (Video Conferencing) troubleshootingLocal, Hungarian server technology tasks:Maintenance and updating of virtual tools in data center lab environment, perform security patch management tasksContributing to create new environments in a lab with the Achitect teamContinuous intervention in systems in order to comply with the expected IT security vulnerability level, consultations with Architect Engineers and international IT security teamsContinuous data provision about the status of lab environment for reportsExploring and learning a wide range of IT systems will be an important taskMicrosoft client and Server environmentsLinux distributionVMware virtualization technologies
What we offer:
Competitive salaryCafeteria (450k annual after probation period)Health care services (Medicover)Laptop, phone (based on employee choice: iPhone 14 or Samsung S23)Home Office – after probation period – hybrid work set-up (3 days from home)Flexible working hoursOpportunity for long-term development and career opportunitiesAnd most importantly a supportive work environment with trustworthy, and approachable management
What will make you a good fit for the role?
Required Experience and Knowledge:Graduate/high school education in relevant fieldDegree is preferred but not essentialRelevant previous work experience is an advantageIntermediate English knowledge in both written and verbalHome Office, however planned and ad hoc office presence expectedRequired skills:Reliability, flexibility and precisionPrecise and accurate administration capabilityWillingness to learn and develop
