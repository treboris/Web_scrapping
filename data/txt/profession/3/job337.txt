Senior Configuration Management Analyst
As a Senior Configuration Management Analyst, your responsibilities will be:
Acting as an active member of an Asset Management team working with both on-site and local field resources to identify and validate the complete life cycle of end user and enterprise assetsEnsuring contractual service support requirements are understood and managed properlyFacilitating Unisys standard processes for ITIL core functions, executing and delivering process outputs and deliverables of the Change Management / Request Management / Major Incident / Critical Situation /Problem Management processWorking with field resources and service delivery teams to identify missing informationEnsuring effective communication and coordination of problem-solving efforts between support teams, account teams, field service personnel and customersWorking with data to eliminate inconsistencies and perform other due diligence analysisPopulating all Software License Data and client-provided hardware and software data in ITSM CMDB (Configuration Management Database)Following defined processes to ensure data accuracy within the Asset Management CMDB SystemPresenting operational and service level reports and explaining service level support available to internal or external customersProviding Ad-Hoc reports for both hardware and software assetsExecuting data updates for the Disposal processReviewing trend analysis to conduct Proactive Problem Management and eliminate reoccurring issuesProviding a point of contact for process-related questions or issues and facilitating process-related meetings such as Daily operations, Change Advisory Board, Problem Review Board, etc.Working closely with Asset Management Leadership to proactively monitor SLA performance and report on them accuratelyProviding training and mentoring for functional teams to ensure that process guidelines are understood and followedConducting workshops as required to identify, assess and address process deficiencies, ensuring a common understanding of process intention and operation, and ensuring organization and individual associates understand SLAs and how they contribute to client service expectations
You will be successful in this role if you have:
At least a Bachelor’s Degree and a minimum of 5 years of relevant experience in an ITIL-based Enterprise Infrastructure environment (eg. Asset Management / Request Management / Change Management / IT Logistics) or an equivalent combination of education and work experience.Strong verbal and written communication skills in English.A disciplined and structured approach to process management in a consistent and methodical manner that reports against service deliverablesAbility to analyze data from multiple data sources (eg. SCCM, ITSM, MFAM.)Experience in working with the uCMDB tool, modifying server statuses (onboarding, offboarding of virtual servers), SLA rating checking, reporting and service tree creationA high degree of understanding of Microsoft Excel and database conceptsSolid ability to create and document processes.
It is also great if you have:
An ITIL v4 Foundation or ITIL Intermediate Certification on Release Control and ValidationFamiliarity with MS Excel VBA (Visual Basic for Applications) and working knowledge in creating MacrosExperience with ServiceNow or similar ITSM toolset (HPSM is highly beneficial)
