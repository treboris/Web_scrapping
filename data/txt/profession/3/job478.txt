ABOUT TCS HUNGARY’S IT UNIT:
TCS Hungary, in its IT services area, implements best-in-class solutions for the Business 4.0 era, enabling strong digital foundations for our customers worldwide. Our work includes hybrid cloud solutions, hand-in-hand with cloud native architectures, building scalable and efficient solutions. Our scope of work covers agile, cloud-based and fullstack application development, DevOps automation and many more.
YOU ARE ELIGIBLE FOR THE INTERNSHIP IF YOU ARE:
A full-time BSc student in your last year enrolled at a Hungarian university studying IT. Required to complete the mandatory internship during your studies which is no more than 320 hours.Additional Information: Internship start date: 26th June, 2023 Duration: 8 weeks Deadline to apply: 21st May, 2023 
The key requirements for the role are:
Fluent English skills.Experience or strong interest in Linux, Shell scripting, Git, Virtualization, Jenkins and would like to extend your skills as a DevOps engineer.Interest in modern DevOps practices.You can imagine yourself in large scale, international development projects.
... and it would be nice if you know:
Git or similar for CI and Jenkins or similar for CD.Deployment of RESTful APIs and related concepts (API management, security, etc.).Microsoft Azure, Amazon Web Services or Google Cloud Platform in any nature.During your internship you will have the opportunity to:learn DevOps principles.design and implement CI/CD pipelines.work with Ansible, Docker and Kubernetes and various PaaS solutions.study large scale, cloud native software architectures.experience working in agile DevOps methodologyexperience working at a fast-growing, truly colorful company.be mentored by senior engineer colleagues regularly.
What we offer:
Possibility to start your IT career in a global and multicultural environment.Digital internship program focused on constant innovation and application of emerging technology.Possibility to work with clients leading in their industries.Internship induction & mentoring program.Comprehensive opportunities for additional training and development through our Talent Development program and online learning platforms.A successful employee engagement & community volunteering platform.
