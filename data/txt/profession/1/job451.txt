No transformation has ever been started without a group of passionate, dedicated and empowered people. We believe that innovation and great results are driven by the right mix of people with diverse backgrounds, personalities, skills, and perspectives, reflecting the world in which we do business. To make sure the mix of people works, we strive to create an inclusive work environment where people of all backgrounds are treated equally, respected, and valued for who they are. Customer Service Representative
As a constantly growing company, Danfoss is looking for motivated employees who want to work with us on our success. We are looking for a Customer Service Representative with English and other foreign language (French preferred) to join our diverse team.
The primary function in this role is to provide commercial support such as order management, order entry, price checking, order clearing, compliance, complaint handling, coordination of delivery and billing.
Location: Budapest
