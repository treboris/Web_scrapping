Csatlakozz jó hangulatú csapatunkhoz!
KBC Bank, the parent company of the Hungarian K&H Bank, provides banking services in 5 European countries.The international IT solutions of the banking group, covering several European countries, takes place with Hungarian and Belgian colleagues in Hungary and Belgium, therefore not only our services are international, but also our teams.Our responsibilities range from day-to-day operation to application development and its operation.We are constantly expanding the Hungarian side of the team, which is why we are looking for enthusiastic, motivated new employees with junior, medior or possibly senior work experience. Join our team and help us modernize datacenter services (networking infrastructure, storage, web security, server virtualization…) so the latest and greatest banking solutions can be brought to our customers faster, better, stronger!Automation and infra-as-code lie at the heart of this modernization.We are looking for a highly skilled and motivated Analyst to join our team in the datacenter domain. In this role, you will be responsible for working on projects related to the design, implementation, and management of our datacenter infrastructure. Your role in our team will be to analyze the needs of the customers (internal ICT clients as well as Group Datacenters' engineers themselves). You will also translate these needs into a functional & technical design for developers to work with.Your job is not only to provide clarity, but also to actively think along so that our datacenter solutions add value for our customers. Key ResponsibilitiesGather and document requirements from stakeholdersDesign and document infrastructure automation solutionsWork with engineers to implement and test solutionsWork with other teams to ensure automation products meets customer's and organization's needs  What you can expect working with us:You can make sure your future career is steady within our companyLife-long learning and plenty of opportunities to challenge yourselfCompetitive salary as well as other benefits such as:10% customizable cafeteria,annual performance-based reward,company phone with mobile internet, that you can use for private purposes as wellemployee discount at the bankYour English language skills won’t be worn out as you can use it on a daily basisFlexible schedule as we don’t use ‘Clock in Clock out’ system As our focus is on teamwork we consider it important to hang out in the "open office" together, but you have the option to spend half of your working hours from homeWe value your insight and are always open to suggestionsInternational environment with a cheerful work atmosphereGreen office in Millennium Park, equipped with:bicycle storage and showersrestaurant with panoramic views over the Danube and the National Theatrebuffets with snacks and coffee and many dining options in the area  What we expect from You:Technical knowledge in areas such as networking, network security, server virtualization and storageGood communication and problem-solving skillsExperience with gathering customer requirements and making technical designsKnowledge of at least one programming language is a mustExperience working with datacenter infrastructure is a plusBachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or related field   What you can expect during the recruitment process:After sending your CV in English, expect a two-round interview process, one at our office in Budapest (in Hungarian), and one online in English with the Belgian team leaderAn efficient selection process is important for both of us, so we try to make it as fast as possible, and even if you are not selected we will give you a detailed
