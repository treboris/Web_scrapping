ZF Hungária Kft. is searching for experienced colleague for the following position:
IT Trainings Specialist - M365 services
Tasks & Responsibilities:
Preparation and quality assurance of communication content for changed or new IT services within Microsoft 365 Creation and maintenance of training content (videos, web-based training, simple shows etc.) Participation in agile projects on the subject of user enablement and reliable processing of project tasks Advising IT colleagues / IT project management with regard to training and communication concepts.
Elvárások:
Higher education degree, preferably in Information Technology or related disciplines Good command of English (min. B2 level) Very good knowledge of using modern cloud based IT Workplace tools (e.g. M365) and devices (Laptops, digital whiteboards, mobile devices etc.) and the associated IT processes and concepts (e.g. ITIL, Evergreening, SaaS, Cloud Computing etc.) Experience in the creation and implementation of IT trainings (e.g. articulate, Vyond, Camtasia etc.) Good communication skills to prepare complex IT issues in a simple and understandable way (e.g. to publish them on our global intranet)
We offer:
Stable company background and long-term working possibility Full of challenges and high-level professional activities at an international company Participation in international projects and commission to foreign countries Career opportunities, continuous training program and language courses Competitive salary and performance-related bonus Cafeteria with optional elements School start support
