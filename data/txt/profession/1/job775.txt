Software Quality Engineer
At Loxon we build world-class software covering the entire lifecycle of lending and risk management and deliver them as integrated solutions for the financial services industry. We have major banking groups among our customers from 25+ countries in Europe and other global regions. We have a unique blend of competence of domain knowledge and technical skills with business professionals having strong background in banking and consulting, and software experts skilled in system & software design, data science and analytics.
We are looking for a results-driven Software Quality Engineer who will actively participate in the software development process and help us create excellent solutions for our clients. As a Software Quality Engineer on any typical workday, you will be busy assessing and increasing software quality through manual and automated testing.
As a Software Quality Engineer you will:
analyze user stories for validity and feasibility;create test cases;develop, execute and maintain (manual and automated) test scripts;create and execute performance test scenarios;review and evaluate test results;detect and track software defects and inconsistencies;provide support and documentation;work with other members of the cross-functional team to ensure quality throughout the software development life cycle.
If you have…
1-3 years of experience in software testing;basic knowledge of Database/SQL;technical university degree (information technology, engineering, mathematician or other relevant fields),fluency in English and in Hungarian;agile mindset, ability to adapt to changing environment;pragmatic and proactive mindset;analytical thinking, attention to detail;quality-oriented attitude… then you could be a valuable member of our team!
Sector(s):
IT Development
Tester, Test Engineer
We value if you have:
experience with HTML;experience with Robot Framework or any keyword-based framework;experience with testing tools (Protractor, Cucumber, JMeter, etc);experience in Java development;any ISTQB certification.
What you can expect from us:
continuous professional development while participating in international banking projects;to be part of an energetic, supportive, highly qualified team;competitive salary and career opportunities;recreational activities (wide range of sport opportunities: fitness pass, AYCM);diverse and flexible cafeteria (among others SZÉP Card, Edenred Card, sport and cultural event pass);Employee Assistance Program (EAP);health insurance;30 days of paid vacation;regular team building activities;in-house meetups and innovation competitions;learning opportunities (Udemy courses, workshops);professional working environment;opportunity to work from home.
