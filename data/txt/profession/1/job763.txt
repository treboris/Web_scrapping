MES Engineer
This position is necessary to configure/support/maintain traceability and other shop floor related systems at Harman sites in Hungary. He/she would be the first contact in case of issues with the software interfaces/application on production sides.
Requirements:
Support/maintain traceability solution and other related software components/interfaces in the electronic production areaInstall/configure and test different software componentsSupport the new projects from traceability perspective, cooperate with the core DigITal teamTake part in the resolution of the incident related to the running systems (gather information, analyze issues, do further escalation if necessary)Setup/correct the configuration according to the production needsTake part in the new MES version test and release processInitiatate MES Process developmentMaintenance and operation of MES systems (troubleshooting) on standby if required
Requirements:
Bachelor degree in Electrical/IT engineeringActive english knowledge at communication level (oral+written)Experience in production system supportKnowledge of Microsoft-based operating systemsNetwork knowledgeSystematic thinking, flexibility, ability to work independentlyAble to work under pressure
What is nice to have:
Any kind of traceability/embedded system knowledgeSAP knowledgeProgramming (C#)
