thyssenkrupp Components Technology Hungary Kft.
thyssenkrupp is an international group of companies comprising largely independent industrial and technology businesses and employing 96,000 people. Across 48 countries it generated sales of €41 billion in fiscal year 2021/22. Backed by extensive technological know-how, the businesses develop cost-effective and resource-friendly solutions to the challenges of the future, especially in the fields of climate protection, the energy transition, digital transformation in the industry and mobility of the future.In the Hungarian development center with our engineering team of 1000 we are present as an innovative and significant partner in one of the fastest growing and most exciting segment of the automotive industry, in the field of electromechanical steering systems. With outstanding quality and high-tech developments we meet the needs of renowned car manufacturers and our developments can be found in 25 % of the manufactured cars all over the world. Our engineers work on new challenges that can change the future of automotive.
Application Support Engineer
Main tasks, responsibilities:
The development of solutions for our future PLM / ALM landscape based on the Siemens PLM application suite is your primary goal. You will take part in developing concepts and in the technical realization. You will configure our PLM system landscape and adapt it from a technical perspective. Furthermore you will help in coordinating our external partners as part of the project activities.  In building up our future engineering landscape you will get in touch with different topics like PLM, ALM, MBSE (Model Based System Engineering), Big Data and Advanced Analytics and you will work jointly together with colleagues from the engineering departments as well as from IT. You will always keep an eye on technological developments in order to be able to assess the beneficial use of digital technologies in the field of engineering applications.
Requirements:
BSc/MSc degree in IT or another relevant field Strong foundation of general IT knowledge with a degree of experience in development and also in application operation Experience in application support (both from technical and human perspective) Passion for new technologies and innovations in the PLM and ALM environment Fluent English (both written and spoken) Excellent problem solving skills Friendly personality and good communication skills Enjoy working in a team "Can do" attitude Willingness to travel (once or twice in a year to Eschen, Liechtenstein) Experience in automotive industry
