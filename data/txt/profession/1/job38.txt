HR Governance Specialist
# közösségi közlekedés, #transzformáció #HR Governance
Task:
ObjectiveHR Governance Specialist play an integral role developing, implementing and revising HR Policies and related management instructions in the consistent implementation of employee regulations. This role will act as a subject matter expert on the aspects of Human Resource (HR) risks, compliance and governance, providing guidance around operational excellence (in controls and process optimization) in HR, while ensuring employee centricity and experiences are considered in all enhancements.Roles & Responsibilities•Act as an internal expert and will be responsible for developing and maintaining, as well as communicating and interpreting HR policies and procedures across the business. Ensure alignment with HR best practice and that these practices are compliant within organisation and legislative requirements. Govern, define and implement policies, procedures and processes to all relevant stakeholders;; develop and maintain a policy system, framework and format to track; and manage policy documentation. You will also contribute to the coaching and development of expertise on policies.•Providing a broad range of consultative services to all levels of employees, including policies, procedures, problem solving, dispute resolution, compliance, litigation avoidance.•Leading the Equal Employment Opportunity Programs in compliance with legislation and management's directives. •Communicating policies and affirmative action programs and completing compliance forms.•Ensuring effective and legally compliant plans to address employee performance issues, performance improvement plans and other documentation•Provide governance and oversight for all employee relations policies and procedures. Responsible for driving Compliance processes within Human Capital.•Coordinate the implementation of controls and process enhancements with various HR functional verticals to ensure compliance with regulations and internal policies and procedures.•Support smooth & timely communication with relevant stakeholders during periodic audits, to ensure compliance obligations are met•Actively partner with Risk Management, Compliance, Legal functions as well as the HR functions to ensure Human Capital policies & processes are compliant with local statutory and regulatory requirements, with proper controls while also being employee centric•Be the BilMog representative for People & Culture division.•Support existing external service vendors and procurement process•Coordinate HR Projects
Expectation:
The successful candidate should hold a Degree with an acquired specialisation in human resources, including policy design and implementation. You should be able to demonstrate a wealth of knowledge within policy and employment contract specialism over the last 5-7 years. You should also have a solid understanding of labour laws with with expertise in policies and contracts, project management.The successful candidate should have a strong understanding or interest in HR policy review, Statutory/Regulatory requirements/developments and process improvement/control across the employee life cycle, so as to partner effectively with all HR functional verticals to improve the HR processes. The successful candidate should also have strong understanding of processes within the HR Value Chain and Employee Life Cycle. Skilled in project management and process review, including change management, and has got strong stakeholder management skills.He/she has high level of integrity, takes accountability of work and good attitude over teamwork. Takes initiative to improve current state of things and adaptable to embrace new changes ans familiar with local employment regulatory guidelines and corporate governance best practices.
What we offer:
Az ArrivaBus Kft. egy német tulajdonú, 13 országban személyszállítási szolgáltatásokat nyújtó vállalatcsoport tagja, a hazai autóbuszos piac meghatározó szereplője. A 20 éves múlttal rendelkező társaságunk több, mint 1300 alkalmazottat foglalkoztat a stabil piaci hátteret biztosító tömegközlekedési szektorban. Megújuló 16 fős HR és Kommunikációs csapatunkba keressük az új csapattagot az új szerepkörre a HR Igazgató közvetlen riportjaként.
