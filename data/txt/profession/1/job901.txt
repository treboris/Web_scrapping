Currently we are looking for
IT AUDITOR (LJD)
Location: Budapest, full remote
Salary: competitive
Type of employment: employee, full time
About our client:
Our client is the largest ICT employer in Hungary, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. They develop, market, and operate agile, cloud-native products and services for the digital world. They see themselves as innovation drivers and make their customers' business fit for the digital future.  
Tasks:
Prepare and be part of internal/external audits of a cloud platform within a dedicated team of specialists.Ensure the compliance requirements for various IT audits.Create and map requirements catalogs of different standards.Be a trusted advisor of cross-functional teams for information and IT security during various audits of the platform.Answer audit questions from stakeholders like auditors, customers, and central departments.Bring technical knowledge to stakeholders to fill out compliance gaps.Escalate risks and issues for resolution as appropriate.
Requirements and qualifications:
Good communication skills.Experience in requirements engineering incl. creating and mapping requirements catalogs of different standards.Knowledge of software security concepts and data protection rules.Fluency in written and spoken English.Completed studies in a technical, engineering, or scientific subject or comparable professional training.1-3 years of professional experience in IT ideally with cloud technologies.Hands-on solution-driven experience with IT audits or certifications, such as:ISO27001;ISAE3402/3000;SOC1, SOC2, C5;CSA Star
Advantages:
Fluency in written and spoken German. Experience with Public Clouds.Scrum, DevOps know-how.Experience with Jira/Confluence.

Employees from outside the EU are welcome!REMOTE WORK Available
