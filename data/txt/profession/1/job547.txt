KBC Bank, the parent company of the Hungarian K&H Bank, provides banking services in 5 European countries.The international IT solutions of the banking group, covering several European countries, takes place with Hungarian and Belgian colleagues in Hungary and Belgium, therefore not only our services are international, but also our teams.Our responsibilities range from day-to-day operation to application development and its operation.We are constantly expanding the Hungarian side of the team, which is why we are looking for enthusiastic, motivated new employees with junior, medior or possibly senior work experience. The IT directorate S2E (Service 2 Employee) manages the world of "your digital workplace" and therefore steers - together with Corporate HR, Facility Management and Communication - the digitalization of all KBC employees. We boost your digitality via the O365 applications and we give you insight into how you can grow in digital collaboration using the digital footprint. We support you in using the digital tools and offer help and solutions to problems via DOCK! Portal, DOCK! Store and DOCK! Line.As part of a financial institution, KBC attaches great importance to security, which is why this is a responsibility that is shared by every employee. Our IT Systems Engineers, who build and maintain the KBC ICT systems, are particularly concerned about the security and robustness of systems and applications.This vacancy is part of the team which ensures that every KBC user and IT developer can use his daily business applications as efficiently as possible, in a modern way, i.e. in a 'future proof' environment.   What you can expect working with us:You can make sure your future career is steady within our companyLife-long learning and plenty of opportunities to challenge yourselfCompetitive salary as well as other benefits such as:10% customizable cafeteria,annual performance-based reward,company phone with mobile internet, that you can use for private purposes as wellemployee discount at the bankYour English language skills won’t be worn out as you can use it on a daily basisFlexible schedule as we don’t use ‘Clock in Clock out’ system As our focus is on teamwork we consider it important to hang out in the "open office" together, but you have the option to spend 3 days per week from homeWe value your insight and are always open to suggestionsInternational environment with a cheerful work atmosphereGreen office in Millennium Park, equipped with:bicycle storage and showersrestaurant with panoramic views over the Danube and the National Theatrefree coffee and snack in our community area where we hang out and play darts, and have many other dining options in the surrounding area What we expect from You:strong problem solving skills in a Windows environment.taking ownership of the stability, performance and security of the new services and the different services our internal customers already useability to independently implement new services within a Windows environmentknowledge of Software distribution toolsstrong security skillas an experienced System Engineer/IT professional, you possess the following skills:broad technical experience in Windows operating systems (WIN10-11).
