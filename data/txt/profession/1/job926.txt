Currently we are looking for
AZURE MIGRATION ENGINEER (JD)
Location: Budapest, IX district (partly home office)
Salary: competitive
Type of employment: employee
ABOUT THE POSITION:
As the Cloud Migration Engineer, you will be executing the migration & transformation strategy for our customers as part of an agile migration team, headed by the Migration Architect. Among your main responsibilities you will be performing migrations from On-Premises to Azure and from any Cloud to Azure migrations, utilizing the most efficient tools available. You will be also creating relevant documentation, defining migration plans and processes, performing landscape deployments (both manual and automated).
TASKS:
Perform various migrations between Clouds or from On-Premises to Cloud based on customers’ requirements and needs, focusing on AzureCreate, define & improve processes and plans related to migrationWork in an international environment and with customer representatives in the migration team, while serving as the technology expertCollaborate with T-systems Internal and External partnersParticipate in the roadmap implementation and feedback to the product teamDirectly communicate with end customers to discuss possibilities of migration, transformation and their optimal designs
QUALIFICATIONS:
We are looking for someone with an ambitious spirit who is keen to make a positive impact for our partners. You are a team player and always ready to learn technologies and ready to support enterprises to adopt public cloud. In addition, you ideally bring the following with you:1 or 2+ years of relevant work experience with Azure cloud technologies, with basic certifications (AZ-900, AZ-104)Understanding of Infrastructure as Code (IaC) and automation possibilities (Terraform, ARM templates, Pulumi)General ICT skills (Networking, OS, Storage, Database etc.)Scripting & Coding knowledge (PowerShell, Bash, Python)Experience with Agile tooling and processes (Jira, Kanban, Scrum)Understanding of migration concepts and migration tooling (Azure Migrate)
ADVANTAGES:
Knowledge of PaaS and SaaS cloud servicesPrincipals and skills related to DevOpsExperience with virtual machine migrationExperience with advocating and implementing Cloud Adoption FrameworksDatabase migration experience (Oracle, MS SQL, no SQL)Containerization knowledgeEnglish language (Fluent)
