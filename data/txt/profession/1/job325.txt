pozícióba.
Voted Best Places to Work for several years, we are a dynamic, San Diego based company with a team of experienced HR professionals offering payrolling, staffing, pre-screening, international services, and overall HR related solutions for temporary workers and contractors. Our dedication to customer service and focus on utilizing technology for streamlining processes is our core philosophy. 
Task:
POSITION SUMMARYThe QA Engineer for our HR Tech SaaS will be responsible for ensuring the highest possible quality standards for the software development team's products. This person will be responsible for testing, verifying, and validating product functionality, analyzing user feedback and developing test plans and scenarios.The ideal candidate will hit the ground running with prior experience in software development, software testing and quality assurance, and user experience design. They should have excellent communication and advanced problem-solving skills as well as an understanding of SaaS architecture. This is an exciting opportunity to get in early with the product and grow with an innovative team, into an innovative market!
Expectation:
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES· Conduct systematic reviews of requirements, design, code and functionality to confirm compliance with specified standards· Develop comprehensive test plans and scenarios.· Perform all types of test execution including manual, integration, regression, user acceptance, usability, user-experience, compatibility, and negative testing.· Investigate customer problems reported and identify solutions or workarounds in a timely and efficient manner· Create actionable test reports to inform product development· Work with the development team to identify and resolve areas of risk· Monitor bug resolution efforts and track successes· Monitor product performance and stability over timeQUALIFICATIONS & SKILLS· Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Information Systems, respected bootcamp or related demonstrated learnings.· 3+ years of experience in software development and software testing· 2+ years of experience in quality assurance and user experience design· Experience with various CMS tools and familiarity with industry best practices· Working knowledge of source control management systems, databases and programming/scripting languages (e.g. SQL, GraphSQL, HTML-CSS, AWS, JavaScript)· Excellent creative problem-solving and strong written and verbal communication skills· All verbal and written communications will be conducted in English, so a high degree of proficiency and attention to detail is a key requirement for the position· Ability to understand complex technical and business requirements and test against them.· Ability to both effectively collaborate with cross-functional teams in multiple time zones and work independently· Knowledge of web application testing tools, defect logging tools and scripting languages· Experience working on USA-based products and applications targeted primarily to US markets/audiences
Other:
Additional preferred experience:· Previous experiences with HR-specific tech development and products· Experience using Jira, Confluence, and Notion to facilitate effective workflows and documentation· Hands-on experience optimizing testing workflows with offshore development teams and using low-cost resources to scale thoroughness and scope of testing
