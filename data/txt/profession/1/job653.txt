Digital Project Manager (Strong Junior-Middle)
We are Laba International Group. We are disrupting education by creating live courses with "big name" industry leaders from companies like Google, Microsoft, Netflix, and Nike. Over the past 7 years, we’ve grown from a Ukrainian startup into an international EdTech company with branches in Hungary, Poland, Ukraine, Turkey, the USA, and the Czech Republic.This year we are launching new projects and currently looking for a Digital Project Manager to help us build all the processes from scratch, implement new ideas (no matter how crazy they are:), and adopt the project to the Hungarian market.Your sense of absolute ownership of your courses and your creativity will be what drives the team to success.
Task:
What exactly you will do:Ideate, brainstorm, and manage the production of online courses, including recruiting and engaging new expertsCollaborate with Marketing and Sales leadership to establish a solid understanding of the vision, purpose, and expected business benefits of each course.Monitor the progress of course delivery by tracking production status, risks, issues, and dependencies. You will proactively address problems raised by the delivery team and resolve roadblocks to enable successful program execution.Lead course reviews with students, delivery teams, instructors, and executive management as required.Facilitate course retrospectives to identify improvement opportunities.Lead by example. You will cover key management roles as needed to ensure the success of course production and delivery efforts.Ensure the efficient use of organizational resources.Live and breathe a proactive course production mindset, filling roles as necessary to meet delivery goals.
Expectation:
We are a perfect match if you meet the following requirements:1+ years of experience in project management managing moderately sized digital projects and programs from start to finishExperience in a digital environment (Project Management, IT, PR, Marketing, Communications, creative agencies, etc.)Experience in online education is a significant advantage but not a mustKPI-driven mindsetStrong written and verbal communication skills, with professional confidence and credibility, so that you can effectively engage and interact with senior and executive-level external stakeholdersStrong business acumen combined with marketing or sales background, with the ability to quickly understand and communicate course ideas and their value to a wide range of audiencesProven experience in leading teams in fast-paced, change-intensive environmentsAbility to manage project schedules and associated dependencies, identify possible issues, risks, and actions, and work with key stakeholders to mitigate themAbility to manage multiple priorities effectively and thrive in an environment of uncertainty and changeOutgoing, energetic, upbeat, and fun personality
Other:
What we offer:Flexible schedule. No one cares when and how many hours you work as long as you get stuff done, projects are moving and your team members are happy.Comfortable office in Budapest with an opportunity to work remotely some days a week.Freedom. Share your craziest ideas – we’ll do our best to help you make them real.Opportunity to grow. All of our courses are free for you.+1000 to your knowledge and skills. You are going to work with various tools, formats and target audiences.Work with the best of the best. Our team members are hand-picked from thousands of candidates, are insanely smart and fun to work with.
