Unique, exciting, fun-loving, and fast-growing – Welcome to Docler Group which is the umbrella company of Byborg Enterprises! Since launching in 2001, we have created products and experiences that entertain millions of people across the globe. We now serve more than 50 million users a day and are consistently among the top visited websites in the world with following in the millions!Job DescriptionThe Operational Project Manager will work directly with  György Gattyán, the Founder & Owner of Docler Holding.György Gattyán, the Founder & Owner of Docler Holding is looking for a quick witted and hands on Operational Project Manager, who would like to be part of a dynamic company and enjoys tackling the challenges that come with a diverse portfolio.  The ideal candidate is someone who gets inspired by a fast paced and innovative environment and has a keen eye for efficient execution.

Task:
Work under the direct guidance and supervision of the Founder & Owner of the Group on various companywide and cross departmental projects, monitor those projects from inception to execution.Be accountable for and align multiple projects and initiatives simultaneously across all business functions.Support the Founder & Owner in decision making, prioritize task lists and pro-actively take on tasks.Coordinate and manage the execution of tasks by the management team and ensure the optimal functioning and reporting of the various departments within the company.Monitor and supervise the progress of the operational projects and ensure efficiency and timely completion of tasks.Implement and effectively communicate the vision and strategy of the company.Manage relations with external partners, internal stakeholders, and the leadership team.Document and distribute detailed meeting recaps with actionable next steps.Ensure the routing for approval and review of all materials.Prepare presentations, concepts, analyses, and communicate effectively with senior management.Any additional task required by the Founder & Owner.
Expectation:
Bachelor’s Degree in relevant field5+ years professional experience required, preferably in a fast-paced environmentOutstanding analytical, problem-solving, and organizational skillsDemonstrated success of project management and KPI tracking, preferably within a startup environmentExperience in working with senior leadership teamExpert knowledge of project management principles and applicationsExcellent written and verbal communication skills in English and Hungarian with focus on crisp, concise, and effective communication, French is a plusProactive, flexible, pragmatic with a ‘can-do’ mentalityAbility to create and maintain effective working relationships with multiple parties, internally as well as externallyExperience in working with executives is an advantage
