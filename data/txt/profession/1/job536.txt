the oracle environment in the KBC eco system and provide the necessary Oracle database support to the application teams. What you can expect working with us:You can make sure your future career is the surrounding areaWhat we expect from You: System engineer unix with oracle knowledge or strong interests to build up strong oracle knowledge.Your main responsibilities areYou maintain frameworks and scripts, mostly written in Perl. You are also able to adapt them to new oracle versions or new requirements.You help the application teams with their oracle issues. This includes investigating performance issues, proposing concrete actions to the application teams, supporting the team to build performant queries. But it can also include changesall actions that are necessary to keep the environment up and running (daily checks, proactive actions in case of technical changes in datacenter, …)Follow up security recommendations (proactively and reactively) and minimize the operational riskmaintenance of all Oracle frameworks used to perform Oracle management and are written in Perl.dealing with all systems interfacing with the Oracle database, like Centrify, UAC, Cyberark, VMWare, Bladelogic, SNOW.responsible for the life cycle management of all Oracle Database products.
