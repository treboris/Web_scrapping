GIAM Analyst (Global Identity and Access Management)
Our partner, which is a multinational bank is looking for a GIAM Analyst (Global Identity Access Management) to join their team. The project starts with a 6-month fixed-term contract but there is a possibility of an extension and there are other opportunities within the company.
Other information
You will work in partnership with other units to build appropriate security architecture in new apps and supports large business-driven technology projects related to information security issues. The GIAM Analyst will assist the Management and Senior Analyst in collaboration and work efforts across the organization to ensure consistent quality and standards of operation.
FELADATOK:
Monitor and maintain daily BAU Volume; Work with the management team in achieving established goals; Provide core customer service support for assigned applications and systems; Initiate process changes and improvements; Provide customer service support and work closely with other business groups to provide support; Provide guidance regarding information security concepts/policies; Identify, register, and resolve potential compliance issues; Communicate the need for change, involve stakeholders in the change process and apply change theory to complete the successful adoption;Appropriately assess risk when business decisions are made and assets;Participate in regular meetings and calls be able to work confidently with senior colleagues, partners, and managers;Escalating, managing, and reporting control issues with transparency.
ELVÁRÁSOK:
Bachelor's degree;2-5 years of relevant experience in a fast-paced environment with global teams in a corporate setting;Proficient English user in verbal and written;Good communication skills;Eager to work with other countries, open to different cultures, personalities and nationalities;Be able to work from 07:00 to 15:00 (Monday to Friday).
ELŐNY:
Knowledge of database platforms and operating systems;Programming language knowledge.
AMIT A CÉG BIZTOSÍT:
Hybrid work model (3 days HO / 2 days in-office);In addition to the base salary: cafeteria and HO allowance.
