Your place in the team
To coordinate the execution of the Business Continuity (BC) programme agreed by senior management to deliver organisational resiliency in an ever changing threat landscape.
Propose an organisation-wide BC management system aligned with business goals and objectives and prepare the BC programme for management signoff Develop and maintain the business continuity programme and policyComplete critical process BIAs to map potential distruption eventsEmbed BC activity into existing processes and communication channelsDeterminde RTOs and RPOs for critical processes, in cooperation with process ownersDevelop BC and DR plans with process owners and regularly test and evaluate those plansEnsure role based training of BCP and DRP participants and the rest of the employees and contractors to a limited extentValidate effectivity and efficiency of BCM and regularly report to senior managementRegularly test emergency communications with designated key employees and keep the contact list up to date. Ensure exchange of information with Mobile Operator partner.Build and maintain a designated Task Force for incident management, monitor capacity and competency of assigned personnel.Plan, communicate and execute Incident Management exercises in a documented manner to grasp lessons learned and define deficiencies to finetune incident management capabilities.Prepare to have BC&CM activities certified according to relevant ISO/IEC standardsSupport Critical Infrastructure Protection tasks as necessary
