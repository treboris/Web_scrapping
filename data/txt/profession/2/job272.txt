JIRA Administrator
We cooperate with a Fintech solution which centralizes as well as simplifies multi-bank connectivity and transaction communications – globally.
Your responsibilities will include
Gather requirements for business processes, and determine ways to optimize/improve JIRA/Confluence/BitBucket build and configureProvide application support for JIRA and Confluence to IT the rest of the businessUnderstanding of JIRA APIs and webhooksWork with business users to evaluate JIRA and Confluence plug-ins, determine licensing needs, and perform testing, installation and configuration of the plug-insArchitect and administer JIRA deployment, focusing on providing a platform for Housing units to administer, track and report on department-wide projects and issuesUtilizes design skills to define JIRA workflows and the related screen schemes for complex and high impact projectsAnalyse current JIRA configurations and migrate to new instancesSupport and configure JIRA plugins as requiredGenerate documentation on workflows and processes implemented in JIRA to support runbooksImprove JIRA security, set access permissions for JIRA projects and Confluence spacesCreate JIRA projects, queries and reports as requiredParticipating in 3rd level oncall support duties as part of the SysOps team
You are looking for us, if You have
4+ years of experience with JIRA administrationExperience with creating, maintaining, and troubleshooting complex JIRA workflows, screen schemes, permission schemes, and notification schemesMinimum of 3-5 years’ hands-on experience working with the Atlassian product suite, with specific focus on JIRA as a Project/Application Administrator.Experience in Application Deployment – Java, Standalone Custom Apps, COTS ProductsAbility to construct Confluence pages and integrate JIRA projects with Confluence pages, a mustHigh degree of familiarity with databases and MS SQL competence is a plusAbility to write custom JQL (Jira Query Language) is a plusDemonstrated experiences in Linux environmentIndependent, proactive and self-confident personality with a high willingness to take on and bear responsibilityFluent EnglishWillingness to travel < 10%
Your Future colleagues

You can expect a motivated team, a good working atmosphere in a friendly environment.Together with our customers, the internal IT and implementation specialists, you will find and implement solutions to a diverse set of challenges in the area of global payments.

We have carefully designed career paths (and a career management system), we provide the appropriate training courses and projects to accomplish your aims.Our community is characterized by mutual respect and an attitude of helpfulness. This high professional quality level and the colorful personalities will help you spend your everyday life in a good mood, why you make the best of your skills and talents.We are happy when you are open and help you discover the world: after six months you will receive substantial discounts that you can use to visit any place in the world taking Lufthansa flights!
