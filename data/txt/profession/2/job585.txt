Task:
We are looking for an IT Technician, dealing with IT Desktop Support within a multinational company directly at our customer's premises in Budapest.Main tasks:Install, upgrade, support and troubleshoot Windows and any other authorized desktop software/applicationsInstall, upgrade, support and troubleshoot End User Workspace Hardware such as Desktop, Laptops, Printers, Mobile Devices, Video and Conferencing Equipment and any other authorized peripheral equipment and devicesPerform general preventative maintenance tasks on devices and softwareFollow and execute instructions provided by Subject Matter ExpertsProvide onsite hand and eye support to CustomersAccurately record, update and document requests using the IT service desk systemMaintain a first-class level of customer service ensuring that all customers are treated efficiently and in an appropriate mannerContribute towards reporting as per customer requirement

At least 1 years of experience in the IT field or in a IT related areaComprehensive understanding of the full range of Enterprise IT ServicesDetailed knowledge of installation and site environmental support solutionsApplies extensive knowledge of the job skills, company policies and procedures to complete complex, specialized assignments/tasks in creative and effective waysProficient Field Technical Support skill setProficient knowledge of common Operating System platforms, installation and troubleshooting skills (Windows, Mac OS – desired but not mandatory)Good knowledge on Hands and FeetStrong Microsoft Office skills (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.)Strong Break / Fix skills Desktops, Workstations, Notebooks and Printers and HandheldsBroad experience of IT with a basic understanding of Networks, Servers and TelecomsExperience including remote control of PCs and video conferencing knowledgeKnowledgeable of basic IT asset management concepts (updating Asset Management system)Strong communication skills verbal and writtenFluent in Hungarian and EnglishStrong Customer relationship building skillsProven capability in client management in the context of demanding service level-based contracts with penalties associated with below-standard deliveryDriving License
