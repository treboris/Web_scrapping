SCRUM master
“This role is ideal for an excellent Scrum Master with technical interest or knowledge, who enjoys enabling colleagues to succeed. In addition to coaching your team, you are expected actively to contribute to the agile transformation in the division in collaboration with your fellow Scrum Masters and Leadership Team,” says Jørgen.
Are you interested in working at a market leading multinational company together with a highly-skilled, cross-functional Scrum Team embarking on their agile journey?
At Grundfos, we develop, produce and sell some of the worlds’ most efficient and energy saving pumps and pump solutions across the globe. We help our customers save natural resources and reduce climate impact. With us, you will be part of an international workplace where innovation, collaboration and development are front and centre in everything we do. Grundfos is financially independent, stable company, where next to our customers, employees are also number ones for us. Grundfos provides free professional training and personal development opportunities in an international environment. The position offers a competitive salary and benefits package, in a pleasant working atmosphere and environment.
You will join our global Research & Development unit in Székesfehérvár, Hungary. Your Scrum Team is part of the Domestic Building Services division, which has the full end-to-end responsibility for our domestic pumps for residential use. The team is responsible for the domestic waste-water pump portfolio with team members in Hungary, Germany and DenmarkIn your role as Scrum Master you will act as true leader supporting the team and organisation by guiding and coaching them to continuously improve their way of working. You assist the team and organisation identify impediments and facilitate that they are removed.In addition to the Scrum team, you will join our Scrum Master Guild, where you will find support and sparring from Scrum Masters also working in the division. Your main responsibilities:Coaching and guiding your cross-functional Scrum team to deliver high-value products.Supporting and coaching the Product Owner in product backlog management, product planning and stakeholder management.Being especially focused on the team’s well-being, and enable them understand how to work as a self-directed, cross-functional team.Communicating with stakeholders and partners to develop and maintain an extensive network within the complete organization.Working closely with other teams delivering to the same programs to remove implements for the teamFurther developing your own skills while also learning and growing together with your fellow Scrum Masters.Ensuring that all Scrum events take place.
We imagine that you have:Certified Scrum Master or equivalent and proven experience with this or generic servant leadership is crucial to the jobEffective communication skills both orally and in written EnglishEffective facilitation skillsA wish to learn and grow your Scrum Masters skillsCapability of traveling (approximately 10 times a year)Knowledge within integrated product developmentAbility to understand technical issues is highly beneficial, but if you do not have this we will together develop it
Whether it’s developing leadership skills or advancing your expertise even further, we’ll support you with continuous learning and development opportunities, to help discuss and steer your long-term Grundfos career path. You’ll be welcomed from day one into an inclusive, trusting environment guided by six core values.
You are always welcome to our offices where you can engage, learn, and have fun with colleaguesOn top of it, flexible working hours and up to 3 days’ additional paid leave for volunteering of your choiceAnnual bonuses, health insurance, parental support, internal well-being consultants and programmesAccess to the modern Grundfos Academy to pursue further both personal and professional developmentDiverse, inclusive environment with employee-led forums offering colleagues a safe place to connect and share openlyDo you want to learn more?If this job sounds appealing, please send your resume and cover letter by clicking “Apply”.We look forward to hearing from you.
Grundfos is one of the world’s leading water technology companies with more than 19.000 employees in 60+ countries all over the world. Our skills commit us to pioneering solutions to the world’s water and climate challenges and improve the quality of life for people. We dare to do things that others cannot or dare not do, as we believe innovation is not only a business opportunity, but an obligation. And what really matters to us is not short term profit, but the impact we make. By becoming part of our united powerful team, you too can drive this change no matter your role.
How to apply/ contact information:
If you like the look of this job and know you can bring your skills to contribute to our purpose, please apply. We need and welcome professional people from all corners – however you identify and whatever your background is.
