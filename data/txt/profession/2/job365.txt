Welcome to OTP Bank
OTP Group provides its universal financial services through several subsidiaries. OTP Bank started its international expansion targeting countries in the CEE region, which offer great economic growth potentials similar to Hungary. OTP Bank has completed successful acquisitions in the past years, becoming a key player in the region. Actually it is present in 11 countries besides Hungary. As a leading financial service provider in the region, the Group operates with 1 400 branches, 36 000 employees and serves more than 16 million customers. If you would like to join us, apply on our career site.
Senior Business Analyst
As a member of our team, you will work together with reporting experts and DevOps professionals on developing and maintaining smooth operation of Group level databases. The main task is managing the development and data quality improvement projects related to our BI products in a supportive and innovative environment.
