Hello there! We’re Vodafone Intelligent Solutions (also known as _VOIS) and we’re looking for a
Product Model Specialist
Your role in a nutshell: 
The Product Model Specialist is tasked to elaborate and manage product information- known as digital product definition (DPD) - using the Vodafone Product Data Model (VPDM). As this information is currently not available digitally, the Product Model Specialist is working with a wide range of roles to collect this information from (Product Manager, Commercial Manager, Product Architect, NPD Project Manager, etc.), understands and interprets the information and captures it in the Product Specification database (PSdb). This information is then managed by the Product Model Specialist throughout the create-manage-destroy data lifecycle. The Product Model Specialist also contributes to the evolution of both the VPDM and PSdb, and provides on demand reporting, data extracts to support various project work throughout the business.
Why you will love your job:
Create, maintain and manage Digital Product Definitions (DPD) based on the latest version of the Vodafone Product Data Model (VPDM)Liaise with Product Managers to ensure the validity of the information stored in the DPDs Perform Data uplift on DPDs when the latest version of VPDM is released Conduct data integrity, compliance and consistency checks on a regular basis Perform on demand reporting based on product data/information Align with key data transformation programs leveraging/targeting the product domains Contribute to the VPDM release plans Contribute to the technical roadmap of the PSdb development Create project and progression roadmap in alignment to stakeholder needs (input generated by demand process and a prioritisation call from functional manager) 
What you will bring to the team:
High fluency in English High attention to detail Excellent organisational skills applied on data & project management Project management skills: Agile (desired), Prince II (desired) Solid understanding on complex database concepts and modelling (desired) Basic understanding of web technologies ITIL: Strong understanding of Change Management and Transformation Good understanding of Big Data principles and best practices Strong negotiation skills Strong stakeholder management skills up to C-Level Experience with ServiceNow (desired) The ability to think both strategically and tactically 
In return we provide you with:
A dynamic environment where innovative ideas are always welcome;A collaborative community where your professional goals and work are supported by a diverse team;Access to internal trainings through Vodafone University and external trainings via other providers;A supportive internal coaching and mentoring culture;Opportunities to participate in company activities and Vodafone Foundation events;Corporate assets including a laptop and mobile phone with Vodafone RED subscription (unlimited talk, text message, and data within the EU)Vodafone HOME discounted fleet services for employees and their friends & familyCafeteria package;Private Medical Services;Employee Assistant Program to support your wellbeing and mental health;Currently, we are trialling a flexible and hybrid way of working to ensure our colleagues’ wellbeing and health & safety; we combine working from home with meeting max 2 times/week in our fully refurbished, digital office in Budapest What you need to know about usWe are the engine that powers Vodafone around the world: our work impacts millions of people around the globe every day. If you want to experiment and learn fast, integrated into an international, multicultural and inclusive team, then _VOIS is the place for you.
