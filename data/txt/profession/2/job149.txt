Csatlakozz jó hangulatú csapatunkhoz!
KBC Bank, the parent company of the Hungarian K&H Bank, provides banking services in 5 European countries.The international IT solutions of the banking group, covering several European countries, takes place with Hungarian and Belgian colleagues in Hungary and Belgium, therefore not only our services are international, but also our teams.Our responsibilities range from day-to-day operation to application development and its operation.We are constantly expanding the Hungarian side of the team, which is why we are looking for enthusiastic, motivated new employees with junior, medior or possibly senior work experience.The ‘Competence Centre Group Information Risk Management’, situated within the directorate Group Risk, is looking to enforce its team with an expert. This international team is responsible to help entities shape their risk practices, and to give reasonable assurance to top management that their information risks are under control.The Competence Centre consists of 3 teams:AssuranceSupportProject Office We are looking for a Risk Expert to join the assurance team of the  “Information Risk Management” department.Our mission is to enable, help and challenge all entities of the KBC Group in the way they handle their information security/cyber security and IT-related risks.To strengthen the team were looking for colleagues who have a sound risk background, but are also eager to help other colleagues in the risk domain. The job focusses primarily on Information Security and IT related risks.If you are fascinated by the risk universe and if you are looking for a job were investigating and helping go hand in hand, then you are the expert we are looking for.  What you can expect working with us:You can make sure your future career is steady within our companyLife-long learning and plenty of opportunities to challenge yourselfCompetitive salary as well as other benefits such as:10% customizable cafeteria,annual performance-based reward,company phone with mobile internet, that you can use for private purposes as wellemployee discount at the bankYour English language skills won’t be worn out as you can use it on a daily basisFlexible schedule as we don’t use ‘Clock in Clock out’ system As our focus is on teamwork we consider it important to hang out in the "open office" together, but you have the option to spend 3 days per week working from homeWe value your insight and are always open to suggestionsInternational environment with a cheerful work atmosphereGreen office in Millennium Park, equipped with:bicycle storage and showersrestaurant with panoramic views over the Danube and the National Theatrefree coffee and snacks in our community area where we hang out and play darts, and many dining options in the surrounding area  What will be your main tasks:Plan & perform independent investigations to give reasonable assurance that the risks are under control.Act as a human 'risk' radar, facilitator & independent observer.Define sound controls & practices to handle risk on a day-to-day basis.Define metrics to measure the maturity of controlsHelp prepare reports for the key stakeholders (Group ExCo, Group CRO, Group IT Risk Community, …) via a structural information feed from the community.  The profile we are looking for combines all the qualities of a natural ambassador, and a strong expert in the domains of Information Security, Cybercrime, IT,  Cloud, vendor, Business Continuity:This vacancy is open to applicants with a university degree. Relevant working experience in ICT or in a scientific discipline is an asset.You are willing to work in a changing and fast evolving environment and are prepared to constantly keep you knowledge up to date. What you can expect during the recruitment process:After sending your CV in English, expect a two-round interview process, one online in English with the Belgian team leader and one at our office in Budapest (in Hungarian)An efficient selection process is important for both of us, so we try to make it as fast as possible, and even if you are not selected we will give you a detailed feedback.
