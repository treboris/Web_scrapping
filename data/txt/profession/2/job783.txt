Project Manager - aiSim
We are looking for a junior/medior Software Project Manager to manage the execution of our aiSim projects. 
As a Project Manager you will work with Edit Nagy and Gergő Heltai directly and work closely with the members of aiSim. aiMotive began developing its innovative aiSim in the nascent days of simulation in automated driving. Building this expertise has allowed us to create the first ISO26262 certified tool available to the broader automotive industry. TCL 3 enables aiSim to be an integral part of a development and verification toolchain up to ASIL D, ensuring deterministic, realistic, and repeatable testing. 
You are an ideal candidate if you are comfortable with both customer-facing and supplier-facing environments. At aiMotive, we are encouraging an open, collaborative, and friendly working environment. 
Job Summary
Managing internal and client projects of aiSimSoftware project management from initiation to closure:Project Charter and InitiationDefinition and planning of the project (scope & budget, WBS, Gantt chart, communication plan, risk management)Project execution (status reporting, tracking in Jira, defining KPI’s, quality control)Project Performance and Control (objectives, deliverables, effort, and cost tracking)Project Closure (Lessons learned, reporting)Managing requirements, expectations, and issues between the customer and the companyPreparation of documentation in Confluence and communication of results, presentation In-depth understanding of development processes
