The CompanyNXP Semiconductors is a semiconductor manufacturing company (chip-maker) enabling secure connections and infrastructure for a smarter world, advancing solutions that make lives easier, better and safer. As the world leader in secure connectivity solutions for embedded applications, we are driving innovation in the secure connected vehicle, end-to-end security & privacy and smart connected solutions markets.The TeamThe S&P (Systems and Processes) department is the linking pin between the business and IT. S&P Business Consultants of our department support all organizational levels of SCO (Supply Chain Organization) with analysis on planning results, improvements, projects, operational support.Establish and maintain a solid network between different organization units that S&P has a relation with. The B2B Business Consultant acts as a liaison between second line support and the end user community globally.
Task:
Purpose of the roleThe Systems and Processes department provides operational support to the NXP SCO organization and drives the global SCO infrastructure programs and operational excellence projects as defined in the SCO roadmap.Key ResponsibilitiesINCIDENT MANAGEMENTProvide support on incidents that hamper end-users to work with the system(s) or execute the processes by solving the deviation in expected behaviour or putting a work-around in place.SERVICE REQUESTSProvide support on “how-to” questions and ad hoc activities that enable end users to execute their function.Provide consultancy to end users and (higher) management on complex supply chain (B2B) topics.TRAININGTrain the user community to operate and optimize the use of the processes, procedures and supporting systems. Pro-active identification of required training needs in the user community.MASTER DATA MANAGEMENTProvide the user community with the system tools and processes to continuously monitor the data consistency and accuracy. Drive improvements to ensure effective and efficient master data processes and high quality of master data within the supporting systems.CHANGE MANAGEMENTInitiate / drive improvements to obtain effective and efficient supply chain processes in line with KPI targets. Improvements can be realized by a change to the software, process, reports or the documentation of the supporting system or business process.Translate business requirements into procedures, processes or functional system specifications and put them forward through the change control and implementation process. PROJECT SUPPORT & EXECUTIONRepresent the business in supply chain management and EDI/CP projects with relation to systems and processes as defined in the agreed SCO roadmap or by local management.Drive and support system related and EDI projects. 
Expectation:
Key skillsPro-activity, self starting, taking initiative and continuously seeking improvement possibilitiesCreative and innovative attitudeCustomer delivery focusTeam-player in a potentially virtual teamAbility to work in cross-functional and multi-cultural teamsAbility to provide support to end users globally Setting priorities together with stakeholders Embedding quality standards in daily workFlexibility in working hours/shiftsTime management and planning skillsStrong problem preventing, conceptual and analytical skillsStrong commitment to achieve personal and organizational objectivesTaking ownershipFlexible, adapts to rapidly changing environmentCommunicates with clarity, structure and conciseness in both one to one and group situations. Adopts presentation to level of listener or reader.Fluency in English is requiredAbility to relate EDI technical processes to customer requirements, Customer Programs and legacy systems
