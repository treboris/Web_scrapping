E-Commerce Coordinator
Maintain and update the e-commerce website and marketplace listings, including product information and promotional campaigns.Create and optimize digital assets such as email campaigns, social media posts, and online ads.Monitor website and marketplace traffic, sales, and customer behavior using analytics tools to identify opportunities for improvement and optimize website performanceImplement digital marketing campaigns across various channels such as email, social media, and online advertising. TEAMThe E-commerce Coordinator will be a member of the E-commerce team within the Digital department, with no direct managerial responsibilities. MAIN ACTIVITIESMaintain and update the brand's e-commerce website and marketplaces, ensuring accuracy and consistency of product information, pricing, and promotions.Manage the product catalog on the e-commerce website and marketplaces, ensuring that it is up to date and optimized for search engines.Manage product feeds for various shopping channels, marketplaces and affiliates.Monitor and analyze website and marketplace metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven recommendations to improve the user experience and increase conversions.Collaborate with the Marketing team to ensure that the e-commerce website and marketplaces are aligned with the brand's overall marketing and branding strategy.Troubleshoot website and marketplaces issues, liaising with technical support as needed.Provide various content to advertising agencies and follow-up on implementation.Stay up to date with the latest e-commerce and digital marketing trends and make recommendations for new initiatives or improvements to existing processes.Assist the digital and e-commerce team with daily tasks.Participate in various digital projects.
Experience using e-commerce platforms such as Salesforce Commerce Cloud.Experience managing product listings on marketplaces such as Amazon.Experience using graphic design software such as Adobe Creative Suite for creating digital assets such as banners, ads, and social media posts.Knowledge in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for website development and customization.Strong technical proficiency, including expertise in marketplace management platform and experience working with web content management systems (CMS) and email marketing platforms.Knowledge of website analytics tools such as Google Analytics, and experience analyzing website traffic and user behavior to optimize website performance.Proficiency in English, any additional languages being a plus.Excellent knowledge of computer technologies in general (internet browsing, social media, SEO, SEM, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.) PERSONALITY, SOFT SKILLSStrong communication skills (both written and verbal) to effectively communicate with team members, stakeholders, and external partners.Attention to detail and accuracy in managing product catalogs and website content.Analytical skills to interpret data and metrics to evaluate the effectiveness of digital campaigns and make data-driven decisions.Positive attitude and a can-do mentality to approach challenges with enthusiasm and energy.Strong problem-solving skills to troubleshoot issues and find solutions quickly. EXPERIENCE REQUIREDMin 2 years experience in e-commerce and online marketplace management
