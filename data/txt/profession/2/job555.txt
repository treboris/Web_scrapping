The Financial Service Center of MOL Group,
MOL GBS Hungary is looking for a
BI and SAP Analytics Cloud developer
MOL GBS is shared services organization delivers finance and HR support for MOL Group companies. The organization is comprised of 500 colleagues located in Hungary and Slovakia with the ambition to extend service scope to all European countries where MOL is present. The scope of services includes Statutory and Financial Reporting, Transactional Accounting, Customer Credit Management, Treasury and Payment Operations, Payroll and HR support.
Your daily routine will be in the position:
Understand business requirementsInteract with developers, BAs and end-usersTranslate requirement into functional and technical specifications for SAC solutionsDesign and implement dashboards and reportsBuild unit test plans and unit test objects as part of sprint increment activitySupport execution of User Acceptance TestProvide support for live objects
You are best equipped for this role if you have:
Bachelor’s degree (or ongoing studies) in Economics, Finance, Information Technology, Engineering, Business Administration or another related field.Knowledge of data modeling and preparing datasets, merging3+ years of SAC experienceknowledge in configuration of live and and import data connectionsexperience in developing SAC stories using multiple sourcesexperience in using Analytics Designer3+ years of SAP BW(on HANA) experienceJavascript experienceMS Suite knowledge (particularly MS Excel)Strong analytical, problem solving and interpersonal skillsStrong communication skills in Hungarian and English
Advantages:
Previous working experience in international environments (or projects)3+ years SQL experienceSAP certifications
