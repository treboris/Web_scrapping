Would you like to participate in the transformation of the Waste Management industry in Hungary?  
Would you be happy to drive the change towards Circular Economy?  
Would you like to work in a professionally talented team with a supportive Leader?  
Then your place is here with US!  
MOL Group is an independent and international oil and gas company, headquartered in Budapest, Hungary. It is active in over 30 countries with a dynamic international workforce of 26,000 people and a track record of more than 100 years in the industry.  
If you would like to shape this transformation and you are willing to work in a complex environment, then your place is with us: we would like to strengthen our capabilities with a  
Finance Digital Product Owner in Waste Management
Responsibilities: 
Responsible for the vision and roadmap of the IT systems supporting Finance and Controlling processes regarding Waste Management. Familiar with SAP.   Works closely together with business representatives to identify development areas, to understand business demands and to define functional requirements.  Aligns development directions with relevant other Product Owners, exchange possibilities with IT department.  Defines priorities based on expected value and available resources, handles priority conflicts and interdependencies.  Manages related project portfolio, steers project delivery, occasionally participates in project work, ensures quality.  Works together, supports and coordinates third party vendors to ensure the business expectations are met during delivery.  Continuously challenges the status quo, follows up market trends, newest technologies and best practices. Benchmarks to other companies.  Participates in yearly financial planning.  
Requirements
University/College degree  6-8 years of work experience, 3-4 years on relevant area   “Customer first” mindset and strong stakeholder management skills  General good understanding of IT systems and architectures, project management practices (including agile), development methods  Advanced presentation, verbal and written communication skills, good English knowledge  Strategic thinking and analytical approach  Flexibility, results oriented mindset and problem-solving attitude 
What we offer:
Permanent position, starting as soon as possible  The opportunity to transform the waste management industry in Hungary and support the transition to circular economy  Professional trainings and a challenging environment to continuously develop yourself  Professional advancement within MOL Group  Enthusiastic team you can learn from  MOL competitive benefits   High quality company mobile phone with advantageous voice and data package for personal usage as well  Free health programs  Flexi time, home office possibilities  Family and other events you can be part of  Own restaurant and available services in the office buildin
