Business Operations & Change Specialist (ServiceNow)
We require aBusiness Operations & Change Specialist at Kyndryl that will support the Kyndryl Enterprise (Internal) ServiceNow Platform Lead to create and deliver strategies and plans which maximise the adoption of the ServiceNow platform. 
Your key responsibilities will be:
Creation and maintenance of KPI’s Metrics scorecards in support of the daily operations and strategy of the Kyndryl Enterprise ServiceNow platformMobilising and facilitating champions communities to drive engagementStakeholder identification, mapping and analysisCreation and delivery of ServiceNow specific communications channels (ServiceNow, Sharepoint, Yammer etc)Training Needs AnalysisDeveloping and driving ServiceNow training strategies and plansCreation and delivery of ServiceNow specific training contentLeveraging ServiceNow functionality to drive business readinessSupport benefits mapping and realisationSupport Stakeholders with business readiness activitiesLeveraging ServiceNow functionality to drive and track adoption
Required Technical and Professional Expertise:
3-5 years ServiceNow development, Agile practices, Stakeholder relationship management experience
Preferred Technical and Professional Experience:
ServiceNow Certs including ServiceNowServiceNow Administration and ServiceNow Application Development Fundamentals ServiceNow Certified System Administrator
