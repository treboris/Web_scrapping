About Nissan
We are a leading global automotive company we can be found 191 countries, with over 160.000 employees worldwide, creating and sharing innovative products and services since 1933.
Nissan has been always passionate about creating innovative technologies and products in the spirit of "do what others don’t dare to do
Here in Budapest 
Here at the Nissan Regional Business Services (NRBS), we are a diverse and international team who actively contributing towards the development, growth and efficiency of the business across our region.
Mission and Objectives: 
In this Business Analyst for Java & Web position you will be responsible for requirements management and business analysis, high level design and solutions architecture.    
Manage operational availability of products/systems and be the subject matter expert for a number of products/systems. 
Tasks and Responsibilities:
Designs solutions that must be aligned with business and IT strategies and comply with the organization’s architectural standards. Might be involved in the full systems life cycle and is responsible for designing, coding, testing, implementing, maintaining and supporting application software that is delivered on time and within budget. Makes recommendations towards the development of new code or reuse of existing code.System and Data architecture design, performance monitoring, product evaluation and buy versus build recommendations
Background & Experience:
Experience in systems analysis, functional design and a solid understanding of development, quality assurance and integration methodologies. Strong knowledge in systems designs techniques (e.g. Data Flow Diagrams, Data Models, etc.). Capable of translating business requirements into application designUnderstanding of relevant technologies. Good understanding of the software development process, including requirement gathering, analysis/design, development tools/technologies, release/version control, contemporary testing methodologies and deployment management Ability to communicate needs and schedules to IS and clients, as well as ensure that plans are in place and standards are adhered to. Good oral and written communication skills, as well as ability to present and explain functional information in a way that establishes rapport, persuades others and gains understanding.  Language Skills:Fluent English verbal and written communication skills
