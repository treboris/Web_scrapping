Functional specialist, Process control, GBS IT
Role purpose:
The Functional Specialist is the solution expert of one or more functional area/module in Process control.
Considered as the “go-to” person when detailed setup or solution design questions are to be addressed on a particular functional area.
Responsibilities:
Responsible for the following tasks within the change control process:Creation of the Functional Design DocumentationDetailed solution designTest scenarios/scripts to validate the solution Within the Operational Support process Functional Specialist is considered as 2nd level support.Act as liaison with the live businesses to proactively engage with solution proposals, keep up networking within the respective functional area/module and ensure leveraging best practicesCollaborates with the Functional Specialists and/or Solution Architects of integrated systems (ERP and MES) to ensure global template consistency and solution fits across locations.Supports multiple projects and BAU activities, so they are not dedicated resources for a single project.Reviews and supports testing of the developer code for developments in conjunction with developers and liaises and resolves faults/questions identified by the business.Area of responsibility: Die management, Manufacturing (press and press saw operations), Quality, ERP/MES integrations
Requirements:
3 years plus working with Process control / MES or variant in a hands-on environmentExperience in operational support of Process control / MES systemsExtensive experience with application configuration, including workflowsExperience defining business requirements, writing functional design documentationExpert level understanding of Process control / MES systems, modules and underlying architecture designExperience integrating Process control with different MES/ERP systems, as well as working with integration toolsFull lifecycle Process control / MES experience in manufacturing environment with specific understanding of manufacturing processes and operationsCollege or University (IT /Business Degree or Equivalent)Expert in depth understanding of business functions. Specific knowledge of at least one of the following modules: Manufacturing (shopfloor), Quality, MES/ERP integrationsExpert knowledge on any Process control/MES systemIntegration understandingAdvanced knowledge of SQL (Oracle database)Knowledge of Power BIStrong spoken and written EnglishExperience with working in remote, multi-culture teams
