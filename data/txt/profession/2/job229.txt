Digital Project Manager
The most important value of Moholy-Nagy University of Art and Design (MOME) is its open mindset to the world in which respect for tradition, aspiration and promoting development all take part equally. Our mission is to find and provide answers to today’s challenges with the tools of art, design, and technology in cooperation with representatives of other fields. The ambition of MOME is to become Central Europe’s leading educational hub for innovation and art by 2030. We have recently reached a milestone on this journey as MOME will represent Hungary in the European Network of Innovative Higher Education Institutes.
Task:
leading the development processes of digital transformation projects (MOME Web, MOME App, smart university, paperless office, digital administration, student life platform etc.), in cooperation with the internal clients and stakeholders at MOME creating project plans, roadmaps, project budgets and documentation leading project teams, organise and assign tasks and deadlines, clear roadblocks etc.managing internal development of web applicationscoordinating external development teams, manage the backlog and monitor their progressparticipating in product design processes (problem definition, brainstorming, prototyping, testing)implement and customise existing software for the needs of MOME citizensable to express your own ideas in a creative environment that helps spark a wide range of new ideas
Expectation:
qualification: BSc or higher preferably in IT or business,  1-3 years experience in digital project management, buisness analytics, process planning, process development analytic mindset and process based perspective strong communication skills, capability of facilitating workshops and drive processes independentlystructured, detail-oriented, problem-solving approach with a strong will to learn about new technologies and innovative solutionsability to express your own ideas in a creative environment that helps spark a wide range of new ideasbasic understanding of software development processesto work with us in the officewritten and verbal english language skills (min. B2) 
What we offer:
Private-sector competitive financial offer Innovative, research and design-oriented work environmentPossibility to express and develop your own ideas inside the organization A fantastic work environment on a brand new campus Opportunity for growth Inspiring and friendly atmosphere, young team
