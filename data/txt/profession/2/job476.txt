CI Engineer
"Be part of the team that aims to continuously improve the operating processes of Grundfos Industrial BU based on LEAN principles.
Lean knowledge is not the most important thing, we let you learn it. It is much more important to have practical thinking, to be able to think in processes and to be able to coordinate the work of those working in a project.
If you like change and are even motivated by the fact that you can control it, then we look forward to hearing from you."- says Zoltán Filep Hiring Manager
What is the job about?
Apply subject matter knowledge and demonstrate technical/business insight. Assist in delivering professional process consultancy. Support and coordinate development and implementation of processes. Work within a unit and/or across functional areas with limited supervision.
Your main responsibilities:
Leading projects within own professional area within Industry division in Europe, USA or AsiaProvide input for developing own professional area.Participate in knowledge sharing across the organization.Provide consultancy to the business regarding different activities and initiatives. Apply broad and advanced knowledge to support the development and implementation of initiatives or projects.Offer expertise support to stakeholders.Evaluate, describe and recommend solutions to improve processes and systems.Deliver high quality research, analysis and recommendations.Plan the project activities, timing, resources, costs, and investments.Manage and motivate the project members and ensure good team spirit.Ensure project keeps the budget.Factory management support as Lean expert. ItV – Indirect to Value: find opportunities and implement location’s action list.Total Cost Down – support success daily cost management. Improve its tool and support strategic implementations.Digitalization – be part of digital journey and act as ambassador at your location. Super user role in case of new tools.Take other task that maybe assigned by the plant manager.
Your background:
We imagine that you have:  Master’s Degree, Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent work experience. Contribute with well-defined elements to developed processes within Continuous Improvement or other relevant areas. Ability to contribute to processes or projects. Ability to contribute business process within Continuous Improvement or other relevant areas. Advantage 0-3 years of relevant work experience before entering level besides formal education. Some exposure or theoretical understanding of Lean tools and techniques. English skill.Willingness to frequent travels
What’s in it for you?
Whether it’s developing leadership skills or advancing your expertise even further, we’ll support you with continuous learning and development opportunities, to help discuss and steer your long-term Grundfos career path. You’ll be welcomed from day one into an inclusive, trusting environment guided by six core values.
In addition, your day-to-day benefits include:
If you’ll be working from your home office, we’ll make sure you are well equipped with a good workstation. However, you are always welcome to our offices where you can engage, learn, and have fun with colleaguesOn top of it, flexible working hours and up to 3 days’ additional paid leave for volunteering of your choiceAnnual bonuses, health insurance, parental support, internal well-being consultants and programmesAccess to the modern Grundfos Academy to pursue further both personal and professional developmentDiverse, inclusive environment with employee-led forums offering colleagues a safe place to connect and share openlyDo you want to learn more?If this job sounds appealing, please send your resume and cover letter by clicking “Apply”.We look forward to hearing from you.
About Grundfos
Grundfos is one of the world’s leading water technology companies with more than 19.000 employees in 60+ countries all over the world. Our skills commit us to pioneering solutions to the world’s water and climate challenges and improve the quality of life for people. We dare to do things that others cannot or dare not do, as we believe innovation is not only a business opportunity, but an obligation. And what really matters to us is not short term profit, but the impact we make. By becoming part of our united powerful team, you too can drive this change no matter your role.
An inclusive team
If you like the look of this job and know you can bring your skills to contribute to our purpose, please apply. We need and welcome professional people from all corners – however you identify and whatever your background is.
