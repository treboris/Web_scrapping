Our Client is a global leader focused on the design, manufacture and distribution of agricultural machinery and infrastructure. As a Fortune 500 company and one of the global leaders in agricultural equipment manufacturing, we have an extensive network of over 9,200 dealers and serve more than 140 countries.
IT Business Analyst / Product Owner – Digital Farming Solutions
Palce of working: Budapest, 11. district – Full REMOTE
The IT Analyst – Digital Farming Solutions is an integral part of the Product Teams in the Agile Software Delivery Process. The DPE enriches the team in the areas of Requirements Engineering, Continuous Testing and Production Support. This position reports to the IT Platform Owner, CEP.
What You Will Do:
Requirements Engineering:Refinement of User Stories and FeaturesMethodical support in generating ideas with Product Owners and Subject Matter ExpertsDetermination, documentation, checking & coordination and management of requirementsDefinition of acceptance criteriaDefinition of the integration points with other systems and communication of the effects of possible system changes in cooperation with other teamsContribution to the definition of wireframes and visual designsSupport of the global development teams in clarifying requirementsVisual representation of business processesSupport in estimating efforts for new developments and improvementsKnowledge transfer for new and changed functions for the change management process Continuous Testing:Quality assurance of the implementation of technical and functional requirementsPlanning and execution of testsDefinition of test conditions and scriptsSupport in setting up test automationAnalysis of test results Production Support:Successful processing of customer and dealer inquiries for troubleshooting and changes as part of the company IT incident processError analysis and support of the development team in troubleshootingMonitoring of the system uptime General:Active participation and preparation for agile ceremonies such as Product Increment Planning,Sprint Planning, Refinements, Sprint Reviews and RetrospectivesResponsibility for compliance with all company guidelines by all employees and teams, as well as compliance with all control processes and standards.
What You Will Bring:
Bachelor`s / Master`s degree in computer science, IT, engineering or equivalent education2-3+ years of professional experience ( as a IT Business Analyst or IT Project Manager could be an advantage)Relevant experience in software developmentKnowledge of agile methods (e.g. SAFe) is an advantageExperience in requirements engineering, testing and production support is desirableExperience in app development (iOS, Android, Windows) advantageousExperience in smart farming and / or IoT is desirableBasic knowledge of agronomy and agricultural vehicles is an advantageGood communication skills in spoken and written English (German is an advantage)Independent and solution-oriented behaviorStrong analytical, holistic and conceptual thinkingExcellent communication skills and team-player attitude as well as the ability to interact with IT departments and any level of managementSuccessfully influencing different groups and teams in a complex environment.
We offer:
Work from home to protect your health, fully remoteWork tools (phone, lap-top) and full technical support,Multinational work environment with a solid background,In-career or out-of-position career development,Attractive salary, performance-related financial and moral recognition, 7 % yearly bonus and cafeteriaPositive atmosphere, high quality, supportive work environment.
