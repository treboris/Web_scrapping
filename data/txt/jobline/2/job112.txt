The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Head of Internal IT – Service HUB Owner (REF1425U)
You, as the Head of Internal IT (Service Hub Owner), will be responsible for leading the operation, development, innovation and supplier management of the internal IT systems of Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions Hungary. Responsibilities include the local network, server, workplace, collaboration and application landscape of a 5000+ company in four cities. You will closely cooperate with local business stakeholders, the global DT IT teams and also other Telekom entities in central Europe. You will be expected to help us being the number one employer for IT experts in Hungary.
We look for candidates who
have previous experience in leading IT organizations (either internal or customer facing)
understand agility, devops and modern engineering practices
enable their teams to make decisions and take responsibility
can strike the proper balance between local and central IT services,
execute central Deutsche Telekom IT strategy in the Hungarian location,
have a solid technology, process and financial understanding of a modern IT organization
are able to co-operate with the local, central and peer stakeholders
are good team players, disciplined and have the ability to get things done
Professional experience / expertise:
senior leader with an agile mindset, entrepreneurial thinking
has professional leadership experience with high complexity roles
business Management know-how
technology competence in Cloud / DevOps / Automation
intercultural expertise on cooperation in an international context
experience in driving / managing change & large transformational projects
advanced English language knowledge is a must
Advantage:
German language knowledge
