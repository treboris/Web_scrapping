Cloud Engineer with DB skillset (GCP knowledge) (REF1501J)
About us:
​As Cloud Migration Services (CMS) part of T-Systems, we are specializing at accompanying our customers on their Cloud adoption journey. Whether it’s a transition from different Cloud or it’s their first time moving from on premises to Cloud, we are there for them to safely guide them through all stages of their transformation because we understand what it takes to become a part of a digital future with innovative solutions.
Our mission: Shaping the safest, easiest and most efficient transformation to a digitized and cloud-native future together with our customer.
Your Tasks:
Perform various migrations between Clouds or from premises to Cloud based on customers’ requirements and needs, focusing on GCP
Create, define and improve processes and plans related to migration
Optimize all onboarding scenarios for customers on public cloud together with 3rd party products for migration
Work in highly dynamic, international environment and with customer representatives in the team, while serving as the technology expert
Collaborate with T-systems external and internal  partners 
Participate in the roadmap implementation and feedback to the product team
Directly communicate with end customers to discuss possibilities of migration, transformation and their optimal designs
Analysis of current setup and following that, definition of requirements
Oracle DB design/configuration on GCP (VM) incl. HA and DR setups
Oracle DB migration strategies and approaches - Oracle migration solution design on GCP (VM)
Migration support and troubleshooting additionally, but not must-have transformation Oracle DB to GCP PaaS services
Your Profile:
We are looking for an individual with strong background in public cloud technologies who is ready to help customers on their cloud journey to public cloud.
In addition, you ideally bring the following with you:
2+ years of relevant work experience with public cloud technologies (GCP), with some professional/advance certification
English Language (Fluent)
Understanding of migration concepts and migration tooling
Understanding of Infrastructure as Code and automation possibilities (Terraform, ARM templates, Ansible, SaltStack)
Some experience with Agile tooling and processes (Jira, Kanban, Scrum methodology)
Very high motivation and the ability to thrive in a dynamic environment
General ICT skills (Network, OS, Storage, Database etc.)
Basic Scripting & Coding knowledge (PowerShell, Bash, Python); Oracle"
