Technical Writer
Tasks
Independent conceptional and editorial work within the editing area Customer Service Documentation
Independently responsible for the creation, revision and overseeing of the technical documentation of relevant product families (such as operating instructions, repair and technical manuals etc.)
Supervision of internal and external service providers for the completion of documentation tasks
Research of technical data as well as the monitoring of relevant standards and legislation
Management and control of procedures and processes in collaboration with those specialist departments involved
Observation of new technologies and information/documentation developments in the technical communication environment, as well as participation in the implementation of the associated processes
Involvement in the quality assurance process for the technical documentation
Involvement in the safety warning team in order to ensure compliance to legal requirements
Our client is a global technology company supplying systems for passenger cars, commercial vehicles and industrial technology, enabling the next generation of mobility. With its comprehensive technology portfolio, the company offers integrated solutions for established vehicle manufacturers, mobility providers and start-up companies in the fields of transportation and mobility
Expectations
Degree as a technical editor, technical degree, or comparable qualification
A minimum of 4 years of working experience
Ability to state one’s own technical understanding in a comprehensible written form and to precisely convey complex technical interrelationships in textual and graphical form
Sound knowledge of MS-Office as well as experience with editing systems, knowledge of the PDM system Axalant preferable
Extensive knowledge of spoken and written English
High level of communication skills
High level of assertiveness
Ability and willingness to work independently and methodically
Willingness to travel
Offer
Stable company background and long-term working possibilities;
Challenging and high-level professional activities within an international company, participation in global projects;
Career opportunities, continuous training program and language courses;
Competitive salary and performance-related bonus;
Cafeteria with optional elements;
100% remote work during pandemic times;
School start support.
