Azure Cloud Migration Architect (REF1500R)
About us:
As Cloud Migration Services (CMS) part of T-Systems, we are specializing at accompanying our customers on their Cloud adoption journey. Whether it’s a transition from different Cloud or it’s their first time moving from on premises to Cloud, we are there for them to safely guide them through all stages of their transformation because we understand what it takes to become a part of a digital future with innovative solutions.
Our mission: Shaping the safest, easiest and most efficient transformation to a digitized and cloud-native future together with our customer.
 Your Tasks:
As the Cloud Architect for Migration you will be responsible for ensuring the Cloud adoption strategy by defining the cloud adoption roadmap aligned with the target platform solution design, business strategies and the existing landscape environment. You will be responsible for creating the optimal migration journey by leveraging TSI standards as well as cloud native tooling and processes. You will be also contributing to creation and improvement of the migration scenarios, set of best practices and leveraging Zero Touch approach wherever possible.  As Cloud Architect, it will be in your responsibility to manage the engagement with cloud service providers through the build and run phases to ensure that customer achieves maximum value from cloud initiatives and services
Implement complex customer requirements in technical solutions in accordance with best practices, cost-efficiency and future security based on Microsoft Azure
Develop both High-Level and Low-Level design for all possible variations of migration or transformation to hybrid cloud products (private/public) based on Azure
Optimize all onboarding scenarios for customers on private & public cloud together with 3rd party products for migration
Cooperate with the product owner on the continuous development of the T-systems cloud adoption portfolio
Collaborate with sales on preparation of complex customer inquiries and tenders
Be an advisor for local and international customers on optimal cloud adoption strategies on Azure
Collaborate directly with end customers, with T-systems partners and international teams
Your Profile:
We are looking for an individual with strong background in public cloud technologies who is ready to help customers on their migration journey to public cloud. In addition, you ideally bring the following with you:
5+ years of relevant work experience with Azure cloud technologies
A deep technical understanding of public cloud technologies and practical experience in public cloud adoption and migration
Experience with advocating and implementing Cloud Adoption Frameworks on Azure and other meaningful best practices
A can-do attitude which helps you to stand out in a fail fast culture
Passion to work in a global and diverse organization and environment
Excellent presentation and communication skills (spoken and written English)
Familiarity with application discovery tools and cloud migration software is an asset but not essential
Experience with Infrastructure as Code is an asset but not essential
