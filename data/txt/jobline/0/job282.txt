Life Solution Designer
About our Partner:
They are now looking to expand their Policy Admin Solution designer team with a LIFE Analyst to help them translate business needs into IT solutions. In this position, you will play a key role in bridging the gap between business analysts, IT developers and Product Owners, and will have the opportunity to participate in end-to-end development cycles from business requirements development to user testing.
Tasks:
Client-side needs assessment, coordination, solution proposals;
Definition, documentation, communication and evaluation of business needs and requirements;
Design, development and evaluation of business process;
Preparation and evaluation of functional specifications, process descriptions, technical specifications;
Identification of problems and requirements of users and customers, preparation of proposals for solutions;
Participation in verification, test support (both business requirements and non-functional requirements)
Requirements:
Minimum of secondary education in business/IT;
5-10 years working experience in business/IT;
Knowledge of finance, preferably insurance sector;
Analytical mindset, systems thinking;
Business analyst experience (specification writing, database design experience);
Relational database model knowledge, SQL user level;
Experience in development, program design, system administration;
Good communication, problem solving skills, proactivity, openness to continuous learning.
Advantages:
Proficiency in Agile methodology;
Knowledge of JIRA system;
Experience in a mainframe environment.
