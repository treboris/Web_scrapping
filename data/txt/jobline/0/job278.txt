Drive digital transformation aligned with our strategy.

Participates in global BI programs
Create and implement integrated data analytics

Seek and build tools/processes to support effectiveness

Liaise with stakeholders to understand business needs

Participate in process improvement and transformation projects

Microsoft Excel, SharePoint and PowerApps experiences
Knowledge and understanding of procurement data sources and processes
Strong presentation skills
Understands the role of BI in strategic planning
Able to connect with people and creates strong network of community

Spoken and written fluent communication in English
