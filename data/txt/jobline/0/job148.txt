Azure Cloud Consultant (REF1579Y)
About us:
As Cloud Migration Services (CMS) part of T-Systems, we are specializing at accompanying our customers on their Cloud adoption journey. Whether it’s a transition from different Cloud or it’s their first time moving from on premises to Cloud, we are there for them to safely guide them through all stages of their transformation because we understand what it takes to become a part of a digital future with innovative solutions.
Our mission: Shaping the safest, easiest and most efficient transformation to a digitized and cloud-native future together with our customer.
Your Tasks:
As Cloud Consultant for Cloud Adoption Services you will be responsible for defining cloud strategies, migration roadmaps and building cloud governance models. You will help our global customers to successfully adopt public cloud to support their strategic goals.
Help customers shape their journey of adopting Amazon Web Services, Azure or Google Cloud Platform and provide them with technical and strategic guidance to accelerate their cloud transformation.
Lead customer engagements from pre-sales to delivery and act as link between the customer, sales and technical project team.
Conduct customer workshops to understand technology and business drivers and present technical concepts.
 Work with our Product Managers to continuously improve our Cloud Adoption portfolio providing insights on customer projects and market trends.
 Advise on technology and strategic vision, define cloud adoption and migration strategies and hand-off to architect and migration teams.
Your Profile:
We are looking for someone with an entrepreneurial spirit who is keen to make a positive impact for our customers. You are passionate about supporting enterprises to adopt public cloud. In addition, you ideally bring the following with you:
A university degree in computer science or a related degree
5+ years of relevant work experience with public cloud technologies (AWS, Azure or GCP)
A deep technical understanding of public cloud technologies and practical experience in public cloud adoption and migration
Experience with advocating and implementing Cloud Adoption Frameworks on AWS, Azure or GCP and other meaningful best practices
Familiarity with application discovery tools and cloud migration software is asset but not essential
A can-do attitude which helps you to stand out in a fail fast culture
Very high motivation and the ability to thrive in a dynamic environment
Passion to work in a global and diverse organization and environment
Excellent presentation and communication skills (spoken and written English)
