Senior IT Developer - CRM
About Our Partner:  
They are a multinational company with a purpose to create financial security and stability for their clients and this is an exciting opportunity for talented professionals! Join The Company’s agile journey with continuous learning and empowerment as a Senior CRM Developer to help them shape their future together. 
Tasks: 
You are part of a tribe and a squad team that has an agile way of working
You have an IT specialization but at the same time you are all round enough to deliver end to end processes which are needed in an agile way of working,
You work together with your fellow colleagues in creating the IT landscape that is supporting the business strategy for the Tribe,
You drive the IT change from a landscape with older technologies towards market IT standards which will be a combination of using custom off-the- shelf products and custom build,
You know how to translate the complex business wishes towards epics/US that can be realized and maintained. 
Requirements:  
2-5 years of working experience,
Proven experience in MS Dynamics v8.x or 365 development experiences,
Knowledge of generic integration pattern knowledge (SOAP, REST, Etc.)
Experience in C# and JavaScript,
Report integration in MS Dynamics,
Proven familiarity with GIT, VSTS,
Hungarian and English in both speaking and writing.
