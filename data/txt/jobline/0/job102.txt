German speaking IT Security Advisor (REF1513T)
As an "IT Security Advisor" you need to understand and monitor the latest common vulnerabilities and exposures (CVE) in the field of IT Security in order to support the clients preventing eventual cyber attacks, security breaches and data breaches. If you are eager to analyze and assess newest cyber risks and are ready to be a trusted advisor of remediation measures, join our Cyber Threat Intelligence Team!
Main responsibilities will be:
Creating vulnerability reports as well as analysis and evaluation of the respective subject
Compiling professional messages for our newsletter subscribers as well as analysis and evaluation of the respective subject
Taking over daily responsibilities of continuous research of contents worthy of reporting, compilation of reports, quality assurance and dispatch
Regular updating of documentation (e.g. work instructions, manuals, customer documentation)
Processing of customer requests in different ticketing systems
Requirements
Completed studies in a technical, engineering or scientific subject
Minimum 2 years of professional experience in IT security
Fluency both in German AND English languages
General knowledge of Internet security, networking protocols, and related technologies
High level of customer focus
Analytical thinking
Exceptional communication skills in writing
Advantages
Any level of IT Security certificate(s) (CompTIA, EC-Council, CISA/CISM, CISSP, etc)
