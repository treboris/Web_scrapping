A Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (korábban IT Services Hungary, ITSH) Magyarország legnagyobb IKT-munkaadója, a Deutsche Telekom Csoport tagja. A 2006-ban alakult, több mint 5000 fős cég az informatikai és telekommunikációs szolgáltatások széles körét nyújtja. A vállalat 2019-ben elnyerte a Nemzeti Befektetési Ügynökség (HIPA) legjobb oktatási együttműködésért járó díját, a PwC Magyarország független felmérése alapján a legvonzóbb magyarországi munkahelyek közé tartozik, és a legetikusabb multinacionális vállalat címét is megkapta. A cég folyamatosan fejleszti négy – budapesti, debreceni, pécsi és szegedi – telephelyét, és várja képzett informatikai szakemberek jelentkezését a csapatába.
Java Architect - Software Architect - PLM area (REF399Q)
Did you know that car and airplane manufacturing systems collect data about literally everything what is in a factory and is related directly or indirectly to the delivered products? You can find all the mechanical details of the used screws, bearings, sheet material and even toilet paper.
In your role you would be part of an international software development team providing such Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) solutions for major European automotive and aeronautic manufacturers, who are thriving to produce more reliable and economical products.
As a Java/Software Architect you will 
Design new features based on customer’s demands
Manage feature development including task estimation, concept presentation and communication with the development team,
Get insight and develop different PLM related applications,
Utilize your development experiences to ensure that customer requirements of security, data Recoverability and integrity are met,
Have to find solutions for everyday software challenges – let it be technical or performance
Deliver application releases to test and production environments
As a member of our team, on this job you will build your expertise in the fields of
Java Solution Design with other Java Experts/Architects
Designing feature developments
Customer Consulting
Requirements Management
Working in an international development team
Review Deliverables and Code of the development team
Analyzing arising application issues
As a candidate you have to have
Significant experience as a Senior Java Developer or Java/Software Architect
Confident appearance and communication skills (customer friendliness)
Structured way of working
Analytical Skills
Knowledge of Agile project methods such as Scrum / Kanban
Very good English language skills
