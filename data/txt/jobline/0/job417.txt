Client Service Manager

Active participation in projects (together with project managers), taking over the non engineering related project communication with clients
Improve the "client experience" within our projects, with maintaining good response times, understand priorities, and provide regular feedback to project stakeholders
Active monitoring of project scope fulfillment, upon need facilitate scope and contract negotiations, manage change requests
Preparation of project controlling and reporting
Regurarly visiting our key clients, actively support for Business Development with delivering leads

3-5 years of Project Management experience in larger projects

Ability to work independently, precisely to high standards
Strong team spirit

Project experience in multinational environment
Experience in machine design engineering
German language skills
