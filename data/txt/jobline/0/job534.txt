Product Owner
About our Partner:
They are a multinational company with a purpose to create financial security and stability for their clients and they are looking for a talented professional to join their open-minded and innovative Technology team as a Security Architect.
Responsibilities:
Act as an ambassador of the new way of working and the new culture;
Translate the tribe strategy and vision to squad-level in collaboration with the Tribe Lead, Product manager and define squad level business KPIs;
In collaboration with the squad and other stakeholders create and maintain short-, mid- and long-term plans;
Define the sprint goal, set the target for each iteration and responsible to define competency requirements to ensure delivery;
Fully responsible for the content of the product backlog, collect and prioritize backlog items which define priorities of daily tasks of squad members;
Understand, clarify customer needs and define, create user stories;
Maintain strong collaboration with other Tribe Leads, Board Members, Product Managers, Product Owners, Chapter Leads and "Run" organization, manage stakeholders' expectations;
Ensure that requirements are in alignment with compliance requirements.
Requirements:
University/College degree;
5+ years' leadership experience;
Fluent English is a must;
Knowledge of agile methodology (Scrum, Kanban)
Advantages:
Work experience as a Business Analyst
