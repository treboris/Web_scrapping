Who is our partner?
Our partner's mission is to provide a tool that makes urban commuting more convenient and contributes to building a sustainable future. Their main product is a mobile application for parking and electric vehicle charging. Furthermore they work closely with big corporations, providing software and hardware related to parking and charging. 
Right now they are looking for their new Software Test Engineer colleague.
Software Test Engineer
Feladatok
The following challenges await the Software Test Engineer:
Designing test scripts and test cases based on given specifications
Conducting functional and user tests
Error analysis, defect tracking and documentation
Taking part in the development and implementaton of new testing methods and principles
Working closely with the software engineer and sales teams
Követelmények
The requirements you should fulfill:
Completed technical education in the field of IT or engineering
At least 3-5 years of experience in software testing
Familiarity with SQL
Reliable English knowledge
Soft skills we are looking for:
Analytical thinking
Proactivity, good communication skills
Ability to work well as part of a team
It is a PLUS if you have:
ISTQB
Some programming knowledge (preferably in Java or JavaScript)
Familiarity with some testing tools and methodologies (Postman / SoapUI /Selenium / Appium / JMeter)
Experience with an issue tracking system (Jira / Redmine / Testlink / Spira)
User level familiarity with Linux
