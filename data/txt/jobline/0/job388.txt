Software Developer (C++)
Our partner is a global supplier of intelligent automation software. The company's software allows businesses to automate and improve business workflows by simplifying the handling of data and documents.
Scope of the job:
Senior Software Developer (specialized for C++ programming language) participates in the architecture, design, documentation, development, coding, and debugging of Windows desktop software solutions in either new software products or enhancements to existing software, under the general direction of his or her direct supervisor (Team Lead).
Your main responsibilities are:
Conducting research, where appropriate and needed, to solve specific design problems
Work independently, or in a group under the direction of a team lead, creating modules, design documentation, subsystems or applications in C++ and C#, but occasionally also using other programming languages or development tools
Work on Windows operating system with various third-party SDKs
Adhere to company’s secure software development process
Positive attitude and ability to work in a fast-paced environment
Support production users
Document his or her work in English
Participate regular team meetings and/or conference calls
Effective communication and relationships with other developers and the QA team
You are the ideal candidate if you have:
3-5 years of professional experience as a software developer
Solid knowledge of the C++ language is necessary
Solid knowledge of the C# language is preferred
Demonstrated OOD experience
Solid knowledge of Windows operating system is necessary
Working knowledge of bug tracking and version control systems (TFS preferred)
Ability to work both independently and in a team environment
Strong troubleshooting and debugging skills
Experience with PDF technology
Experience with Microsoft technologies (SharePoint, Microsoft 365, Office applications)
Experience with Document Management Systems
Experience with virtualisation technologies (VMWare, Citrix, Microsoft App-V)
Knowledge of COM is preferred
What our client offers:
Good team spirit in a small multinational environment
Competitive salary package
Modern office environment in the 13th district, near to M3 metro station
Regular home office opportunity

If you're interested in this role, click 'apply now' and attach your English resume to us.
