Payment Transactions Professional - German OR Italian Speaking
On behalf of our multinational client, we are looking for a Payment Transactions Professional with German OR Italian language skills. If you would like to take the next step in your career, read further and learn how!
Responsibilities:
Apply cash remittances and credits/adjustments
Send cash application statue
Validation of interfaces
Schedule and verify payment run
Maintain payment blocks
Send payment register/plan for review and approval
Disburse payment
Reconciliation & Reporting
Posting of interest and bank charges
Prepare statistics and reports
Cashflow forecast
Process improvement
Requirements:
University degree in Economics/Finance or relevant experience
1+ years of experience in accounting (primarily PT/AP)
Good understanding of the overall PT processes
Good English and German OR Italian language skills (written and spoken)
SAP knowledge
What our client offers:
Experience in an SSC-start-up environment
Flexible working conditions
Attractive remuneration package
Career opportunities

Budapest
