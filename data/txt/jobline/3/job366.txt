Our client is a leading European airline company and we are looking for a new member to their functional application support team. Within the team they fly 110 aircraft to 145 destinations, bringing millions of passengers to where they need to be, developing sustainable travel and pushing the boundaries of air travel towards to the future.
Application Support Specialist
Tasks
Functionally manages the IT applications, batches and macros maintained locally
Responsible for the accurate and timely creation of internal – and external reporting
Liaises with central IT departments regarding applications maintained centrally
Responsible for access management
Handles IT Application incidents
Responsible for timely, complete and accurate integration of interfaces outbound from local applications into the relevant accounting systems
Requirements
Ongoing or finished College/University degree with a focus on ICT (Information Communication Technologies)
1- 2 years of experience of application management and reporting
Strong knowledge of Windows and Microsoft Applications and database handling
Intermediate English in writing and verbally
Familiar with ITIL and managing tickets
Motivation to learn with Agile/SCRUM process
