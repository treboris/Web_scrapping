Knowledge content and EDM Lead
Tasks:
You will ensure GDPR and other country specific data privacy regulations are considered in the design of ServiceNow, including retention and purge requirements.
You will be responsible for ensuring a great employee experience is achieved in the existing portal and content is readily available, easy to search and updated timely. 
You will ensure all relevant procedural/ legislative requirements are fulfilled.
You will be responsible for routinely updating the knowledge management database to reflect current information, ensuring consistent practices and information for all employees and managers. 
You will manage day to day operations and configurations required for the ServiceNow knowledge base and electronic document management (EDM) tools. 
You will serve as the key point of contact for matters related to the HR SSC knowledge management portal and EDM.
Our multinational partner in the manufacturing industry is looking for a Knowledge content and EDM Lead to join their dream to develop new solutions to enhance the lives of millions of people. 
If you would like to contribute to the happiness of people around the world and you meet the below mentioned requirements, then apply to not miss the opportunity to be a part of a special and supporting multinational team.
Expectations:
Fluent English language skills
4 years of experience working in HR
Strong understanding of HR processes
Prior experience working with Knowledge Portals and document management systems, ServiceNow and Workday strongly preferred
At least 2 years of direct experience working with knowledge management portals, configurations and operations in a large global corporate environment supporting multiple languages and time zones
Strong knowledge of GDPR
Strong project management skills
Ability to training team members effectively
