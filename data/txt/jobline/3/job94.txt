The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
IT Security Manager (Corporate Security) (REF822R)
As member of the team of Deutsche Telekom Security you will play a major role in the design, implementation and maintenance of coherent sets of policies, processes and systems to handle the security, risks and controls to ensure an appropriate level of information security and compliance for business/enterprise customers consuming IT services from our company.
Your tasks:
Support the development of our security architecture
Assist and advise security organizations in production, service delivery and customer care
Assist the implementation of security management processes
Support and coordinate information security related projects
Help elaborating material and practices for internal purposes and for customers as well
Know-how and practical experiences in IT Security
Basic expertise in IT Service Management practices and processes (e.g. ITIL)
Several years of relevant professional experience
Skill in the use of MS Office applications (incl. Sharepoint)
English spoken and written (C1+)
Experienced
Advantages
German language skills (B1+)
IT security related education and/or certifications (CISSP, CompTIA, CRISC, etc.)
Organizational skills and expertise IT and modern IT services
* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
