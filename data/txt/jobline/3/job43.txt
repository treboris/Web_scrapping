Senior Java Developer for Cloud of Things (REF521E)
Join our dynamic team at Deutsche Telekom TSI Hungary Kft. as a Java Developer for Cloud of Things (REF521E) and work remotely from anywhere in Hungary!
As the largest ICT employer in Hungary, we provide a wide range of IT and telecommunications services, and we're constantly growing and expanding our team. We were even awarded the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019!
In this role, you'll be working on Cloud of Things, our high-performance, cloud-based IoT application platform developed by Deutsche Telekom that helps connect devices and machines remotely. You'll have the opportunity to work on developing and maintaining backend microservices, refactoring and optimizing code, and creating high-level solution designs.
Since we are looking for senior colleagues to join, to be successful in this position, you should have strong knowledge of Java stack technologies, Spring Boot, strong knowledge of various database programs, especially MongoDB, maybe have basic knowledg of MQTT messaging protocols, and experience using Kubernetes and Docker. Additionally, it would be beneficial to have experience with VertX.io, Kotlin, HiveMQ Broker, Ansible.
Of course, we are happy to support you in learning these additional technologies as well in case you are interested.
Don't miss this chance to work on innovative projects with a highly respected company in the IT industry. Apply now and become part of our team of over 4500 talented professionals! Please note that our remote working option is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulations.
