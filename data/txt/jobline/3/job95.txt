Our partner company is a leading Swiss software engineering firm specializing in the design, implementation and integration of large-scale business and security software solutions. Renowned Swiss and German customers from various industries and the public sector rely on their services and products. Their development center in Budapest is continuously growing so they are looking for new experts for our ongoing and brand new projects.
Software Engineer
Feladatok
In your daily work, you will:develop effective solutions for complex business problems in various industriesintegrate new components and technologies into existing software systems and architectureswork on all layers of the software stack (web and mobile UI, business layer, database)participate in important project milestones; like estimation, releases, elaboration, etc.maintain and improve code quality (especially through code reviews)give and receive constructive feedback
Követelmények
For this challenging position, you ideally have:confident knowledge of object oriented programming (Java is a plus) and/orconfident knowledge of front-end (Javascript, Angular, React) or mobile developmentgood communication skills in English (spoken and written)
