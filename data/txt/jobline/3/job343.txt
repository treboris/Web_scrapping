German-speaking Service Desk Agent
Would you like to work at a leading multinational company? Do you speak English and German fluently and would like to build your career? If yes, do not hesitate to apply!
Responsibilities:
Answer incoming calls to the Service Desk
Provide first line support for users requiring assistance 
Provide basic administration of Access Management, End User Management, accounts and passwords
Escalate incidents to the appropriate IT operations resources in accordance with established procedure
Monitor open tickets for accuracy, urgency and problem resolution 
Ensure continuous availability during shift in the telephony system 
Identify of any areas of improvement
Requirements:
Fluent English and German (written and oral)
Strong knowledge of Microsoft based operating systems 
Excellent communication and customer service skills
Problem solving skills
What our client offers:
Cafeteria and Private Health Care Insurance
Flexible working arrangements and home office possibilities
Structured onboarding support
Diverse career paths 
Development opportunities (language courses, online learning courses)
Company and team events and possibility to join social groups
Advantages:
Experience in IT / in Service desk
