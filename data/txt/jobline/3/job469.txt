Finance Business Partner
Would you like to work at the second biggest company of the CEE region? Our client's controlling team provides professional support in finance related questions to the business in 10 countries. We are looking for a Finance Business Partner, who is motivated to coordinate the financial planning and forecasting on the Group level for our partner.
Responsibilities:
Reviewing business performance
Being responsible for CAPEX, OPEX analysis
Developing the controlling reports
Presenting the results to the management
Comparing and evaluating actual with forecast numbers
Requirements:
At least 5 years’ professional experience in Financial Controlling
College or University degree in Finance/Economics/Business or equivalent
Fluent in English
Excellent financial system and Excel skills
What our client offers:
Career opportunities 
Multinational and professional team
Opportunity to realize your process improvement ideas
Training possibilities
Workplace:
Budapest
