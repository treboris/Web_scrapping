IFRS Senior Analyst
On behalf of our well-known partner, we are looking for an IFRS Senior Analyst!
Responsibilities:
Be responsible for compilation and the control of annual and quarterly stock exchange reports, and financial statements of the Group, manage and control the reporting process;
Supervise all financial and accounting processes of specific group contracts;
Cash flow analyses on a quarterly basis;
Support audit works;
Act as the first point of contact and ensure the information flow between inner and external functional organizations;
Keep updated the accounting and consolidation policy in line with IFRS such as harmonizing reporting processes and policies used within the group members and parent company;
Take part in project work in the professional area in terms of financial and accounting part 
Requirements:
Deep understanding of IFRS and openness to developing it
University, college degree with a specialization in finance
Professional experience: min 2 years (preferably at Big4 or public company)
Fluent English
What our client offers:
Competitive salary, cafeteria, private health insurance
Hybrid working structure, flexible working hours
