Software Test Engineer
The following challenges await our new Software Test Engineer:
Execution of tests using HIL (Hardware-In-the-Loop) simulators in the field of conventional and hybrid truck transmissions
Manual and automated evaluation of measurements (using Vector tools such as CANape, CANalyzer; FAMOS)
Development and execution of automated tests
Documentation and presentation of test results
Improvement of tests and testing strategies
Collaboration in an international team
Our partner is a global technology company supplying systems for passenger cars, commercial vehicles and industrial technology, enabling the next generation of mobility. With its comprehensive technology portfolio, the company offers integrated solutions for established vehicle manufacturers, mobility providers and start-up companies in the fields of transportation and mobility.
The requirements you should fulfill:
University/College degree in Vehicle Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechatronics, Mechanical Engineering, or Computer Science
Good command of English and/or German, both written and spoken
Basic knowledge/understanding of coding, scripting, programming (Python, C, C#, C++ or other)
It's an advantage if you also have:
Experience in automotive function or system development
User-level knowledge of Matlab / Simulink, Vector tools (CANalyzer, CANape development tools)
Category C driving license
What our partner offers:
Participation in international projects and missions abroad
Training programs and language courses opportunities
Excellent workplace atmosphere and attractive working environment
Competitive salary, performance-related bonus and cafeteria
Different sports options
School start support
