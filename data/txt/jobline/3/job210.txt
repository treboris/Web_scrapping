Our international Australian partner is focused on the future of mobility on the field of autonomous and electric vehicles. They are looking for an experienced Senior Software Developer to join to their growing team. 
Senior Software Engineer
Tasks
Being an experienced senior member in a software team developing safety-critical and mobility-focused embedded software
Design new algorithms and develop adaptions to existing algorithms for vehicle motion control, user interfaces, actuator and energy management with the software development team
Manage implementation of systems tests, debugging and diagnostics and analyse resulting data
Plan, design, and optimize automated test cases and CI/CD pipelines with the software test team
Participate in design and code reviews within our software development lifecycle
Generate and analyze requirements and specifications from stakeholders
Collaborate with other software engineers, electrical, mechanical, systems, and product teams to resolve issues
Requirements
An Engineering or Computer Science degree, or equivalent experience
A passionate interest in electric transport and an interest in developing new generations of electric vehicles
Proven experience in building and releasing production ECU or controller software packages in relevant engineering projects
Proficiency in embedded software development in C/C++, model-based development in Simulink
Proficiency with automotive or industrial communications such as CAN and Ethernet
Experience with the v-model automotive software development lifecycle
Experience with software functional safety implementations and optionally, ISO26262 certifications
Strong understanding of vehicle dynamics, control theory and sensors
Enjoy working in a fast-paced and growing global team environment
Ability to work independently with a strong team ethos
Capable of mentoring junior engineers in the software engineering domain
Minimum of 5 years of experience in software development in industry
Proficient in Hungarian and English
It's an advantage if you also have:
General knowledge of embedded electronics development and the ability to read schematic diagrams
Understanding of the standards of functional safety ISO26262, IEC61508 and/or ASIL
Technical experience related to electronic systems at a component, sub-system and system level
An ability to analyse test results, highlight abnormalities and present outcomes to an audience with a range of technical expertise
Cross functional knowledge across the disciplines of electronics, mechatronics, and software
Experienced with Microsoft Office suite
Strong interpersonal and communication skills
Offers
Work at an exciting technology company within Hungary delivering globally in a multi-national group
Enjoy a clear sense of ownership in your field, with the satisfaction of working on developments that create the future of a new category of autonomous vehicles
Work alongside a friendly and passionate team on cutting edge technology
Have the opportunity to travel to other company offices including Australia and the UK
Enjoy modern office space and great work environment
