Robotic Process Automation Specialist
Our partner is planning to kickstart a huge cross-country automation project. Here is your chance to join before the start, define your processes and projects, and choose the technologies you wish to work with.
Responsibilities:
Analyze requirements and develop relevant use cases for automation and RPA opportunities
Execute proof-of-concepts, prototypes, minimally viable products (MVPs) and deployment and sustain solutions in production
Develop test plans and test the new modules 
Design & document technical specifications for RPA implementation projects
Work with local and regional teams 
Participate in design sessions and workshops to gather requirements for automation & RPA opportunities
Requirements:
Strong understanding of basic automation (eg VBA) technologies
2-4+ years of demonstrated RPA development
Exposure to Microsoft Power Platforms (Power Apps, Power Automate, and Power BI) and Sharepoint Online
