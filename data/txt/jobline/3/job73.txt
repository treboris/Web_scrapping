Present in the market under the Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions brand name, IT-Services Hungary (ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group and is the largest ICT employer in Hungary. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Experienced Business Analyst – ServiceNow (REF1622G)
Role focus:
Contact person to agree business requirements, project support/coordination and technical consulting in customer facing and internal projects for the technical implementation in the ServiceNow platform
Creating technical design documents and presentation for the ServiceNow implementation according to the process and technical landscape 
Coordination of parallel running activities for implementation and configuration of the ServiceNow platform
Ensure the implementation of customer requirements
Support and consulting related to ITSM processes and ServiceNow platform
Tasks:
ServiceNow technical customer support for onboarding projects:quality check of the initial data provided by Project
Managers, Service Delivery Managers, and approve the starting of the onboarding workflow
involvement in process definitions for incident or order management (as ITSM support)
monitoring the workflow in ServiceNow, remove obstacles, initiate and coordinate issue solving if needed
coordination of operation teams during executing the requested configuration
participate in testing activities during  SIT (internal test) and UAT (customer test) phases for onboarded customers
execute simple operational tasks
checking and correcting system settings
support by defect analysis, tracking
BI reporting project coordination
analyzing customer expectations in the field of reporting eg. SAP Business Objects, PowerBI
project management and coordination in reporting sub-stream of customer onboardings
work closely and effectively with technical teams and requestors to ensure the requirements are met
Skills:
Master’s degree (or similar) in the areas of IT  with Servicenow / Agile / ITIL certification
Professional experience in the ServiceNow and its components
Experience in analyzing and assessing business requirements, creating business concepts Very good knowledge and experience in Project Management (waterfall and agile)
Great communication skills and experience with internal and external customer communication
Good understanding and years of practice in the field of ITSM processes
Fluent German, intermediate English language capabilities
Experienced
* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
