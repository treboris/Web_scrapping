Continuous Improvement & Internal Controls SME
Our partner is a leading player in the industry with over 1 000 locations around the world. If you would like to join a diverse, multinational company, send your application today! Feel free to apply even if you live further away from Budapest, since in that case you can work FULLY REMOTELY!
Responsibilities:
Work with the Continous Improvement and SCE Management on designing the CI and ICS strategies and support the execution of them
Participate in and lead control issue resolution
Communicating about technical issues to non-technical experts
Monitor, analyse and suggest changes if necessary
Prepare specification of KPI and other reports
Ensure that processes are operating at the required level
Manage process design improvements
Stakeholder management
Reporting
Propose automation, and optimization opportunities to Finance Team Leads and the Management, evaluate new technologies
Requirements:
College/university degree
5 years of relevant experience
Experience in end-to-end processes and process design
Accounting experience in AR/AP
Basic P&L and BS knowledge
MS Office knowledge (especially Excel and PowerPoint)
Project management skillset, proactive attitude
ERP system knowledge (e.g. SAP, Navision etc.)
What our client offers:
3-days home office opportunity with home office contribution
If you live more than 50 km away from Budapest, you can work FULLY REMOTELY
Flexible working hours
Medical insurance

Budapest
