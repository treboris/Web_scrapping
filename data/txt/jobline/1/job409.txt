Devops Engineer
General purpose of the job
DevOps engineers build, test and maintain the infrastructure and tools to allow for the speedy development and release of software. DevOps practices aim to simplify the development process of software.
Job description Duties & Responsibilities:
CI solution support and development;
Creation of pipeline projects in Jenkins and Gitlab to automate autotest launches, deploy environments for testing;
Infrastructure administration in AWS and Oracle Cloud;
Working with tools for collecting diagnostic information;
Monitoring support and development (Zabbix, Graylog, ELK stack);
Creation of scripts for processing the results of autotests.
Knowledge, skill and abilities
Good problem-solving skills
Customer-oriented thinking

Worload

Requirements
Knowledge of script programming languages;
Experience cloud services AWS and Oracle;
Experience with Ansible;
Experience with Weblogic - tuning, operation, troubleshooting;
Experience with LINUX, GIT,
Basic knowledge of working with databases;
Knowledge of CI / CD processes and development methodologies, understanding how these processes are automated;
Experience in assembling projects in Java and working with Spring (will be a plus).
