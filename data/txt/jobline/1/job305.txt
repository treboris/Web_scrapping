Our fast-growing client is searching for SAP consultants with deep experties to their newly set-up IT team.
SAP ABAP Developer - Specialist
Feladatok
Tasks in new position:
Be an expert in ABAP, doing development tasks, and reading ABAP codes
Programming in every area, not focusing on just one SAP module
Work with ABAP objects, doing reports, programming applications with Fiori
Doing system development tasks
Fulfilling user accepts, working with interfaces
Responsibilities:
Detailing the solution design in close cooperation with our solution architects
Development of ABAP Add-On’s, User Exits and interfaces in our SAP environment (ECC6.0, S/4, EWM,HR)
 Interface development via ODATA and REST API on SAP Gateway
Implementation of SAP notes
Development or enhancement of FIORI apps
Solution documentation and testig
Követelmények
Requirements:
Good knowledge in all areas of the SAP ABAP development workbench and in ABAP-OO
Experience in FIORI, ODATA, CDS views will be an advantage
Knowledge in the CHARM process and test management process on the SAP Solution Manager is a plus
Knowledge in the development in an HR environment will be beneficial, but is not a must
At least 3 years of development experience
Good English skills (spoken and written)
Experienced working in an international, virtual team
