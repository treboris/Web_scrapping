Report Automation Expert
Report Automation Expert position is open at our prestigious, worldwide-known multinational partner. The company provides long term career opportunity with a competitive salary in a professional working environment.
As a Report Automation Expert your tasks will be to:
Prepare, create and develop central report’s standardised formats,
Develop automated report set used by all sales and marketing teams,
Provide high data quality and validity in reports
Creating documentations, manuals from all the processes
Minimize the unnecessary manual reoccurring workload through automation
 What you will need to fill the position:
5+ years of experience in a similar field
Advanced English language knowledge
Strong VBA, Power BI skills
Medium level knowledge with MS SQL, TSQL
High level of business understanding
Experience with managing end users
Analytical attitude
Ability to see in system
