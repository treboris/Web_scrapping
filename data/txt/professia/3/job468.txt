Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesDear reader of this job advertisement, we are looking for a Senior SAP Developer colleague, if this is the kind of job You seek, keep reading. :)General Information about the working environment.• Our success is based on consistent performance orientation and the motivation and competence of our employees.• Our corporate with system integrators, enterprise archi-tects, and project leads and bridge the development team to guide, develop, and maintain a world-class application in ECC/S/4HANA.• This role involves hands-on pro-gramming and troubleshooting highly complex scenarios across various modules in ECC/S/4HANA.If you are passionate about this role and if you are eager to tackle difficult challenges and make an impact in an international company undergoing a massive digital transformation, you have to look no further.We also offer the possibility to WORK REMOTELY (within European countries) or to use our modern and inspirational workplace.SAP Modules that we use frequently :• SD, PP, MM, FI/COYour challenges will be :• You (as part of the SAP Development team) will be working closely with the product teams, Project leads, and Enterprise Architects.• You will demonstrate technical leadership and recommend innovative team solutions to the team.• Contribution toward the  the product development, i.e. hands-on programming (ABAP) as part of a project implemnetation.• Analyzing of risks and an impact of proposed solutions, including security and performance aspects. Employee perks, benefits• Remote job is the "new normal" but if You feel the need to see your colleagues on site from time to time, You are very welcome (in our office in Bratislava, new office in Kosice or in the Czech Republic offices).• Our company is 30 years old but we do our best to always get the new education, make development and to work with new technologies.• We have a really great leaders in Czech Republic / Slovakia, who actually listen to what we are saying :).• Other benefits will be presented / discussed (some of them are more negotiable here in Slovakia since we are still "new" here.
