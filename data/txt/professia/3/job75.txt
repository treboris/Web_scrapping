POSITIONDESCRIPTIONAccenture Advanced Technology Center is looking for Data Engineers who are ready for a challenging IT career.You have the opportunity to grow as Data Engineer under Accenture’s wing and help our clients develop applications and generate the next wave of innovative solutions for the most decisive players of different industries. WHAT WILLYOU DOAs an Data Engineer, you are helping our projects with realizing their digital strategies. Collaborate with business stakeholders to identify and meet data requirements Designing, implementing, and testing data solutions using Azure Data services Provisioning data storage solutions, ingesting streaming and batch data, transforming data, and implementing security requirements and data retention policies. Supporting Data Architects with technical questions and decisions Being a Subject Matter Expert for specific areas and support others in these areas Building BI solutions using existing cloud provided services WHAT WEEXPECT•	Strong knowledge of Cloud services: AWS, Azure.•	Familiar with the concepts of relational and non-relational data, and different types of data workloads such as transactional or analytical.•	Experience with technologies in the Cloud Environment (like S3, Databricks, RDS or Blob , Azure SQL database)•	Hands-on experience with the following: Informatica PowerCenter, Informatica BDM or ICS•	Ability to write scripts in Python (using Pyspark, pandas …), SCALA is an advantage•	Ability to adapt to various data systems•	Experience with delivering projects within an agile environment WHAT WEOFFERSalaryFrom 2 800 EUR/monthAccenture offers a competitive compensation package.
