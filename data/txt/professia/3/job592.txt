Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesPurpose
Senior technical expert responsible for design and implementation of an assigned technology.
As Cloud Migration Services (CMS) part of T-Systems, we are specializing at accompanying our customers on their Cloud adoption journey.
- Whether it’s a transition from different Cloud or it’s their first time moving from on premises to Cloud, we are there for them to safely guide them through all stages of their transformation because we understand what it takes to become a part of a digital future with innovative solutions
- Our mission: Shaping the safest, easiest and most efficient transformation to a digitized and cloud-native future together with our customer.
Key Accountabilities: 
- Implement complex customer requirements in technical solutions in accordance with best practices, cost-efficiency and future security based on Microsoft Azure)
Develop both High-Level and Low-Level design for all possible variations of migration or transformation to hybrid cloud products (private/public) based on Azure
Optimize all onboarding scenarios for customers on private & public cloud together with 3rd party products for migration
Cooperate with the product owner on the continuous development of the T-systems cloud adoption portfolio
Collaborate with sales on preparation of complex customer inquiries and tenders
Be an advisor for local and international customers on optimal cloud adoption strategies on Azure
Work in highly dynamic, international environment with committed team while serving as the technology expert
Collaborate with T-systems partners such as Microsoft
Directly communicate with end customers to discuss possibilities of migration, transformation and their optimal designs Wage (gross)From 2 500 EUR/monthFinal salary is negotiable.
