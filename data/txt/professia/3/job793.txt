×Ľutujeme, spoločnosť ponúkajúca danú pracovnú pozíciu ukončila zverejnenie ponuky na stránke.Ďakujeme za porozumenie.Zobraziť podobné ponukySummer Development Academy Zilina - full time internship for students during summer
Place of work
Žilina Region, Slovakia, Žilina region (Job with occasional home office)
Contract type
part-time
Salary
From 6 EUR/hour
Job description, responsibilities and dutiesSummer Development Academy is a unique and quick way how to learn and get trained on the job of a software developer. A Scrum team of students is working on a project from the medtech industry, learns how to work in a team, learns how to develop an application with back-end, front-end and how to deploy it to the cloud. Usually we're using mostly .NET and Angular and the MS Azure.After this two month full-time internship, you might continue as a working student on some of our projects, where you will continue to grow and gain experience.The internship will be in whole July and August, 40 hours a week. First week we usually do the introduction to technologies and therefore it is needed to be present starting first week of July.https://www.siemens-healthineers.com/sk/development-center/summer-dev-academy/ba-zaOther requirements:•	you have ideally finished the second year of IT focused university•	you have a basic understanding of the object-oriented programming principles•	you have some knowledge and ideally also experience with oop back-end technologies, .NET, Java, etc.•	you ideally know what unit tests are•	you have some experience with databases, ideally SQL•	you ideally have an experience / knowledge of some front-end technologies, you know what Typescript is•	you can communicate and collaborate in English•	you're looking for learning, getting experience and for contributing to some productWhat we offer:•	6€ gross / hour + compensation for meal tickets a month•	learning technologies you'll see on our projects•	chance to continue cooperation with us during the year or to do bachelor/diploma thesis with us•	introduction to modern technology stack, code quality standards, real job experience, support from senior colleagues
            Required education, skills and personality requirements
