Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWhat will be your responsibilities?· As Build Systems Administrator, you are member of a Scrum team and stay in close contact with your team colleagues, located in 3 locations (Herisau, Bratislava, Oviedo) on a daily manner.· You are responsible for the CI/CD systems, pipelines, and configuration· You are responsible for long-time stability of the said system· You will perform responsive actions on broken builds, help users understand and leverage system features· You clarify requirements with the responsible Product Owner, develop concepts and solutions together with your team and Metrohm’s IT.· You support your team and the software engineers in their technical development.· You coordinate and implement updates and maintenance of the server infrastructure with the corresponding teams Employee perks, benefits•  Interesting projects based on the newest technologies•  Opportunity for self-improvement and career growth•  Internal & external software development education and trainings (certifications)•  Metrohm Employee Exchange Programs•  Several introduction trainings (partially in Switzerland)•  International environment, team buildings•  Flexible working time + overtimes compensation, home office•  25 vacation days/year, sickness compensation•  Personal Budget & Green Budget•  Language courses (en, de) fully paid by company•  Referral & relocation bonus•  Parking spaces directly in the building•  Lunch Vouchers covered by Metrohm, free fruit, coffee in office, Air Hockey :-) Information about the selection processIf you do not have updated CV, just send us your contact information or link to LinkedIn profile to [email protected] and we will gladly speak with you.
