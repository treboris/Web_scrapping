Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesAbout the Role
This role is a key innovative position within the New Propositions & Digital Partnerships team within Group Digital & Technology. The team plays a key role in helping to foster a culture of innovation, automation and digitisation for Swiss Re.New Propositions & Digital Partnerships is a multi-disciplined team with capabilities to embed innovation culture within SwissRe and has a focus on creating and building digital capabilities to explore new opportunities within the insurance market. As part of SwissRe, New Propositions & Digital Partnerships collaborates with various other areas of the organisation to support them in achieving their commercial goals by delivering innovative and effective digital capability for their clients and partners.
We are looking for a Lead for the New Propositions & Digital Partnerships Prototype Lab with a strong development/engineering background and leadership abilities to enable and support the Lab team to output best in class prototyping. If you are passionate about technology, engineering, innovation and leadership, then read on! : 
Key Responsibilities:
- To design and develop high quality reusable code as well as innovative prototypes 
- To create robust, changeable and scalable technology applications – from ideation, to prototyping to production delivery, across a mix of green-field and existing modern applications
- To define the next evolution of the lab considering internal and external market context 
- To work alongside and within existing engineering teams in various initiatives running across the digital transformation unit
- To support define and implement technology best practice across SwissRe while being a role model of technical and behavioural excellence 
- To help build and shape an increased engineering and delivery team culture, responsible for designing and implementing products across our technology platform with a quick iterative approach and focusing on ability to scale features and business processes
- To be aware of and able to innovatively respond to the differing compliance, risk and regulatory control
- To promote engineering excellence, focusing on effective cross-function collaboration and end-to-end responsibility, from rapid prototyping/proof of concepts to delivery, ensuring simplicity, maintainability and scalability of the implementations Employee perks, benefitsYou can look forward to extra rewards and benefits including:
