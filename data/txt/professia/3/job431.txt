Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesAre you looking for new technological challenges, interesting projects, inspirational colleagues and an informal working environment?
We would like to strengthen our team and we are looking for new colleague who will be responsible for developing and maintaining high-quality game graphics using the PixiJS framework ensuring optimal performance and user experience across multiple platforms and devices, collaborating with cross-functional teams, developing and maintaining game-related tools and plugins, conducting in-depth analysis and troubleshooting, identifying and implementing solutions.
