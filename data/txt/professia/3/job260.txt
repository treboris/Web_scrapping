Software Architect for Point of Care Diagnostics
Place of work
Bratislava, Slovakia (Job with occasional home office)
Contract type
full-time
Salary
3 200 EUR/monthFrom 3200€  + VAR**We are required by law to disclose the basic wage component (minimum salary) for the advertised positions. We carefully consider your professional qualifications and experience in our compensation package and/or when offering you other positions.
Our goal is to pay our employees fairly, about the market situation and we are ready to welcome high-quality candidates to our team.
Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are looking for a Software Architect to join our team that develops control software for a new generation of Point-of-Care diagnostic devices (refer here for the existing Point-of-Care product lines: https://www.siemens-healthineers.com/point-of-care-testingWe use agile methods of software development and apply best engineering practices to create a high-quality product that helps to save lives around the globe. We deal with complex challenges in an interdisciplinary environment with a global outreach. If we need help or coaching, we can always reach out to a solid network of experts within our organization.More about our projects can be found at https://www.siemens-healthineers.com/point-of-care-testingAs a Software Architect, you will be responsible for:•	defining frameworks for implementation of required application features.•	driving the usage of effective tools and methods used during software development inside of the team.•	Communication with other groups involved in the development also outside of SH company.•	Negotiation of necessary interfaces and works on software design documents.Job requirements:•	knowledge of at least one of computer languages: C# or C++ on advanced or expert level.•	The candidate shall be able to define formal software design /preferably using Enterprise architect tool/. Therefore, he must understand the UML language and the software modeling process.Experience in any of the following fields is welcome:•	 cross-platform development•	 embedded systems•	*NIX operating systemsPersonality requirements and skills:•	 Proactive and self-directed work style•	Good analytical and problem-solving skills•	Good aptitude for learning new technologies•	Ability to work in a team environment•	Good interpersonal and communications skills•	Good written and verbal communication skills•	This position requires leadership ability and being the role model of a software engineer.Basic wage component (gross) and other rewards: starting from 3200 € brutto /month + VAR* depending on the seniority level of the candidate.
