Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are seeking a skilled PHP Developer to join our team as a contractor, working on behalf of a leading provider of dispatch technology for taxi companies. 
As a PHP Developer, you will be responsible for creating and maintaining web applications and REST APIs using Laravel and MySQL, as well as HTML, JavaScript, and Git.
This is a full-time remote position with flexible working hours, giving you the freedom to work from anywhere. You will have the chance to collaborate with a multinational team and work with cutting-edge technologies to contribute to the development of a world-class taxi dispatch platform.
Additionally, once or twice a year, the client offers the opportunity to attend the party in our Dublin office, with expenses paid by the company.
If you have excellent communication skills in English and are passionate about creating top-quality software, we'd love to hear from you!
• Develop and maintain web applications and REST APIs using PHP and Laravel with MySQL as the database.
• Write clean, well-documented code that follows best practices and adheres to project specifications.
• Collaborate with other team members to design, implement, and test new features.
• Troubleshoot and debug issues as they arise, working closely with support to ensure timely resolution.
• Participate in code reviews and provide constructive feedback to other team members. Employee perks, benefits• Enjoy the flexibility of a full-time remote position with the ability to work from anywhere.
• Benefit from flexible working hours to help you balance your personal and professional commitments.
• Take advantage of the opportunity to visit our Dublin office once or twice a year, with expenses covered by the company, for occasions such as the annual Christmas party.
• Collaborate with a diverse, multinational team of talented professionals.
