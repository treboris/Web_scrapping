Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesGlobalLogic is inviting an experienced Senior Software Developer in Test C++ to join our engineering team.
Our project is a robotic-assisted surgical system which is a master-slave, software-controlled electromechanical system designed for surgeons to perform telerobotic minimally invasive surgery. Our Client is a global leader in medical technology. It designs, develops, manufactures, installs, services, distributes, and markets a wide variety of medical devices. GlobalLogic is taking part in the creation of innovative surgical products and services that focus on obesity and diseases and conditions of the gastrointestinal tract, lung, abdominal wall, pelvic region, and head and neck.
Requirements:
- At least 4 years of experience in software development/testing.
- Understanding of testing theory and test processes.
- Upper-Intermediate level of English.
- Good understanding of *nix systems.
- Solid technical background.
Nice to have:
- Advanced C++ knowledge
- Good Python knowledge
- Experience in embedded software or software for medical device testing and/or development.
- Experience in requirements analysis.
- QNX.
- Networking experience.
- Experience with regulated software development processes.
- Good estimation, planning, and reporting skills.
Responsibilities:
- Participate in software testing of the Robotic-Assisted Surgery system. 
- Work closely with C++ developers. 
- Analyze requirements, create test designs and test cases, and review execution results. Collaborate with a distributed team. 
- Communicate with software developers and testers on the customer side. Strictly follow a formal process. Requirements for the employee Candidates with education suit the positionUniversity education (Bachelor's degree)
