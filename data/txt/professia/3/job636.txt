Linux Embedded Device Specialist - Point of Care
Place of work
Bratislava region, Slovak Republic (Job with occasional home office)
Contract type
full-time
Salary
2 100 EUR/monthFrom 2100 EUR brutto /month + VAR*
Job description, responsibilities and dutiesOur Point of Care Development team in Slovakia is looking for a software engineer who has hands-on experience in working with embedded devices running LINUX OS.https://www.siemens-healthineers.com/point-of-care-testing/critical-care-solutionsAs our Linux Embedded Specialist you will:work with our suppliers and software teams to define minimum viable OS kernelprogram diverse less common peripheral devices like special cameras, low power electromotors and magnetic sensorsbe our First Point of Contact (externally as well as internally) for questions pertaining drivers, secure boot sequences and generally all challenges between hardware LINUX OS and applicationWhat are we expecting:knowledge of C, C++ and scripting languages is requiredknowledge of embedded devices ideally ARM architecture based computers running LINUXability to build a LINUX OS from repositories should be demonstratedwe are looking for a good communicator as in this position discussions with diverse stakeholders around the globe is necessaryWe are considering engineers with different seniority from fresh graduates to experienced developers with optimally 3-7 years experience.Own projects running for example on RASPBERRY PI and shared on GITHUB is a big plusWhat do we offer:Variable and Christmas bonusHybrid type of work – combination of telework and work from officeFlexible Working HoursBridge days – free extra paid leave 6 days per year  3 sick days per year (no doctor’s permit needed) In case of sickness 100% salary reimbursement 20 days/ year, this includes max. 10 days/ year for of family careAdditional pension plan300 EUR for regeneration of work force via cafeteria system Wellbeing program – Psychological, Legal and Financial CouncellingFamily care program (subsidy for newborns, maternity leave, kindergardens, summer camps)Retention program (work anniversary, life anniversary, employee loans)Training and development program (business and product trainings, e-learning, language courses, soft skills trainings,…)Adjustable standing desk as a standardParticipation on world famous IT conferences like Microsoft IGNITE for best employeesWide project portfolio in healthcare domain and job rotation within company (Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence, Healthcare IT services, …)Basic wage component (gross) and other rewards: starting from 2100 EUR brutto /month + VAR**We are required by law to disclose basic wage component (minimum salary) for the advertised positions. We carefully consider your professional qualifications and experience in our compensation package and/or when offering you other positions.Our goal is to pay our employee's fairly, with regard to the market situation and we are ready to welcome high-quality candidates in our team.
