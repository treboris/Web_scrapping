Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesVisma in Kosice started its journey in 2018. Today we have more than 180 employees who work on more than 15 products in various technologies such as C#/.NET, JAVA, Angular, React, PHP, Python, mobile development, and more.
Now we are looking for a EXPERIENCED ANGULAR DEVELOPER, who will help us with the development of our own product named Nordeca.
Nordeca provides a SaaS platform for location-based intelligence to customers in the commercial and residential real estate market, as well as mission critical map data deliveries. Nordeca is based in Oslo.
This application is presented to the caseworkers in Angular as the front end and the engine that powers the system is JAVA. There are a lot of other technologies involved.
Just to mention a few:
BE: Java, Kotlin, Spring MVC, Spring Boot,SolR
FE: HTML5, Anuglar.io, OpenLayers, TypeScript
