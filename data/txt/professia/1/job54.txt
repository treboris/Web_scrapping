Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are looking for 4th member to our local development team. You will be participating on development of .NET applications based on SharePoint & Azure, supporting customers (internal and external) in a multinational environment, participating in delivering solutions for users in the offices worldwide (45 countries on three continents).
Your responsibilities:
• Design, implementation and testing of functional components for the FE (Angular, TypeScript, HTML/CSS) and BE (.NET) layer of enterprise applications based on SharePoint (on-premise and online) & Azure;
• Preparing Unit tests, preparing and run load tests for business critical processes;
• Refactoring and optimization;
• Preparing and updating the build and release pipelines;
• Defining configuration templates for Azure deployment;
• Participate in SCRUM ceremonies;
• Support customers (both internal and external) in a multinational environment;
• Working with Incidents / Service Requests tickets (root cause investigation, fixture, data correction);
Depending on the seniority of the candidate, their other responsibilities may also include:
• Designing the architecture of new applications and data structures;
• Communicating with the product owner and business side of the customer;
• Investigating and fixing security vulnerabilities;
• Analyzing data and preparing reports;
• Analyzing documents for integration interfaces. Employee perks, benefits• Familiarity of a small sized firm and security and possibilities of a large company
