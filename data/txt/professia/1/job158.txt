Job description, responsibilities and dutiesC# senior developer for Common Host PlatformWhy „Projekt ČLOVEK“ is the most importantThe result of our development work serves doctors and medical specialists around the world. Thanks to it, they can diagnose and treat people better and faster (Najdôležitejší IT projekt je ČLOVEK).If people are the most important IT project for you, join us in improving the health of millions of people around the world.We are seeking an experienced C# CI/CD Software Developer who joins existing international team and recognizes an importance of setting the groundwork for a successful team in Slovakia.Together with our international team about 20 colleagues mainly from Slovakia you will participate in definition and creation of software platform and services that is used in software deployment area across almost all Siemens Healthineers products.You will learn innovative technologies and apply the knowledge in experimenting and innovation initiatives.We expect:•	Experience and advanced knowledge in C# development,.NET Core /ASP.NET•	„Clean code“ software craftsmanship (https://manifesto.softwarecraftsmanship.org/#/en) is not foreign expression to You•	You are team player•	Experience with git or other source control•	Experience with Agile development methods•	Advanced spoken and written English•	University educationWe welcome:•	Experience with CI/CD environment, preferable Azure DevOps•	Working knowledge of open-source technologies, and cloud services•	Coaching and mentoring team members•	Experience with Hyper-V or other virtualization environments•	PowerShell experience•	Deep Windows 10 OS knowledgeWe value:•	Proactivity, creativity•	Focus on details and quality•	Ability to approach problems from different perspectives•	Coaching and mentoring team members•	Experimenting, innovation activities
