Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are seeking for a talented candidate to fill our Java Development Engineer position to strengthen our team to be able to provide reliable and secure financial software solutions for our clients.Main responsibilities will include but not be limited to:• Designing and analyzing new solution in cooperation with the team• Developing new features/modules based on the internal requirements as well as requirements from clients• Analyzing and fixing detected code errors• Creating, maintaining and using Unit tests to verify the quality of applications• Reviewing code of less experienced developersRequested technologies:• Java 8, JUnit, Mockito• Spring 5, Spring Boot 2, Spring Cloud Kubernetes• Hibernate ORM• SQLNice to have:• Spring Data• Apache Camel• Docker, Ansible, Kubernetes, Jenkins Employee perks, benefits• Motivating salary and compensation package including regular bonuses, referral bonus• Paid vacation days and sick days also for contractors• Additional bonuses for life events (wedding, birth of child,..)• Friendly work environment with cutting edge technology and challenging projects• Opportunity to work within the team of development professionals and also independently• Business and technical trainings provided• Regular team building activities sponsored• Christmas dinner, Summer Family day• Coffee, tee and fruits for free• Mondays' breakfast, Birthday cakes Requirements for the employee Candidates with education suit the positionUniversity education (Bachelor's degree)
University education (Master's degree)
Postgraduate (Doctorate)Educational SpecializationITLanguage skillsEnglish - Upper intermediate (B2)Other knowledgeJava - AdvancedSQL - SkillfulXML - AdvancedPersonality requirements and skillsIdeal applicant has to love programming, be creative and able to see solutions what others do not see.
