For more than 5 years GlobalLogic team of 40+ engineers develops new models and maintains installed base.
Due to team extension we are opening new Embedded Linux Developer position.
Main technologies:
Embedded C, Networking, Linux
Features of the software:
∙ Connecting to the Internet in various ways: xDSL, Ethernet, DOCSIS, Wi-Fi, 3G, 4G (LTE)
∙ Extensive functionality: IPv6, Firewall, VoIP, Web Server, WiFi, UPnP, Remote Control, Parental Control and others
∙ Works on different hardware platforms (mainly MIPS and ARM)

- Basic training (C/Linux/Networking, up to 3 month)

GlobalLogic headquarters in Slovakia is located in Kosice and the company also operates in Zilina and Banska Bystrica. In 2023 we plan to expand to Bratislava as another Slovak location.
The Kosice delivery center was established in June 2014. With almost 300 employees in Kosice, we work on many projects in healthcare, mobile embedded systems, telco-media, automotive and location based systems for the world’s leading companies. What makes us unique is our strong design team that gives GL a powerful competitive advantage as an IT company in the Kosice region. Kosice technical stack includes , Node.js, Embedded Linux, C++, C#, .NET, SpecFlow, React, Redux, Drupal, PHP, Symfony, GO, Python, MVC, MSSQL, AWS, iOS, Android, Win Mobile, GSM, 2G, 3G, 4G, LTE...
Zilina and Banska Bystrica delivery centers joined the GlobalLogic family in January 2016 through the biggest acquisition in the GlobalLogic history. 100+ employees work on mobile development, embedded systems, automotive and telecommunications projects for the leading companies worldwide. Zilina technical stack comprises Embedded Linux, C, C++, Qt Embedded, , NDS, LUA, GPS, IoT, M2M, Yocto, CAN, Google Tests, BitBake...
