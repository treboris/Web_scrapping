Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are currently looking for a Hardware Developer to be a part of our development team on electronic department in Košice or Michalovce. The successful candidate will participate in development of control, user interface and communication modules for home appliance development projects.Creating and modifying electronic circuits from components to final products for home appliance applications in the following fields depend on HW group focus:	Microprocessor and microcontroller circuits	Motor inverters	Small switched power supplies	Communication boards (Ethernet, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth)	I/O circuits with various displays and touch panelsSimulation of the electronic circuitsWorst-case analysisEMC and EMI testing of the final circuit boards Employee perks, benefits	Working for a leading company of home appliances	13th salary, loyalty rewards	Yearly bonus depend on results	Access to wide range of social benefits via Benefit plus card	Special employee’s discount program for Bosch and Siemens home appliances	Supplementary pension contribution,3rd pillar (DDS)	Stable working conditions	Referral bonus 2.000 EUR	Extra travel compensation bonus	Flexible working time	7.5 hour work day	 Partial Home office working (Up to 50%)	Additional free days in case of selected personal/family events beyond existing labor law	Trainings and development opportunities (hard and soft skills trainings, language courses, product trainings, e- learning, LinkedIn, Seduo…)	Adjustable standing desk as a standard	Participate on exhibition visits (e.g. IFA, Embedded World, Amper etc…)	Team building events, International Children's Day event, Sport activities Information about the selection processTogether with your CV, send us a job application or motivation letter and a copy of the highest education certificate!We will contact just persons who are suitable for the job.
