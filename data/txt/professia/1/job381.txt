Front-end Developer - Team LeadJob description, responsibilities and dutiesIn the AI & Data Analytics division within the Advanced Technology Solutions team we are working on exciting projects for some of the most recognized companies. Majority of our projects are connected with application development, python, machine learning, computer vision, data analytics and so on. But from time to time we choose projects which are out of our basic scope.
Currently we are looking for experienced Full-stack developers for a team lead position (JavaScript/TypeScript, React, Node.js) who will help us to build both internal and external products.
As a Full-stack developer, you are going to start with one of our largest and most sophisticated products being developed as part of the multinational team. It is a great opportunity to get experience with a complex stack of technologies applied at a large enterprise scale.
What is waiting for you as new member of our team:
- analyzing business requirements to come up with cutting edge solutions for clients’ business challenges;
- actively leading a team of developers;
- doing code reviews and tasks distribution within the team;
- being responsible for a team deliverables;
- actively contribute to architectural decisions, proposing best possible solutions;
- support full-cycle development and work on features;
- creating top-quality clean code, perform unit tests;
- peer programing;
- using technologies such as React, Redux, Node.js, Kafka, NIFI, Cosmos DB, Python etc.
- agile methodology;
- interaction with the client and colleagues across different locations. Employee perks, benefits• Hybrid work - Home Office/Office
• Supportive environment, interesting and dynamic work in an international environment
• Progressive professional development and growth opportunities
• We appreciate your extra work, every minute counts!
• Opportunity to work for the best international consulting company and the biggest international clients
• Internal trainings and development programs based on individual needs of employees
• Well-Being initiatives - workshops and lectures, Life Coaching program - Psychological, Law and Financial consulting
• Employee benefits (Cafeteria, sports facilities, cultural and social events, additional days off at Christmas, sicks days…) Personality requirements and skillsTechnical skills
- good knowledge of JavaScript,TypeScript and Node.JS;
- experience with any front-end frameworks - React.js or Angular.js;
- HTML, CSS and responsive design;
- Nifi, Kafka, Azure Cloud is an advantage;
- ability to get into the existing project and code;
- experience with designing applications from a scratch and making architectural decisions;
- understanding of Agile methodologies including Scrum;
- Git, Webpack.
Others
- university degree (IT, technical or business background);
- strong analytical mindset and sharp logical thinking;
- english language (fluent - B2 level);
- enthusiasm to learn new things;
- team player;
- previous experience from a consulting company (nice to have). Information about the selection processIf interested in this position, apply by clicking on "Apply here" and create your profile in our recruitment system.
