About us
SiemensWe are a technology company focused on industry, infrastructure, transport, and healthcare. By combining the real and the digital worlds, we empower our customers to transform their industries and markets, helping them to transform the everyday for billions of people.Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are looking for a senior back-end developer for a project intended to improve energy efficiency and sustainability of buildings – offices, hospitals, hotel chains, etc. You will be working with up-to-date technologies in an international scrum team which closely collaborates with colleagues in Switzerland and Germany. Our goal is to implement a highly responsive, scalable and secure solution with attractive look & feel which will be used across the world by our external customers – facility managers, building owners and others.In this role you will be responsible for:•	Analysis, design and implementation in Java or C#•	Implementation of unit tests for your code•	Document your design and code•	Participate on scrum events – daily stand-up, sprint review, demo, retrospective•	Communicating with product management and team members from other teams about interfaces and dependenciesMandatory skills:•	Object oriented analysis, design and implementation•	Experience with programming in object-oriented programming language (Java, C#, typescript)•	Experience with cloud infrastructure (e.g. AWS)•	Knowledge of design patterns•	Experience with working in a SCRUM teamNice to have skills:•	UML•	SQL•	Service Oriented Architecture (rest)At Siemens we are always challenging ourselves to build a better future. We need the most innovative and diverse Digital Minds to develop tomorrow‘s reality. Find out more about the Digital world of Siemens here: www.siemens.com/careers/digitalminds
