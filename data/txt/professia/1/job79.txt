POSITIONDESCRIPTIONAccenture Advanced Technology center is looking for technology enthusiasts.We offer challenging IT career with focus on application development, promoting work-life balance and encouraging you to be your best.Develop applications with a cloud-first mindset, experience end-to-end project delivery using Agile and DevOps and help shaping innovative technologies in our state-of-the-art Accenture Labs.Most of our projects are based on modern technology stacks, for more details read further.As senior developer you will have the opportunity to become architect (if you are not on that level already) and act as team leader. You will be mentoring juniors and mediors within your team, and act as SME in some areas to support your growth in your career and make our projects successful. WHAT WILLYOU DO• Work in a team to design and code custom-build solutions based on Java technologies (mainly Spring, occasionally Java EE) and related frameworks• Integrate technical and application components• Follow defined methodology and standards to create a software of high quality• Guide and coach your colleagues• Take opportunity to work with Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain and other modern technologies• Build and maintain a cloud-native web application on AWS and custom Java Development frameworks• Iterative prototyping for key pages to optimize and simplify the user experience• Be part of a community focusing on Agile delivery and principles• Enable rapid Development and cost – efficient application by using cloud – native technologies and DevOps• Maintaining, tuning and repairing applications in order to keep them performing according to technical and functional specifications WHAT WEEXPECT• Experience with Java development• Experience with application frameworks (Spring, Java EE)• Experience with SQL/NoSQL databases• Ability to communicate in English fluently• Experience with technical designNice to haves:• Experience with web development (JavaScript)• Experience with continuous integration/deployment• Experience with containerization• Experience with architecture design• Experience with team management and project management WHAT WEOFFERSalary2 900 - 4 500 EUR/monthAccenture offers a competitive compensation package. As required by the Slovak law we state, that the legal monthly minimum gross base pay starting from 2 900 € to 4 500 € depending on your professional and personal qualifications in the required areas.Technical and soft-skills development: • Trainings on architectural and technology trends• Trainings on presentational and communication skills• Language courses• Certifications: (paid by company) Java, Spring Framework AWS / Google Cloud / Azure Scrum Artificial Intelligence / Blockchain … and others• Large network of experts throughout the world• Support in attending conferences• Discount on buying Accenture sharesRecognition: • Guaranteed paid overtime or overtime vacation• German language bonus up to 400€ monthly/gross depending on language proficiency and level of seniority• Flexible Benefit System - budget to be spent on services of your choice• Competitive bonus structure• Refer-a-Friend – get a bonus in the employee referral program• Loyalty rewardsFlexibility: • Flexible working arrangements (time & place of work)Future: • Wide range of trainings and expert lectures• Regular performance review and career growth possibilities• Ongoing career guidance and mentoring (Global Mentoring Program, Lean In)• Employee share purchase planFamily & Health: • 3 extra days off• Sick leave salary compensation (up to 100%)• Home-office equipment• Family oriented benefits (Wedding, Parental and Baby days-off and bonuses)• Employee assistance services (professional psychological, financial and legal help)• Multisport card Interested?Apply for this job
