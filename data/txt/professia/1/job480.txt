About us
SiemensWe are a technology company focused on industry, infrastructure, transport, and healthcare. By combining the real and the digital worlds, we empower our customers to transform their industries and markets, helping them to transform the everyday for billions of people.Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are looking for DevOps Engineer for projects creating cloud-based solutions for smart buildings. You will be part of our Scrum team working on implementation of front-end, back-end and AWS cloud infrastructure features. We Develop and operate platform, based on AWS EKS to maintain smart buildings energy management in a cloud-based repository in close cooperation with our colleagues from Switzerland, Germany and other countries. We use this platform and applications to help our customers with cost reduction through predictive maintenance and through visualization and analysis of data coming directly from IoT devices installed in the buildings. Team is responsible to support developers with automation and Gitlab CI/CD process and improvements among others.Check our web site at https://new.siemens.com/global/en/products/buildings/building-x.html In this role you will be responsible for: · Development and operations of cloud infrastructure as main focus. · Development and maintenance of Kubernetes cluster · Contribution to CI/CD process in GitLab · Document your implementation · Participate on scrum events – daily stand-up, demo, retrospective, planning · Communicating with product management, architects and team members from other teams about dependencies.We expect: · University Education (IT) or relevant work experience 3+ years· Infrastructure as a Code- Terraform · AWS Services overview · Kubernetes knowledge · GIT (or similar source code management system) · GitLab CI practical experience · User level of project tracking tool (Jazz, JIRA, …) · Experience and willingness to work in agile team Nice to have skills: · Ansible, Chef · Docker· Working experience with object oriented programming languages · Knowledge of design patterns · UML · SQL Personality requirements and skills: · Good analytical and problem solving skills · Good written and verbal communication skills · Team player · Willingnes to learn new technologies
