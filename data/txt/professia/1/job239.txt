 Monitoring Engineer

POSITIONDESCRIPTIONAccenture Technology Solutions is the place for someone looking for a challenging IT career and access to the kind of training, expertise and development tools that only a large company can offer.Our international reach also means you could have the opportunity to be part of a truly global organization of smart, technically-minded people and work with dynamic clients in countries all over the world. WHAT WILLYOU DO•    Initiation and coordination of monitoring projects•    Creating architecture for monitoring solutions•    Communicating with customer stakeholders, including leadership, support teams, and system administrators•    Creation of monitoring business cases and other inputs needed for monitoring process•    Creating of automating workflows•    Finding solutions for different data ingestions (Kafka, Prometheus, SAP, Grafana, MQ, Azure...)•    Working on a project to ensure monitoring solutions implemented•    Developing new capabilities for our clients across multiple industries beyond static reports in Splunk•    Implementing application, infrastructure, business processes and KPI monitoring solutions•    Ingest, explore, and visualize data•    Performing application and infrastructure analysis and troubleshooting•    Performing optimization of existing clustered Splunk deployments•    Provide best practices in the adoption, expansion, and operations of Splunk Enterprise and Splunk Cloud•    Recommend and execute improvements to the existing Splunk architecture and design with growth and scalability in mind to optimize performance, stability, reliability, and agility•    Responsible for reporting on current infrastructure status and planning for future usage•    Investigate alerts and anomalies and participate in incident response.•    Operational support of the CI/CD pipeline in the area of monitoring and logging WHAT WEEXPECT•    3+ years of experience in data engineering•    Familiar with ETL and Data Cleanse and Integration•    Experience working with complex application environments, with the ability to understand relevant sources of machine data, such as performance logs, application logs, audit logs, security data sources, network-centric data, etc•    Several years of hands-on experience in Service Monitoring-Tools (experience in Splunk is highly preferred, experience in Nagios, Spectrum, open source is also an advantage)•    Knowledge of Git/Jenkins as well as other tools for automation solutions•    Professional experience with Linux/Unix operating systems•    Practical experience of cloud concepts either using AWS or Azure•    Strong knowledge of networking protocols and log formats•    Experience deploying / managing  monitoring solutions, platforms, and technologies•    Good communication skills•    Advanced English, spoken and written WHAT WEOFFERSalary1 800 - 3 000 EUR/monthAccenture offers a competitive compensation package.
