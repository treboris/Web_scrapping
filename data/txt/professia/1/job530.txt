Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesAre you interested in implementing high-quality customer solutions? Since establishment in 2002 client has experienced great success with market-leading solution. The increased demand leads to the expansion of team. Project Tech stack: Java 8+, Kubernetes, Docker, MS SQL, Swagger, Zkoss, Maven, Oracle DB Advertiser Brief description of the companyGlobalLogic is a leader in digital product engineering services. We help our clients design and build innovative products, platforms and tools, and thus improve the customer digital experience in the modern world. By offering integrated solutions and combining strategic design, complex engineering, and vertical industry expertise, we help our clients imagine what’s possible and accelerate their transition into tomorrow’s digital business.
GlobalLogic headquarters in Slovakia is located in Kosice and the company also operates in Zilina and Banska Bystrica. In 2023 we plan to expand to Bratislava as another Slovak location.
The Kosice delivery center was established in June 2014. With almost 300 employees in Kosice, we work on many projects in healthcare, mobile embedded systems, telco-media, automotive and location based systems for the world’s leading companies. What makes us unique is our strong design team that gives GL a powerful competitive advantage as an IT company in the Kosice region. Kosice technical stack includes , Node.js, Embedded Linux, C++, C#, .NET, SpecFlow, React, Redux, Drupal, PHP, Symfony, GO, Python, MVC, MSSQL, AWS, iOS, Android, Win Mobile, GSM, 2G, 3G, 4G, LTE...
Zilina and Banska Bystrica delivery centers joined the GlobalLogic family in January 2016 through the biggest acquisition in the GlobalLogic history. 100+ employees work on mobile development, embedded systems, automotive and telecommunications projects for the leading companies worldwide.
