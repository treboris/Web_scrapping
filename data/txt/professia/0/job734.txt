Junior Microsoft .NET/C#/CRM Developer
Do you have background in IT with some experience in C#. .NET or CRM development? Have you worked as an IT Specialist and are you ready to consider
a career change into Microsoft world? This might be a suitable opportunity for you!
Position is located in Bratislava, Kosice or Banska Bystrica.
What would be your responsibilities?
• Requirements gathering and analysis
• Configuration and development
• Testing and deployment
• Support and maintenance
• Active communication with the customers, developers and other service delivery teams in order to perform regular operations tasks as well as solve the application issues
What do you need for your success?
• Knowledge of Microsoft development stack
• Experience in programming (C#, entry level .NET)

• Strong communicator, ability to provide technical advice to client and understanding the needs of stakeholders of a regular IT project such as architects, project managers, business users and operational staff.
