Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesHello! The AT&T Global Technology Support Services (GTSS) group is searching for new team members to join our dynamic, international team.GTSS is responsible for administering hundreds of servers, developing & supporting dozens of infrastructure tools, and managing relationships with multiple Suppliers.Our mission is to provide best-in-class service to internal AT&T customers and Supplier employees around the world.As an Infrastructure Tools Engineer you will be responsible for the development and production support of infrastructure tools written in JAVA and PHP.You will work with end users, application administrators, and database administrators to understand requirements and create highly responsive applications with exceptional functionality.In addition, you will be responsible for the production application environment, work with Infrastructure Support Engineers to manage issues and outages, and partner with System Engineers to keep your application servers updated and functioning at peak performance.Training, mentoring, and oversight will be provided by our Senior Infrastructure Tools Engineer.
