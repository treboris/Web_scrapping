SharePoint / Power Platform Developer
        We are looking for senior developers with a primary focus on SharePoint, Microsoft 365, and Power Platform. In our fast-growing team, you can work on different worldwide projects, try all Microsoft technologies, or improve your current skills while doing various MS certifications.
We are ready to provide you supportive and motivating working environment and gratify you with our benefits!
The position is located in Bratislava, Kosice, or Banska Bystrica.
 What would be your responsibilities?
    • Requirements gathering and analysis
    • Configuration and development
    • Testing and deployment
    • Supporting and maintenance
    • Active communication with the customers, developers, and other service delivery teams in order to perform regular operations tasks as well as solve the application issues
 What do you need for your success? 
    • Hands-on experience with SharePoint (On-Premise, Online) from commercial projects
    • Knowledge of the Microsoft Power Platform (PowerApps, MS Flow) and related technologies
    • Knowledge of Microsoft development stack
    • Experience in programming at least 3 years (C#, .NET) is a plus
    • Proficient in English, German is nice to have
    • Strong communicator, ability to provide technical advice to clients and understanding the needs of stakeholders of a regular IT project such as architects, project managers, business users, and operational staff
 What can you expect of us? 
    • Salary starting from 2300 - 4000 EUR gross, depending on relevant experience  and language proficiency
    • Additional Days Off
    • Flexible Working Conditions
    • 100% Paid Sick Leave
    • Critical Illness Insurance, Life & Disability insurance, Medical Center
    • Personal & Career Development
    • And many more: ibm.biz/benefits2021
