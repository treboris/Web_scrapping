Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWhat is waiting for you Programming, recovery and debugging of control systems of single-purpose machines. Programming of PLC (Beckhoff, Siemens) automation, visualization, robots, testers, servo systems and other industrial automation, including creation of superior control or database systems Collaboration with the designer of the electrical part of single-purpose machines. The design itself may also be marginally required provided the employee is suitably qualified. Producing relevant documentation such as block diagrams, circuit diagram drawings, function descriptions, BOMs, manuals and other documents The work results mainly in electronic data, and the employee shall take steps to ensure that the data is not damaged or lost. The worker proceeds with the creation and debugging of the program to prevent the possibility of damage to the single-purpose machine and to avoid injury to the operator. Participates in the design of safety circuits. Participates in single-purpose machine improvement activities.
