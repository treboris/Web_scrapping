Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and duties Analysis, design, development and testing of real-time based SW for automation machines Handing over the machines to customer factory (including installation at customer) Over 70% tasks involve travelling (you will spent this time abroad on customer sites or at our head office) Remote support of installation of solutions at customer, Hotline, providing support tocustomers and colleagues Foreign business trips Analyzing of customer's requirements and taking part in solution proposal and effortestimation for new projects or requirements Handling functional and safety defects, Taking part in training of colleagues, Reporting Documenting the process flow and SW solution Employee perks, benefits Attractive additional financial benefits such as holiday allowances and health bonus (1 extra salary) Flexible working times  depending on the respective workplace Language courses payed by company (150 € / semester) Regular employee interviews for individual career advancement Multisport card Social benefits (weddings, births, work, life anniversaries) Employee events Meal vouchers valued at € 5.50 / day with the option of boarding in the company Private parking in front of the company Additional surcharge of 25% from the minimum wage up to 8 hours a day at customer site Information about the selection processThis position is being filled for the sister company Muehlbauer Automation s.r.o Requirements for the employee Candidates with education suit the positionFollow-up/Higher Professional Education
