Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesJoin our team and become a part of the global, fast-growing success here at Sportradar. Atechnology-first software development company with huge emphasis on getting rid oftechnological debt and building services using the quality focused engineering practices andcloud-first principles. Imagine yourself working in surroundings of sports data, live play-by-playstatistics, virtual sports, microservices, and betting – everything done in an agile spirit andalways striving to deliver the most value for our customers.The ‘Odds Tribe’ is building a new technology hub in Bratislava. We are looking for people thatwant to help us build this new location into a world class engineering facility. This meanshelping us create the right engineering culture and values for Sportradar in this location. Wewant you and your help to make Sportradar Bratislava the best place to work, thus attract theright talent.THE CHALLENGE:• Be part of creating the Sportradar Bratislava engineering culture.• Build high performance services/products on top of our real-time sports data collected24/7 globally for more than 30 different sports.• Take an active part in making the most out of our AWS partnership• Become a domain expert in the exciting world of real-time hi-tech sports data• A real opportunity to influence the design and technology Employee perks, benefitsThe opportunity to work and develop within an inspiring and fast-growing company.• The possibility to help shaping the future of Sportradar. All developers are directlycontributing to products used by our clients in the global sports business.• Involvement in shaping your own workday and career through high degree of freedom tochoose technology and your personal development.• A collaborative environment with colleagues from all over the world (Engineering officesin Europe, Asia and US).• A company culture that promotes social aspects, sports, physical exercise and fun.• Innovative and cross-team challenges like Ship-It, office sports tournaments in TableTennis and Table Football.• Competitive salary and benefits (e.g. retirement pension and insurance plan)• Possibility to work from home office when needed.
