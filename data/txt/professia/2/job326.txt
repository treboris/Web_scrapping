 IT Application Developer PLSQL INTERNInformation about the position Place of workBratislava, Slovakia (Job with occasional home office) Job description, responsibilities and dutiesNexi offers an internship program designed to provide opportunities for students interested in IT to learn more about this industry and about how our business work. Responsibilities may include creating design documents for the projects, coding, testing and deploying the changes. 
Your job would be:
• Analysis, design, development, optimization, administration, customization, testing and documentation of SW application for payment transaction processing and card issuing
• Design and implementation of new functionalities
• Contribute to overall team performance, including support and assistance of other team
• Documenting problems, bugs and resolutions Wage (gross)7 EUR/hour Employee perks, benefits• Opportunity to gain work experience in international corporate environment while studying
• Flexible working hours
• Possibility to find full time job opportunity after graduation within the company Requirements for the employee Candidates with education suit the positionUniversity studentEducational SpecializationITLanguage skillsEnglish - Upper intermediate (B2) and Slovak - Intermediate (B1)Other knowledgeMicrosoft Office - SkillfulOracle - BasicPython - BasicJava - BasicPersonality requirements and skills• Knowledgeable with searched technologies at least on basic level
• Ability to work and navigate with Microsoft Office Products
• Ability to work as a team player
• Able to analyze information and draw conclusions
• A positive level of self-motivation
• Work schedule is flexible and set up for 20 hours a week
• At least written communication level in English is expected Advertiser Brief description of the companyNexi is European leader in the design, creation and management of technology infrastructures and services for Financial Institutions, Central Banks, Corporates and the Public Sector, in the areas of payments, cards, network services and capital markets. Nexi Group provides its services in over 50 countries, and also operates through its subsidiaries in Austria, Croatia, Czech Republic, Germany, Greece, Romania, Serbia, Slovakia, Hungary and South Africa. The company also has branches in Belgium and the Netherlands, and representation offices in the UK and Poland.
