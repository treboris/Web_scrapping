Senior .NET DeveloperKistler Bratislava, s.r.o.Place of work: Bratislava, Slovak RepublicWage (gross): 2 700 EUR/monthContract type: full-timeInformation about the position Job description, responsibilities and duties  Purpose of the PositionDevelopment of a Cloud-powered IoT application platform Main tasks  Backend development of software component functionalities for a Cloud-based application platform, which enables distributed measurements with IoT devices Detailed software design of software components Producing software design documentation compliant with Kistler quality standards Participation in collective architectural discussions Writing unit/integration tests for developed components, ideally in a TDD/BDD style Continually detecting and eliminating any tech debt, to ensure an optimal flow rate and keeping high code quality Defect fixing, debugging, code reviews    Employee perks, benefitsWhat you can expect from us The way we work  Hybrid working model – home office up to 60% a week (based on the agreement with your team lead). We trust that you know how to organize your time to stay productive Regular performance review and career growth possibilities  Health & Well-being  Electric stand-up desk and ergonomic chair 100% salary reimbursement for 10 days of sick leave/caring for a family member  25 days of vacation for everybody  Recognition  Compensation of overtimes Compensation of travelling time in case of business trip (extra allowance) Flexible Benefit System - starting from 1000€ per year in the Benefit Plus portal - platform with thousands of suppliers of your choice, including Kistler specific offerings Attractive referral bonus     Extra contribution in significant life events – weddings and childbirths, rehousing Relocation bonus for employees who are moving because of Kistler (over 100 km)  Career & Development  Budget for education, certificates, trainings - 500 EUR/year Online English & German language courses Library, Online Educational Portals: Pluralsight, Safari books, LeanPub, Egghead Technology “sharing” groups, Coding Days  Social life  Team-buildings, Company breakfast, Christmas Party, Grill Party, Family/Kid’s day, Pizza Thursdays etc. Football table and board games in the office  Extra benefits  Special offers and discounts (Metro cards, SND, Curaprox etc.) Lunch tickets in amount of 5.50 EUR On-site Joga or Pilates classes Great coffee, tea, fresh fruit  Information about the selection processInformation about the selection process: Do you like it so far? Let us know, send us your CV. After receiving and reviewing your CV we will contact you for short telephone pre-screen. Following this, if successful, we will set up meeting with your future team leader and one of our technical specialist for more detailed discussion about your experience and motivation and to answer your questions about company and project itself. The final stage of the hiring process is meeting with head of Kistler Bratislava to get to know you better and align on the personal and position expectations. Our HR colleagues will be informing and guiding you through every step of this process. Good luck. We are looking forward to meeting you.Expected date of completion of the selection process26 April 2023 (there are still 22 more days) Requirements for the employee Candidates with education suit the positionUniversity education (Bachelor's degree)Language skillsEnglish - Upper intermediate (B2)Number of years of experience3Personality requirements and skillsWhat we expect from you Must have  Necessary experience in (profound expert knowledge) Software-Engineering Application of OO design patterns and SOLID principles Analytical and systematic working style Ability to debug complex components Ability and willingness to learn and adapt to complex development environment Affinity to work in an international environment Commitment to deliver outstanding quality Commitment to work with cutting edge technologies of cloud computing Very good command of languages: English in speech and writing  Nice to have  Experience with microservices (event-driven communication + REST API) Experience with Kafka, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, GitLab CI/CD pipelines Test Driven Development practitioner
