Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesExciting Opportunity to Lead New IT Development Team and Work with Latest Technologies!Are you a passionate leader with experience in software development and team coordination? Do you thrive in an agile environment and strongly focus on quality and automated testing? If yes, we have an exciting opportunity for you!We are building a new IT development team in Košice and looking for a talented colleague to lead it.
