Tianma Microelectronics has a subsidiary in Europe since 2004 already. Tianma Europe is 100% responsible for sales, marketing, quality and logistic support of Tianma’s LCDs in Europe. It provides a wide range of display solutions, including professional LCD TFTs, passive LCDs and capacitive touch displays. Combining world-class technology with Tianma’s state-of-the-art facilities, it offers high quality products to it’s customers in multiple markets including automotive, medical and industrial.
To reinforce our European IT team based in Duesseldorf we are looking for a highly motivated
IT ENGINEER (F/M/D)
As a valuable member of the IT team, you support, maintain and create our IT structure, are the contact person for our employees, solve IT problems paired with a positive attitude and high engagement
Job Content
User support for MS Office, ERP (Oracle Fusion), CRM (MS Dynamics)
Administration of server network and cloud applications
Setup and maintain clients, F&P services etc.
Test and implement new server, cloud, and desktop applications
Maintain existing applications and provide resolution for software problems
Responsible for documentation management of quality related documents
Create documentation for new applications and maintain existing documentation
Act as communicative link to headquarter IT in China
User support, training, problem solving
Your Profile
Degree in Information Technology or Business Informatics combined with at least 3 years of relevant experience
Experience in maintenance of ERP systems, MS Server and Networkadministration
Experience with data bases ( SQL) and ORACLE applications, CRM Microsoft Dynamic
Structured operational and strong analytical skills
Pro-active, persuasive with flexible attitude and cultural awareness
Creativity and team spirit
Advanced Excel skills and MS Office
Excellent communication skills in English and German, Chinese is an advantage
We offer
In addition to an attractive salary we offer variable incentive bonuses as well as non-monetary benefits
Flexible working time
30 days’ of holiday and special paid leave
An international, professional and dynamic team
Excellent Healthcare Benefits and Team events
Advanced Training program
