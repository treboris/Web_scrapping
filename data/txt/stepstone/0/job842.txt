Your role:
Become part of 3DEXCITE, the Dassault Systèmes Marketing and Sales brand. We provide comprehensive solutions for collaboration and 3D product content creation that enable our customers to evolve their marketing beyond messaging.
As a 3D Rendering Application Developer (m/f/d), you join our team responsible for enhancing product data for high-end 3D visualization purposes. You will work on front-end and application logic components for the management of cameras. 
Your responsibilities:
Analyze user requirements and translate them into valuable feature increments
Execute UI, architecture and code reviews with the corresponding experts
Develop reusable and easily testable components
Improve the product quality based on feedback from colleagues, QA as well as from internal and external stakeholders
Your qualifications:
Degree in computer science or similar specialization
Good skills in C++ and strong background in object-oriented programming
Advanced understanding of Rasterizing (OpenGL) and Raytracing concepts
Good knowledge of best practices and design patterns
Self-reliant, solution-oriented way of working with outstanding team and communication skills
Very good English language skills, German or French language skills are an advantage
What's in it for you?
A permanent job allowing flexible working times ensuring a good work-life-balance
Becoming a member in a multi-cultural and world-wide operating R&D team
Working with state of the art rendering technologies
Getting insights into various industries targeted by the 3DEXCITE portfolio
An open, creative, dynamic work environment in a motivated team
Many different training opportunities covering domains going beyond your daily business
