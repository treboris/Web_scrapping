We are seeking a working student within our Global MBM Defense Security Operations Team
Our Global MBM Defense Security Operations Team is seeking a working student to support with our global strategic activities and our daily business.
The activities you'll be supporting with vary from supporting proof of concepts to security incident management.
Examples are:
Supporting the Cyber Security Incident Management process
Piloting new technologies for Security Incident tracking
Daily administrative work (activities related to Project Management and Reporting)
Support in development of a Security Incident reporting dashboard
Support Threat Report generation for our CISO and Executive Committee
Qualifications
Started Computer Science, Information Technology, or other IT-related field of study
Basic Knowledge of Cyber Security incl. risk management, incidents and threats
Proficient English language skill; German language skill is a plus
Experience with Microsoft Office (Excel and PowerPoint), Experience with PowerBI is a plus
International experience and basic Project Management skills would be desirable
