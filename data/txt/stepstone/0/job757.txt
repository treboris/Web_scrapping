Your future. Pump it up!
The Würth Elektronik Group with more than 8200 employees in 50 countries manufactures and sells electronic and electromechanical components, circuit boards and intelligent power and control systems. The business unit Würth Elektronik eiSos with Headquarters in Waldenburg, Germany, is specialized on electronic and electromechanical components and one of the most successful in the Würth Group.
What to expect
Building test environments for test items
Performing measurements on radio modules and sensors in the measurement laboratory
Independent evaluation and preparation of measurement data
Collaborate in product-related development, release and documentation
Create product libraries
Developing circuits and layouts of test boards
What you bring
Studies in the area of electrical engineering/electronics
Knowledge of high-frequency range, soldering and handling of measuring equipment such as oscilloscope, network analyzer, spectrum analyzer
Knowledge of Latex and the development tools Eagle and Altium are desirable
Good language skills in German and English
Your strengths: independence, initiative and openness
What we offer
Outstanding corporate culture that puts a high value on mutual esteem and team spirit
Extensive orientation training and numerous learning and development opportunities
Diverse benefits such as our WEtality health and sports programme, corporate events, commuting allowance
We live and value diversity
Flexible working hours taking into account the lecture schedule
