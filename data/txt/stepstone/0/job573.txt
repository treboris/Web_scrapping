At CARIAD, it’s our mission to transform automotive mobility for everyone, everywhere, making it safer, more sustainable, and more comfortable in every way.
To deliver on that promise, we’re building a unified technology and software platform, including a vehicle OS and cloud platform, as well as a unified architecture. As a 100% subsidiary of the Volkswagen Group, we’re developing solutions for all of its brands, including Volkswagen, Audi, and Porsche, and will bring our software to over 40 million vehicles by 2030.
Is this an easy task? Not at all! To tackle such a huge challenge, we need a great team. And that’s where you come in. You’ll join more than 5,000 CARIDIANS already working on the latest vehicle features and functions like automated driving, state-of-the-art charging technology, as well as a new digital ecosystem. Together, we’re bringing sustainable change to one of the largest companies in the world.
YOUR TEAM
We develop the standardized cockpit electronics and software system for future vehicle E/E architectures. We will provide the base for the Infotainment Platform enabling a rich, simple and comfortable infotainment and digital customer experience across all Volkswagen Group brands.
The System Base team is a group of experts and technology enthusiasts located in the Architecture Development Area at CARIAD. With deep system knowledge and technological skill, we work on projects from the first minute until the product goes to production. The team is responsible for a broad field of base functions like Operating Systems (Linux, AUTOSAR, Android, QNX), Performance, Stability, Power Management, Virtualization, Tracing and Logging of the Cockpit Infotainment System. We design, develop, manage system and software requirements together with our partners and suppliers. During the development cycle a major part is the error analysis together with our internal and external SW partners.
As "System Base Architect” you will be&nbsp;responsible to specify, support Bring-up and&nbsp;development of&nbsp;the Platform with focus around the&nbsp;Android System.
#OneInfotainment
WHAT YOU WILL DO
Development of the primary system-base functions within all the relevant integrated computing platforms
Define the platform requirments in regard of Android, Memory Management, Performance, Device Driver, Kernel
Creation of detailed specification of Platform &amp;&nbsp;System Functions
Control and cooperate with Software Development departments within &amp; outside of&nbsp;CARIAD
Develop tools, write scripts to analyze the system and support the Bring-up and&nbsp;Release of the System functions within the different platforms
Assess system hardware and software implementation in terms of system requirement specifications, change requests and system integration
WHO YOU ARE
University degree in Engineering, computer science or comparable course
Several years of experience in system and/or software development, preferably in the area of body or infotainment systems
Thorough understanding of automotive electronics and software development projects including current and emerging processes and technologies
Knowledge of functional, vehicle and control unit architectures and SW platforms, operating systems (deep knowledge in AUTOSAR, Linux, Android), programming languages (deep knowledge of C, C++, Java beneficial), software technologies in a virtualized system, and automotive tools (e.g. Vector CANoe)
Ideally process-know how within ASPICE and agile development methods&nbsp;
Fluent written and verbal communication skills in&nbsp;English; German is a plus
