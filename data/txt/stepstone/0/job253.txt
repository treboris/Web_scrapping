Discover your exciting role
The Solutions architect proactively and holistically leads and supports EA activities that guide the development and management of a portfolio of SAP S4 solutions. SAP S4 Solutions include systems (applications, processes, information and technology), shared infrastructure services, and shared application services and components to enable and drive targeted business outcomes
Explore your tasks and responsibilities
Translate business and technical requirements into an architectural blueprint to achieve business objectives and to document all SAP S4 solution architecture design and analysis work
Manage the full cycle development process for regional and global SAP S4 projects from gathering requirements, designing the SAP S4 software solution, and developing the Business Intelligence programs, reports, workbooks, or data extractions for the different stakeholders
Permanently analyse and design the target SAP S4 architecture landscape in respect to business applications landscape and technology landscape by considering Exyte’s business goals, strategy, business processes
Work in a Matrix Organization with Business Owners, Service Manager and other Solution Architects
Develop a detailed roadmap for the evolution of the SAP S4 enterprise solution portfolio from current to future state, as defined by the Enterprise architecture
Create architectural designs to guide and contextualize SAP S4 solution development across products, services, projects, and systems, including modules, technologies, processes, and information
Work closely with the program management office or project management office to ensure the execution of plans corresponds with what is being promised throughout the SAP S4 Solution project or product lifecycle
Lead and guide global and regional IT personnel in SAP S4 functional area for SAP S4 development and integration projects
Assess global and regional IT demands in collaboration with business management including prioritisation of demands targeted to the SAP S4 domain and preparing make-or-buy decisions for their implementation
Facilitate business conversations and moderate the definition of design principles in order to govern the incoming demands and to assess demands from different stakeholders on architectural fit
