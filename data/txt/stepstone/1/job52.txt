A vacancy for an IT Infrastructure Engineer / Architect (d/f/m) has arisen within Airbus Defence and Space in Manching.
We develop top notch, high fidelity Engineering and Training Simulations for a variety of aircraft. Our simulations are basically complex, distributed realtime systems. For the development and operation we rely on a powerful IT infrastructure. In order to take our Infrastructure to the next level, we are currently looking for an additional experienced IT Infrastructure Architect / Engineer. You will work in an international team of passionate Infrastructre Engineers at our site in Manching, Germany.
You have the chance to directly shape the evolution of our infrastructure and products. Being part of our Infrastructure Team means that you fully buy into a "Service Ownership" culture. You empower our Software and DevOps Engineers to take our products to the next level.
Your location
Located about an hour's drive north of Munich, Manching is an up-and-coming market town that offers a wide range of  leisure and cultural activities. Here, you can enjoy the quality of life in the countryside while the pleasures of near-by cities are still within easy reach.
Your benefits
- Attractive salary including holiday pay, Christmas bonus and profit sharing
- 30 days holidays and extra days-off for special occasions
- Excellent upskilling opportunities and great development prospects
- Special benefits: employer-funded pension, employee stock options, discounted car leasing, special conditions for insurances, public transport subsidy, discounts at local businesses
- On-site-facilities: Medical officer for check-ups and other health-related services, canteen and cafeteria, kindergarten nearby
Your tasks consists of
- Capture the infrastructure needs and requirements from our Developers and DevOps Enginners
- Define the techology strategy for our IT Infrastructure
- Develop, implement and maintain infrastructure service
- Design IT infrastructure architectural blueprints for our products
- Compile infrastructure policies and define standards
- Maintain our internal IaaS Services and the underlying technologies (e.g. Proxmox, Kubernetes)
- Maintain our internal PaaS Services (e.g. Jenkins, Nexus)
- Coordinate closely with corporate IT services like ITSec, Networking
We are looking for a candidate with the following skills and mindset:
- Master degree (or equivalent) in Computer Science or a related discipline
- At least 5-8 years of professional experience in the design and operation of Enterprise IT Infrastructure
- A deep understanding and experince with Infrastructure-as-Code Tools (Terraform)
- High Level of expertise in at least one or more related fields (e.g. virtualization, storage, automation...)
- A good understanding which High-Availibility, Scalability and Fault tolerance mean
- The urge to automate as much as possible
- Experience with dynamic Povisioning and Immutable Systems
- Solid Multi-Distro Linux Skills
- Experience in one or more Scripting / programming languages (Python, Bash, C++, Go...)
- Experience with the Atlassian tool stack (Jira, Confluence)
- Excellent English (negotiation level)
- Proactivity is key
Related Technologies
- Virtualization (Proxmox, oVirt, KVM)
- Infrastructure provisioning Tools (Ansible, Puppet)
- The Hashicorp Toolstack (Terraform, Packer, Vault)
- Container based Infrastucture (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Storage Solutions (Ceph, GlusterFS, ZFS, Samba)
- Monitoring (checkMK, Icinga, Prometheus)
- Webserver (Apache, Nginx, HAProxy / Taefik)
- Continuous Integration (Jenkins)
- Authentication (LDAP, Active Directory, Kerberos)
- Networking (VLANs, Cisco IOS, DNS, DHCP, PXE)
- Version Control Management (Git)
