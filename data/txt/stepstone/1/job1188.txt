Statista is one of the world’s leading statistics portals.
As a multiple award-winning innovative digital company, Statista provides customers such as Google, Bloomberg, Forbes, Procter & Gamble, or Porsche with high-quality research & analysis, data intelligence, and content marketing products and services.
With 1100 employees from over 70 nations and 13 subsidiaries in Europe, North America, and Asia, Statista derives its innovative energy from its internationality and diversity.
Statista provides a company culture based around learning by doing and entrepreneurship, offers great career opportunities, and combines the pioneering spirit of a start-up with the stability of an established company.
Your Team
In our Editorial Research Intelligence Department, approximately 200 researchers work on the statistics and topic pages for our different national platforms. Each day, while focusing on their specific fields, our experts research and prepare new data from the world’s most significant sources for our platforms: https://www.statista.com/.
Your Tasks
Automation of internal process steps with R or Python
Conception and implementation of solutions for the analysis of structured and unstructured data from a variety of sources and their pre-processing
Identifying optimization and automation potentials within Statista’s content management processes
Developing, maintaining, and improving ETL processes and NLP solutions
Internal contact person for the first publication of large datasets
Structuring of data and verification of data quality
Development of new data sources and preparation of data with R and/or Python
Your Profile
Successfully completed studies in the fields of data science, economics and social sciences, mathematics, computer science or a comparable field
At least 2 years of professional experience in data analysis and/or process automation
Profound knowledge of R and/or Python
Very good knowledge of spoken and written English
Ability to describe complicated issues concisely and understandably
Confident handling of the Microsoft Office package, in particular very good Excel skills
Structured, organized, and independent working style with an emphasis on quality, detail, and accuracy
Enjoy developing, organizing, and analyzing large amounts of data
High-quality standards for your own work
Your Benefits
