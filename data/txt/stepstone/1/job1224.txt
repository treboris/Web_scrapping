Summary
Imagine what you could do here. At Apple, new ideas have a way of becoming great products very quickly. Bring passion and dedication to your job and there's no telling what you could accomplish!
The Audio & Music Apps group is looking for a senior software engineer. We are an international team with a presence in Rellingen/Germany, Edinburgh and Cupertino. The Rellingen engineering team works on GarageBand iOS, Logic, GarageBand macOS, and MainStage. Your position will be at our German office in Rellingen near Hamburg.
Responsibilities
Our team practices fast-paced development that relies heavily on a tight relationship between engineering, QA and product design. This position requires a highly ambitious & creative engineer with strong technical and interpersonal skills. Responsibilities will include working on new features for iOS and/or macOS applications, developing new audio and music related technologies and applications, and supporting the existing features of our apps. Attention to detail and critical thinking skills are required.
Qualifications
Key Qualifications
Strong software development skills with C++, Objective-C, or Swift.
Proven ability to develop and ship high quality software products
Strong knowledge of Xcode and related developer tools
Experience with SCM Systems (e.g. Git, SVN)
Experience implementing UI's on iOS and/or Mac platforms
Education and Experience
Master's Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or other related field or equivalent professional experience in software development is required.
