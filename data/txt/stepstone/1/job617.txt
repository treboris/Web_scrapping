Ihre Rolle
Responsible for managing the collaborative care component of Health Data Platform within the ZEISS Medical Ecosystem
Define core platform strategy, vision, roadmap, and requirements that are cross-cutting for different business and customer segments in alignment with the business leads
Convert the strategy and strategic initiatives into well-developed plans and objectives over different time frames
Act as a SAFe Product Manager for the Health Data Platform Value Stream by prioritizing customer needs to drive a solution set throughout the entire product life cycle
Work closely with product owners to define core platform features, monitor and manage key APIs, and manage vendor integrations
Define pricing strategy for services related to the Health Data Platform
Support Launch Processes and Country or Customer Enablement for all offerings based on the Health Data Platform in collaboration with other departments to ensure successful product launch
Conduct regular market surveys, focus groups to assess customer and sales channel needs and market trends
Be the expert in regards to the competition offerings
Provide customer facing documentation regarding the Health Data Platform to marketing or other departments
Work closely with sales and services to provide trainings or support tools regarding core platform elements
Key contact for Business Managers, Product Managers regarding customer and sales related topics for the health data platform
Define right metrics and KPIs for success
Ihr Profil
University degree in medical physics, computer science, engineering, or a relevant adjacent subject (Science, Optics, Medical Technology or similar disciplines); alternatively business degree and strong technology and healthcare experience
At least 7 – 10 years work experience and minimum of 5 years in Product Management
Ideally work experience in health care, technology or cloud-based platforms
Proven ability to work in an international work environment and matrix organization
Fluent in business English; willingness to travel internationally 20-25%
Ideally tech- savvy and see the power of digital platforms
In-depth knowledge of market and customer requirements in medical imaging, post processing applications, Healthcare IT, and big data analytics
Experience in agile software projects and are familiar with software development methodologies (SAFe, DevOps)
Strong knowledge of modern Cloud technology (Azure) and Web technologies (Type Script, HTML, CSS, Angular)
Experience with qualitative data (user feedback, single-use cases) and quantitative data (metrics, analytics)
Ability to write SQL queries and use data analysis tools and APIs
Excellent communication skills and can efficiently prioritize and align various stakeholders to a common goal
Collaborative skills to work with cross-functional teams
Excellent writing style and can also present complex issues in an understandable manner
Work effectively in an interdisciplinary and multicultural team environment
Champion new and innovative ideas with strong analytical skills and entrepreneurial thinking
