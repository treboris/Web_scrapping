Your tasks
About the role:
The Salesforce Administrator (German speaking) will be responsible for custom product development and maintenance, user management, system security, reporting and ongoing optimization efforts for our existing CRM platform and future integrated sales tools, their features, third-party applications, and integrations.
What You’ll be Doing:
Create and maintain custom and standard objects, fields, views, flows, and other components and functions, adhering to project specifications and timelines
Generating reporting for our Sales Leadership teams in region
Coordinate work with Project Managers and other Administrators
Manage system changes without interruption to the user or impact on other workflows and processes.
Research and manage third-party applications that support and enhance business processes and end-user experience
Act as our sales tools subject matter expert for integrations with other internal data systems
Maintain, enhance, and create workflows, configurations, and components that increase benefits and usability for end-user
Manage new releases of sales tools and efficiently roll out new features
Respond to user requests for support in a timely manner
Continually seek ways to enhance the end-user experience further
Manage user licenses and security settings, including sharing rules, roles, permission sets,
Monitor application storage usage and archive data as needed
Develop process documentation and field maps of custom work into our sales tools
Assist with migration from older systems/processes in line with our customer engagement roadmap
Ensure data integrity by merging duplicate records, performing bulk data uploads and updates as required, and removing unnecessary fields and data as needed and in compliance with data retention policies
Create and adhere to naming conventions and organizational data standards
Your profile
A bit about you:
3+ years of Salesforce CRM or other similar Sales tools administration experience
Current Salesforce Administrator Certification
Fluent in both verbal and written German
Experience of having worked within an Agile environment
In-depth knowledge of the standard capabilities of these sales tools and have successfully expanded on those capabilities through the use of custom code and integration with external systems
Ability to work independently, as well as part of a team and engage with global stakeholders
Team player with a positive attitude
Great communication skills with an enthusiastic personality
Ability to utilize critical thinking skills in decision-making situations, good organizational and record-keeping skills, and good independent judgment
Strong problem-solving skills, data and software applications
Experience with Salesforce Communities
Experience with custom buildouts that are not dependent on standard Salesforce objects
