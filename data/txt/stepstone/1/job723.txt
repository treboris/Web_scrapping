BizLink is a global provider of connectivity solutions in the field of data and energy management. We provide solutions for industries following global megatrends such as electromobility, industrial automation, medical technology and data centers with cables and cable systems.

Inhouse Consultant Business Applications - Microsoft Dynamics NAV / Business Central (m/w/d)
Your responsibilities:
Advising the departments on the further development of business processes and their mapping in the course of the migration project to Dynamics 365 Business Central
Development of requirements definition, coordination of process ideas with internal customers, and coordination of changes in the IT landscape
Development, coordination, implementation, and documentation of solution proposals following internal guidelines in cooperation with the locations
Creation of training materials and documentation as well as user training for new and existing workflows or processes in German and English
Processing of service and support requests in coordination with the Business Units
Maintenance of the existing Microsoft Dynamics NAV environment
Required qualifications:
Completed degree in business informatics, business administration or comparable economic training
Extensive work experience and in-depth process knowledge in the areas of production, production planning and supply chain management and their mapping in the relevant Microsoft Dynamics NAV/BC modules
Knowledge of C/AL programming and/or AL programming
Knowledge of object-oriented programming languages, e.g. C#/.NET, and web technologies preferred
Project management experience in the manufacturing industry
Work experience in conception, effort estimation, documentation, and testing with a focus on the manufacturing industry
High consulting competence and IT affinity
Strong analytical and conceptual skills
Agile and out-of-the-box mindset
Focus on solutions considering the big picture
Outstanding team player in the workplace
Strong presentation and communication skills 
Excellent knowledge of German and English, both written and spoken
Our offer:
Assignments with room for creativity in a motivated team
Continuing training opportunities and career prospects within a highly successful and international group of companies
Supportive working environment with short decision-making processes, flat hierarchies, and company events
Attractive remuneration incl. tariff supplement, Christmas bonus, and holiday pay
Employer contribution to a company pension scheme or AVWL
30 days of holiday plus additional paid leave for specific occasions
Promotion of your health through modern and ergonomic workplaces
