 Ihre Rolle
As IT Application Specialist Customer Experience Management, you will be the technical owner with global responsibility for applications with focus on Customer Experience Management (CEM software), Net Promoter Score (NPS) and customer survey applications
You will ensure the holistic process of optimal application operation, from the initialization and implementation as well as operation to the analysis of errors and faults, to their elimination, to the monitoring of the applications as well as the execution of functional tests
Working in IT projects and coordination of changes and enhancements and advise users on all questions regarding the software and its interfaces is also a key responsibility in this role
Ihr Profil
Degree in business informatics, computer science, or similar or completed vocational training in the IT field with further training as an IT professional and relevant professional experience
Experience in project management, application management and support as well as knowledge in (digital) marketing, automation, and experience in CEM, NPS or other Survey Software
Good knowledge of SaaS and cloud solutions as well as REST API's - Solid knowledge of HTML and CSS - Ideally knowledge in real time analytics or OLAP engine (Online Analytical Processing engine)
Secure communication and presentation skills
Good English and German language skills, written and spoken
