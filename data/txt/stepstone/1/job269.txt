  Product Security Manager - Cyber Security (d/f/m
  Your responsibilities:
Is IT your passion and Cybersecurity your career pillar?
Come and join us at our amazing Headquarter in Wetzlar, with a hybrid work solution!
Responsible for the implementation of the global Danaher cybersecurity policy for products in Applied Microscopy, you will support the project teams as a technical expert for cybersecurity and participate in the development and refinement of a state-of-the-art process.
Analysis of product safety risks and their mitigation throughout the development process and product life cycle, establishment of an effective product security lifecycle through professional threat modeling, security testing and vulnerability testing will be your bread and butter.
  Support and advise senior management, product management, project management and R&D managers about cybersecurity standards and best practices for all of the division's products
Work continuously on reviewing, refining and revising all relevant R&D processes to adapt to company requirements
Review and approve product risk assessments for each development milestone
Assist project teams in preparing product security management plans - including design control requirements, patch management strategies and implementation plan
Plan and conduct workshops on threat modeling (e.g. STRIDE, attack trees) and risk assessment with product development teams
Define security rule sets and support their implementation in static and dynamic code analysis tools
Ensure and verify that development teams follow industry standards for system hardening and secure coding
Implement measures for supply chain security by creating and maintaining software bills of materials (SBOM)
Establish and maintain an effective vulnerability screening process for all products within the business unit; initiate, support and direct the incident management process
Support all parties involved in patch management and in the treatment of vulnerabilities (internal and external)
Track current developments in the area of information security and business trends in the industry, for example by participating in benchmarking/ OpCo events/in professional associations 
    Our expectations:
University’s degree in Computer science, Information Technology or Engineering
Many years of professional experience in Software Development/Software Architecture in manufacturing industries 4.0
Strong background in Cybersecurity and extensive know-how of the latest software technologies
Experience in managing projects without disciplinary authority
Good knowledge of conducting risk and impact assessments and defining solution strategies
You possess a basic understanding of privacy-enhancing technologies and regulations such as GDPR
You have experience working with international teams and customers
You are a real team-player with the ability to effectively interact and communicate with everyone involved at various technical levels
Your English is fluent in written and in spoken; German would be a plus
