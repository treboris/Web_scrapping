IT Consultant - Cloud Specialist (m/f/d)*
for HR1.4 – Global People Strategy & Transformation
The department Global HR1.4 (Global People Strategy & Transformation) is responsible for HR Digitalization, People Strategy Development as well as Project and Change Management.
The current HR IT project HoRizon is a joint initiative of HR and IT aiming to implement globally standardized HR processes and related technology for the RI Group. We are looking for an enthusiastic IT Cloud Specialist to support our department in implementing and maintaining SAP SuccessFactors.
Your job
Developing and supporting Global Process Owners in implementing HR processes and solutions based on new functions and releases in SAP SuccessFactors, by collecting relevant business requirements
Evaluating and applying system release functionalities in coordination with affected stakeholders, taking cost-benefit aspects into account
Solving complex SuccessFactores related issues in close collaboration with IT colleagues
Monitoring and updating overall permission concepts
Supporting global SuccessFactors related projects
Participating in communications and change management, affecting HR technology platforms and impacted processes
Your profile
Several years of experience in HR Cloud system handling (ideally SAP SuccessFactors), and experience in end-to-end implementation
Deep knowledge of SAP SuccessFactors module Employee Central; additional knowledge of modules (e.g. Succession & Development and Compensation) is a plus
Good knowledge of HR processes and how they are supported
Good understanding of business contexts, processes and feasibility, taking into account cost-benefit aspects
Very good communication and project management skills
Excellent analytical/ problem solving skills, ability to think globally and network
Strong written & verbal communication in English
