LS telcom AG is the world's leading provider of technologies and consulting services for the efficient use of the radio spectrum in all radio services. In an increasingly digital-networked world, we ensure that all spectrum users benefit maximally from radio services. We optimize spectrum management and use in all markets and market segments and enable new business models in vertical markets based on the Internet of Things (IoT).
Main tasks
You are designing and developing customer defined reports based on the administrative and technical LS telcom software
Development of complex reports
Generation of graphical layouts in charts and reports
Building of customer specific reports for printouts
You are responsible for allocated development tasks in close cooperation with your team or department head
You are working independently where required, on analysis, design and layout of customer report requirements
Qualification Profile
You have successfully completed training, education or studies in the field of Information Technology
You have a keen interest in operating on-premise, public and private cloud solutions
You have good English skills, German skills preferable
You ideally already have some knowledge in one or more of the following areas like Crystal Reports, Jasper Reports and / or ORACLE APEX
What we offer you
Varied, demanding and interesting field of activity
Flexible working hours to reconcile work and private life
Collegial and highly professional team in a modern, international working environment
Career opportunities here in Germany or worldwide
