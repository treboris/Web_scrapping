A Career at HARMAN
As a technology leader that is rapidly on the move, HARMAN is filled with people who are focused on making life better. Innovation, inclusivity and teamwork are a part of our DNA. When you add that to the challenges we take on and solve together, you’ll discover that at HARMAN you can grow, make a difference and be proud of the work you do every day.
What You Will Do
Cross customer projects root cause analysis of field failures with emphasis on lower application stack e.g., operating system, driver etc. for our Infotainment system and amplifier
Failure localization from customer view down to technical / component view
Analysis of analog and digital functional blocks (e.g., power supplies, audio blocks, microcontroller / SoC units, etc.) within complex hardware architectures
Responsibility for analysis tasks and coordination within HW and SW Engineering departments as required (e.g., NTF-Process)
Reporting of failure description, root cause and remedial actions
What You Need
Bachelor or Master in Electrical Engineering, Computer Sciences or a related technical discipline
Minimum 3 years of experience in automotive development
Knowledge in Hardware and Software of Automotive systems (Telematics, Infotainment, Amplifiers) as well as bus systems (PCIe, USB)
Knowledge of automotive networking (MOST, CAN, etc.), automotive diagnosis and flash tools
Excellent Knowledge of digital signal processing (DSP), preferably in audio signal processing, including DSP programming (e.g., Analog Devices and Texas Instruments)
What Makes You Eligible
Knowledge of DC / DC converters and digital circuits as well as EMC-compliant circuit design
Knowledge and hands-on experience with scripting languages, tools and operating systems (e.g. POSSIX conform operating systems
Knowledge of problem solving tools & techniques like 5-Why, Y-Effect, Fishbone diagram, 8D or similar will be preferred
Well-structured and results-oriented
Very good analytical, logical and problem solving skills
Excellent communication, coordination and teamwork skills
Business fluent in German and English
Driving licence and willingness to travel
What We Offer
HARMAN offers a diverse and inclusive work environment that fosters and encourages career development opportunities, professional training and competitive market base compensation
You will also receive top-notch employee discounts on Harman’s Consumer Audio (JBL, AKG, HARMAN Kardon) and Samsung products
Flexible work schedule with a culture encouraging work life integration and collaboration in a global friendly working atmosphere and good work-life-balance
Attractive salary package based on the Metall & Elektro Tarif Baden Wurttemberg and an Employer contribution towards a company pension plan
Professional development opportunities through HARMAN University’s business and leadership academies which also includes extensive technical trainings
Internal talent management leadership acceleration programs
“Be Brilliant” employee recognition and rewards program
