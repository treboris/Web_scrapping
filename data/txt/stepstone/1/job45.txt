About Brenntag
Brenntag is the global market leader in chemical and ingredients distribution. The German based international company manages complex supply chains for both chemical manufacturers and consumers by simplifying market access to thousands of products and services. It combines a global network with outstanding local execution. Therefore, Brenntag is the industry’s most effective and preferred channel to the market for partners.
Brenntag operates a global network of about 700 locations in 78 countries. With its global workforce of more than 17,000 employees, the company generated sales of around EUR 14.4 billion in 2021.
Our team in Essen currently has an opening for a Domain Lead Information Security Governance and IT Compliance (m/f/d)
Your Role & Responsibilities
In this role you will support the global CISO in designing, building, and operating an adaptive, resilient, and business-enabling Brenntag's Information security management system (BG-ISMS) with a zero-tolerance for internal and external digital risk by adopting and shaping global digital security best practices and standards.
Key Activities and Responsibilities
Design and implement Level 1 to 4 information security policies and procedures
Develop and implement an information security risk management strategy and processes
Perform a global information security risk assessment  
Develop and maintain an information security asset inventory and the associated risks ranking
Track the effectiveness of cyber risk controls measures through regular audits and maturity metrics
Evolve a strategy for 3rd party cybersecurity assessment  
Enforce information security governance in all relevant business operations
Coordinate the information security governance and compliance committee 
Develop a strategy for IoT/OT and supply-chain security management
Provide a high impact leadership to the team
Own and evolve the cloud security control framework that translates the cloud Security directive into a layering of controls related to our CIA Classifications
Provide leadership to the information Security GRC team
Understands and enforce the cloud security governance framework
You ensure the cloud platform adheres to and is embedded in the enterprise security architecture
You have experience with topics such as security-by-design, privacy-by-design, secure coding
Your Profile
Masters or Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science/Information Systems or equivalent experience in Information Systems preferred
3+ years developing and implementing security operations and technology in large, complex enterprises in multiple industry verticals, across a wide range of technology platforms as well as 3+ years on any Cloud Platform (AWS, Azure, Google, others)
Deep hands-on experience leading the design, development, and deployment of business software at scale in SaaS, PaaS, and IaaS environment
Experience with service-oriented architectures, private and public clouds, and web services security
Strong skills in security principles such as least privilege access, defense in depth, preventative vs. detective controls, Infrastructure and Network Security, Data protection, and Incident response
Professional experience and good technical knowledge of application security, system security, network security, authentication/authorization protocols, and cryptography
Experience in industry standards such as PCI DSS, ISO 27xxx, SOC, HIPAA, GDPR, and NIST/DoD frameworks and benchmarks
Good understanding of Enterprise Networks, Security, and Identity Access Management
Configuration management using CloudFormation and/or Chef/Puppet
Experience with agile approaches and Experience in DevOps or DevSecOps and how they impact risk management and compliance
Hands-on technical expertise in technology automation, implementation, integration, and/or deployment using scripting and/or IaaC
Nice-to-Haves: AWS/Google Solutions Architect, AWS/Google Security Specialty Certified; CISSP, CCSP, CISM, and/or other comparable certifications; Information security certificates such as: CEH, CISSP, CCSP (Certified Cloud Security Professional) etc.
