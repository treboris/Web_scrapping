Your tasks
Good random numbers are critical for most security concepts. The generation and analysis, however, is not trivial in many automotive, embedded systems.
Different sources of entropy that are available in our products shall be identified (accelerators, timers, ...), analyzed, and integrated in the overall system.
For our generic random bit generator framework, the evaluation of different noise sources available in our products has to be done. 
The framework might also be extended by additional features and a test suite might be developed for it.
You will get an overview about our portfolio and you will contribute with your work to further increase security of future projects.
In close coordination with  security management and  product engineering this will provide you the possibility to gain insight in our different product areas.
