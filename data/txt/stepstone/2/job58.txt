Einleitung
  Aioi Nissay Dowa Insurance Company of Europe SE is a company headquartered in Luxembourg and active on the German market as an insurance company in the private and commercial customer sector. The company is part of one of the largest insurance groups in the world, MS&AD Group Holdings, Inc., an insurance partner of the Toyota organization and a major shareholder of Box Innovation Group Limited, one of Europe's leading telematics providers.
As the insurance partner of Toyota, one of the largest automobile manufacturers in the world, we develop and distribute outstanding insurance and financial services products for brands such as Toyota and Lexus, as well as specialised products for our car dealership organisation.
Our European IT Organisation provides internal IT services throughout Europe for approx. 1,000 colleagues 
Ihre Aufgaben
Take complete responsibility for the organization of the IT Application Services (Front Office) as well as the development of our IT (FO) applications according to the principles of ITSM and ITIL as well as the operation and development
Providing leadership and strategic guidance for the team “IT Application Services (Front Office)” with members from both internal and external sources to keep our daily business going on a high level
Development and implementation of IT strategies for the area of IT Application Services (Front Office) in line with our overall IT strategy
Initiation and execution of projects to (further) develop and implement new and updated IT (front office) application solutions and services to meet the business needs of our European offices
Identify and analyse gaps to ensure good service delivery (quantity and quality of work, timescales, and budget) and to further develop our service
Ihr Profil
5+ years of experience in a team-leading role in IT application management and experience in the further strategic development of a team preferably, in an international environment
Strong leadership skills and perennial management experience in personnel management with distributed international teams and intercultural collaboration
Comprehensive long-term experience in the operation of Front Office applications, preferred in the insurance sector
Valid knowledge of IT project management and software development methods (waterfall, agility, scrum) as well as programming (Java) and database management (Microsoft SQL, MySQL, PostgreSQL and DB2, etc.)
Fluent in spoken and written English, German language skills are optiona
