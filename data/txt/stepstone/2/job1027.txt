Einleitung
NEXUS / ASTRAIA GmbH, based in Munich (Germany), is one of the world's leading manufacturers of innovative software solutions in Obstetrics and Gynecology. With 20 years’ experience, a dedicated team, and close working relationships with our customers as well as scientists and ultrasound manufacturers, our products are always up-to-date on the latest scientific data. 
Currently we are looking for a
Medical Software Developer (m/w/d)
  Your tasks
NEXUS / ASTRAIA GmbH is a  widely loved and internationally installed java-Desktop application consolidating 20 years of science driven medical development work.
We are looking for a motivated programmer to help both with the maintenance of this application, as well as to be involved in the future redesign and implementation of the planned next-generation web based replacement.
   As a full-time developer you will be working on project custom solutions, bug-fixes and feature requests, as well as the refactoring and redesign work necessary for moving NEXUS / ASTRAIA into the future.
   Your Profile
You must bring: 
+ At least 2 years of Java development experience, very familiar with at least the Java 8 API.
+ Proficient with either SVN or Git versioning system
+ Fairly proficient with SQL (i.e., understand how to run a query via JDBC, and know how to use the various JOIN types; INNER, LEFT or RIGHT OUTER
+ Familiar with the concept of the separation of concerns and how this applies to application development (i.e., MVC)
+ Familiar with one or more web development frameworks
+ Very good knowledge of written and spoken English
  We will value knowledge in:
+ Familiar with medical application development, or with medical related systems and workflows
+ Familiar with popular database persistence frameworks
+ Familiar with the Spring framework
    We offer
+ An interesting and varied job in a complex environment
+ A high degree of personal responsibility and a creative working atmosphere
+ Personal development in a well-coordinated team with a good working atmosphere
+ A secure and attractive workplace
+ Flat hierarchies with short communication channels as well as quick and direct decisions
+ Opportunities for further development according to your skills and performance
+ An attractive and performance-oriented salary
