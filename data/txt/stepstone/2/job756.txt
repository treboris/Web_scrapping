Company Description
upday is a media and tech company and the leading news app in Europe with more than 25 million MAUs. Founded in 2016 as a joint project between Axel Springer and Samsung Electronics, upday became profitable after just 3 years in the market.  
If you’re looking to join a dynamic and international team full of fantastic talent that has the energy of a start-up but the stability of an established media company - look no further, we are your match! 
Senior Data Engineer (all genders)
Your Challenges
team player with first experience working in an agile environment
keen to develop your skills, and those of your team
interested in data-warehouse structures (data marts) to meet business operation needs
fascinated by production-ready prediction/categorization pipelines to bring machine learning to the live app
captivated of data manipulation and training/evaluation solutions to help our research on the data
Your Profile
you have 5+ years of experience in data engineering
you can handle perfectly with Python, Airflow and AWS and similar
You dealt with more data than a single machine can handle
Do you possess experience in any of the below? It´s a big plus!
Snowflake, Snowplow, Spark, Hadoop (or similar distributed data processing technologies), MLOps technologies
