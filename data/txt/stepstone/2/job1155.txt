YOU ARE PASSIONATE ABOUT
Participating in a strong, agile development team to improve development and deployment processes in AWS, Azure, and VMware-based environments
Being part of a cross-functional DevOps team that supports various business units and exchange knowledge with experienced DevOps engineers
Taking ownership of the supported product and bringing in your expertise to enhance its quality
Achieving reliability and reproducability through Continuous Integration, Continuous Delivery, and methods like Infrastructure-as-Code
Making daily work easier for yourself and the team, working on areas aligned with your strengths
YOU AND US - THE PERFECT MATCH
Familiarity with agile work methods and the DevOps methodology
An interest in being the driving force to bring development and operations closer together
Experience in or the willingness to learn how to operate highly-available AWS/Azure cloud environments
Business fluent proficiency in English
