flaconi is one of Germany’s leading online beauty retailers. Our online shop’s portfolio offers more than 850 international brands and 55.000 products in the categories of perfume, skin care, make-up, hair care, natural cosmetics and accessories, covering from drugstore to organic to premium products.
flaconi was founded in 2011 and has a team of over 700 employees working from three locations: Our HQ in Berlin-Charlottenburg, our logistics center in Halle and our concept store in Berlin-Mitte. We are also represented in Austria and Poland.
What you can expect
As Working Student IT Support, you will have the opportunity to work alongside our IT Operations Team and have the chance to gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge software and hardware, learn new technologies, and discover the latest trends and technologies. This role provides an excellent opportunity for personal and professional growth, and could potentially open doors to future career opportunities within our organization.
What you can expect in this role
With your enthusiasm for technical support, you will assist the Infrastructure team offer first and second-level support for end-users, including diagnosing and resolving software and hardware-related problems
Moreover, one of your tasks will be to prepare the hardware for your colleagues, and provide support for all equipment, including seamless setup and configuration (On- and Offboarding)
You will develop, write and maintain comprehensive technical documentation and procedures for an improved and streamlined user experience
Furthermore, you will proactively monitor and maintain flaconi's applications and networks, ensuring that they are up-to-date with the latest software patches and network updates
In addition, you will provide technical training to employees and support our knowledge sharing within our innovative organization
Your essential experience and education
Enrolled student (Bachelor or Master in Technology, or an equivalent field) with an availability of at least one year (On-site in our HQ in Berlin 20h/week)
A customer oriented attitude and good communication skills with the ability to communicate/solve technical issues also for colleagues without technical knowledge
First experience with Operating Systems, Cloud Software and Hardware, as well as basic knowledge of networks and network security
You are passionate about technology
Fluency in English, German is a plus
What's in it for you?
Full transparency: For this role, we offer you an hourly salary between 12€ and 14€. The salary you can expect will always be within this span and is based on the skills that you show during the recruitment process and the progress of your studies
Employee discounts & other perks: Great discounts in our online shop as well with our partner companies, cool team events & company parties!
Work-life balance: 29 Vacation days, flexible working hours, up to three days of homeoffice a week and the possibility of a paid sabbatical
We care about your well being: We want to support you also during the difficult times. For this, we offer you and your close relatives counseling sessions with mental health professionals
flaconi Fitness: A wide range of sports options & discounts on memberships
Trainee Get Together: We promote the exchange between interns, working students and trainees at flaconi as part of our network
A comfortable working space with everything you need: High end technical equipment, fruits and drinks, no dress code and dogs are welcome in our office!
