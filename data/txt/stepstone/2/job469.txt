Company Description
Publicis Sapient is a digital transformation partner helping established organizations get digitally enabled, both in the way they work and how they serve their customers. We help unlock value through a start-up mindset and modern methods, fusing strategy, consulting, and customer experience with agile engineering and problem-solving creativity. As digital pioneers with 20,000 people and 53 offices worldwide, our experience spanning technology, data sciences, consulting, and customer obsession – combined with our culture of curiosity and relentlessness – enables us to accelerate our clients' businesses through designing the products and services their customers truly value. Publicis Sapient is the digital business transformation hub of Publicis Groupe. For more information, visit  publicissapient.com. 
Job Description
 Your Impact
As a Senior Manager Technology with a focus on Financial Services you will play a leading role in helping our clients in their digital and cloud transformation journeys, along with with supporting pre- and post-sales efforts. This is a technical, client-facing engineering delivery role with a high impact and immense personal growth opportunity.
Qualifications
  Your Skills and Experience
Prior experience (ideally 3+ years) in a senior engineering/architecture position within a software development team
Prior experience in building, leading and mentoring large scale software engineering teams
Fluency in both German and English languages is a must
Extensive experience of architecture, design, implementation and support of enterprise scale distributed applications designed to run in the cloud or across hybrid cloud and on-premises environments
Knowledge of current development trends pertinent to cloud engineering such as service meshes & API gateways
Polyglot programmer with extensive experience in at least one of Java, Kotlin, and Node JS
Extensive experience working on cloud, ideally across multiple cloud providers (AWS, GPC, Azure)
Solid understanding of modern DevOps practices, including automation, continuous delivery, continuous deployment, and continuous integration methodologies
Active participation in opensource, blogs and technical conferences is desired
Financial Services sector experience (especially working in a bank) is desired
Excellent communication skills, as you will be leading conversations with clients
Willingness to travel
