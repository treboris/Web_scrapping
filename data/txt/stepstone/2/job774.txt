Our passion for technical challenges drives us every day. With around 400 employees at our sites in Switzerland, Germany and China, we develop and produce the most innovative power electronics for the E-Mobility.
BRUSA HyPower AG, based in Buchs (Canton St. Gallen), was founded in 2021 as a spin-off of BRUSA Elektronik AG.BRUSA HyPower specializes in electrical energy conversion systems such as DC/DC converters and onboard charging systems. The company develops power electronics products for on-highway, off-highway and stationary applications for its international customers.
E-Mobility is the future. Become a part of it! Join us in Buchs (CH) immediately or by arrangement as a
Software Test Manager (m/f/d)
What you will move in this role:
You are responsible for the creation of test plans and concepts, as well as the estimation of test efforts and risks
The coordination ans control of all software test activities under consideration of time and costs belong to your tasks
You support the requirements analysis and the description of test specifications, as well as the creation and review of test and test reports
You will be responsible for identifying test risks and reporting final results internally to development and management
Supporting project management in the creation of requirements coverage reports are also part of your responsibilities within the company
What skills and experience you should bring:
You have a successfully completed degree in the field of computer science, electrical engineering or comparable; ISTQB - Certified Tester, Advanced Level Test Manager or comparable further training is an advantage
You have sound experience in the execution of tests and functional tests of software in the automotive sector
Through your career to date, you have an in-depth understanding of the development processes according to Automotive SPICE v3.1, ISO-26262 and other relevant standards
You have practical experience in project planning and implementation
You maintain a results and quality oriented work style and have good problem solving skills
Your English skills are very good (written and spoken); good German skills are an advantage
