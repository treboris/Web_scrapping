Olympus is passionate about creating customer-driven solutions for the medical, life sciences and industrial equipment industries. For more than 100 years, Olympus has focused on making people’s lives healthier, safer and more fulfilling by helping to detect, prevent and treat disease, furthering scientific research and ensuring public safety. Olympus is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, with more than 30,000 employees worldwide in nearly 40 countries. Olympus Europa, headquartered in Hamburg, Germany, serves the EMEA (Europe, Middle East & Africa) region and employs more than 7,800 people in 21 countries. Apply now!
Your Responsibilities
Join us and be part of a leading MedTech company and a truly Global IT team where over 650 talented professionals, across 5 regions, work collaboratively to provide the best technology solutions that deliver innovation and value to our health care industry partners and patients around the world. Being part of Global IT at Olympus means living our vision of leading technology innovation globally to power our company purpose – making people’s lives healthier, safer and more fulfilling. We do this through placing our customers and patients at the heart of everything we do. At Olympus, our people are our greatest asset. We create a place where people can grow, be their best and feel welcome, valued and included.
Strategy and Roadmap Definition, Demand Management and Delivery
Plan, develop, own and drive a global IT strategy for the assigned business domain (Finance / Procurement)
Foster, shape and manage the business demand for the IT investment in its assigned business domain across all geographies and business units
Identify approaches to meet the business’s needs as well as the dependencies (both risks and opportunities) with other domain Business Relationship Managers
Ensure that the prioritized demand is satisfyingly translated into valid IT solutions and clearly defined projects (right scoping and proper benefit definition)
Establish and maintain multi-year roadmap of the business capacity model and the system landscape for IT systems related to domains in scope
Ensure satisfying IT delivery. After project completion, establish a learning and improvement review process to continuously enhance business value generation
Stakeholder Management and Relationship Development
Actively manage the relationship and satisfaction of all key stakeholders (CFO, Senior VP, VP) in the finance and procurement functions across the different geographies
As the IT organization’s chief advocate, inform and influence the business unit executives so that they understand, support and adequately fund projects that are in concert with IT goals, such as enterprise architecture and the infrastructure/application strategy
Establish and run an internal VoC process (Voice of Internal Customer) to evaluate levels of satisfaction with the various IT services and to identify improvements required in IT/business units in order to further increase the value delivered by IT projects and services
Your Qualifications
Master’s degree in Computer Science, Business Administration or equivalent and ideally related experience
BRM Institute certification advantageous (not mandatory)
Several years of experience as a Global General Manager or Senior IT Consultant in the assigned Finance / Procurement domains
Several years of experience in value creation and demand planning activities
Strong working understanding of major business processes, key performance indicators, best-in-class practices, business, and technology trends in the assigned business domain
Solid understanding of the current and planned technical architecture, platforms and solutions that support the business and sound understanding of the broader technology environment
Experience of both solution consulting and implementation/support
Experience in successfully managing teams and executive stakeholders in multiple geographies
Demonstrable interpersonal and problem-solving skills as well as demonstrable coordinating skills
