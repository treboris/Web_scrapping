Tasks
The Mercedes-Benz Group AG is one of the most successful automotive companies in the world. With Mercedes-Benz AG and the divisions Mercedes-Benz Cars, Mercedes-Benz Vans and Mercedes-Benz Mobility AG, the vehicle manufacturer is one of the largest and most successful suppliers of premium cars.
At Mercedes-Benz, we shape the automobile generations of the future. We are a highly motivated team within the development of the UI software for MBUX, i.e. the part of the MBUX software that provides our customers with the user interface. Our work is what our customers see and experience directly when they interact with MBUX and is therefore a formative part of the user experience in our vehicles. Specifically, our team develops the UIKit (UI Software Core) internally.
We are looking for senior software engineers that have a passion for embedded software solutions preferably in the automotive industry.
Your tasks
Develop UIKit features for our automotive infotainment system MBUX in an agile development workflow (C++/Linux/Android)
Create, collect and clarify architectural requirements with internal and external stakeholders
Develop high quality code and verify using unit, system, and integration test code to ensure code quality and robustness
Work close to hardware for testing and optimization on target platforms
Act according the standards of the automotive industry, in-car applications and software development processes
Create and maintain code design documents on high level and/or component level
Qualifications
What you bring to the table
University degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or a comparable qualification
Minimum 3 years experience in Android based application development preferably for automotive infotainment platform
Experience with the Android SDK, Core java, NDK
Experience working with Android frameworks and its customization
Experience working in agile software frameworks such as SCRUM, Kanban, SAFe, or LeSS
Experience in applying international software standards such as CMMI, Automotive SPICE, or automotive cybersecurity standards is a plus
Business-fluent English, German is a plus
