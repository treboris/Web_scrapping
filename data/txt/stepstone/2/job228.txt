marktguru is the operator of the award-winning smart shopping app of the same name, which is one of the most popular and most-used consumer services on the German-speaking Internet. The Munich-based technology company was launched in 2013 as a pioneer in the field of digital brochures and individual offers in the German market. Every day, marktguru offers consumers thousands of top advantage offers with extensive savings opportunities when shopping, whether in stationary or online retail. As a free shopping planner and assistant for millions of users, marktguru (app and web) provides up-to-date information (brochures, offers and promotions) from top brands from all relevant areas of daily need and combines these with innovative location-based user services and a powerful cashback system.
What you can expect
We are seeking a talented Site Reliability Engineer with a focus on databases and infrastructure to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for ensuring the reliability, availability and scalability of our systems and applications. This includes designing, developing and maintaining our databases and infrastructure, as well as working closely with the development team to improve performance and scalability.
What you can expect in this role
Design and implement infrastructure and database solutions in collaboration with the development team
Monitor and maintain the availability, reliability and scalability of our systems and applications
Develop and maintain automation scripts for deployment and infrastructure management
Manage and optimize databases, including schema design, query optimization and performance tuning
Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to infrastructure, applications and databases
Generate ad hoc reports for our customer support and daily operations team using MySQL/MariaDB
Manage and optimize our infrastructure, including server administration, network management and security
Work with the development team to improve system and application performance
Continuously improve our systems and processes through automation and monitoring
Your essential experience and education
Several years of experience as a Site Reliability Engineer, Database Administrator, Database Developer, or Infrastructure Engineer
Strong understanding of Linux and Windows systems administration and networking concepts
Experience with cloud computing platforms such as AWS, Azure, or GCP
Proficiency in at least one scripting language such as Python, Bash or PowerShell
Profound knowledge of database design and administration, like MySQL, PostgreSQL or SQL Server
Strong understanding of application development and deployment processes, including .NET Core and Windows Server
Excellent communication and collaboration skills in English
Ability to work independently and as part of a team
What's in it for you?
We work together in a highly motivated team which is responsible for the fantastic successes of our marktguru platforms in Austria and Germany
We believe in straightforward cooperation that allows everyone to contribute their strengths in the best possible way
We are an innovative, and successful company in a growth- and future-oriented industry
A flat hierarchy
Central office in the heart of munich or vienna
Flexible remote work arrangements
