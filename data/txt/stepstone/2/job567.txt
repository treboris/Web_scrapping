For our location in Regensburg we are looking for a full-time (40h / week) position for the next possible date:
Software Developer (f/m/d)
Reference code: 500-02-23
At INSYS test we build test systems which match our customer specific requirements.
To do so, we can either use our own technologies to build a customised test bench or integrate together purchased equipment with customized software (or mix of both).
Example of the latest test systems developed are Life Time Tester, Electrical Tester for LV124 norm, High Voltage tester and many more.
Your tasks – diverse and varied:
New and further development of the existing tools and customer software
Integration of interfaces for automotive bus systems and measurement data acquisition
Working in a structured software development including version management and programming guidelines
Start up for specific projects and validating of the quality
Responsibility to get and set up IT components
Coordination with customers on system integration
Documentation of all modules and software structures
Familiarization with new systems and techniques
We expect from you - enthusiasm and passion:
Bachelor’s degree in computer science or comparable training
Several years experience in C# required
Good knowledge of GUI design with WPF
Knowledge of electronic circuit technology would be an advantage
Independent and structured working style
Open to learn new technologies and methods
Communication skills, openness, initiative and flexibility
Very good knowledge of English, good knowledge of German is an advantage
We offer you - fair pay and trust:
Flat hierarchies
Permanent employment
Flexible working hours
Mobile work
Dynamic team
Corporate events
Mobility offers
Employer-funded pension
We look forward to receiving your application!
