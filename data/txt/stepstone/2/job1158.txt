Olympus is passionate about creating customer-driven solutions for the medical, life sciences and industrial equipment industries. For more than 100 years, Olympus has focused on making people’s lives healthier, safer and more fulfilling by helping to detect, prevent and treat disease, furthering scientific research and ensuring public safety. Olympus is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, with more than 30,000 employees worldwide in nearly 40 countries. Olympus Europa, headquartered in Hamburg, Germany, serves the EMEA (Europe, Middle East & Africa) region and employs more than 7,800 people in 21 countries. Apply now!
Your Responsibilities
Lead, motivate and develop the EMEA SCM MDM team
Implement global, regional and local processes and procedures in EMEA, following guidance of the newly defined global MDM organization
Lead MDM workstreams in projects, e.g., ERP roll-outs, data catalogue implementation or a global master data governance system
Functionally own master data applications and MDM business architecture in the EMEA headquarters
Continuously improve our applications to fit new regulatory and market requirements and strive for automatization and efficiency
Own functional authorization roles for MDM-related applications
Work closely with the Global Master Data Organization, the Operational Business Service team and many other departments such as product management, regulatory and planning
Act as the central communication and escalation point for MDM topics in EMEA
As a member of the Olympus data community, participate in joint projects and initiatives, such as increasing data literacy and striving for FAIR (find, access, interoperate, reuse) master data
Your Qualifications
Degree in Business Informatics, Business Administration, Economics, Computer Science or a comparable field of study and long-standing relevant professional experience
In-depth understanding of requirements of master data as well its impact on business processes and digitalization
Good knowledge in master-data-relevant SAP areas like SAP MM, SD, MDG or comparable applications and understanding of end-to-end data flows
Knowledge about master data governance and related tools, e.g. data catalogues
Experience from project management and operational activities with a focus on master data management
Experience in leading teams, e.g., as disciplinary lead, as functional lead or in mid to large cross-functional project environments
Experience in business consultancy to create value for internal customers
Very good command of English and German, both written and spoken
Efficient communication on all hierarchy levels, in complex and multinational teams
Proactive mindset and good organizational skills with a drive for results, hands-on mentality and attention to detail
Your Competencies
The interview will be competence-based and the following competencies will be assessed:
Build for the future
Deliver results
Lead people
Lead self
Model and champion our core values
