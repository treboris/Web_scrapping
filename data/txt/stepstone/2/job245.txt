Vitesco Technologies is a leading international developer and manufacturer of state-of-the-art powertrain technologies for sustainable mobility. With smart system solutions and components for electric, hybrid and internal combustion vehicles, Vitesco Technologies makes mobility clean, efficient and affordable. The product range includes electric drives, electronic control units, sensors and actuators, and exhaust-gas aftertreatment solutions. In 2020, Vitesco Technologies recorded sales of EUR 8 billion and employs almost 40,000 people at around 50 locations worldwide. Vitesco Technologies is headquartered in Regensburg, Germany.
Your tasks
As Vitesco SAP Technical Solutions Architect (m/f/d) you will be the Expert for all aspects related to the enterprise-wide technical SAP Architecture as well as for technical SAP platform integration standards. As main responsible for that area you define and overlook the entire technical SAP environment for both, from an architectural as well as from a strategic standpoint. You will play an integral role regarding our Vitesco SAP landscape transformation to Cloud and S/4 HANA.
Your new responsibilities:
Holistically design and drive the technical transformation of our SAP backend environment focusing Cloud, S/4 HANA and SAP Business Technology Platform
Continuously assess and adapt innovative SAP IT architectures as well as state of the art technologies by considering Cybersecurity standards and other important IT dependencies
Technology Architect and advisor for SAP IT projects with regards to technical architecture and technical integration needs
Guide and consult Vitesco IT SAP community by defining and establishing technical architecture guidelines, procedures and standards
Orchestrate and lead Proof of Concepts with different partners as well as internal IT teams to support decision making on upcoming demands and business cases
Represent SAP IT Basis Team in relevant Vitesco decision bodies or councils to ensure technical governance
Present solution proposals to Vitesco IT Management
Support security audits and analysis of existing procedures
Operationally support SAP Backend and SAP Basis services delivered to Vitesco Business
Collaborate with SAP Application teams, other Vitesco IT teams and involved partners to ensure understanding of our technical SAP transformation approach
Your profile
Academic degree in Information Technology or comparable qualification
Long-term experience in designing technical architectures for complex IT landscapes integrating cloud services or multiple Hyperscaler (preferably in the area of SAP)
Long-term experience in various IT domains like IT Infrastructure, Network, Virtualization technologies, Cybersecurity or Cloud (MS Azure and AWS)
Profound knowledge of SAP Backend operations and service delivery from a technical perspective (SAP Basis) desirable
Very good knowledge of Cybersecurity standards, how to apply these standards and the terminology in general
Excellent presentation skills
Fluent in German and English (written and spoken)
Strong attitude to lead change
Eagerness to proactively challenge conventional practices and result-driven approach
Planning and organizational skills, high level of dedication and flexibility
Willingness to travel (5-10%)
Applications from severely handicapped people are welcome.
