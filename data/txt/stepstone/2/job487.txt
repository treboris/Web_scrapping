Your role:
Dassault Systèmes is the second largest software manufacturer in Europe and has been a dynamically growing, innovative company since 1981.
We are currently hiring for an exciting opportunity as a Senior 3D Unreal Artist (m/f/d). You will possess a highly technical and visual background in Unreal Engine and others appropriate to your craft. You will be a key member of our visual direction team that will lead the transition into Unreal Engine for our creative studio and create the next level of interactivity for our automotive customers.
Your responsibilities:
Define and develop new visual standards and push quality outputs in Unreal Engine
Act as lead in a specified area of expertise: Real time 3D production workflows, lighting, shading, rendering
Actively contribute to production team knowledge center or job aids that supports transition into Unreal Engine
Lead development support for tools, software testing, and pipeline documentation
Define, follow, augment and direct production procedures and lead local effort for future innovation
Determine overall training needs for staff and ensure staff members receive timely and appropriate training and development
Your qualifications:
At least 6 years of relating experience
Must have proficient experience in Unreal Engine
Experience running a production unit or leading cross functional teams with responsibility of delivering projects is required
Must possess an exceptional portfolio you can't wait to share with us
Requires expert level knowledge of experience in a CGI production environment
Previous automotive, professional game, entertainment and visual effects experience is preferred
What's in it for you?
In this role you can improve yourself by:
Taking over responsibilities as project lead
Guiding and leading other artists with your expertise
Organizing and executing trainings for colleagues
Evolving into a technical director and driving pipeline development
