About MED-EL
MED-EL is a leading manufacturer of innovative medical devices for the treatment of various types and degrees of hearing loss. Our unique portfolio of implantable hearing solutions benefits thousands of individuals in more than 130 countries worldwide. With headquarters in Innsbruck, Austria, MED-EL has over 2,500 employees around the world.

Your Tasks
Manage the automated installation and configuration of our endpoints for colleagues around the globe via Intune and SCCM
Manage VDI environments based on VMware Horizon (on-premises) and Azure Virtual Desktop (Cloud) to stream business applications and desktops to our colleagues around the globe
Implement controls and automation to ensure the reliability, security and compliance of our digital workplace
Monitor the digital user experience and take proactive actions to improve it
Contribute your ideas and take responsibility for the implementation of cloud deployments in coordination with the IT cloud architect
Your Profile
Passion for IT, innovation and innovative technologies should be part of your DNA
At least two years of experience with Intune or SCCM
Practical knowledge of Windows client operating systems, knowledge for Apple Mac OS X or Ubuntu is a plus
Motivation to improve own skills and drive change to stay innovative and provide our colleagues the greatest user experience possible
Excellent command of written and spoken English, German is a plus
