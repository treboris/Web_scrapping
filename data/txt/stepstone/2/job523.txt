Contract type: Permanent
Working time: Full-time
What you will do
Build manager & run Self Service Reporting with KPI’s governance community globally
Perform Webinar, training along with BI key users in our business units to drive reporting and visualization as a service
Manage Global demands for reporting and visualization  
Developing, communicating and tracking the KPI definitions in the area of responsibility and thus ensuring our BI governance
Coordination and execution of preliminary studies and requirements analyses for analytics projects
Support in larger implementation projects, if necessary also in project management functions
Technical optimization and further development of our frontend solutions
What you will need
Completed studies in business administration, business informatics and at least 3 years of experience
Advanced knowledge/ hands-on of analytics frontend solutions (SAP Lumira, SAP Analysis for Office, SAP Analytics Cloud, Power BI)
Relevant professional experience (IT or specialist area with a strong focus on Business Intelligence and Advances Analytics)
Knowledge of common reporting requirements and control mechanisms in various LOB’s
Knowledge of SAP BI solutions (BW on HANA or BW/4HANA) besides hands-on experience is a plus
Proven experience from projects or operational activities with a focus on analytics, including demand management, functional specification and implementation
Strong analytical and conceptual skills as well as a high level of initiative, assertiveness and creative drive experience in the cooperation of virtual, often international, (project) teams as well as stakeholder management
Strong communication and presentation skills
Very good written and spoken German and English skills
What you can expect
tesa is one of the world's leading manufacturers of adhesive tape solutions, with a range of more than 7,000 products.  With passion for technology, we drive innovation, reliability, and sustainability to shape a better future.
Our solutions make a difference in the work, products, and lives of our customers across a variety of industries, including Automotive, Electronics, and Home applications. With this, we address a broad segment of business customers, but also you as a private consumer.
You might be surprised to discover the many places where our adhesive solutions are at work, from the battery in your electric car, to the display of your mobile phone, and even in the elevator in your building. Wherever you look, you'll find tesa - holding the world together since more than 125 years.
