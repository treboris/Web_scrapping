Welcome to Bosch
Do you want beneficial technologies being shaped by your ideas? Whether in the areas of mobility solutions, consumer goods, industrial technology or energy and building technology - with us, you will have the chance to improve quality of life all across the globe. Welcome to Bosch.
The Robert Bosch GmbH is looking forward to your application!
Employment type: Unlimited
Working hours: Full-Time

Your tasks
We are Bosch Digital. Our mission is to shape the digital future of Bosch together with our business divisions. We invent, code and create digital products, services and solutions that spark enthusiasm and improve quality of life. Bosch Digital is home to more than 10,000 unique and diverse talents around the world. Our agile mindset and working culture are a key driver of digital transformation at Bosch and gives both our associates and their ideas room to grow.
As an IT System Engineer you will be a part of our Output Management team taking responsibility for design and development of existing and new solutions.
In our team you will lead and develop the cloudification of output management.
Shape the future of output management solutions and bringing up your ideas.
Last but not least, you take into account new requirements for our services and ensure the smooth operation of our existing system solutions.
Your profile
Education: completed university studies (bachelors`s degree) in Computer Science, Information Technology, Business Informatics or a comparable subject
Personality: team-oriented, flexible and good at communication
Working Practice: analytical, structured, independent
Experience: first experience in software development, preferably in C# and in Linux advantageous
Languages: good communication skills in written and spoken English as well as German
