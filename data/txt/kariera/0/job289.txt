Dynamics 365 Developer for unique projects 
Are you ready for a change?
Do you have experience in Microsoft technologies?
If so, our Microsoft team is waiting for you! We are looking for developers with a primary focus on MS Dynamics 365. In our fast-growing team, you can work on different worldwide projects, try all Microsoft technologies, or improve your current skills while doing various MS certifications. 
We are ready to provide you supportive and motivating working environment and gratify you with our benefits!
The position is located in Bratislava, Kosice, or Banska Bystrica.
What would be your responsibilities?
    •Requirements gathering and analysis
    •Configuration and development
    •Testing and deployment
    •Supporting and maintenance
    •Active communication with the customers, developers and other service delivery teams in order to perform regular operations tasks as well as solve
    •The application issues
What do you need for your success?
    •Hands-on experience with Dynamics CRM or AX or Dynamics 365 from commercial projects
    •Advanced knowledge of the Dynamics CRM or AX or Dynamics 365 platform and related technologies
    •Knowledge of Microsoft development stack
    •Experience in programming at least 3 years (C#, .NET)
    •Proficient in English, German is nice to have
    •Strong communicator, ability to provide technical advice to client and understanding the needs of stakeholders of a regular IT project such as architects,
    •Project managers, business users and operational staff
