Salesforce Marketing Cloud - Marketing Campaign Executioner
We are looking for a new colleague who will join our Client Innovation Center in brand new role of Marketing Campaign Executioner/Business Analyst. S/he will be client facing and will execute marketing campaigns with end users. This includes review of campaign brief provided by end users, analysis of campaign feasibility based on data availability, testing of HTML templates rendering, running & monitoring of campaigns, creation of reports. This position is suitable also for graduates who are interested in the combination of IT world and marketing.
You can work on this position from Bratislava/Banska Bystrica/Kosice.
What do you need for your success? 
Preferred skills:
 Excellent communication in English, and experience in working in international environment in a client facing role is a plus
• Experience in campaign execution role, hands on experience of a tool used to run marketing campaign (such as Salesforce Marketing Cloud / IBM Unica / IBM Watson Campaign Automation and others) is a plus
• Strong analytical abilities to analyze customer data and determine if campaign brief provided will be enhancing customer experience
• Understanding of relational databases architecture
• Knowledge of HTML/CSS is a plus
