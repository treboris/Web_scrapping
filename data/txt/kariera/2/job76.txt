Test Automation SpecialistIBM06.04.2023Job informationPlace of work
Bratislava
Bratislava, SlovakiaPosition / industry
Information Technologies - Software; IT testerJob description, information about job
As a Test Specialist at IBM, your analytical and technical skills will directly impact the quality of the software we create. Come work in an agile environment where you will help each iteration reach the next level. Whether the testing is manual, automated, or cognitive, you hold a key role in releasing the best deliverables to IBM’ers and our clients.
Your Role and Responsibilities: 
• Automation Tester with scripting languages knowledge who will be working on
new development and maintenance of already existing applications
• Automation Tester writing tests using various software-testing frameworks (e.g.
Cucumber, Selenium)
Our team is a balanced mixture of younger and experienced IT Specialists with a
national and international background. Senior team members are prepared to share
their knowledge and experience with you, and to teach you how you can work and
think from a pragmatic and result-oriented view. The ideal candidate must display
good written and oral skills with demonstrated interpersonal and organization
abilities and must be able to work in a varied, fast paced environment.
You can work from Bratislava, Kosice or Banska Bystrica
What would be your responsibilities?
The individual will be responsible for implementing and maintaining automation test
scripts of software components and interfaces. Have a broad understanding of
various coding methodologies, testing frameworks, tools and know when to apply
each method to achieve the desired outcomes.
Required skills:
 Multiple years of testing automation experience (hands-on)
 Ability to create test scenarios
 Basic knowledge of Java programming language for test script creation in
Selenium WebDriver
 Performance testing experience and tool knowledge (e.g. JMeter)
 Self-motivated, detail oriented and organized, team player
 Good communication , organizational, and presentation skills
Frameworks / Tools / Programming languages:
 JUnit, Jest, Cypress, Cucumber/ Gherkin, JMeter, SoapUI
 NodeJS or Java experience (not required to be proficient)
 TypeScript and JavaScript
Nice to have skills:
 Basic understanding of DevOps concepts
 Basic knowledge of AWS or Red hat OpenShift
 Basic knowledge of Containers, Kubernetes
 Jira / XRay
 German languageOffered salary
