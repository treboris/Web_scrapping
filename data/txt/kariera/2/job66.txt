SAP Consultant
Join IBM as SAP Consultant for the Global Business Services unit. We are looking for people with SAP modules knowledge, willing to participate on international implementation projects and maintenance support in near region. This role assists clients in the selection, implementation, and production support of application packaged solutions  in order to achieve client expected business results.
Position is located in Bratislava, Banska Bystrica, and Kosice.
What would be your responsibilities?
• Analysing and mapping clients’ business requirements, processes and objectives.
• Providing functional expertise, guidance, presentation, and instruction on SAP products to clients.
• Implementation or maintenance of SAP installations at international customers.
• Developing necessary product modifications to satisfy clients’ needs.
• Acting as professional team member in an international team and builds strong liaison with client for troubleshooting.
• Assisting in internal activities - remote consulting, preparing training materials, teaching on-site courses.
• Developing own professional learning of SAP products and technologies.
What do you need for your success?
• Experience or knowledge of SAP PP/ BOBJ / BW/BI / SD / (e)WM / FI/CO/ HR (SuccessFactors) / MM / APO / TM / ABAP / Fiori
• English language, German is a big plus
• Ability to communicate in a fluent manner with internal and external stakeholders
• Good analytical skills and attitude to make a difference
• Willingness to learn new technical skills
• Willingness to travel
What can you expect of us?

• Performance evaluation on regular basis
