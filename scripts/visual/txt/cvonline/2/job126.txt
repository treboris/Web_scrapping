Security Officer 
Duties and Responsibilities: 
Information Security System and full compliancy:  
Development. implementation and execution of the information security system: policy, strategy and roadmaps, with an initial focus on PCI/PA DSS compliance and PIN based security
Meanwhile implement and maintain the company security framework achieve and maintain ISO 270001 certification.
Ensure Radar Payment fits E2E into their customer’s financial compliancy and security requirements. Achieve certifications like ISAE 3402 in case it is required from customer side
Develop, Ensure and maintain all necessary structure and content of documentation and establish compliancy base lines for the organization
Interact with any inspecting authorities.
Ensure security awareness for the employees
Organize, keep and manage the trainings to be able to maintain the security system on all corporate level 
Secure business acquiring and implementation:
Development of technical (functional) specification to ensure effective security defence on Radar Payments implementations
Execution preventive measures to comply with information security;
Check and sign off all FSD documents to ensure customer solution will comply with all necessary security policies above
Conclusion and management of contracts with partners
Secure a compliant daily operation:  
Monitoring and verification of works performed by partners; Establishment of rules and guidelines aimed at compliance with information security and monitoring of their implementation; Monitoring, analyses and follow-up of information security incidents;
Develop and maintain appropriate reporting system on security incidents both internally and customers direction Ensure secured environment: Managing activities such as information security risk assessments, vulnerability assessments, penetration tests, etc
Operational management of users and their access privileges in all systems including Operating Systems, Networks, Databases and SV application systems
Operational management of corporate HSM systems, proper key management 
SSL certifications generation
