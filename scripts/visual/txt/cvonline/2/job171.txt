Pozíció leírása / Job description
Our client is a prestigious German automotive client for whom we are looking for an IT business analyst for their Budapest business center to take part in international IT projects.
-Develop new IT products, processes or standards accordingly to the organizations business strategies and consistent with the overall design of the organization’s information systems arhitecture with a direct impact on business unit/function overall results
-Responsible (operational or as project lead/management role) for the implementation and/or rollout of central/regional IT products, projects/processes or standards and integration with local/regional IT systems landscape
-Act as an IT business analyst and consultant during entire IT project(s) lifecycle, gaining the trust and engagement of the parties involved (internal or external) in order to accept and adopt new products, concepts, practices and approaches
-Apply advanced/well developed knowledge of IT job area and broad knowledge of other different job areas in order to analyse complex issues and propose and/or implement methods designed to improve as-is products and processes.
-Provide input into new products, processes or standards and mayconceptualize truly new products and/or processes.
-Share knowledge of practices and procedures in order to train and on-board new team/project members; lead internal or external team(s)/project members for specific purposes.
-Closely collaborate with CEE/other or HQ counterparts.
-May lead different projects within the CEE cluster or region.
