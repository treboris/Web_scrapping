Pozíció leírása / Job description
We are seeking an experienced IT Engineer to join our client being engaged in the development of new bio-based technologies. The IT Engineer will be responsible for designing, implementing and maintaining the organization's IT systems and infrastructure, both locally and globally. The successful candidate will be a subject matter expert in IT and will have extensive knowledge of network security, data backup and recovery, cloud computing, virtualization and data architectures.
Design, deploy and manage IT systems and infrastructure, data-related technologies, including hardware, software, and network components
Develop and maintain IT policies and procedures
Monitor and maintain the organization's IT security systems, including firewalls, antivirus software, and intrusion detection systems
Manage data backup and recovery systems to ensure the organization's data is safe and secure
Evaluate and recommend new technologies to improve the organization's IT systems and infrastructure
Lead IT projects from conception to completion, ensuring they are completed on time and within budget
Provide technical support to end-users and troubleshoot any IT-related issues that arise
Train end-users on new IT systems and technologies
Manage vendor relationships and negotiate contracts with third-party IT service providers
Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements related to IT systems and infrastructure
                Elvárások / Requirements
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or a related field
Minimum 5 years of experience in IT infrastructure engineering or a similar role
Strong knowledge of network security, data backup and recovery, cloud computing, virtualization, and data architectures 
Experience with data engineering tools and technologies, such as data warehousing, data integration, data pipelines, ETL, and big data platforms is an advantage
Experience with Microsoft Windows Server and Active Directory
Fluency in English
Ability to lead and manage IT projects
Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
