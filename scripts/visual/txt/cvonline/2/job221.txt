Your new role
Your responsibility will be to administer the various components of the currently used  power-plant automation systems. You will also ensure the functionality the availability of the installed equipment
The execution of installations, updates, upgrades, increments and migrations for our technical control system both in-house and on-site will be included in your duties
Independently you will perform preventive and corrective service assignments for these control systems at our customer sites
You will analyze system incidents via remote access and directly on-site and make all necessary corrections
Your area of responsibility will be rounded off by providing client counselling and customer-specific consulting
What you'll need to succeed
Ideally you already have previous professional experience in the field of power-plant automation systems.
You possess basic knowledge of plant technology and automation technology
You have a detailed knowledge in the area of stand-alone network systems with the associated components is an advantage
Fluent German and intermediate English language skills are mandatory
You are able to work also in teams and individually (depending on the project size)
You have willingness to travel to Germany (up to 50%) and you are able to work remotely the remaining time – you will work on projects which are geographically situated in the Bavarian region
What you'll get in return
Opportunity to have an active engineering role on future handling complex projects with a high digitalization level
Working with an international teams and business environments
Excellent opportunity for professional development on a new tailored organization their business
Competitive benefit package (basic salary, bonus, daily allowance for travelling periods)
