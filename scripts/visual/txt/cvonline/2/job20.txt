Change manager 
We are looking for a Change Manager in one of Europe’s biggest manufacturing centers, who will be responsible for the coordination and transformation activities, which are triggered by the ongoing implementation of a new ERP system.
As a Change Manager you will be responsible for...
Analysing the nature and the impact of the system change
Preparing the organisation for the upcoming changes beyond the implementation of the new system
Organising information shares and system onboarding
Creating communication plans and ensuring the transparency
Supporting team dynamics and motivation
Supporting the company's cultural evaluation with communicating, enablement and monitoring
Leading the improvement and transformation measures related to change management
What you will need to succeed?
Several years of experience in change management
Good communication skills
Proficiency in English both in written and spoken
Strong organisational skills
Experience in system related implementation projects such as ERP, MES, APS
It would be nice if you had...
Coaching background
Change manager experience in manufacturing background
Knowledge of agile software development technologies
