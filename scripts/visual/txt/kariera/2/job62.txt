As a Package Consultant at IBM, get ready to tackle numerous mission-critical company directives. Our team takes on the challenge of designing, developing and re-engineering highly complex application components and integrating software packages using various tools. You will use a mix of consultative skills, business knowledge, and technical expertise to effectively integrate packaged technology into our clients' business environment and achieve business results.
Your Role and Responsibilities :
Join IBM as Siebel Consultant for the IBM Client Innovation Center! This role assists clients in the selection, implementation, and production support of application packaged solutions in order to achieve client expected business results.
 What would be your responsibilities? 
Supporting international teams in the Design-/Build-/Test-/Run-phases of large customers projects
Delivering services remotely from Bratislava as well as on client site
 What do you need for your success? 
3+ years experience with programming in business/web/DB applications 
Siebel 8.x / 7.x experience in one or more of: Quote and Order Management, Marketing and Loyalty, Business Intelligence and Analytics, - Customer Data Integration, Self Service, Sales and Call Centre
Functional analysis & design experience including business process and applications configuration design
eScript, Workflow, Test Automation, Migrations and Upgrades, Server Administration and Deployment, integration architecture development (including batch, synchronous and asynchronous procedures)
Ability to work creatively and analytically in a problem-solving environment
Strong industry knowledge; client facing experience
Ability to absorb professional knowledge quickly and develops skillsOffered salary
