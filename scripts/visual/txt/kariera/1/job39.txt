Azure Developer to Microsoft team
Are you interested in Microsoft world and are you a developer experienced in C# .Net who is looking for new challenges? Then IBM offers you a position of Azure Developer on which you will work on the design and development of software solutions based on the client’s requirements. You will work on international projects focused on “cutting-edge” Microsoft technologies within MS Azure. Moreover, we offer you a motivating working environment as well as continuing growth in Microsoft Azure and other Microsoft technologies!
 The position is located in Bratislava, Kosice and Banska Bystrica.
What would be your responsibilities?
    • Delivery of Azure based technical solution (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS)
    • Participating on requirement gathering, analysis and technical design 
    • Active communication with the customers, developers and other service delivery teams in order to perform regular operations tasks as well as solve the application issues
What do you need for your success?
    •  Hands-on experience with Azure from commercial projects (or other cloud technologies e.g. AWS)
    •  Knowledge of Microsoft development stack, design patterns
    •  Proficient in English
    •  Strong communicator, ability to provide technical advice to client and understanding the needs of stakeholders of a regular IT project such as architects,
    • Project managers, business users and operational staff
Nice to have:
   •  Good understanding of Azure infrastructure architecture, certification is an advantage
   •  Experience with development of various Azure PaaS services and their publishing and monitoring in Azure portal 
   •  German language
What can you expect of us?
    •  Salary starting from 2200 - 4000 EUR gross, depending on relevant experience and language proficiency
    •  Additional Days Off
    •  Flexible Working Conditions
    •  100% Paid Sick Leave
    •  Critical Illness Insurance, Life & Disability insurance, Medical Center
    •  Personal & Career Development
