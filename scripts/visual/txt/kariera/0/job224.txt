Your Role and Responsibilities :
You will be responsible for integration of DevSecOps practices into the projects and across the various platforms. 
You will support the cloud transformation and cloud development initiatives, following the best practices
You will be implementing the automation processes based on the DevSecOps philosophy
You will be responsible for application of security principles based on DevSecOps practices.
Required Professional and Technical Expertise :
Solid experience and high expertise in Cloud development, operation and maintenance (one of AWS, Azure, Google, IBM Cloud)
Solid experience in Container technologies, Docker, Kubernetes
Experience in DevSecOps practices, processes, and technologies
Experience in continuous integration and continuous delivery concepts and tools
Knowledge of professional software engineering best practices for the full software development life cycle, including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing, and operations 
Experience in software automation, debugging and performance tuning
Experience in software development or scripting
Overview of application security, security hardening and applicable policies
Preferred Professional and Technical Expertise : 
Demonstrated experience in cloud transformation
Demonstrated experience in OpenStack or OpenShift or Kubernetes
Demonstrated experience in Agile approach
Demonstrated experience in Microservices model, Serverless architecture and Domain driven architecture.
Demonstrated experience in continuous testing and testing automation
Demonstrated experience in security and networking
Demonstrated experience in System Operation
