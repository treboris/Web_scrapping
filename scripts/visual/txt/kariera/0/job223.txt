Your Role and Responsibilities :
•          You will be responsible for design and implementation of the cloud-native software that solves actual business problems using cloud technology
•          You will develop workflows and process following the best practices and cloud architecture
•          You will support monitoring and maintenance activities
•          You will document developed solutions
Required Professional and Technical Expertise :
•          Hands-on experience with software development and Cloud services
•          Good knowledge and experience with development in at least one of the following cloud: AWS, Google Cloud, Azure
•          Knowledge of Object-Oriented Programming and Design Patterns
•          Knowledge of Microservice architecture and Serverless solutions
•          Knowledge of container technologies (Docker, Kubernetes, OpenShift)
Preferred Professional and Technical Expertise :
•          Knowledge of Java 8 (or above), Python, NodeJS
•          Knowledge of Spring Framework, Spring Boot, Hibernate/JPA
•          Knowledge of RDBMS (Oracle, MySQL or PostgreSQL) and/or NoSQL Databases (e.g. MongoDB)
•          Knowledge of network communication/integration solutions (SOAP, REST API, JSON)
•          Basic knowledge of Front-end Frameworks (Angular, React or Vue.js)
•          Basic knowledge of MQ frameworks, Event Streaming platforms (Kafka)
•          Basic knowledge of CI/CD concept and tools (e.g. Jenkins)
•          Basic Knowledge of Git, Maven/Gradle
•          Linux OS background
•          Experience with Agile/DevOps
