Software Test Engineer
Your new role
Participate in an international team rolling out a manufacturing execution system globally
Identify, design and document test cases based on user stories and development team requirements
Strong collaboration with product owner and development team
Support QA activities by automated test data generation
Maintain and update test documentation
Provide input about test coverage, propose test cases
Support root-cause analysis of bugs
Build CT pipelines, automate test reporting
Create and share test reports with Product Owners and development team
What you'll need to succeed
Profound education in Computer Science, Information Technology or Information Systems (Master, Bachelor or comparable) or equivalent working experience
Experience in at least one object-oriented programming language (preferably Python)
Experience with Selenium test framework
Experience with Git pipelines is an advantage
Technical understanding of web applications, web services, REST services, and SQL queries
Experience in software testing and software UI and unit test automation
Fluent English in spoken and written
German or other foreign language is a plus
Willingness to travel
Personality: 
Good communication and problem-solving skills
Proactivity
Result oriented and practical thinking
Ability and willingness to learn new things quickly
Efficient, independent and team-oriented form of work
What you'll get in return
A modern office in the 11th district is waiting for you to support your creativity with fun community places, cafe rooms, an own cinema and a skybar
You can be part of a successful and growing company in the field of technology
We support you with flexible working hours and home office opportunity (60 % of the working time in many of our positions)
Competitive salary and wide range of other benefits (cafeteria & bonus) are provided to you
You can learn new languages in small groups, selecting from 5 languages
We organize leisure time activities (board games, sport activities)
