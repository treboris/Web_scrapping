Our plant built in 2017 operating with state-of-the-art machinery and modern work environment is responsible for manufacturing thyssenkrupp springs and stabilizers. Control System Engineer (Debrecen)
Tasks:
Obtaining orders and issuing invitations to tender
Comparing offers, initiating orders, setting and monitoring deadlines
Coordinating schedules between production and external suppliers
Supporting the planning activities for new plants, machines and production processes
Recognizing causes for malfunction, evaluating and fixing the malfunction and/or having it fixed
Proposing and implementing improvements
Start-up of new plants and equipment components
Design, planning and programming of complex control systems
Design of circuit diagrammes using CAD programmes
Ensuring the compliance with safety and environmental regulations
Keeping costs within the budget in the relevant area of responsibility
Responsible for order and cleanliness at the respective workplaces
Skills:
Engineering degree
At least 3 years relevant experience
Knowledge of different control systems (e g. S7, TIA, KRL)
Knowledge of servo drives
English knowledge
Computer and SAP skills
Willingness to standby
Success-oriented, independent thinking
Team-worker ability
Good communication and able to work on own initiative
Offers:
Competitive salary and other benefits
Participation in international projects
Successful and fast-growing company
