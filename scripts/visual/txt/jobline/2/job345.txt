Performance Analyst
Tasks:
ongoing analysis of returns of fixed income, multi asset, and equity funds
processing of data and data quality checks
participating and coordinating periodic and ad-hoc reports
working closely together with other teams within the company
improvement of processes and system landscape
strong collaboration with Austrian colleagues
Our market leader and innovator partner is looking for a Performance Analyst employee. If you have just finished your university studies and you are passionate about analyzing big data sets or you have 1-2 years of experience in performance analysis, we are looking forward to your application.
Skills:
bachelor’s degree in finance, business administration or mathematics
good analytical and IT skills (advanced Excel knowledge) 
ability to analyze large data sets
passionate about learning new technologies, and eager to delve into attribution and performance-related topics
intermediate knowledge of financial market instruments
fluent English knowledge
Advantages:
experience in Performance attribution
experience in the usage of Bloomberg 
German language knowledge
Offers:

competitive salary, and benefits (cafeteria, bonus, etc.)
laptop and phone
