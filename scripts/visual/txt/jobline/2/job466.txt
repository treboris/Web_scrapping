Junior Golang Developer
Our client is a global software engineering and IT outsourcing services supplier, looking for a Junior Golang Developer. You’ll be part of a team of professionals who are ready to find the best tailor-made IT solutions for their multinational clients in various industries and solve complex problems. You’ll create high-quality software products that move digital marketing business forward. Our client believes in agile methods, quick hypothesis testing, and shining eyes. They need you, a Golang developer, to help them make all of their plans come to fruition!
Pozíció leírása / Job description
Together with the mentor, a professional development plan will be developed specifically for you;
You will hone your skills in developing web applications and using modern frameworks;
Learn the intricacies of working in a team of responsive and professional colleagues;
You will improve your knowledge of English at an incredible pace;
You will have a chance to test your skills in a real project.
Elvárások / Requirements
Solid knowledge / strong interest in Golang and understanding of the concurrency model of the language;
Excellent knowledge of the HTTP protocol;
Knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript at a basic level and the pursuit of excellence;
Knowledge of SQL, experience with one of the relational databases;
Git skills;
Fluent English
Will be plus:
Knowledge of Vue, React, Angular;
Experience with NoSQL;
Experience with Docker.
Amit kínálunk / Offer
Stable long-term work environment
Competitive salary with a flexible system of revisions
Benefits and social packages
Comfortable A+ office in Budapest 
Every necessary tool and device in the office will be provided to comfortably perform all project tasks: computers, meeting rooms, spacious modern kitchens with professional coffee machines, comfortable recreation areas with game consoles, board games, and a selection of literature for every taste
Paid English courses and conversation clubs
Opportunities for professional and personal growth
Mentoring program, internal and external professional training programs
Support and care from our partner's friendly team
