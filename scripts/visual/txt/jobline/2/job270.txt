Let’s be successful together!
Our multinational Partner is looking for an experienced colleague for the position below:
Sales support engineer
Feladatok
Own the bookings forecast for the segment and ensure monthly delivery of the volume commitment, as well as execute immediate recovery actions if any risk is identified;
Proactively work with customers to increase digital integration;
Be fluent in the benefits of digital integration and articulate a clear action plan for implementation;
Act as single human point of contact to secure customer satisfaction during and after the transition;
Provide pre- and post-sales support and regular follow-up;
Engage with customers through online chat tools, e-commerce platforms and other digital means (including Teams and phones) to assist customers in product selection, application support, access to expertise and placement of orders;
Understand in full Emerson’s unique selling propositions and actively contextualize them to the customer industry needs to maximize business;
Monitor digital tools and CRM for leads. Proactively act on leads to convert potential business in the assigned segments;
Understand and use available data analytics and reports to optimize account growth strategies, including whitespace analysis, pricing actions and customer segmentation;
Actively coordinate with colleagues and orchestrate the support of subject matter experts, Product Managers or other company stakeholders to create a compelling and differentiating value proposition for the selected customers;
Monitor and understand the competitive environment at each account and work to displace competition and defend Emerson’s position;
Keep track and communicate customer activities to the wider organization through CRM;
Proactively document and share success cases, customer feedback and quantifiable business results in the assigned customer segment.
Elvárások
STEM degree or comparable technical education;
Demonstrated experience selling technologies to customers in the relevant industry;
Experience in engaging with customers through digital platforms and driving e-commerce plans;
Excellent communication and presentation skill both in English and local language.
Amit kínálunk
Yearly bonus, meal contribution;
Sport facilities;
Life and accident insurance;
Work-life-balance, with flexible working hours;
2 days a week home office option after the trial period;
