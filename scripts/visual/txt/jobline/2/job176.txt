Cisco Data 2nd Line Engineer
Your new role
Supporting and maintaining a global network for our dedicated customer 
Working closely with design architects (TDA), project managers and other technical resolver groups 
Supporting the 1st line engineers with technical information 
Solving underlying problems 
Supporting other engineering teams to find rootcause 
Willing to learn new concepts, new technologies, accepting challenges 
Home office is available in this role depending on further agreement 
What you'll need to succeed
Soft skills:
Communicating effectively and efficiently while adapting to your audience and getting the message through as intended 
Ability to comprehend and to oversee various aspects of a problem or a situation 
Focusing on all aspects and details of a task and delivering your output with a high level of accuracy 
Ability to find root causes of problems and to focus on their quick and efficient solution
Professional skills:
In depth understanding of LAN/WAN/WLAN/BGP/MPLS/OSPF/EIGRP/DMVPN/IPSEC technologies 
Advanced level of troubleshooting (end to end) 
CISCO CCNA, CCNP certification or equivalent knowledge 
Service desk experience is an advantage
Language skills:
English on a fluent level 
Any European language on a conversational level is an advantage
