Senior Frontend Engineer
About our Partner:
Our multinational partner, in order to become the most technology-driven airline in the world, is looking for a talented Senior Software Engineer.
Tasks:
As a Senior Frontend Engineer, you will be working in a challenging, dynamic Agile scrum environment where things can and will change on a day to day basis.
Furthermore you will: 
improve customer experience by building high end features;
help manage day to day releases;
work together with other front-end teams to keep the standards high;
work in close contact with Google Angular core team;
be a front runner in Angular stack, always on the latest version;
try out new technologies that add value to our product and customer experience.
Requirements:
Javascript ES2020-ES2021, TypeScript and Typings;
Agile development and Scrum;
Angular, GraphQL (Apollo), Node.js, RxJS;
DevOps & CI/CD in the Cloud (Azure);
Moreover:
responsive design, accessibility (WCAG) and automated testing are no-brainers to you;
And:
you are able to steer architectural decisions and technological innovation;
you have to ability to coach and steer more junior team members;
you have vision on quality and usability;
you are aware of latest trends in your field;
you can express your professional opinion well and also listen to the input of others
