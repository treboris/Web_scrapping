ServiceNow Developer (REF363C)
The role has opened due to ever growing international business demands. As a ServiceNow Developer your main responsibility is to implement the requirements & user stories in ServiceNow platform while ensuring that SN OOB recommendations and industry best practices are applied.
Tasks:
Creating and implementing end to end solutions for customers, addressing requirements through technical architecture in consideration of process design, ServiceNow Technical Best Practice and Standards
Defining and implementing technical solutions aligned with client’s business problems
Supporting the scoping of service engagements involving at least one ServiceNow product
Taking responsibility from simple to complex integration tasks regarding the interface creation with different external client applications/systems
Participating in Agile ceremonies, assessing and estimating requirements
Taking responsibility for coding, testing, debugging, and creating technical documentation
Understanding business needs to be able to offer the best technical solutions
Requirements:
1-2 years of experience in SW development (low-code / no code also works)
Long-term commitment and willingness to invest in learning and working with ServiceNow
Ability to work in an international environment with local and international teams
Understanding of service management concepts, data models, integration practices
Experience implementing solutions using the Agile/Scrum methodology
Experience designing and implementing IT and Non-IT related solutions
Experience with programming languages, databases and scripting e.g., SQL, PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JSON, Angular, AngularJS
Personal qualities: eagerness for self-development, strive for quality, problem solving skills, reliable and thorough personality and a good sense of humor would come in handy working with us, IT geeks
Strong oral and written communications skills (both business English and Hungarian)
Nice to have skills and capabilities
Working proficiency in German language
Good presentation skills
Experience with configuration and development of ServiceNow Platform
Knowledge of ServiceNow platform key capabilities and solutions, OOB features
ServiceNow certifications (CSA, CIS-ITSM, CAD or others)
Experience in interface design and implementation using web services (REST, SOAP, JSON)
ITIL knowledge and certifications (Foundation, Managing Professional
