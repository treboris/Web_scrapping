Security Architect
About our Partner:
They are a multinational company with a purpose to create financial security and stability for their clients and they are looking for a talented professional to join their open-minded and innovative Technology team as a Security Architect.
Responsibilities:
Setting information security expectations across the company;
Defining the principles of security architecture;
Design and maintain security frameworks (coding guide and privacy management by design);
Create a plan and strategy for a local information security architecture;
Conducting and managing the deployment of new security solutions.
Requirements:
Min. 5 years relevant exprience in IT/Information security, network security, cloud security, enpoint security, secure coding,
Up-to-date knowledge of information security framework
(OWASP, NIST800-53);
Knowledge of Cryptography;
Fluency in english.
Advantages:
Knowledge of MNB regulations;
Knowledge of risk management methodologies;
OSCP / Cloud Security / CSX-P / CISSP / CEH certification.
