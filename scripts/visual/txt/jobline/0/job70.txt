Lead Designer in Test & Operations automation (REF1534Q)
Tasks
In EMS Team of DTT we are continuing, expanding, and developing the first automation steps and adapting them in order to increase quality in validation, rollout and operation.
Collecting and understanding the client needs and translate them into technical language
Develop strategy, architecture, concept and design of automation (operations, tests of EMS of one of major network vendors + reusable for others)
Organize, structure, plan and prioritize their implementation steps
Create sustainable, maintainable and modular solution
The implementation and development should be actively accompanied, and these should also be used in parts, e.g. in the active participation in the test environment
Supplementation, expansion and automation of existing processes / mechanisms not automated by the manufacturer
Goals:
Increase quality in validation, rollout and operation
Shorten lead times
Improve software quality through higher test coverage
Increase transparency
Create reusable solution
Requirements
Ability to develop sustainable and well maintainable design approaches and criteria
Security-by-design - very strong awareness of data protection and security in general and, in particular, in the automation environment
Frameworks: Postman, Ansible, ReadyAPI, Behave, VS Code, etc
Programming languages YAML, Python, Gherkin, json, bash, etc.
Jira, Git
Linux
English and German knowledge communication level
Strong communication skills with a distributed team (in Germany) using various technical means, but also willingness to communicate personally with team members
Good to have:
Experience as “fixed access” network engineer, to have clear understanding of the subject area

Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs, Szeged
