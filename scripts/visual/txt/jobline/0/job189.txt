Testautomation Developer (REF1535D)
In EMS Team of DTT continuing, expanding, and developing the first automation steps and adapting them in order to increase quality in validation, rollout and operation. This primarily includes the areas of the interface to the supplier, test environments, and active operation.
Tasks
Develop and implement automation of tests and operations in close cooperation with team
Automation of existing processes / mechanisms not automated by the manufacturer
Create sustainable, maintainable and modular solution
Performing of developed automated tests
Analysis of test runs, reporting
Participation in administration of environments
Detailed development documentation
Goals:
Reduce repetitive activities
Increase quality in validation, rollout and operation
Improve software quality through higher test coverage
Doing the best to avoid failures
Requirements
Frameworks like Postman, Ansible, ReadyAPI, Behave, VS Code, etc
Programming languages YAML, Python, Gherkin, json, bash, and the like
Strong expertise in Linux
Jira, Git
Good communication skills with a distributed team (in Germany) using various technical means, but also willingness to communicate personally with team members
English communication level, German is a plus (any level + willingness to learn and use)
Good to have:
Experience as “fixed access” network engineer, to understand the subject area well
