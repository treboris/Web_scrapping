IT Business Analyst
Do you have a few years of experience as an SAP Business Analyst and would like to step forward and work on a complex, international project? If your answer is yes, we may have an offer for you.
Responsibilities:
Drive business process optimization with focus on improvements of the assigned applications
Create functional, non-functional and technical specifications together with business representatives
Define technical specifications in close cooperation with other IT Solutions
Develop the conceptual design and/or architecture of solutions supporting business processes in cooperation with the business representatives 
Provide customer care and ensure adequate quality of selected services 
Ensure the realization of solutions and perform quality assurance, including operational vendor management
Support project leaders in planning
Drive the integration within solutions to reduce complexity
Influence business and IT stakeholders to obtain confirmation/sign-off of new solutions and their implementation
Requirements:
5 years of IT work experience in an international matrix organization
Being a recognized expert in the assigned group of applications
In depth understanding of the organization, the work processes and the way systems and applications are used by the business partners
In depth understanding of the data bases of the related applications and their interconnectivity to the SAP ecosystem of the company
Strong experience in PP/PM/PS/MM/SD
Understands architectural issues, factors them into decisions and recommendations
Fluency in English
What our client offers:
Working from home opportunity 
Competitive compensation package, including bonus
Opportunities to develop within the company across various locations and roles
Workplace:
Budapest
