The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Cloud Software Architect for Microsoft Azure (REF1512U)
Your job
You love to own a problem and are creative in resolving it in a scalable way, collaborating with a talented, really nice and honest team of cloud engineers
You enjoy creating functional and technical designs for cloud operations at scale, breaking them down for implementation by cloud engineers. This includes taking technological, architectural, and design decisions, often jointly with product owner, developers, and other stakeholders
You participate in software design meetings, creating architectural plans, gathering requirements and exploring edge cases
You see documentation of your designs as a useful method so engineers know what to implement and so we can refer to them later
You create high and detailed level designs, establishing proof of concept environment
You develope manual and automated landscape deployments and validations, integrating with CI/CD pipelines
When designing you always have the complete customer experience and set of services in mind (no customer-specific solutions)
You are not afraid of starting from scratch, and you are also good at improving existing implementations
You have state-of-the-art knowledge in the software engineering process and are committed to further developing the quality of software development
You can get the team behind your ideas while being open for others’ ideas
You proactively drive the topics of the day, organise, and facilitate meetings
You see yourself at eye level with all team members and stakeholders, no matter who they are and where they come from, appreciating their respective expertise
You contribute to maintaining the product roadmap (customer-facing features as well as any technical debt)
You support our revenue contribution and OKR business goals
Your skills and profile
You are a perfect match if you love to dream big and take responsibility for making these dreams come true
You have an ownership mindset and a getting-things-done attitude
Systems thinking is your go-to approach
Microsoft Azure and Azure DevOps is your home turf
Deep understanding of the entire developement lifecycle (designing, developing, testing, deploying, monitoring)
Understanding of current software architecture and design patterns (clean architecture, microservices, etc)
You are really experienced in coding and scripting in PowerShell, Python and .NET
You value inner clarity as a means to make your point with confidence
Demonstrating knowledge by holding Proof Of Concepts, presenting on public events
You love to temporarily work directly with our customers
You are fluent in spoken and written English
Munkavégzés helye
Budapest, Debrecen, Szeged, Pécs
