Master data specialist - German-speaking
On behalf of our multinational partner, we are looking for a <strong>German-speaking Master Data Specialist</strong>! Do not hesitate to apply today and join a great team!

Being involved in the transition of master data processes from European entities to Hungary 
Being responsible for master data processing and management (Accounts Payable/ Accounts Receivable) 
Providing master data accuracy, driving MD clean-up projects
Managing review and execute change requests from customers, vendors and colleagues
Supporting master data projects

College or University degree or equivalent experience, preferably in accounting, business or IT related fields

Very good SAP knowledge
Strong English and at least intermediate German language skills 
Self-starting attitude and ability to work independently 
Strong team-player
