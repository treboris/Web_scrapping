Confident Role
We are looking for an IT Engineering Manager with the profile below.
Our IT Engineering team is responsible for the development, operation and support of a variety of internal tools, either developed in-house or third-party ones. Part of the team fulfils a global function and supports users globally. This part of the team is technically lead by managers in the parent company (based in Liechtenstein).
Roles and responsibilities:
- Line management of all members of the IT Engineering Team
- Technical leadership of the team
- Liaison with technical counterparts from the parent company based in Liechtenstein
- Own and develop the technical strategy of the team
- Project portfolio management
- Project management of high profile projects
- Development of the team
- Performance management of the team
- Regular reporting to the Head of IT
Must have requirements:
- Solid people management skills
- Performance management experience at team level and at individual level
- Project management experience
- Good communication skills with different (technical, non-technical) parties and at the right level of detail
- Ability to communicate a vision to the team
- General IT background
- Background both in software engineering and operational topics
- Exposure to Agile methodologies
- Experience with Linux (command line)
- Experience with relational databases
- Understanding how support organisations work
- Can do attitude
- Problem solving
- Can perform under pressure
- Good English (written and oral)
- University degree or equivalent
- Willingness to travel occasionally
Nice to have requirements:
- German language
- Work experience in an international environment
- Experience with CI/CD
- Experience with container orchestration
