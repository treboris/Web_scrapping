M365 Platform Management & Operations Expert
Tasks
Responsibility for the M365 evergreen process
Ensuring and developing a professional M365 license management and optimize usage via relevant up- and downgrades
Automation and optimization of M365 operating processes (PowerShell, PowerPlatform)
Advice to M365 service owners regarding overarching processes and regulations
Processing of M365 support tickets
Engage in the continuous Merger and Acquisition (M&A) projects to bundle and act as single point of contact for M365 relevant activities
Our client is a global technology company supplying systems for passenger cars, commercial vehicles and industrial technology, enabling the next generation of mobility. They allow vehicles to see, think and act. In the four technology domains of Vehicle Motion Control, Integrated Safety, Automated Driving, and Electric Mobility, the company offers comprehensive product and software solutions for established vehicle manufacturers and newly emerging transport and mobility service providers. Our client electrifies a wide range of vehicle types. With its products, the company contributes to reducing emissions, protecting the climate and enhancing safe mobility.
The new colleague will work in normal office hours (no shifts), and a very flexible HO policy is available with multiple options.
Requirements
Experience in the operation of cloud solutions (preferably M365)
Process automation skills and KPI / dashboard creation is an advantage (PowerShell, PowerApps, PowerBI)
Customer focus and trouble-shooting experience
Ability to work in an international and virtual team environment as well as independent work
Good level of English language knowledge
Offer
Stable company background and long-term working possibility
Full of challenges and high-level professional activities at an international company
Participation in international projects and commission to foreign countries
Career opportunities, continuous training program and language courses
Competitive salary and performance-related bonus
