Would you like to be a member of an international team of enthusiastic colleagues? We are looking for a Life-cycle Assesment Expert in Budapest for a market-leading automotive supplier company, engaged in producing manual and automated transmissions. If you want a position in hybrid work, please apply.
Sustainability Specialist - LCA Expert
Feladatok
Contribute with a team of passionate, committed co-worker, in reaching our environmental goals;
Ensure Transparency on environmental Claims Coordinate activities for enhancing environmental awareness and considerations across Division E functions;
Provide a holistic view on all relevant environmental impact categories Be Division E’s go to-LCA expert;
Estimate environmental impact of Division E products by conducting LCAs on a continuous basis;
Support Division E departments in evaluating and choosing materials and technologies based on environmental performance;
Guide in the evaluation and comparison of suppliers’ environmental claims;
Work closely with the internal and external stakeholders and partners to develop the LCA-methodology;
Deliver LCA communication, support and training across Division E and externally;
Secure compliance with standards within the area and monitor upcoming standards and regulations;
Develop internal processes and way of working with LCA;
Követelmények
Hold Master’s degree in Environmental engineering, science, sustainability field or closely related fields;
High knowledge in manufacturing, engineering and/or product development;
Several years of experience with LCA from industry – preferable automotive or as a consultant and experienced user of Gabi LCA software;
Advanced knowledge of life cycle inventory (LCI) data, collection process, and building of LCI blocks associated with specific technology for automotive systems;
Familiar with the most important LCA standards and guidelines, including ISO 14040/44, EU PEF;
Knowledge of Input/Output LCA modeling, mass, and energy balances, sustainable innovation, scope 3 GHG assessments, systems thinking, and/or circular economy;
Strong quantitative or technical background with an excellent aptitude for analytical thinking;
Natural curiosity to investigate issues, conduct research, design solutions, and translate results into business practices;
Knowledge and passion for sustainability, environmental systems, life cycle thinking, product sustainability, and climate change;
Fluent English language knowledge must have;
Amit kínálunk
Working with a very diverse and very motivated team
Stable company background and long-term working possibility;
Full of challenges and high-level professional activities at an international company;
Participation in international projects and commission to foreign countries;
Career opportunities, continuous training program and language courses;
Competitive salary and performance-related bonus;
Cafeteria with optional elements
