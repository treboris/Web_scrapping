About us
SiemensWe are a technology company focused on industry, infrastructure, transport, and healthcare. By combining the real and the digital worlds, we empower our customers to transform their industries and markets, helping them to transform the everyday for billions of people.Job description, responsibilities and dutiesAt Siemens we’re driving the energy transition. In the Grid Control segment, we’re mainly working on equipping control centers with a software bundle called Spectrum Power™.Beyond traditional grid operation, Spectrum Power™ solutions actively help grid operators manage, orchestrate, and optimize their transmission and distribution systems. Over 1,300 Spectrum Power™ systems in operation keep the lights on for hundreds of millions of people around the globe.https://new.siemens.com/global/en/products/energy/energy-automation-and-smart-grid/grid-control/transmission-management.htmlWe are looking for an Engineer  or Developer to join our team for energy market applications. The position will allow the successful candidate to bring (electrical) engineering knowhow together with software skills and work in a field with enormous impact on the future.Your new role in customer projects incl. requirement discussions with engineers from the customer side, implementation, testing, bug fixing, and project specific customizations.Responsible for the development of project specific enhancement which reach from small customizations to completely new features.Working on customer projects mainly in the Grid Control Hub for Europe, but possibly around the globe.In the future: possible development to project manager or technical expert.Your profile• Technical education  preferably in power engineering or computer science. Also other backgrounds are welcome, e.g.  electrical engineering, physics, mathematics, automation engineering, …• Experience with JAVA• Interest in one or more of the following topics:o energy markets & portfolio optimization, incl. renewable energy sourceso database design & optimization (SQL)o UX/UIo interfaces & integration of software solutions in customer environments• Analytical mind: Deciphering complex problems both in communication with customers on a technical level and in the engineering part of projects• Interest in software engineering and programming• Team player with good communication skills incl. intercultural communication• Advanced English language• Willingness to travel 5-10% of the time
