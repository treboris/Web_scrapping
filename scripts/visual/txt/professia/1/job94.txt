Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesAs a frontend engineer at Bitcoin Suisse in Bratislava, Slovakia you will play an important role in developing, testing and maintaining modern web app projects. You will be working in close collaboration with product owners, designers, solution architects and project managers. Our products vary from large digital platforms to the dynamic landscape of DeFi products. 
How we work
As a technological leader in the fast-paced crypto space, we are required to react quickly to the changing market demands. This is why we believe in agile project management methodologies. By continuously delivering small increments, we learn through the feedback we receive to optimize our products until they excel. While we are dynamic in spirit, we believe in our staff taking initiative to empowering themselves where they can drive impact rather than hierarchically assigned ownership. 
What will you be working with?
• Build and maintain modern web applications using TypeScript and React 
• Maintain and extend our internal libraries and tools 
• Help optimize our workflows and technology stack 
• Research and create prototypes to explore new ideas 
• Make sure things look right across various browsers and devices 
• Work together with our cross-functional team to define, design and deliver new features 
• Review code from other team members Employee perks, benefits• exciting projects in the crypto space
