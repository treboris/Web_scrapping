Our goal is to pay our employees fairly, about the market situation and we are ready to welcome high-quality candidates to our team.
Job description, responsibilities and dutiesThe software developer for this position The junior role requires at least one year experience with above mentioned technologies or diploma work in relevant field.We are looking for a young professional who can work with a range of tools necessary to deliver a state of the art application. The front end technologies are preferably HTML 5 and Angular. The back end will be created with C# communicating with MSSQL server database. The project shall deliver a modern application for world wide use and deployement. The app will control and display medical data gathered from diagnostic devices to users of Siemens-Healthineers medical systems.We use agile methods of software development and apply best engineering practices to create a high-quality product that helps to save lives around the globe.More about our projects can be found at https://www.siemens-healthineers.com/sk/kariera-v-dcWhat are we expecting?·        First experience in software engineering in C#, HTML5 and Angular·        Understanding and ideally experience in using and applying OOD, OOP, and ideally design patterns·        Understanding of the software development life cycle (SDLC) and engineering practices such as TDD/BDD, CI/CD, code review, etc.·        Understand and have working experience with the majority of the following tools:o   Visual Studioo   Windows virtual machineo   C#o   .NET Core FrameworkPersonality requirements and skills:·        Proactive and self-directed work style·        Good analytical and problem-solving skills·        Good aptitude for learning new technologies·        Ability to work in a team environmentBasic wage component (gross) and other rewards : starting from 2100EUR brutto /month + VAR*
