About us
SiemensWe are a technology company focused on industry, infrastructure, transport, and healthcare. By combining the real and the digital worlds, we empower our customers to transform their industries and markets, helping them to transform the everyday for billions of people.Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are looking for a senior full-stack developer for a project creating cloud-based solution for optimizing of costs related to maintenance of assets in buildings – offices, hospitals, hotel chains, etc. You will be working with cutting edge technologies in a scrum team which closely collaborates with teams in Switzerland and Germany. Our goal is to implement a highly responsive, scalable and secure solution with attractive look & feel which will be used across the world by our external customers – facility managers.https://youtu.be/lVOLLK3iew4At Siemens we are always challenging ourselves to build a better future. We need the most innovative and diverse Digital Minds to develop tomorrow‘s reality. Find out more about the Digital world of Siemens here: www.siemens.com/careers/digitalmindsIn this role you will be responsible for:• Analysis, design and implementation of front-end, backend and cloud infrastructure features• Implementation of unit tests for your code• Document your design and code• Participate on scrum events – daily stand-up, demo, retrospective, planning• Communicating with product management and team members from other teams about interfaces and dependencies• Coaching of junior team membersWe expect:• Experience in software development projects• Object oriented analysis, design and implementation• Knowledge of design patterns• Working experience with Java SE• GIT SCM (or similar source code management system)• User level of project tracking tool (Jazz, JIRA, …)• Experience and willingness to work in agile environment
