About the product
Digital Onboarding Toolkit (DOT) is a full technology stack for developing remote identity verification applications. Consisting of mobile, web and server components, DOT can authenticate and extract data from identity documents and verify identity of an individual using industry leading facial biometrics and liveness check. DOT helps organizations and their users create instant trust with each other.Advertiser Brief description of the companyInnovatrics is a Slovak company and a global leader in SW biometric technology and solutions for identity management. With our innovative and award-winning algorithms for fingerprint matching and facial recognition, we empower all types of organizations around the world to build powerful biometric identification solutions quickly and easily. Innovatrics has won awards IT company of the year 2015, IT product of the year 2016 and Diamond of Slovak business in 2017.
