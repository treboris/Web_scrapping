Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesLooking for a job of your dreams? QORPO Game Studio is the ultimate destination for talented people who want to bring their skills to the next level, all while working in a modern and friendly environment.
Our team of game developers, amazing artists and great marketers is passionate about creating immersive experiences that captivate players and keep them coming back for more. And with our state-of-the-art studio equipped with the latest technology, you'll have everything you need to bring your ideas to life.
QORPO is now looking for skilled front-end developer. 
What you will do:
- work on complex web applications supporting the whole ecosystem of a games development company
- participate in code reviews, standup meetings, sprint planning, and retrospectives
We are facing a variety of tasks on our daily basis that fall mostly into three categories - designing and developing new features, maintaining existing features in the underlying codebase and sometimes prototyping new features as POCs. As a medior/senior developer on our team, you are responsible for timely delivery with focus on code quality. All together in collaboration with a team of developers, testers and UI designers.
Our requirements: 
- 2+ years of proven experience in delivering quality websites
- knowledge of JavaScript frameworks such as Angular or React
- hands-on experience writing responsive layouts
- experience with subversioning
- written and verbal communication skills with the ability to clearly explain technical concepts to others in English Information about the selection processWe will only contact applicants who meet our criteria the best.
