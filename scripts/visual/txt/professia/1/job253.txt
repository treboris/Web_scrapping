Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are young innovative company that is engaged in software product´s development for the processing and editing aeronautical and navigational digital data according to the AIXM standard. 
We are looking for a C# and .NET developer, who will be developing user functions in the field of visualization and editing of AIXM data, geographic data and digital NOTAMs. 
Responsibilities:

•	documentation of developed code
