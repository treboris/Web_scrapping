Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesLooking for a job of your dreams? QORPO Game Studio is the ultimate destination for talented people who want to bring their skills to the next level, all while working in a modern and friendly environment.
Our team of game developers, amazing artists and great marketers is passionate about creating immersive experiences that captivate players and keep them coming back for more. And with our state-of-the-art studio equipped with the latest technology, you'll have everything you need to bring your ideas to life.
Now we are looking for BackEnd Python Developer
What will you do:
- creating functions on the server side for the proper
- functioning of the game or for operation over the blockchain
- creation and implementation of solution design, prototype and production software
- solving software errors
Our requirements:
- the position is also suitable for a senior developer who wants to switch to Python
- knowledge of Python used in back-end development
- knowledge of RDBMS, especially PostgreSQL
- experience with asynchronous and parallel data processing
- knowledge of SOA principles
- ability to work in an agile environment
- basic knowledge of Linux administration
- previous experience as a software developer
Good to have:
- experience with scaling high-load applications for the cloud
- experience with Solidity and Blockchain technology
- basic knowledge of AWS cloud services Information about the selection processWe will only contact applicants who meet our criteria the best. Thank you for your understanding. Requirements for the employee Candidates with education suit the positionSecondary with school-leaving examination
