IT Infrastructure Capacity Planning InternTo drive innovation in Capacity Forecasting and Modeling at Dell Technologies, we need forward-thinking people on our IT Infrastructure Capacity Planning team. As part of this team, we devise data driven capacity strategies and roadmaps.Join us as an IT Infrastructure Capacity Planning Intern on our Capacity Planning team in Bratislava, Slovakia to start the best work of your career and make a profound social impact.What you’ll achieveAs an IT Infrastructure Capacity Planning intern, you will employ global enterprise infrastructure related data and modelling techniques to produce meaningful insights and metrics. You will learn to identify trends that impact infrastructure forecasting and planning. You will also gain insights to best practices and knowledge shared within the capacity planning organization.You will:Became a member of IT Development Program - ongoing development sessions and trainingsLeverage existing monitoring tools to document anomalies and identify areas of improvement to automate the global capacity dashboardsPerform global ordering system efforts for Capacity Planning teamMonitor compliance dashboards, prepare reports, and ensure complianceWhat you can learn:Working on agile projectsGain experience in automation, coding, programmingImprove your technical skillsThe opportunity of career growth and work full-timeChance to attend various volunteering activities, team buildings and internship daysTake the first step towards your dream careerEvery Dell Technologies team member brings something unique to the table. Here’s what we are looking for with this role:Essential Requirements University student in IT fieldGood English communicationBasic understanding of databases and SQL languageCoding skills (Python, R)Basic understanding of IT infrastructure (Network, Storage, Server)Microsoft Office (Excel, Outlook, Word)Desire for a professional role in IT InfrastructureAbility to find opportunities and make recommendations for process improvementsDesirable Requirements Good understanding of data science, business intelligence practices and techniquesBI experience (Tableau, Power BI)Basic understanding of virtualization and CloudWhat’s next?1st step: phone screening with HR Consultant2nd step: online interviews with managers3rd step: feedback and/or offer communicationHourly salary: starting from 6,60 EUR grossBenefits When you join the Dell team as an intern you become a part of a winning company that rewards you with following:Being part of a community of more than 90 interns at Dell BratislavaAccess into Learning studio – more than 1,000 online trainingsDell LaptopPossibility to continue after your graduation in professional development programsNetworking groups – collaboration, community and personal development (ERGs)Here’s our story; now tell us yoursDell Technologies helps organizations and individuals build a brighter digital tomorrow. Our company is made up of more than 150,000 people, located in over 180 locations around the world. We’re proud to be a diverse and inclusive team and have an endless passion for our mission to drive human progress. What’s most important to us is that you are respected, feel like you can be yourself and have the opportunity to do the best work of your life -- while still having a life. We offer excellent benefits, bonus programs, flexible work arrangements, a variety of career development opportunities, employee resource groups, and much more. We started with computers, but we didn’t stop there. We are helping customers move into the future with multi-cloud, AI and machine learning through the most innovative technology and services portfolio for the data era.
