Full stack developer for Patient Engagement application
Place of work
Bratislava Region, Slovakia, Bratislava region (Job with occasional home office)
Contract type
full-time
Salary
2 500 EUR/month+ VAR**We are required by law to disclose basic wage component (minimum salary) for the advertised positions. We carefully consider your professional qualifications and experience in our compensation package and/or when offering you other positions.
Our goal is to pay our employee's fairly, with regard to the market situation and we are ready to welcome high-quality candidates in our team.
Job description, responsibilities and dutiesIntroduction:Would you like to contribute to the healthcare solutions of tomorrow?Are you passionate about web development?Are you enjoying agile development practices within a scrum team?In case you answered 3 times yes, we have a right job for you!You can help us saving lives of patients having Stroke, enabling physicians and patients to meet in Virtual Visit, or supporting physicians with new features on Physician Portal.About projectThe project is based on a remote cooperation with a partner located in Austria. We have 6 scrum teams working on a global Healthcare product. Scrum teams are combined to include both sides (Slovak and Austrian). The Customers are based mainly in Europe (for instance Germany, Austria, Switzerland) and lately expanding to Australia, Brazil or Chile.More about project: https://www.siemens-healthineers.com/digital-health-solutions/digital-solutions-overview/patient-engagement-solutions/e-health-solutionsYou will work with:•	React/typescript and web technologies•	Java, Liferay, Kotlin•	Linux, Docker (K8S kubernetes), Oracle, PostgreSQL, Tomcat•	GitLab, Jira, Confluence, SonarQube, Jenkins, CI/CD•	Agile/ScrumWe expect:•	Experience with web development•	Experience with React (other frameworks are beneficial)•	Active contribution to team decisions•	Continuous improvement mindset•	Fluent EnglishWe value:•	Analytical skills, structured approach•	Ability to work independently•	Focus on quality•	Open for learning, improvement mindset•	Team player
