Front-end Developer - juniorJob description, responsibilities and dutiesIn the AI & Data Analytics division within the Advanced Technology Solutions team we are working on exciting projects for some of the most recognized companies. Majority of our projects are connected with application development, python, machine learning, computer vision, data analytics and so on. But from time to time we choose projects which are out of our basic scope.
Currently we are looking for junior or experienced Front-end developers or Full-stack developers (JavaScript/TypeScript, React, Node.js) who will help us to build both internal and external products.
As a Front-end developer, you are going to start with one of our largest and most sophisticated products being developed as part of the multinational team. It is a great opportunity to get experience with a complex stack of technologies applied at a large enterprise scale.
What is waiting for you as new member of our team:
- analyzing business requirements to come up with cutting edge solutions for clients’ business challenges;
- full-cycle development and work on features from front-end perspective;
- creating top-quality clean code, perform unit tests;
- peer programing;
- using technologies such as React, Redux, Node.js, Kafka, NIFI, Cosmos DB, Python etc.
- agile methodology;
 - interaction with the client and colleagues across different locations. Employee perks, benefits- hybrid working policy: flexible work time and home office;
- regular salary increase and performance bonus;
- friendly and enthusiastic environment at work;
- meal contribution and Multi-sport card;
- we appreciate your extra work, every minute counts;
- sick days and additional days off for Christmas;
- cafeteria system;
- opportunity to develop your career in a team of international experts;
- internal learning platform and external trainings;
- participation in international Technology Summits and events;
- occasional travel to work on international assignments across Europe;
- “be well, work well program” - human dynamic advisory, workshops for mental health topics, social events and activities, vouchers for activities. Personality requirements and skillsTechnical skills

- experience with any front-end framework(React.js, Angular.js, Vue.js,...);
- HTML, CSS and responsive design;
- React.js, Nifi, Kafka, Azure Cloud is an advantage;
- ability to get into the existing project and code;
- understanding of Agile methodologies including Scrum
