Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesThe Risk Modeling Platform (RMP) provides the strategic platform, applications and analytics services to support Zurich's risk capital modeling processes. In the role of Senior Software Engineer, you will join a dynamic and agile DevOps team.As a Senior Software Engineer, your main responsibilities will include:   Full-stack software engineering responsibilities   DevOps support for Zurich’s Risk Modeling Platform (RMP).  The RMP provides a set of platform micro-services enabling complex risk models and applications, including solvency and risk-based capital models.   In addition to DevOps support, employ a variety of languages & tools to combine systems together, ensuring that the architecture & systems will support the needs of Business. Experience with Agile & DevOps practices   Desirable: Experience with private / public cloud set-up & services.   Advanced knowledge & high degree of competence with a range of programming languages / technologies combined with a desire and aptitude to broaden that experience:   Required: (C#, JavaScript)   Highly Desirable:  .Net Core, Scala, Java, R     Desirable: Container systems (e.g. Docker, Kubernetes)   Desirable: DevOps / CI/CD toolsets (e.g. Atlassian suite, Ansible)   Ability to work effectively in a global organization, under time pressure and to manage ambiguity   Fluent in English (spoken and written)
