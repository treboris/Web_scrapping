Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWHAT WILL YOU WORK ON:  Follow company code style, revision control, configuration management, and documentation standards.  you will learn new procedures in bioinformatics and mass spectrometry  Work across team of scientists, developers, and product managers  Support instrument graphical user interface (GUI) design as required Employee perks, benefits  Employment with an innovative organization with exciting and fulfilling work  Gross hourly salary from 5 Euro  Flexible working schedule you can easily combine with your studies  Exciting company spirit which stands for integrity, intensity, involvement and innovation  The opportunity to work with a truly global organization Requirements for the employee Candidates with education suit the positionUniversity studentLanguage skillsEnglish - Intermediate (B1)Other knowledgeC# - SkillfulPersonality requirements and skillsWHO DO WE LOOK FOR:  Student working towards Bachelor degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Chemical Engineering or other related engineering field.  practical knowledge of .Net and C#  strong in programming areas like object-oriented principles, design patterns, algorithms design and data structures  communicative English  willingness to work approximately 15 to 20 hours per weekWE WILL BE EXCITED, IF YOU ALSO HAVE:  Grasp of statistical approaches, Machine Learning  Experience with SQL databases and database management systems  Experience of team software development with working knowledge of unit testing, source code control, defect tracking, and version management  Experience with containerization and virtualization technology, such as Docker, Kubernetes, etc.  Knowledge of Python Advertiser Brief description of the companyThermo Fisher Scientific Inc. is the world leader in serving science, with revenues of more than $35 billion and approximately 90,000 employees globally. Our enterprise chromatography data system enables chemists in most laboratories around the world in any industry to perform high throughput analysis of chemical samples. From quality control in pharmaceutical laboratories to chemical analyses to doping controls at major international sports events, our software plays a critical role.
Our software development department is responsible for new product development and maintenance to support challenging customer requirements, as well as to control hundreds of highly complex chromatography instruments and components.
