Job description, responsibilities and duties C# Test Automation Developer Are You passionate about testing and automation? Do you want to be part of creation something new, share Your knowledge and learn new one together with young team? In case Your answer to previous questions is yes, we offer You opportunity to use your knowledge and passion in our team in creating new solutions in area of deployment of medical software. As a C# Test Automation Engineer you  will: Create test scenarios according to the requirement specification Develop the automated tests, regular test run and test result evaluation Install  and operate of test environment in virtual machines Develop and maintain internal test automation framework written in C#   Required skills: Experience in software engineering in C# Understand and apply  the OOD, OOP principles, and design patterns Investigation skills, analytical and logical thinking    Personality and other requirements: You are proactively driving your professional growth You are team player You can speak and write in English    Basic wage component (gross) and other rewards : starting from 1900EUR brutto /month + VAR* *We are required by law to disclose basic wage component (minimum salary) for the advertised positions. We carefully consider your professional qualifications and experience in our compensation package and/or when offering you other positions. Our goal is to pay our employee's fairly, with regard to the market situation and we are ready to welcome high-quality candidates in our team.
