Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesOur dedicated Integration Competence Center has established over 1000 EDI connections with customers and partners. We cover everything from standardized interfaces to tailor-made solutions and will guide and support you throughout the entire process, starting with an in-depth requirements analysis and leading up to all the details of technical implementation. We support a variety of protocols such as HTTP, FTP and AS2, with which all common systems can be easily connected, and offer a range of formats for WMS and TMS systems such as CSV, XML, JSON, EDIFACT and VDA.As a member of the Integration Competence Center team, you are responsible for the development of companywide integration solutions to drive our IT landscape and enable fast and easy customer onboarding to our IT systems. You are a major participant to our company success and enable further growth through business process automation and data processing.Main tasks:        Analysis, planning and implementation of system interfaces for B2B business        Maintenance and support of existing EDI implementations        Analysis of project requirements together with business analysts and sales        Technical concept creation for development of EDI workflows suitable for business processes Employee perks, benefitsWe believe you deserve more, which is why, we reformed not often used benefits into competitive finance based bonuses.Ongoing mentoring and courses to help your carrier growth.Work life balance home office structure.Referral program.Language support – We support proactivity, if you want to learn any foreign language Requirements for the employee Candidates with education suit the positionUniversity education (Bachelor's degree)
