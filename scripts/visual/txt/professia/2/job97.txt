Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWhy join us?Do you want to be a software developer in the medical field working for our Development center, writing applications that could help save lives or make access to healthcare easier? Then this is exactly the position you are looking for. Join our team in developing a new generation of tool that assists thousands of users in their daily work.What are we working on?Our solution is helping ~ 15.000 service technicians to repair medical devices faster or as reliably as possible so that they are available to cover the patients needs. We have a very experienced team of 4 developers, a test automation specialist, a product owner, and an application manager. We are cooperating closely with foreign colleagues, from Germany or other places, therefore we communicate extensively in English.For more information check out mor about the Services Knowledge Base projectSearching for a better solution.Part of our new solution is also an implementation of a new application search engine. Based on OpenSearch, this solution will replace the old search engine based on SOLR. Optimizing search, making it faster, more responsive and ensuring that the result is exactly what the customer is looking for, it’s all part of our work.Do you have what it takes?We are looking for an experienced C# developer that has also some interest in Vue.JS and/or cloud technologies, although these are not mandatory. We want to deploy a new solution for the Services knowledge base (SKB) Project, create a new web application version in .NET core and deploy the solution to AWS. So, if you have knowledge or experience in these technologies or are a backend developer that is willing to learn, you may be the right person for our team!You should:	Be proficient in C# and .NET Core	Know or be open to explore frontend technologies (Vue.JS)	Care about the quality of work and of code	Be ready to learn and understand the necessity to do so continuously	Have experience in teamwork and agile software development	Be either from Bratislava so we can occasionally meet, or willing to travel to see usLinks:About our company: https://www.siemens-healthineers.com/sk/development-centerAbout our project: https://www.siemens-healthineers.com/sk/projekty-dc/monitorovanie-sprava-servis#chapter-block-100012c006991408More about me: https://www.linkedin.com/in/martin-surman-31b7a5169/
