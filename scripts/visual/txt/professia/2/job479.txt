About us
SiemensWe are a technology company focused on industry, infrastructure, transport, and healthcare. By combining the real and the digital worlds, we empower our customers to transform their industries and markets, helping them to transform the everyday for billions of people.Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are looking for a Lead software developer for a project aimed at developing a future generation of a building automation system. You will build Eco System with cloud, edge device with wired and wireless device integration in a Scrum (SAFe) team.We expect: • 5+ years of professional experience with modern C++ for Embedded Systems• Proactive and motivated personality with excellent communication skills to cooperate with other disciplines as well as other locations in a global setup• Expertise in system architecture (framework) development• Experience with UML• OSS concepts and frameworks (edge, container, microservices)• Quality awareness with focus to automated CI/CD (GitLab runner, Lava) and configuration management (CMake)• Using, collaboration and contribution of Open- and Inner Source components/frameworks with focusing on upstream• Good knowledge of EnglishNice to have skills:• Network concept (IPV6, 6LoWPAN, thread) and implementation with strong focus to solution security• IoT standards (W3C, Building Topology Ontology, thing description)• Scripting Languages (Python)• GermanAt Siemens we are always challenging ourselves to build a better future. We need the most innovative and diverse Digital Minds to develop tomorrow‘s reality. Find out more about the Digital world of Siemens here: www.siemens.com/careers/digitalminds
