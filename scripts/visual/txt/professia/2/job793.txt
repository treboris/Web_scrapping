Information about the position  Join Solar Turbines!The Control Platforms team is looking for a Software Application Development Engineer who will work closely within a global team and cross-functionally with several Engineering organizations. This is growth position designing, developing, and testing new software applications in a team-based environment utilizing the Agile development process.What will be your task at this position?· Work directly with embedded computers and control systems hardware· C++ development for the Windows and Linux Operating Systems· C# development· Work both individually and collaboratively with a cross-functional team of engineers to optimize and iterate on software solutions· Leverage Agile practices during the whole software development lifecycle· Analyze and create logical technical diagrams, flowcharts, and models· Design, develop, and test the software components to meet customer and end user needs· Apply the principals of object oriented and secure software development· Work on complex application/technical problem identification and resolution· Troubleshoot and maintain existing code base to fix bugs and implement improvements.What do we expect from a suitable candidate?Basic Qualifications:· Master’s degree in computer science, relevant experience in Software engineering· Proven record of strong technical understanding· At least 5 years of C++ development experience (Windows/Linux)· Understanding of Agile methodology in DevOps environment· Experience with standard development tools (gcc, make/cmake, git)· Experience with unit testing (GTest/GMock)· Understanding of TCP/IP communication· The ability to work collaboratively with team members· The ability to diagnose and troubleshoot production application issues· The ability to apply best coding practices and standard design patterns in software development· Pro-active thinking – being flexible and creative· A self-motivated mentality – takes initiative to exceed expectations· Ability to adapt quickly to a changing and complex environment· A passion for understanding the latest technology trends· Good written and oral communications skills in English- Upper intermediate (B2)Considered as plus:· Experience with control system / embedded device data acquisition· An understanding of test-driven development· Experience with Azure DevOps· Proficiency with Visual Studio and VS Code· Proficiency with C# and Python· Experience with security technologies (signatures, encryption, hashing)· Strong presentation and communication skills· Ability to work independentlyWhat is Solar Turbines offering to you?· Excellent professional growth and career opportunities in an international environment of a worldwide leader in a very exciting and growing sector of industry· Generous pension Plan - Company Contribution to III. Pillar· Life Insurance· Extra 5 days’ vacation per annum on top of the legislative entitlement· Employee assistance program for employees and their family members· Employee Referral Program· Volunteering Day benefit· Flexible working hours· Remote Work / Home Office Requirements for the employee Candidates with education suit the positionUniversity education (Master's degree)Language skillsEnglish - Advanced (C1) Advertiser Brief description of the companyHeadquartered in San Diego, California, USA, Solar Turbines Incorporated, a subsidiary of Caterpillar Inc., is one of the world’s leading manufacturers of industrial gas turbines, with more than 15,000 units and over 2 billion operating hours in over 100 countries.
