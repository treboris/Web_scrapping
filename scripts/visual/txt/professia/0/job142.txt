Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesImmerse yourself in the world of power tools and experience how proud it makes you to build the best ones in the world in a family-owned German company with a long-lasting tradition. Be a part of teams that design, develop and produce tools that are high-performing and robust yet reliable and lightweight. Every tool can only be as good as the person behind it. That's why we are looking for employees who want to make a difference with their 3,000 colleagues worldwide. 
We are looking for an experienced Software developer in Embedded Systems. You will develop the embedded software for the drive subsystem of power tools. Become a part of a group consisting of enthusiastic software developers and work in interdisciplinary teams on a project basis
Your task:
- Hardware-related software development for innovative power tools based on different microcontroller platforms from the idea to series production
- Development of partly safety-critical software with current development tools
- Creation of process and standard-compliant software documentation of the entire development process as a basis for approvals
- Active participation in the software development process
