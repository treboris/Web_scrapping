Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesAre you a C++ developer eager to learn and improve? Join us and help the team improve vital elements of our navigation solutions. This offer is an excellent opportunity to join our Software Engineering team, working on the Software Development Kit, which is the heart of all successful apps used by people worldwide. Help us improve and design cutting-edge algorithms for route computation, search, map matching, etc. Compute graphics help us improve our engine to visualize navigation data if you are interested. Of course, We don’t try to invent the wheel. Still, in our specific applications, we need to develop and maintain our data structures and supporting tools for multithreading and asynchronous environment. Working on our SDK means a significant opportunity to understand the modern mobile operating system better. You can also learn Kotlin and Swift because our libraries provide interfaces. We know that you don’t want to work with old technologies. We are always looking for the latest tooling like Visual Studio 2022, the latest compilers, standards, and best practices. What are your preferences in a development environment? Windows or Linux? Microsoft Visual Studio or CLion? It’s up to you.
