Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are building the best open source identity management & governance software in the world, midPoint. Join our expert development team and help improve a graphical user interface that is based on the Apache Wicket framework. Make the most of the opportunity to design, develop, and maintain midPoint’s GUI while having a considerable amount of freedom in your work.
What you will do:
• Collaborate with colleagues including UX designer to design and implement a user-friendly GUI
• Be part of development team with the main responsibility for the user interface
• Participate on bugfixing, testing, stabilizing, and preparing midPoint for release
• Learn and understand how users and identity experts are using midPoint to make the UI even more user-friendly
• Come up with ideas and put them into practice to influence midPoint’s look and feel
• Be the person responsible for your time management and workflow to meet agreed deadlines
• Maintain high standards for code quality and refactor the code reasonably
