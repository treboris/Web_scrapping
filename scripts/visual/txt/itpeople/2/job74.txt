CLINICAL DATA MANAGER állás Budapest
Egyéb IT pozik állások 
CLINICAL DATA MANAGERIf you have always dreamed of working within a multinational company, here is an excellent opportunity to try it yourself! Our partner company performs its daily work in the IT services and business solutions fields with nearly 3000 employees. Thanks to the continuous expansion, we are looking for a new colleague for the following position:Clinical Data Manager Tasks:Performing day-to-day data management activities e.g. data cleaning, metric creation, and risk identificationExecuting data listing strategies as per defined study guidelines and review for patient data consistency, quality and completenessUtilizing appropriate data management tools to monitor the quality and timeliness of data flowEnsuring the delivery of error-free, quality data for all study-defined milestonesWorking closely with the study data lead and providing solutions and suggestions for the new requirementsReviewing the protocol and any other customer specifications for implementing any client-specific processSupport Leaders and act as a backup in the execution of study set-up. Conduct and closeout activities by the defined process guidelines and study requirementsMaintain a complete understanding of study documentation to perform proper execution of Clinical Data Management activitiesBenefits:Wide range of benefits, including a private health care packageCompetitive salary with Cafeteria benefitsCompany events, hobby clubs, and sporting initiativesRequirements:A Degree in Life SciencesMinimum 1 year of experience in study data managementExperience in Medidata RAVEGeneral knowledge of Clinical Trials and the Pharmaceutical IndustryContract type: full-time, indefiniteWorking hours: 40 hours/week, hybrid
