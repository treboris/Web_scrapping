Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentAegon is a multinational financial services group headquartered in The Hague, the Netherlands. The company has multiple locations across the globe, with core markets in The Netherlands, the UK, and the US, growth markets in China, Brazil, and Iberia, and an Asset Management organization with locations in the UK, US, NL, and Hungary. Aegon Global Business Solution Center is supporting these businesses with IT, Finance, Risk, Model Validation, Actuarial, Audit, and Asset management services with a growing team of approx. 300 people, based in Budapest, Hungary.
At Aegon, they foster a diverse workforce and an inclusive culture. They are a people business, and they recognize that delivering excellent client outcomes comes from harnessing distinct perspectives. They value diversity in the full range of human uniqueness without limitation, including race, gender, ability, language, culture, beliefs, age, origin, background, perspectives, and experiences.Elvárások / RequirementsThe requirements you should ideally fulfill for this role:
At least a Bachelor's degree in IT (or equivalent experience)
3+ years of experience in web application development usingASP.Net MVC / ASP.Net Core MVC
Some experience in front end web technologies (JQuery, Vanilla JS, AJAX)
Strong experience in TSQL query language, query optimization anddatabase designing
It's a PLUS if you have:
Experience in SQL Server Reporting Services and SQL Server Integration Services
Familiarity with cloud technologies
Soft skills we look for:
Self-starter with the ability to drive results independently and through cross-functional virtual teams
Proven ability to support multiple projects and priorities simultaneously in a deadline driven environment
Amit kínálunk / OfferThe new programmer analyst's focus will be support and development for an internal financial support tool and application access management tool. Other responisbilities include:
Being a subject matter expert for specific areas, applications, or technologies
Requirements gathering with end users
Unit testing and coordination of end user testing
Query optimization
Managing software upgrades
Problem investigation and timely resolution
Note that individual may also be in an on-call rotation for 24x7 support.Kapcsolattartó / InformationCompensation:
Competitive salary
Comprehensive health package
 Brand new office at an excellent location
 Hybrid working
Opportunities for growth and advancement
 A highly people centric, positive and supportive work environment
