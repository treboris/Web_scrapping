The Healthcare Innovation Manager will be responsible for identifying strategic opportunities and contacts, initiating and organising activities and platforms for collaboration, and delivering results for mutually beneficial links between the Company and its partners.Essential ResponsibilitiesInitiate, plan and execute activities to facilitate research collaboration and to raise awareness of opportunities. These include delegations, conferences and networking events, attracting the best academic and research players and opinion leaders. The job holder will work collaboratively with colleagues and external partner organisations on these activities.Design, develop and support the setting up and administration of new external funding programmes, with special respect to the EIT (European Institute of Innovation and Technology).Assist in organising and administering of grant calls, funding pitches, and innovation challenges; followed by review, award and post award monitoring processes of various funding programmes, including preparation of publicity, review documents, communications and tracking of grant awards.Build and maintain sector expertise and a network of contacts with relevant decision makers, companies, entrepreneurs, experts and investors in Hungary and the EU. The job holder should be able to open doors, understand business needs, identify opportunities and deliver business results.Qualifications/ RequirementsAcquaintance with the life sciences or Digital Health landscape in Hungary and Europe.Bringing a personal network of professionals is a strong plus.BA required, MA or higher preferred. Preferably in biotech / medical science related fields of expertise.5  years working in research, academic collaborations, consulting, finance, project management or at a start-up.Experience organising events is a strong plus.Strong presentation skills (oral and written).Fluent English and Hungarian (spoken, written and reading).Comfortable working in an IT environment (Microsoft Office)
