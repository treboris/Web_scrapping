Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur client is a global technology company supplying systems for passenger cars, commercial vehicles and industrial technology, enabling the next generation of mobility. They allow vehicles to see, think and act. In the four technology domains of Vehicle Motion Control, Integrated Safety, Automated Driving, and Electric Mobility, the company offers comprehensive product and software solutions for established vehicle manufacturers and newly emerging transport and mobility service providers. Our client electrifies a wide range of vehicle types. With its products, the company contributes to reducing emissions, protecting the climate and enhancing safe mobility.Elvárások / Requirements
Bachelor Degree in computer science, business administration, or engineering is required
Minimum of 5 years of MES experience highly preferred in the automotive manufacturing and/or quality systems.
Minimum of 3 years of IT/Project Management experience within an international environment preferred
Working knowledge of information systems required, with experience in automotive manufacturing and/or quality systems highly preferred
Critical thinking, excellent communication and research skills
A demonstrated ability for managing large/complex projects preferred
Business fluent English skills, both written and spoken
Amit kínálunk / Offer
Self-dependent management of complex global production and quality launch/rollout projects, while focusing on IT standards, business satisfaction and operational excellence 
Ensuring launch IT Production projects success in scope, quality, time and cost by controlling project budget, deciding countermeasures on his own authority within the specified project canvas and managing project risk actively
Special focus is placed on defining, organizing and conducting training and communication as well as user experience and knowledge sharing across all projects to ensure trends are paid respect in the short term and long term alike
Leading the respective launch projects with the necessary departments especially ensuring handover from build phase to service management as well as controlling that all infrastructure requirements for implementation are met
Drive programs and roadmaps to support business change. e.g. new production facilities and represent these within the corporate, divisional and regional domains
