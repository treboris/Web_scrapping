Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur partner is one of the world's leading automotive technology companies, for its headquarters in Veszprém. The factory produces high-quality sensors for vehicle safety systems for all car manufacturers in the world.
We are looking for a Software Test Development Engineer for their growing team. Elvárások / RequirementsThe requirements you should fulfill:
Completed bachelor or master’s degree in any field of engineering or computer science
Experience in C and/or Python programming
Good problem solving, practical thinking, engineering mindset
Confident English communication
Collaborative, open-minded, team player attitude
It's an advantage if you also have:
Knowledge in Doors, Jira, GitHub, Capl, CANoe
Experience in embedded programming, real-time systems
Knowledge of relevant automotive standards (ASPICE, AUTOSAR)
Experience with agile development methods
Amit kínálunk / OfferThe following challenges await their new colleague:
Software Test and Test Development of Advanced Driver Assistance System projects
Analyze internal and customer requirements
Write and execute test cases, evaluate test results
Support in analyzing root causes of detected failures
Create test reports, resolve test environment related issues
Kapcsolattartó / InformationWhat our partner offers:
Flexible work schedule (home office possibility)
Competitive package (private health insurance, cafeteria and bonus system)
Laptop and company mobile
Financel support package (for renting/buying a flat, for families regarding school starting cost and commuting costs/weekend travelling costs to permanent address)
Canteen and coffee bar
Coaching service and company events
Parking space for cars and bikes
