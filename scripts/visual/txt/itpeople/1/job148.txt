Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur partner has more than 40 years of experience in research and development in the field of lottery systems. They are a worldwide leading supplier of lottery products, with more than 170 employees in Budapest, and currently serving more than 400 customers on six continents.
We are looking for a Test Engineer for their growing team. Elvárások / RequirementsThe requirements you should fulfill:
Completed technical education in IT
Minimum 2 years of experience as a test engineer in full time job
Technical experience in test management tools (Jira, Spira), SQL, Oracle database and Linux
Effective in problem analysis and resolution in a multi-component system
Excellent English knowledge (spoken and written)
It's an advantage if you also have:
ISTQB CT-FL
Experience and knowledge in Test Automation
Amit kínálunk / OfferThe following challenges await our new Test Engineer:
Creating and reviewing test specifications, methods, plans, cases, and test data
Test execution and monitoring for function, integration, system, performance, field and acceptance tests (manual and automated)
Error analysis and classification, defect tracking
Support of the company organization through expert advice
Working according to company development processes
Continuous improvement of testing environment and processes
Create and maintain automated tests
Kapcsolattartó / InformationWhat our partner offers:
Competitive package (cafeteria, health insurance and 13. -14. months salaries)
Mentoring Program, trainings and team buildings
Flexible work schedule (home office possibility)
Indoor bike and motorcycle storage
Excellent working atmosphere (recreation room)
Open company culture
Economically stable and growing enterprise
