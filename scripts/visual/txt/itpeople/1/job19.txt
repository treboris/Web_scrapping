Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur partner is a global supplier of software engineering and IT outsourcing services. You’ll be part of a team of professionals who are ready to find the best tailor-made IT solutions for their multinational clients in various industries and solve complex problems.
We are looking for proactive, fresh grad QA engineer for the following position:
JUNIOR MANUAL QA ENGINEERElvárások / RequirementsRequirements you should fulfill:
Experience in Web/Mobile/Desktop application testing 
Knowledge of testing methods and technologies, understanding of formal software development processes (SDLC)
Experience in building testing strategies for various projects and evaluating test coverage
Experience in working with bug-tracking systems, and version control systems
Understanding of client-server architecture
API testing experience
SQL basics
At least intermediate-level English skills
Ability to switch quickly between tasks
Flexible analytical and algorithmic thinking, desire for self-development, persistence in finding solutions, and achieving goals
It is a huge PLUS if you have experience with the following:
Higher education in IT or a related field
Basic programming or scripting skills
Automation experience in Java, Python, or another language
Experience with building servers to ensure continuous integration
Experience in performance testing
Use of traffic analyzers (Fiddler, WireShark, or others)
Amit kínálunk / OfferWhat you will do in the first three months:
Participate in the analysis of the application’s business logic and cover it with tests
Come up with an individual development plan together with your lead and improve your skills based on it
Kapcsolattartó / InformationWhat can our Partner offer?
Stable long-term work environment
Competitive salary with a flexible system of revisions
Benefits and social packages
Comfortable A+ office in Budapest
Every necessary tool and device in the office will be provided for you to be able to comfortably perform all project tasks: computers, meeting rooms, spacious modern kitchens with professional coffee machines, comfortable recreation areas with game consoles, board games, and a selection of literature for every taste
Paid English courses and conversation clubs
Opportunities for professional and personal growth
Mentoring program, internal and external professional training programs
Support and care from our client's friendly team
