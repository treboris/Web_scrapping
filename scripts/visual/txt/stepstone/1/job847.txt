YOUR TEAM
Within the department of “Process Management &amp; Product Compliance (TP-C)” we promote the paradigm change from classic vehicle development to agile software development and strengthen the sense of a common development across all organizational departments. For this purpose "Process Excellence" (TP-C1) provides compliant process-and method reference models. Empowering every developer to focus on creating value for our customers!
WHAT YOU WILL DO
You are the head of the department responsible for engineering process-strategy, -management, -modelling, -analysis, and –maintenance
You build up and lead the department, with focus on the individual development of each team member
You will allocate personnel resources and assign responsibilities
You will ensure the definition and update of the product lifecycle &amp; development process within CARIAD, in respect to legal and group guidelines
You create an environment to foster and manage knowledge. Your aim is to develop guidelines and standards for the organization
Internal alignment of needs and provided solutions with your stakeholders, our projects and business units
You provide an interface to the VW-group (Konzernschnittstelle) and contribute to the PMT strategy, internal committees and working groups
WHO YOU ARE
Successfully completed degree in business informatics,&nbsp;computer science, economics, electrical or mechanical engineering or a comparable course of study
At least 5 years of experience in process management
At least 3 years of experience in developing software based products
Experience in leading a team of experts
Sound knowledge in ASPICE and SAFe
Fluent German and English language skills
Knowledge of VW group processes and a strong VW group network is a plus
NICE TO KNOW
Remote work options
Temporary work from abroad in selected countries
Flextime / optional working hours
Company pension plan
Annual professional development
Sabbatical option up to 6 months
30 days paid + 10 days unpaid leave
Possibility for VW Group car leasing
