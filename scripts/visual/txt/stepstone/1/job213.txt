About us
Do you want to build our Expert Services organization and drive our most important digital transformation projects?
This is your opportunity to become a member of our Technology Consulting division within E.ON's Digital Technology organization. As a partner for our businesses, our division makes a major contribution as an internal professional services organization to accelerate the delivery of E.ONs Digital Transformation Strategy. E.ON is a leading European utility company, playing a key role in shaping the energy transition and connecting everyone to good energy.
If you have a passion for technology and want to make the world a greener place, you can become a change maker (m/f/d) in our energetic and diverse team!
As Head of Digital Technology PM / PMO Services (f/m/d) you are responsible for developing and coaching a team of PM/PMO professionals, managing our E.ON internal accounts in terms of engagements delivered for those accounts as well the pipeline of future engagements, and delivering on projects you are directly accountable for.
You interact with senior (C-Level) stakeholders and you will be driving the implementation of E.ON's largest digital transformation programs to empower us on our way towards a digital-first energy company.
Your task
What you are responsible for
You lead a team of PM / PMO professionals
You are responsible for developing and shaping our PM / PMO community and methodology
You are driving our largest and most important Digital Technology growth and efficiency programs; aiming to drive digitization cross-regionally and cross-functionally and turning E.ON into a digital-first energy company
You engage with senior stakeholders at board and supervisory board level to ensure buy-in
You take over a leading role in building our Expert Services practice in Germany and our regional organizations engaging in people and business development, capability building, and recruiting
You build trusted relationships with existing and new clients to understand their needs and tailor our offers to meet their current challenges
Your profile
What you are good at
You have a university degree in Business, IT or a related field
You have broad experience leading complex digital technology programs; including (multi-)project management, design, development, testing, rollout and operations
You have strong consulting skills from strategic thinking and problem-solving to effective communication and strong sense of customer-orientation to deep organization and planning skills
You have broad technology knowledge and functional expertise in at least one of the following areas: Business Transformation, Change Management, Digital Service & Platform Development, Rollout & Industrialization
You have a proven track record in leading delivery teams across multiple locations and vendors and managing external partners to successfully achieve the required outcomes in dynamic environments
You are experienced in building and running a practice; including capability and people development
Ideally, you have knowledge of the energy sector and the grid business
You are fluent in English and have very good German language skills
What you can expect from a Career in E.ON Digital Technology
We offer the unique opportunity to combine your love for technology with your passion to work on a topic of high relevance for society. You will be able to contribute to E.ON's ambitious digitalization strategy, to apply and broaden your expertise on a variety of assignments, to work with cutting edge technology and to have the opportunity to grow your career internationally across the wider organization.
