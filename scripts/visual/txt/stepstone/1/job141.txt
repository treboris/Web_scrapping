About us:
Aioi Nissay Dowa Insurance Company of Europe SE is a European public limited company headquartered in Luxembourg and active on the German market as an insurance company in the private and commercial customer sector with its headquarters in Ismaning. The company is part of one of the largest insurance groups in the world, MS&AD Group Holdings, Inc., an insurance partner of the Toyota organization and a major shareholder of Box Innovation Group Limited, one of Europe's leading telematics providers.
As the insurance partner of Toyota, one of the largest automobile manufacturers in the world, we develop and distribute outstanding insurance and financial services products for brands such as Toyota and Lexus, as well as specialized products for our car dealership organization.
Our European IT organisation provides internal IT services throughout Europe for approx. 1,000 colleagues.
Your Role and Responsibilities:
You will support our IT organisation in meeting all internal and external governance and compliance requirements
You will implement, optimise and build up our IT GRC standards, procedures and processes
You will Implement and improve our IT risk management approach in the Group IT, for example by consolidation and reporting of core risks
You will maintain and manage IT governance frameworks as well as report regularly to the relevant IT and business stakeholders
Your profile:
Professional experience as a part of an IT GRC team or in the areas of governance, risk management, compliance, quality management or audit
Experience in conducting risk analysis and maintaining a risk register in an IT environment
Profound knowledge of IT governance frameworks and best practices such as COBIT and ITIL
Knowledge of other relevant to IT operations regulations and standards (e.g. ISO 27001/2, NIST, BSI guidelines, VAIT, GDPR)
Basic understanding of and experience in IT environments, systems, services and processes
Ability to explain complex issues and very good communication skills in English and German
