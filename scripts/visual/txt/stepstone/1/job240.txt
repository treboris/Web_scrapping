If you want to be part of keeping the world moving, the KION Group is the right place. Across more than 100 countries worldwide, our solutions improve the flow of material and information within factories, warehouses, and distribution centers. KION´s success is based on our 41,000 committed international employees.
For our location Frankfurt am Main (Airport/Gateway Gardens) we are looking for you to strengthen the team as Data Cluster Lead (f/m/d).
Your tasks:
You own the Sales & Service data cluster’s strategy, objectives and implementation
You will lead and mentor the Data Owners in your clusters
You are accountable for the data objects definition of your data cluster and contribute that the data is properly defined, following standards and policies across the organization.
You provide functional expertise to define data models, governance, processes, harmonization and data quality rules
You oversee Data Quality Management activities for all data objects within your data cluster and provide guidance on prioritization and metrics
You review data migration reports with the Data Owner and will recommend corrective action if necessary
Your profile:
You have a degree in business administration, business informatics, engineering or comparable studies with several years of professional experience preferably some time in a leadership role
You bring relevant experience in a Master Data Management orientated role in a technical and commercial context and are motivated in leading the Data Cluster Sales & Service  
You have in-depth knowledge of (SAP) Data model, industry leading data quality and data protection management practices especially in Sales & Service data objects  
You are highly skilled in change management concepts and strategies, including communication, culture change and performance measurement system design  
You have excellent analytical skills with focus on details as well as problem-solving competence  
You enjoy communicating and facilitating in English with senior level management from technology and business in an international and intercultural environment 
We offer
A company with a bright future: As a listed M-Dax company, we already employ more than 41,000 people in over 100 countries and are thus playing a key role in driving the future of intralogistics  
Move on together: Standardized and documented feedback process with international career opportunities and individual development offers  
Vibrant community: Regular company events and professional workshop formats with opportunities for internal networking   
Individual benefits: State-of-the-art IT equipment & mobile devices, job rad, free fruit & drinks in the office & corporate benefits, childcare facilities 
Our basics: Flexible working hours, part-time models, possibility of regular mobile working, attractive remuneration package, employer-funded company pension scheme, possibility of deferred compensation with employer subsidy
