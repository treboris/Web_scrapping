Who we are
Endress+Hauser Digital Solutions (EHDS) is Endress+Hauser group’s product center for Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), digitalization, digital communication, integration, Plant Asset Management and digital solutions. While Endress+Hauser has an excellent reputation as a leading manufacturer of process measuring instruments; we believe, Digital solutions for process automation are also an important part of its offering. In order to grow the EHDS business unit, we are currently looking for a Head of DevOps - Digital Solutions.
Roles + Responsibilities
You oversee and guide all activities of our DevOps and directly related teams
You lead cross-functional teams in a hands-on manner, diagnosing complex issues on the various platforms, and playing a leading role in software/platform/application architectural evolution
You lead the design, build, and operation of highly secure and scalable software.
You guarantee the reduction or removal of bottlenecks and inefficiencies in the DevOps activities and engineering department
You ensure both quality assurance and continuous software testing
You will be responsible for empowering the teams to create a positive climate, high collaboration, and self-responsibility
You will be responsible for the operational business of the DevOps teams (lean, compliant) and the economic success (cost effectuation, value of platform)
Qualification + Profile
University degree (ETH, Uni, FH) in Information Technology or similar 
Passionate about Digital and SaaS oriented businesses
Extensive programming experience (Web-Application and Web-Architecture)
Entrepreneurial thinking and result orientation
Extensive expertise working with IIoT Platforms and services (DevOps)
Strong knowledge of methods in SW-Development in an agile context –CI/CD, TDD, Security
Strong knowledge of architecture of distributed Cloud-Applications, including cost drivers and operational efficiency
Experience in (organizational) change management and guidance through strategic decisions
Sound experience in Leadership and coaching of teams based on agile values
Benefits + Perks
We offer you the flexibility of an agile organization and at the same time the security of an internationally active, successful family business 
Opportunity to take advantage of offers for company pension schemes, health management and a better work-life balance 
We have flexible working hours without core hours 
We work in state-of-the-art offices with modern work equipment 
Of course, we also attach great importance to the personal and professional development of our employees and offer a comprehensive range of further training courses
