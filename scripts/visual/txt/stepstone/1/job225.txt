Ihre Rolle
You will play a crucial role in shaping the future of Data Analytics and actively contribute to the success of the digital transformation at ZEISS
In this challenging function, you will help our business units to establish Master Data standards and increase the quality level of Master Data in ERP, CRM and consuming systems
You will leverage analytical tools to perform data integrity checks and cleansing, identify and correct/update data discrepancies, duplicates, and dated records
You will provide support in resolving data quality problems with subject matter experts
You will be part of a central Master Data Governance Team in which you create the foundation for a data-driven organization
You will drive Master Data projects, key group programs and work in cross-functional teams with the ZEISS business units at ZEISS Digital Partners
Ihr Profil
Successfully completed studies, preferably business informatics, information technology or a technical education in informatics, paired with a passion for master data and data quality
Strong communication skills and the ability to communicate between different stakeholder groups from technology to business
Experience with SAP MDG, ERP, CRM, Salesforce or comparable systems preferable
Experience in project management
Fluent English and advanced German language skills
Career starters also appreciated
Part-time work possible
