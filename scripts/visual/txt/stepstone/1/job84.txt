Einleitung
NIPPON GASES Europe, including Nippon Gases in Germany, is part of the Japanese Nippon Sanso Holding Corporation. This is a global company with more than 100 years of experience in the world of industrial gases. NIPPON GASES Europe currently has over 3,000 employees in 14 countries. More than 150,000 customers in a wide range of industries, including food and beverages, metallurgy, chemicals, electronics, energy and healthcare, benefit from the innovative products and exceptional commitment of our employees. Safety, compliance, diversity & inclusion, environmental awareness and customer focus are our priorities and guide our actions.  
We are Nippon Gases, „The Gas Professionals“, a global company at home in the world of industrial gases.
  We are looking for support for our team as soon as possible:
IT Service Desk Koordinator DE & EU
Düsseldorf
Tasks
- Supports end users with IT requests.
- Support IT projects in the local and European area.
- Process changes to the IT management/ticketing system.
- Active tracking and support of active hardware ordering standards.
- Active follow-up and support on active software ordering standards.
- Active tracking and support of capex/budget/ordering processes.
- Interact with suppliers and contractors when extended support or third party support is required.
Profile wanted
- Completed vocational training in the IT field (e.g. IT specialist or IT systems electronics technician)
- Business fluent in English
- At least three years of professional experience in IT support
- Strong experience with ITSM platforms such as ServiceNow
- Experience with hardware/software purchasing standards
- Experience with local and European projects as well as working in a European area
- Good knowledge of Microsoft Windows operating systems, Microsoft Office
- Good knowledge of ITSM / IT ticketing systems
- Operational knowledge of client hardware and licensing standards
- Basic knowledge of ITIL is a plus
- Good communication skills
- Solution-oriented work
What we offer
- 30 days holiday
- Performance-related salary according to Chemie tariff
- Flexible working time model with the possibility of mobile working
- Modern workplace in a crisis-proof company
- Individual development and training opportunities
- Company pension scheme and additional accident insurance
- Corporate benefits
