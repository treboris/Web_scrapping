Einleitung
biomedion GmbH is a market leading software company focused on the life sciences industry. With a broad product portfolio and subject matter expertise, our solutions address industry intelligent information and imaging needs. Our products are innovative, scalable, award-winning and based on state-of-the art technologies
We are looking for an experienced, skilled and driven person to join our team as a IT System Administrator (m/f/d).
Ihre Aufgaben
First point of contact for management, employees and auditors for questions about user accounts and access permissions, other activities in the context of user administration; e.g. creation and maintenance of (user) master data
Administrator for Windows Active Directory and the Exchange Admin Center; Initiation, monitoring and control of authorization checks in IAM systems (SSO, SAML)
Administration of users and access rights in biomedions’s application systems and business applications (BCS, Hubspot, DevOps, etc.)
Continuous further development and maintenance of the order management system (ITSM) as a key user
Interface function between the internal requesters and the various biomedion departments
Hardware purchasing and maintenance, hardware- and software- asset management; maintaining, controlling and deploying all software licenses
Planning, installation, configuration and operation of end user’s hardware (Windows, MAC OS, printer and scanners etc.);
Level 1 support for end user IT onboarding, maintenance of software and services for firewall and virus protection
Troubleshooting and processing of end user inquiries (2nd & 3rd level - also on site as part of the field service)
Level 1 support as system admin on: Cloud Servers (AWS), Virtualization environment (AZURE) and Windows/Linux server operations
Participation in and implementation of training courses
Support of IT administration as well as implementation of IT projects; e.g. participation in ITSM / ITIL and IAM projects to optimize identity and access management and the service infrastructure
Elaboration of requirement specifications for external IT service provider; Technical contact for the external IT service provider
Ihr Profil
Bachelor's or master’s degree in computer science or equivalent qualification
Microsoft/AZURE Enterprise Certified Admin and are familiar with data onboarding and system administration (especially Linux)
Several years of work experience in Client Administration (Windows, Linux and OSX)
Pronounced customer and service orientation (ITIL Certification good to have)
Proven organizational skills and an ability to multi-task efficiently, ability to manage and complete projects from beginning to end
Proactive and self-motivated, able to work as part of a team in a fast-paced and international environment
Extensive knowledge in the installation and configuration of operating systems windows, Linux and application software’s
Knowledge in the use of software distribution systems
Basic knowledge of homogeneous Windows networks (IP, DNS), AWS-Cloud-Services and AZURE
Fluency in English and German, very good verbal and visual communication skills
Professional experience in setting up, operating and maintaining SaaS platforms
Experience with agile working methods (e.g. Kanban, Scrum)
Good MS Office knowledge
Absolute diligence with a high degree of flexibility and commitment
Certification on AWS, Microsoft AZURE will be an advantage
Wir bieten
A very dynamic environment with global exposure
An open, positive, and respectful company culture
High Level autonomy, flat hierarchies and clear, direct communication
A team-oriented transfer of know-how
Occupational pension plan
Supplementary company health insurance
