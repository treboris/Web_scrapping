Knorr-Bremse is the global market leader for braking systems and a leading supplier of other rail and commercial vehicle systems. Knorr-Bremse’s products make a decisive contribution to greater safety and energy efficiency on rail tracks and roads around the world.
Senior Network Specialist (m/f/d)
LOCATION: Munich / Germany | COMPANY: Knorr-Bremse Services GmbH München | REQUISITION ID: 2801 | Full-time | Regular 
YOUR TASKS
Ensure corporate-wide Cloud and DC operations on industry level based on best practice processes and Project Management in the area of IT Infrastructure in general and with a special focus on the corporate network
Designing, implementation and continuous optimization of the End2End network solutions and network security for excellent end-user experience and efficiency, with a special focus of the Corporate Datacenters (On-prem and Azure)
Maximizing workplace and network performance through ongoing monitoring and troubleshooting including Network Security, Client environment, LAN, WLAN, WAN
Coordination of Datacenter LAN Requirements, Network Security, Implementation and Lifecycle Management
Incident, Problem and Change Management in a complex security environment including on-site availability for Data Center, Cloud and headquarter
Reporting network status to key stakeholders
2nd level and follow-the-sun support
YOUR PROFILE
Degree in computer science, comparable subject area, or adequate work experience; proven record in medium or large enterprises
Language proficiency in German and English (minimum C1)
At least seven to ten years experience in customer networks
Distinct customer or end-user orientation and corresponding experience mandatory; deep experience in CISCO networks, e.g. Catalyst, Nexus, UCS, Cisco Wi-Fi 6, IP Telephony and Office 365 Suite (esp. MS Teams telephony) and ability to work independently 
Proficient in Palo Alto Firewall, Radware, and SD-WAN network
Strong understanding of and experience with Network Monitoring (Riverbed, SteelCentral, Aternity, Cisco DNA center, Cisco Prime, l, Solarwinds, SmokePing, MRTG) and Network protocols, services, or technologies like BGP, OSPF, IP / MPLS, QoS, MPLS Traffic Engineering, Multicast, SD-WAN and NAT
Experience knowledge in project management (e.g. experienced PMI Project Management Professional PMP)
