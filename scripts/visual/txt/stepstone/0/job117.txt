Aufgaben
In your role as the IT Digital P&ID Specialist, you will be responsible for the operation of the P&ID drafting applications. You will work in close coordination with administrators to ensure correct functioning of these applications for all thyssenkrupp Uhde offices worldwide.
In coordination with management and business stakeholders from other departments, you must define and continuously refine a product vision based on strategic and operational requirements.
You will lead and coordinate developers and support engineers in multiple locations around the globe.
Among your tasks is assurance of performance, maintainability, scalability and security of the product.
Represent the IT and product to management, committees and eventually the board.
Ensure correct functioning of interfaces to other tools and platforms in an open environment with other specialists.
Produce accurate and concise documentation that is available to all internal customers.
Be in constant contact with software vendors to leverage knowledge and new features to improve operations.
Take responsibility for the ultimate success of the product and its impact on the core business of thyssenkrupp Uhde.
Profil
You have a masters's degree in Engineering or Informatics, or comparable experience/education.
Outstanding communication and analytical skills help you understand and translate ideas and pain points from stakeholders into achievable solutions.
Strong stakeholder management and influencing skills. You are proficient at securing agreement and commitment from others, as well as influencing strategic decisions within the company.
You have experience and feel at ease working in multicultural teams.
You have experience and knowledge in one or more database-driven P&ID solutions (e.g. SmartPlant P&ID, AVEVA P&ID, AVEVA Diagrams, Comos P&ID, etc.)
Experience working in large scale engineering projects, especially in the plant engineering field, is desirable.
Basic knowledge in other 2D and 3D CAD tools is helpful (e.g. AVEVA PDMS/Everything3D, SmartPlant, AutoCAD, etc.)
You are comfortable working with Agile methodologies (Scrum, Kanban).
Experience with database administration is desirable (SQL, Oracle).
Programming knowledge in object-oriented languages and scripting is advantageous (C#, PowerShell).
Basic knowledge in the Atlassian suite (Jira, Confluence, Bitbucket) is desirable.
Fluent English (B2) or German (B2) language round out your profile.
