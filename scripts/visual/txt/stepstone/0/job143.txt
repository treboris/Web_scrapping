Evotec is a life science company with a unique business model focused on delivering highly effective new therapeutics to the patients. The Company leverages its multimodality platform, the “Data-driven R&D Autobahn to Cures”, for proprietary projects and within a network of partners including Pharma, Biotech, academics, and other healthcare stakeholders. With more than 4,500 highly qualified people at 17 sites, Evotec aims to create the world-leading co-owned pipeline for innovative therapeutics. For additional information please go to www.evotec.com and follow us on Twitter (@Evotec) and LinkedIn.
At Evotec’s headquarters in Hamburg, Germany, the IT department is seeking a highly motivated and dedicated
IT Business Analyst (f/m/d) Finance ERP
Full time and permanent
Your key responsibilities
Drive the optimization of Evotec's ERP applications and modules to meet the requirements of the Finance function
Keep close contact with your sponsor, the Finance community, and act as lynchpin between Global Business IT and Finance
Partner with both IT and Finance teams to identify and deliver improvements regarding relevant processes within the ERP system
Apply best practice project management skills to develop technical solutions to the business requirements of your Finance colleagues
Act as an ambassador and knowledge leader for ERP topics and transfer this knowledge into the Finance community by organizing and leading related communities of practice
Ensure that Finance processes are compliant with reporting requirements as well as the Sarbanes-Oxley Act
Support internal customers with daily ticket support
Be responsible for maintenance, support, and projects regarding the ERP applications, and modules
Identify and deliver improvements regarding the business processes within the tool
Your ideal qualifications
B.Sc. or M.Sc. in Business Informatics or a comparable qualification at the interface of Business Administration and IT
Extensive experience with ERP systems, minimum IFS and SAP desirable
Good understanding of Finance concepts and processes
Interest in data recording, maintenance, and migration projects
Extended experience in system analysis, roll-out, and process optimization
Experience in leading a small project team
Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English, German language skills would be an advantage
Ability to work independently and as a part of a team with a strong eye for detail, flexibility, and organizing skills
Our offer
A position within a vigorous and exciting professional environment promoted by an open culture and a spirit of community
A diverse, international workforce with a dynamic working environment that fosters creativity, innovations and teamwork
30 days of annual holiday, flexible working hours, and in-house canteen
Capital forming benefits, holiday allowance, annual bonus depending on performance, and monthly allowance for public transportation (HVV-ProfiTicket)
