(Senior) Data Engineer (m/w/d)
BNP Paribas Cardif is the trusted insurance partner for more than 90 million customers. Our employees are dedicated experts in creditor insurance, extended warranty and income protection who, as part of the BNP Paribas Group with world-wide activities, contribute to shaping a changing world with their skills, expertise and knowledge.
Taking responsibility:
Design and automate data modelling pipelines from different data sources
Prepare and accompany the roll-out of ETL process to ensure data availability and consistency in our data warehouse
Translate business requirement of reporting needs and communicate with different stakeholders
Contribute as a valued team member to testing, debugging, QA, and documentation of data pipelines and systems
Carry out ad-hoc data analyses and dashboards
Support for the development of the overall organization towards a Data-Driven Company
Convincing skills:
Complete degree in informatics, computer engineer or similar studies with at least 5 years of professional experience
Very good analytical thinking, solution-oriented and team player, self-responsible
Advanced SQL skills
Experience in the development of ETL jobs and Microsoft SSIS software solutions
Data visualizations experience with Power BI or Qlik Sense
Experience in data quality desirable
Fluent in English and German
Experience with agile project management methods (e.g. Scrum) desirable
What we offer:
Benefits: company pension scheme, endowment insurance, accident insurance, private supplementary health insurance, travel advance, bonus program
Subsidized lunch, free beverages, weekly fruit basket
Training & further development: language classes, in-house seminars
Health care: mobile massage, discount at various fitness providers, company doctor, health days
Work-life balance: flexible working hours, 31 days annual leave, parent-child room, special leaves for specific reasons, etc.
Our added value: new, modern premises in Gerlingen near Stuttgart
Miscellaneous: car leasing, corporate benefits, company and team events
Dynamic prospects in a stable environment
The BNP Paribas Group offers a unique working environment in Germany: our 12 companies together encompass a learning organization in which our employees share their knowledge and are committed to ensure that our customers benefit from this wealth of experience. Join our team and drive a changing world forward with your own ideas.
