Vitesco Technologies is a leading international developer and manufacturer of state-of-the-art powertrain technologies for sustainable mobility. With smart system solutions and components for electric, hybrid and internal combustion vehicles, Vitesco Technologies makes mobility clean, efficient and affordable. The product range includes electric drives, electronic control units, sensors and actuators, and exhaust-gas aftertreatment solutions. In 2020, Vitesco Technologies recorded sales of EUR 8 billion and employs almost 40,000 people at around 50 locations worldwide. Vitesco Technologies is headquartered in Regensburg, Germany.
Your tasks
Vitesco Technologies Identity and Access Management platform is the central hub to manage the almost 50k digital identities of Vitesco Technologies including internal and external employees. IAM also automatically provides access to key IT systems for these identities in an end-user friendly, yet secure and compliant way.
Your new responsibilities:
Proactively develop, implement, maintain, and continuously optimize our next-generation Identity and Access Management services in the Vitesco IT landscape based on innovative and new technological strategies
Assure high-quality and reliable service operation in a self-driven mode by providing 3rd level support and steering external service providers in accordance with ITIL processes and SLAs, including process and KPI monitoring and analyses, followed by proactive improvement and mitigation activities
Represent the service throughout the organisation and to all internal and external stakeholders to co-create the roadmap with the product owner
Develop concepts to educate end-users in the use of the service, conduct trainings and promote self-service offerings
Drive small and mid-size projects to further develop the service (e.g., as a scrum master or subject matter expert) including gap analysis, process design, requirements engineering and vendor selection
Manage the budget and costs for the services in scope
Your profile
Academic degree in information technology or similar education
Long term work experience in IT related area especially Service Management
Several years of experience in Process Design, ITIL and Data Analysis
Profound knowledge in the data flows and tools in the area of Identity Governance processes as well as HR related processes
Profound Experience in provider steering
Basic knowledge in Active Directory, Azure Active Directory, Cloud Computing (SaaS)
Basic knowledge of agile methods, project management
Business fluent English language skills and German language skills beneficial
Passion and willingness to learn continuously, high level of creativity and self-driven analytical thinking
Applications from severely handicapped people are welcome.
