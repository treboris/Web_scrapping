Your tasks
As an IT System Engineer (Enterprise Output Management) you will take responsibility in our team for design and architecture in output management.
Furthermore, you are responsible for creating output management solutions to accelerate digital transformation.
Moreover, you will support in technical and strategic issues and form a global network to promote innovative technologies.
Last but not least, you take into account new requirements for our services and ensure the smooth operation of our existing system solutions.
Your profile
Education: completed university studies (bachelors`s degree) in Computer Science, Information Technology, Business Informatics or a comparable subject
Personality: team-oriented, flexible and good at communication
Working Practice: analytical, structured, independent
Experience: several years of professional experience in the field of Information Technology, Output Management, Shell Scripting, PERL in lifecycle (developing, maintain, operation, support, continues improvement), know-how in Expert of Unix (preferably AIX), IT Service Management practices (Incident-, Problem-, Change Management)
Knowledge: Printing Technologies (Laser, Label, RFID, …) and Printing Languages (PCL, ZPL), Output Management VPSX, Understanding of Dev-Ops
Languages: good communication skills in written and spoken English as well as German
