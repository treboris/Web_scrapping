Hillenbrand (NYSE: HI) is a global industrial company opera­ting in over 40 countries with over 10,000 asso­ciates ser­ving a wide variety of industries around the world. Guided by our Pur­pose — Shape What Matters For Tomorrow™ – we pursue excel­lence, colla­boration, and inno­vation to consis­tently shape solu­tions that best serve our asso­ciates, custo­mers, communi­ties, and other stake­holders. Hillenbrand's port­folio includes brands such as Coperion, Milacron Injection Molding & Extrusion, and Mold-Masters, in addition to Batesville.
To strengthen our team, we are looking for a Cyber Security Engineer (m/f/d), preferably in Stuttgart. This person will play a key role in develo­ping and expan­ding a comprehen­sive and global OT/IIoT cyber security program to address upcoming cyber security challenges. As a Cyber Security Engineer (m/f/d), you will have a passion for cyber security and possess good obser­vation and strong problem-sol­ving skills in addi­tion to your tech­nical back­ground.
Cyber Security Engineer (m/f/d)
Your tasks
Develop, execute and track the performance of security measures to protect information and network infrastructure and computer systems
Design computer security strategy and engineer comprehensive cyber security architecture
Identify, define and document system security requirements and recommend solutions to management
Configure, troubleshoot and maintain security infrastructure software and hardware
Install software that monitors systems and networks for security breaches and intrusions 
Monitor systems for irregular behavior and set up preventive measures 
Plan, develop, implement and update company’s information security strategy
Educate and train staff on information security best practices
Your profile
A successfully completed degree from a college or university in software and network engineering, systems engineering or comparable or a successfully completed computer science degree in systems engineering or comparable 
At least 5 years of professional experience in IT security operations
Direct experience with 1 or more of the following areas: Armis, Vulnerability Management (Rapid 7), CrowdStrike, CyberArk, Proofpoint
Demonstrated knowledge of risk assessment and vulnerability management methodologies
Proficient in network technology, infrastructure support, server management and maintenance
Knowledge of regulatory compliances and frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001, SOX, SSAE18, SOC 2, COSO, CCPA, SEC and PCI DSS
Very good knowledge of German and English, both written and spoken
Strong process orientation with good judgment, attention to detail, accuracy and follow through
We offer
A dynamic team where you can bring your own ideas
A work environment with flat hierarchies, flexibility, and quick decision-making processes
Challenging and interesting tasks with a high degree of personal responsibility
