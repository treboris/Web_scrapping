In our E-Mobility Innovation Center in Rastatt, we are specialized in the development of fuel tank systems, fuel modules, pumps and thermal management. In the rapidly growing field of thermal management, we develop solutions and products for future forms of mobility, including electrified and electric vehicles. These are produced in our European manufacturing plants as well as in the associated satellites for the leading automotive manufacturers.
We are successful and growing continuously – become part of our team!
For our E-Mobility Innovation Center in Rastatt we are looking for a
Smart Factory – Digital Twin Engineer (m/f/d)
at the earliest possible date
What you can expect:
Technological contact, companion and consultant for the departments and IT in the implementation of TIFS Smart Factory strategy and process digital twin
Project management of automation and digitization projects with internal and external suppliers (3D Manufacturing simulations, Layout and material flow optimization in digital twin)
Planning and connection of production lines to MES (Manufacturing Execution System) / SCADA systems
Establish Digital twin-based simulation verification process, and training of key users within the plants
Investigate a state-of-the-art digital twin trends and appropriate manufacturing technologies and continuously upgrade the environment and global expertise
Works on methods and concepts to improve efficiency and reducing different forms of waste related to equipment utilization, material flow, factory and production layout and workplace ergonomics
What you offer:
Successfully completed (technical) university studies or professional experience in a comparable profession
Very good programming skills in the field of PLC but also high-level languages such as: Rockwell Allen Bradley, Siemens S7, TIA
Ability to coordinate global improvement efforts and drive best practices across multiple locations and work in a team
Communication skills and high motivation are basic requirements
Demonstrated skills in project management, team building, problem solving and experience
Fluent verbal and written German required, English preferred
What we offer:
Permanent contract
Exciting and varied projects in the R&D environment
Challenging tasks in an international and innovative company
Individual development opportunities
High degree of initiative and personal responsibility
Working in a dynamic team in which team spirit and appreciation are of great importance
Flexible working time model with the possibility of home office (hybrid working model) for a good work-life balance
Flat hierarchies and short decision-making processes
Free coffee / water and discounted lunch in our canteen
