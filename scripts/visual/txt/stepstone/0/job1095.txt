Einleitung
With more than 30 years of experience in Partial Discharge (PD) measurement, Power Diagnostix
is a market leader offering a broad range of products for portable, on line, off line, and monitoring PD solutions. To strengthen our team in Aachen, we are looking for motivated and qualified employees.
Ihre Aufgaben
Developing state-of-art software (web and Windows apps) for Partial discharge measurements and monitoring systems
Design and implementation of communication interfaces and accompanying data
Designing software modules and carrying out an implementation and testing strategy
Regular reports on projects status to the line manager
Helping to solve problems that arise during software tests and those which are reportedby end users
Updates of existing software product lines
Ihr Profil
You hold a degree in a relevant engineering discipline (engineering, computer science, or a comparable qualification)
At least 5 years of experience in software development and GUI design
You work in a structured way and can set clear goals for you and your team
You have an in-depth knowledge of C#, JavaScript, HTML, GIT, Jira and Visual Studio relevant toolchains
You have excellent English skills (the company’s business language)
Wir bieten
Power Diagnostix is a dynamic and international company in a growing market. You will work on a variety of tasks during flexible working hours with performance linked wage in a pleasant and friendly working atmosphere.
