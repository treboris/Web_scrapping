Welcome to Bosch
Do you want beneficial technologies being shaped by your ideas? Whether in the areas of mobility solutions, consumer goods, industrial technology or energy and building technology - with us, you will have the chance to improve quality of life all across the globe. Welcome to Bosch.
The Robert Bosch GmbH is looking forward to your application!

Your tasks
In recent years, Bosch has established the Bosch Center for Artificial Intelligence (BCAI), a world leading enterprise research center for artificial intelligence. In addition to cutting-edge research, the BCAI has the mission to enable Bosch employees in data-driven methods. For that purpose, BCAI’s Enabling team has developed an education portfolio to train engineers with different backgrounds in software and state-of-the-art data-driven technologies.
As a Senior Software Engineer for AI Enabling you will contribute to the future of the enabling team. Your task will be to guide experienced Bosch engineers in adding the data-related software engineering competency to their profile. To that end you will design and conduct trainings as well as exercises.
You will assume a mentoring role in complex data and software engineering projects that have a significant impact on Bosch's global business. In doing so, you will get a unique insight into the different business areas of Bosch.
Being in this central role, you will have the opportunity to shape Bosch's global data and software strategy in various ways.
To keep sharp, you will have the opportunity to work hands-on, be it on our own training infrastructure, or on different strategic projects.
Your profile
Education: excellent degree (PhD/Master) in computer science, natural science, engineering or a related discipline
Personality: excellent communication skills and drive to make a difference
Experience and knowledge: excellent programming skills (we mostly work with Python, but also Java/Scala), general software engineering (architecture, quality, devops, algorithms), teaching complex topics, first experience with data-related technologies such as distributed systems or databases (e.g., PostgreSQL, mongoDB), experience in Big Data (e.g., Spark, Kafka, Hadoop) or Cloud (e.g., Azure, AWS) technologies is a plus
Languages: fluent in English, German is a plus
