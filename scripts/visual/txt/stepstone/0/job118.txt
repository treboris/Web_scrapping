Mercedes-Benz Mobility at a Glance
With around 10,000 employees, Mercedes-Benz Mobility AG specialises in financial and mobility services. The products range from financing, leasing, vehicle subscriptions, rental and fleet management to digital services for charging and payments, as well as insurance and innovative mobility services.
Mercedes-Benz Mobility is a division of Mercedes-Benz Group AG, which also includes the vehicle manufacturer Mercedes-Benz AG, one of the largest suppliers of premium and luxury passenger cars and vans. As a financial services provider, Mercedes-Benz Mobility supports sales of the Mercedes-Benz Group's automotive brands worldwide. In Germany, Mercedes-Benz Bank AG, as part of Mercedes-Benz Mobility AG is one of the leading vehicle financing banks and offers around one million customers financial flexibility as well as deposit business and fixed-interest-rate accounts in the direct banking business.
Flexible mobility offers such as Mercedes-Benz Rent or EQ subscriptions, enable an uncomplicated and flexible entry into e-mobility, complementing the service portfolio. With its subsidiary Athlon, Mercedes-Benz Mobility is present in Europe in operational fleet management and vehicle leasing and is responsible for around 400,000 vehicles.
Mercedes-Benz Mobility lives diversity and inclusion
Diversity and inclusion are not only a concern of ours, we actively shape them. Employees from 100 different nations enrich our culture. Through numerous initiatives, we promote intercultural understanding, respect for one another, openness and willingness to change within the company, as well as non-profit organizations that are committed to this.
We are part of the organization that is responsible to plan, build and run the future European IT landscape ("OneHouse"). This consists of more than 30 systems, which we are responsible for integrating. This standardization lays the foundation for a seamless customer journey online and in retail when purchasing our leasing and financing products.
Our Team "Architecture & Infrastructure" is mainly responsible for the OneHouse-Architecture. One important part of our job is the integration of OneHouse. That is to make sure that all applications work together and the interfaces are well designed and implemented and use state of the art technologies. You will join a newly founded team with already 4 motivated colleagues and we are right now looking for 3 further members in our team.
As an solution architect for OneHouse the following is waiting for you:
An international working environment
New and further development of software projects with regard to the overall architecture of OneHouse
Cooperation with international, agile software development teams (Dev-Ops)
Design of new features/applications/functions together with our partners and development teams regarding the overall architecture
Development and conception of solution and system architectures and integration of these into existing IT infrastructures in a cross-functional team
Support for the integration of all systems into the overall architecture
Ensuring that the integration of the systems is working by driving the specification and implementation of the interfaces together with the relevant Productowners and their team
Introduction and quality assurance of a cross-system software architecture based on Microservices & APIs
Building a new system landscape based on a modern technology environment, e.g. Microsoft Azure, K8s, Containerization (Docker), data streaming technologies, IaC/ Terraform, Helm, Harbor
Close cooperation with and coordination of all solution architects of the individual applications and the other architects in OneHouse
