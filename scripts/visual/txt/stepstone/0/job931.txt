Olympus is passionate about creating customer-driven solutions for the medical, life sciences and industrial equipment industries. For more than 100 years, Olympus has focused on making people’s lives healthier, safer and more fulfilling by helping to detect, prevent and treat disease, furthering scientific research and ensuring public safety. Olympus is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, with more than 30,000 employees worldwide in nearly 40 countries. Olympus Europa, headquartered in Hamburg, Germany, serves the EMEA (Europe, Middle East & Africa) region and employs more than 7,800 people in 21 countries. Apply now!
Your Responsibilities
Support in defining the EMEA digital business solution strategy and positioning (in alignment with sales and other business units) to set the department for business success
Drive go-to-market initiatives for the upcoming launch of digital health portfolio solutions (segmentation, targeting, pricing and promotion) to ensure a holistic and successful market launch
Define regional product knowledge requirements, review and highlight needs to management
Support EMEA exchange of best practices within product management scope
Ensure proactive and timely information of local Customer Solutions / Digital Business teams on product roadmap and no gos/best practices
Ensure communication of market requirements towards global upstream business unit, influence product pipeline (e.g., by organizing user evaluations and representing towards Manufacturing Business Centers) and drive alignment of other business unit products with digital business solutions
Maintain an overview on competitive products, maintain countermeasures, lines of argumentations and value propositions for sales
Collaborate with other departments (MarCom, CorpCom) to develop marketing material, product information, technical specifications and product documentation
Your Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Engineering, IT or similar professional education; master’s degree preferred
Professional experience in the medical field, especially in product management of medical devices, and ideally initial experience with digital health solutions
Experience in a function working on downstream product management and in supporting sales and marketing advantageous
First knowledge on the restrictions and limitations of medical device applications
Proven interest and experience in data-driven solutions
Fluency in English; German language skills beneficial
Strong conceptual and analytical skills
