Tasks
Inside Mercedes-Benz Cars Research & Development (RD) we shape the future of upcoming Mercedes-Benz vehicle generations. We stand for most innovative products of superior quality and aspire to be the leader in the fields of electric mobility and vehicle software. Therefore, it is our daily business to work on the technologies and vehicles of tomorrow.
Description:
Data, AI and connected vehicles play a central role in the digital transformation of Mercedes-Benz Research and Development. The focus of our department is on data and connectivity. A wide variety of rich data sources including data from our test and endurance vehicle fleets, our customer vehicle fleet, from test benches, workshops and production lines defines substantial potential for our product development as well as business.
Within Mercedes-Benz Operating System (MB.OS) we are re-defining the way how the car communicates with the cloud. To increase the focus on the connectivity topic within MB.OS a new team is being established in our department and we are looking forward to welcome you as our new team member.
Our Connectivity Team is looking for your support to create something big. We are a product centric team, responsible to implement 'vehicle to cloud connectivity solutions' and define a new Connectivity Strategy.
You support the elaboration of a secure, robust Internet of Thing (IoT) connectivity architecture for the interaction of vehicle applications with services in the cloud, e.g., to be able to offer autonomous driving functions
Ideally, you have a good understanding of subsystems (embedded, network, cloud application, customer frontend) and become acquainted with the overall system requirements
You prepare project related documents and strategy presentations
You work in an agile development environment
You cooperate and collaborate with international teams in order to develop state of the art solutions
Qualifications
You are studying Computer Science, Information Technology, Electrical Engineering, or a comparable qualification
Ideally you have knowledge of cloud technologies and IoT products
Ideally you have knowledge of existing network protocols, architectures, and the specific purposes
Business fluent German and English
You like to work independently but also in a team
You have an analytical way of thinking and a strategic way of working You are interested in learning new things and developing yourself further
Excellent communication and teamwork skills
Ability to prioritize tasks, set yourself goals and work self-organized to achieve them
Openness and self-motivation to learn, give and receive feedback and develop yourself further
Ability and flexibility to work in intercultural and international teams
