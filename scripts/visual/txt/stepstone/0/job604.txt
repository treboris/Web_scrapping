About us
E.ON Digital Technology GmbH | Permanent | Full time
You have a passion for technology and want to make the world a greener place?
Then become a changemaker (f/m/d) and join our team as a Senior DevOps Engineer (f/m/d) at E.ON Digital Technology (EDT).
E.ON Digital Technology plays a key role in shaping the energy transition by leading E.ON's digital transformation across Europe.
We explore new paths through developing ideas, breaking new ground, making visions reality, and bringing new technologies to life. In flexible working models and teams, we deliver sustainable technology solutions to meet the energy challenges of tomorrow.
Your task
Responsibilities
You act as a lead engineer for DevOps Platforms based on tools such as Gitlab, SonarQube, Sentry, Artifactory, Fortify, Aqua Security.
You collaborate closely with solution architects to design state-of-the-art DevOps enabled solutions
You consult groupwide project teams for implementing DevOps processes and tools to support Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) as well as Infrastructure as Code (IaaC) in agile projects
You mentor and support developers on DevOps practices and provides trainings to development teams
You ensure compliance to enterprise architecture, global information security policies and engineering strategy by applying standard methodologies, processes and tools
You design a secure application-release automation process to make security an integral part of the CI/CD pipelines
Your profile
Qualifications
Deep understanding of software product development and delivery
Several years of experience in design and implementation of DevOps processes in agile software development projects
Deep understanding of standard DevOps tools such as Gitlab, SonarQube, Sentry, Artifactory, Fortify, Aqua Security
Good experience in software development based on Java, JavaScript or Python, R, and Go
Solid conceptual and analytical skills
Openness to new topics and challenges combined with out-of-the-box thinking
Independent and self-responsible way of working paired with the ability to work in a team
Strong communication and presentation skills
High degree of energy drive and enthusiasm
Fluent in English and German
Willingness to travel
