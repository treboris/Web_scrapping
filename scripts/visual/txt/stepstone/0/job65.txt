BizLink is a global provider of connectivity solutions in the field of data and energy management. We provide solutions for industries following global megatrends such as electromobility, industrial automation, medical technology and data centers with cables and cable systems.
With flat hierarchies, short decision-making processes and a corporate culture based on entrepreneurial thinking and acting, we rely on bright and courageous people with whom we can actively shape the future. 
Join us.
IT Project Manager (f/m/d)
Your responsibilities:
Management of complex, international IT projects
Definition and transformation of infrastructure or application landscapes
Establishment of IT project structures and reporting tools in relation to organization, content, business management, and timing
Organization, setup, management, and leadership of international project teams
Project controlling and monitoring (costs, deadlines, content)
Responsibility for project communication with all parties involved (within and outside the project team)
Required qualifications:
Completed studies in business informatics or comparable qualification with a high affinity for IT
Professional experience in the area of IT infrastructure or IT application management
Knowledge of architecture frameworks and/or methods (e.g. TOGAF, BIZBOK, BPMN)
Knowledge in project management methodologies (Waterfall and/or Agile)
Practical experience in Agile, PMI, Six Sigma, or other methodologies
Experience working with stakeholders at all levels as well as excellent communication, change, and consulting skills
Distinctive analytical mindset combined with a structured and methodical way of working
Agile and out-of-the-box mindset
Strong presentation and communication skills, both verbal and written
Focus on solutions considering the big picture
Outstanding team player in the workplace
Excellent knowledge of German and English, both written and spoken
Our offer:
Assignments with room for creativity in a motivated team
Continuing training opportunities and career prospects within a highly successful and international group of companies
Supportive working environment with short decision-making processes, flat hierarchies, and company events
Attractive remuneration incl. tariff supplement, Christmas bonus, and holiday pay
Employer contribution to a company pension scheme or AVWL
30 days of holiday plus additional paid leave for specific occasions
Promotion of your health through modern and ergonomic workplaces
