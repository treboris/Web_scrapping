Employment type: Unlimited
Working hours: Full-Time
Joblocation: Reutlingen
Your tasks
You support architecture decisions and software development by designing, developing and integrating SystemC-based virtual prototypes of Automotive ASICs and SoCs.
You are responsible for modeling customer-specific functions and algorithms, simulation, verification and analysis of virtual prototypes as well as for assessing the development result.
You coordinate system partitioning and project plans with customers and within the engineering team and collaborate with internal and external development partners.
You proactively improve the development methodology, processes and design concepts.
You think from concept to series production, weigh deadlines, quality and costs and align your actions according to project goals.
You coordinate requirements with relevant experts.
You have an eye for (design-) details and - at the same time - look at the big picture regarding system and customer requirements and can communicate with clear statements in both directions.
Your profile
Education: above-average university degree (Master/Diploma/PhD) in computer science or a comparable subject, preferably with focus on microelectronics
Personality and working practice: team player, goal- and customer-oriented, structured, enthusiastic, enjoys challenges and problem-solving methods
Experience and Know-How: excellent understanding of digital circuit modeling and embedded software development: SystemC, C/C++, VHDL/Verilog, processor architectures, on-chip buses, digital IP and knowledge of methods and tools for developing and prototyping digital circuits
Languages: fluent in written and spoken English
