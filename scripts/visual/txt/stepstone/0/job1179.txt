ARRK Engineering is part of the international ARRK Group and specialized in all services relating to product development. Within the ARRK Group we implement product development from virtual engineering to prototypes and low volume production.
Deine Aufgaben
Development of software for control units, as well as measurement equipment for the interior and cockpit electronics of automobiles
Development of application software and user interfaces for Windows and mobile platforms
Specification and coordination of software and system architectures
Concept development, execution of requirements analysis and design of software models
Development and execution of manual and automated tests
Integration and commissioning of the software on the corresponding hardware
Dein Profil
Successfully completed studies in the field of electrical engineering, computer science or comparable training
Sound knowledge of modern software development methods and
architectures using object-oriented principles
Experience with C/C++ and other languages as well as with microcontrollers and embedded systems
Confident handling of subject-specific methods and tools, e.g. UML
First contact with the V model, SCRUM, AUTOSAR or bus systems is an advantage
Business fluent English and good German language skills are an advantage
