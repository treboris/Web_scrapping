Looking for an exciting and permanent job offering a wide variety of different assignments? As a member of an interdisciplinary team, you will work on future-orientated projects in various high-tech sectors.
Classic AUTOSAR Expert (m/f/d)
Location: Friedrichshafen, München
Reference: REF3640C/SST
Job Description
Development of Classic AUTOSAR Base Layer including COM-stack, diagnostic services, diagnostics, SW-loading
Configuration of classic AUTOSAR stack
Creation of the software requirements based on the customer order and quality standards
Design, development, integration and testing of software features
Active collaboration with global software engineering teams as well as contribution to quality optimization and improvements
Qualifications
Completed studies in computer science, embedded systems, electrical engineering/electronics or a comparable course of study
Solid experience in embedded software development, ideally in the automotive environment
Excellent knowledge of programming language embedded C/C++ and Classic AUTOSAR
Knowledge of security standards ISO 26262, ASPICE and agile methods would be a plus
Excellent written and spoken English, good knowledge of German is a plus
