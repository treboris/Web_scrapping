Who we are
Food, machinery or T-shirts: Hapag-Lloyd moves goods around the globe with over 250 container ships. We connect more than 600 ports on all continents and are one of the largest liner shipping companies. More than 13,000 employees work on board, ashore or in one of our 350 offices. Together, we transport around 12 million containers per year. Our corporate values "We care. We move. We deliver" serve as coordinates on our way. They guide our collaboration with each other as colleagues and with our customers to achieve the best possible quality. In the long run, we are committed to climate and environmental protection as well as human rights and many other social issues.
Your tasks & responsibilities
Our PPS team is a cross-functional team focused on processes and performance improvement related topics. It will be crucial for creating best practices and gaining insights for Area Germany & Central Europe, especially in major hubs such as a QSC.
Working across departments will enable you to identify the root cause of a problem and define effective measures to increase efficiency and create synergies.
You will participate in further developing our cross-functional project structure 
You are responsible to schedule and arrange meetings for our project review structure
Furthermore, you will play an active role in our review meeting structure (moderate) and support us in preparing meeting minutes and recaps
One task will be also the optimization of our project tracking
In addition, you assist our team in tracking our projects and their performances
All in all, you will be part of our multi-national team
Your Qualifications
For us, it's not just the professional qualifications that have to be right. It is important to us that you also fit in as a person. Please feel free to apply even if you don't have all the right skills. We can work on that together.
You are matriculated in a fulltime course of study with the specialization in economics, or a comparable major
You are eager on development and implementation of new concepts
You are confident in using MS Office (Outlook, Word, PowerPoint and Excel) tools
You are keen in using other project management tools
You enjoy knowledge transfer, communication and trainings
You bring a basic analytical competence and have a structured way of working
You are curious to deal with various topics and help to shape them
You have very good communication skills in German and English (spoken and written)
