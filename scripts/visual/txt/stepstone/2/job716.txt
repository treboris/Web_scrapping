Einleitung
We have set ourselves ambitious goals for 2023 and are committed to growing in a sustainable way. Our roadmap includes the digitization of further markets and products as well as numerous additional services. For this, we are looking for you to join our team in the role of an Senior Software Tester.
Ihre Aufgaben
Your tasks
You act as a quality gate for our releases – no feature goes into production without your approval!
You are an essential part of an Integration(Regression) Team that tests new features in conjunction with existing functionalities for releases, this includes:
the maintenance of existing test cases to align with new features
the execution of Regression Suite every release
the participation in specifying the test cases based on user stories
the participation in executing the E2E tests
the internal prioritization of tasks and found bugs
the documentation and reporting of test results
The test techniques in accordance with the ISTQB standard are your basis
You actively participate in test retrospectives to ensure the continuous improvement of processes
You support and participate during the team’s transformation to Test Automation 
Ihr Profil
 We are looking for
5+ years of professional Software Testing experience
Experience and good understanding of Agile Methodologies
Analytical thinking and the ability to think abstractly
Solution-oriented and a precise way of working with a high level of user perception
Strong documentation/communication skills and an excellent team player
Extensive experience with Jira, Confluence and/or other Test Management tools
Basic knowledge of the financial sector
English level C1, German knowledge welcome
Leadership skill is a plus
ISTQB, ITIL certification is a plus
