You are looking for the extraordinary, for something special, that certain wow!-thing!? You find it at umlaut. In an interdisciplinary team you’ll provide our well-known customers with technological and organizational advice. Together, we catapult them into a new technological age with our engineering services. In doing so, umlaut bundles its many years of expertise in all industries, including automotive, energy, aviation, and telecommunications as well as in the public sector.  The full-service company is part of the global Accenture family since 2021. With more than 5,000 engineers and experts at over 50 locations worldwide, umlaut strengthens the digital engineering competence of this leading consulting and technology company. Join us when we transform the way customers design, construct and manufacture their products - and establish sustainability in the company on töp!
Ihre Aufgaben
You will work in an international team in the field of Aerospace Engineering for Aircraft Systems like the combat fighter Eurofighter, the future next generation fighter, unmanned systems or military or commercial helicopters
You develop software for safety-critical embedded systems for our customers of the aviation industry
You are responsible for the analysis and review of the High-Level SW requirements as a basis for the SW developing activities and verify the implementation of the software or system's requirements
Analysis of subsystem requirements and capture the software high-level requirements
Adaption of software top-level architecture
Design the software architecture and capture of low-level requirements for software components
Coding the software according to the coding guidelines and SW standards
Perform software unit testing, the SW/SW integration on host and SW/HW integration of the safety-critical embedded, real-time computer at software test bench level to verify the software components and documents the software test results
Establish or support the preparation of all software documents
Support the definition of high-level SW requirements
Ihr Profil
You have successfully completed your studies in the field of computer science, electrical- or aerospace engineering, mathematics, physics, or comparable aviation engineering qualification
You have experience in embedded SW programming with ADA (Ada83, Ada95, Ada 2005) and / or C
You have experience in defining and implementing structured software tests
Having experience with realtime system like Windriver VxWorks is an advantage
You have experience with Eclipse, GNAT/GPS or other SW development environments
You have experience with Adacore
Having experiences in test systems is an advantage
You have first knowledge of bus systems (MIL-STD, ARINC, Serial) (is an advantage)
Experience in Vector CAST and DOORS is also an advantage
You have knowledge of DO-178B
You speak fluent English
German skills are a great advantage and willingness to learn German is mandatory
Important note: Due to legal requirements, unfortunately only applicants with German, Spanish, British or Italian nationality can be considered for projects in the military environment
