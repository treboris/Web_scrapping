Introduction
Jenoptik is an international photonics group with representatives in over 80 countries. Optical technologies are the foundation of our business. We employ around 4,000 people worldwide.
Your tasks
Architecture design/blueprint for new software products and services for digital transformation based on cloud/ on-premise and IOT technologies
Choosing the right IOT/cloud tools for transforming businesses keeping the financial feasibility and success KPIs in mind
Close co-operation with project/product managers and business consultant for customer requirement understanding and profitability goals
Guiding and consulting the Full Stack S/W development team to deliver to business requirements
Assist during agile product development to keep the team on track with the business and customer requirements
Migrating and scaling existing new applications to cloud platform
Co-ordinating with internal IT for compliance and enterprise security requirements
Develop/establish a technology roadmap for our various business units
Coach team members on bridging gaps in technology understanding
Organizing internal webinars to educate cloud technology and it's benefits for different business units
Your profile
University degree in Information Technology/ Computer science engineering or similar
Full stack S/W and cloud architect including more than 2-3 years of hands-on experience in designing cloud/on-premise/IOT architectures
Solid understanding of Full stack web technologies (Html, CSS, JavaScript, React/Angular, Java, Python, multiple Databases) etc.
Preferred Certified Azure solution architect (Expert)
Knowledge of security controls for the Public cloud (encryption of data in motion/rest and key management)
Knowledge of Linux, Windows containers, and Kubernetes
Knowledge of Azure stack for products and services: Computing, databases, Machine learning services, security protocols, IOT Services (IOT hub, Kafka, MQTT, IOT Edge etc.), Azure Functions
Experience with alternate cloud providers (e.g., AWS)
Experienced in working with agile tools (e.g. Confluence, Jira, Dev-Ops etc.)
Strong communication skills, Leadership skills, Team player, Intercultural skills, conflict management
Business fluent in German and English
