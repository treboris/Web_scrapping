Perspective in an international setting!
We are a leading international manufacturer of high-quality medical technology products. With our headquarter in Tuttlingen (DE) and various production, sales and service offers, we employ 8,500 people in more than 40 countries wordlwide.
To strengthen our Global HR Learning & Development area at our Tuttlingen location, we are looking for a
Learning Experience & Technology Specialist (m/f/d)
Reference number: 61012_1DPA
Your tasks:
You provide troubleshooting to users, trainers and administrators in the learning management system (LMS)
You monitor the LMS in close cooperation with the IT department
You support instructional designers and instructional technicians with technical issues in the LMS
You collaborate with IT for administration of the system, including software updates, releases, and interfaces
You create standard and ad-hoc reports and analysis of LMS usage
You support trainers, training administrators and learning experience designers in creating training elements, training plans, programs, surveys, and reports in the LMS
You manage and support the Global LMS Training Administrator Community
You facilitate the LMS Training Administrator Qualification and Access Process
You maintain and update training content for LMS Training Administrators
You create and maintain intranet communication for the Training Administrator Community
Your profile:
You have completed training or studies in the IT environment
You have training or have already gained experience in educational science professions with a focus on adult education
You have at least 2 years of experience in a support and service role
You are familiar with Articulate 360, SCORM & AICC as well as HTML
You also have knowledge in LMS Administration & Troubleshooting and SAP SuccessFactors Learning
Your German and English skills are very good

Further education offers: Open in-house seminar program, extensive e-learning options, various professional training courses and much more
Corporate benefits and bicycle leasing
Subsidy for private pension provision and company health management
Multiple childcare options
Health, sports, cultural and leisure activities
