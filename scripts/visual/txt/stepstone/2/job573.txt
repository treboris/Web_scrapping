We are Uniper: an international energy company with about 11,000 employees.
We combine a balanced portfolio of technologically advanced large-scale assets with outstanding technical and commercial expertise.
These assets and capabilities enable us to deliver flexible, bespoke, competitively priced energy products and services with agility, precision and speed.
Your tasks
The Short Term Trading & Dispatch Solutions department in Uniper IT is looking for you!
For the integrated Power Scheduling operations Team we are looking for an operations Engineer joining the integrated DeV/Ops Team for critical Power Scheduling Applications
Work as Operations Engineer in an integrated international team and be responsible for the operation of cloud based Applications for critical Applications
Be responsible for the continuous improvement of the operations of the Application landscape and aligning 3rd Party Vendors, internal and external Teams
Use modern tools for operational monitoring and further optimize the operation of the cloud instances
Identifies operational problems by observing and studying the functioning and performance of systems
Develops operational solutions by defining, studying, procedures and processes to be followed from the internal and external Team
Responsible to root cause analysis Incidents and work with the integrated Dev Team to implement the learnings in the Application
Applies incident, problem, change, knowledge, access, release and deployment management in day-to-day work, and contributes to their improvement
Your profile
At least 3-5 years of professional experience in similar roles like Application Management, Operations Analyst, Operations Engineer
Experienced with cloud (e.g. Azure) based application operations and cloud based environments
Experienced with steering of internal / external Service Providers and staff
Broad & deep knowledge of end-to-end IT application support process, solid operational experience in application support/issue/Incident handling
Knowledge of IT operations incl. ITIL
Excellent analytical skills to create holistic understanding of a problem end-to-end
Logical thinker, able to own, investigate and resolve problems and incidents issues, resourceful and creative with the ability to work calmly and effectively under pressure
Ability to work both independently and within an agile team in combination with the strong desire to accept responsibility
Strong analytical and structured way of thinking, high organizational skills with the ability to present complex topics targeted to the audience
Experience with products from Volue, Kisters, ION, PSI is a plus
