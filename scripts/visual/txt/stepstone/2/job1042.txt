ARRK Engineering is part of the international ARRK Group and specialized in all services relating to product development. Within the ARRK Group we implement product development from virtual engineering to prototypes and low volume production.
Deine Aufgaben
Development of web backends and microservices primarily with Node.js and Java Spring Boot
Design of web architectures
Working on web applications in a cross-functional team
Connecting SQL and NoSQL databases
Support with the development of CI/CD pipelines
Working on concepts and specifications in coordination with customers
Dein Profil
Completed studies in computer science, mathematics, physics, or a comparable field of study
Development experience with Node.js and/or Java Spring Boot, RestAPI, SQL and NoSQL databases
Knowledge of quality standards and professional methods in software development, such as Clean Code, TDD, SOLID Principles, etc.
Knowledge with frontend technologies like Angular and React is an advantage
Knowledge with Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, OpenShift is an advantage
Good English and German skills, both written and spoken
