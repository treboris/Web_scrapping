Technolas Perfect Vision GmbH is one of the major players in the area of refractive surgery. Our innovative technologies enhance quality of life for people all over the world.
For our team in Munich we are looking for a
Senior Embedded Software Engineer
Your responsibilities will be:
Create software architectures for embedded systems based on software requirements, define software components and interfaces between them
Design and implement software components and automated tests
Review software detailed designs and software codes (software/firmware/programmable logic)
Coordination of internal, external development (SW/FW/FPGA PL)
Support creation of related test protocols and reports
Integration of automated tests in our CI/CD environment (Azure DevOps, Jenkins)
Coordination of software system integration
Documentation in accordance with the QM-system, including traceability
Support of risk management process
We are looking for someone with:
Computer scientist (Informatiker), engineer (Ingenieur), or study in a STEM field with related experience
Proven track record of professional experience with modern software architectures for embedded systems
Work experience in a regulated industry and/or functional safety
Work experience in cooperation with and control of external development teams
Open and communicative personality who likes to work in multidisciplinary teams
Analytical and solution-oriented problem-solving capabilities and high-level of accountability
Experience with agile development processes
English written and spoken, German is a plus
