Einleitung
  Quest Global is an organization at the forefront of innovation and one of the world’s fastest growing engineering services firms with deep domain knowledge and recognized expertise in the top OEMs across seven industries. We are a twenty-five-year-old company on a journey to becoming a centenary one, driven by aspiration, hunger and humility.
We are looking for humble geniuses, who believe that engineering has the potential to make the impossible, possible; innovators, who are not only inspired by technology and innovation, but also perpetually driven to design, develop, and test as a trusted partner for Fortune 500 customers.
As a team of remarkably diverse engineers, we recognize that what we are really engineering is a brighter future for us all. If you want to contribute to meaningful work and be part of an organization that truly believes when you win, we all win, and when you fail, we all learn, then we’re eager to hear from you.
The achievers and courageous challenge-crushers we seek, have the following characteristics and skills:
Ihre Aufgaben
Perform testing activities for the development and release of software for the High Performance Computing platform in accordance with ASIPCE
Development and implementation of test concepts for testing the software components in consideration of the Functional Safety ISO 26262
Definition and review of test requirements with the responsible departments, customers, suppliers and external partners
Creation of test cases, execution of tests and preparation of test reports as well as ensuring consistency between all work products
Analysis of problem reports and support of software development
Ihr Profil
Master's or Bachelor's degree in engineering (preferably: electronics, computer science, telecommunications, mechatronics) as well as ISTQB Certified Tester or comparable
Very good experience in
Cybersecurity (ISO 21434) Test case development related to Functional Safety (ISO 26262)
Debugging, defect logging and test result analysis
Manual and automated test execution with CANoe, vTESTstudio, Python
High degree of independent and autonomous work in the project team as well as good teamwork
Good communication skills verbal/written in English, other languages e.g. German are an advantage    
Experience with tools such as PTC, Azure, Doors desirable
Wir bieten
Unlimited employment with individual training and development opportunities 
Flexible working hours including overtime compensation 
Exciting projects at a high technical level 
Strong teams with an open and friendly working atmosphere as well as flat hierarchies 
New and modern equipped office building 
Attractive company pension schemes 
A subsidized membership with Urban Sports Club 
Regular employee events (Summer party, Christmas party)
