Einleitung
To advance our cloud strategy in Darmstadt we are looking for you as
Senior Cloud Engineer for Remote Application Streaming (m/w/d)*
Locations: Darmstadt or Berlin in Germany
This role will be focused on design, implementation and maintenance of running 2d/3d applications from the cloud as interactive real-time video-stream including the topic of security controls for protection of the relevant user-data and patient-data.
Ihre Aufgaben
 Design and/or implementation of a cost-efficient system for deploying Windows-based 2D- and 3D- software into cloud environments (AWS and/or Azure) for real-time remote access from web-browsers and mobile devices (Android, iOS) using customized commercial and/or open-source technology as thin clients
Integrate the existing cloud storage system for persistent data (user and patient data)
Partner with our Information Security and Data Privacy experts for HIPAA and GDPR
Cost estimation, cost simulation and cost analysis
Design for high security, scalability and availability including Logging and Monitoring of the environments for thousands of sessions in parallel
Programming or scripting in C#, Typescript, Python or Go, depending on the design and the exact role in the team  
Ihr Profil
3 years of experience in design, building and maintaining cloud-based software systems, preferably in AWS
Experience (at least as a user) with Remote Desktop, AWS AppStream, Vmware Horizon, Azure AVD or similar services for remote software usage, preferably with 2d- or 3d- applications or games
Experience with JIRA and Confluence
Information Systems, Computer Science, or other technical degree, or equivalent
Very good English oral and written
