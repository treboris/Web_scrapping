Software Engineer Test Automation High Performance Controller (m/f/d)
Location: München
What to expect:
As a Software Developer in Test Automation, you are responsible for the following tasks:
Analysis of requirements for high performance controllers
Automation and execution of test cases in Continuous Integration
Development of the framework for test automation
Setup and maintenance of computers and hardware for test automation
Engage yourself within an ambitious and motivated team!
What you bring along:
Successfully completed studies in the field of computer science, electrical engineering or comparable professional experience in the subject area
You already gained solid knowledge of object-oriented programming with C#
You have experience in working with Vector CANoe as well as SOME/IP
Knowledge of bus systems and protocols such as CAN, DoIP, SOME/IP
Ideally, you already have experience in the automotive environment
Excellent English skills required, German optional
