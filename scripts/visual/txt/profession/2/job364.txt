Egészségbiztosítás
The General Electric Company (GE) is an American multinational company operating for more than 125 years. GE has long been a global leader in Healthcare, Power, Renewable Energy, and Aviation. Today, GE also leads in delivering solutions across Additive Manufacturing, materials science and data analytics.
Program Management Director (SAP & Finance)
The Program Management Director will own planning, design, development, deployment, and support of all digital technology solutions for Finance, PM, HR & Legal functions, as well as manage the whole SAP strategy and deployment for GE Hydro business.
Responsibilities:
Partner with key stakeholders in Finance, Project Management, HR and Legal functions to create a clear systems strategy to support growth and productivity targets of GE HydroLead ERP strategy, planning, deployments and SLAs for GE HydroWork in close partnership with GE Power to manage SAP/PMX plans, deployments and SLA’s to meet Hydro prioritiesLead the plan, design, development, deployment, support and maintenance of systems, on the different business functions, maximizing business valueManage the application portfolio for Finance, Project Management, HR & Legal domains driving diferenciation, optimization and standardization cross regionsBe technology advisor to the businessManage budgets and project execution between internal DT and functional stakeholdersLook at industry standards and solutions/tools/applications to fit business needs and specifically with the goal to optimize solutions and reduce TCOCollaboration across company’s businesses to find synergies and leverage opportunitiesAttract, lead, motivate and develop team to support the operations and growth of GE Hydro
Qualifications:
In-depth knowledge of finance related SAP modules (FI, CO) is a mustSenior level experience in IT program and product management, collaborating in a global, matrixed environment.Demonstrated experience in working with ERPs, with functional expertise, as domain expert, driving business process improvements, preferably in Finance domain. Other domains are also considered.Demonstrated experience in building strong teams with high level commitment & motivation.Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Finance or IT
Skills:
Ability to challenge status quo and hold people accountableAbility to conduct tools assessments with a focus on business needs current and futureAbility to create strong partnerships with business executive leadersAbility to influence positively and energize the teams and partners to embrace technology and reach the goals
