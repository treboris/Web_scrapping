Hello there! We’re Vodafone Intelligent Solutions (also known as _VOIS) and we’re looking for a
NodeJS Developer
Role purpose:
The Digital Operations Team solves distinct business problems within Vodafone Business with a focus on Sales Support. We do this by creating internal applications and IT solutions that simplify processes and allow our sales teams to focus on helping our customers and delivering excellent products. There is a great deal of technical autonomy in how solutions are developed, with a strong emphasis on understanding the business requirements and freedom to deliver NodeJS code that is fit for purpose. We're looking for a talented and enthusiastic NodeJS Engineer to join our team, bringing with you a broad range of experiences and a passion for modern development technologies and practices. Working with the prototyping manager and developers across the team, you'll create agile web prototypes, operating under a rapid agile model to solve business needs for our internal customers. You'll create solutions quickly but elegantly with a product-oriented mindset, with a focus on purpose and customer value. You'll be given freedom to challenge the status quo and suggest technologies and ways to better supply data & information to Vodafone Business and our global customers.
What you will do:
Working with the Prototyping Manager to address data needs through the use of latest available technologiesEnable data discovery through elegant solutions that can be widely accessed and address business needs using NodeJS backendTrack and analyse metrics to ensure that prototypes are adopted and used across the organizationAutomate & combine data streams within business intelligence tools to provide new insights and reporting capabilitiesWork with Prototyping Manager to assist Vodafone Business to consolidate CMI data sources into a single instance
What you will bring to the team:
Fluency in English5+ years Node experience Well versed in Test Driven DevelopmentExperience in DevOps: deploying applications, including hosting multiple sites on the serverHTTP server experience in nginx or ApacheAbility to configure in a Linux environmentExperience and an aptitude for working with data warehouses & business intelligence toolsMotivated to create & develop more efficient business applications, processes and proceduresExperience working in a complex matrix environment and effectively interacting with multiple stakeholders
