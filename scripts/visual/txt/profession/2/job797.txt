Functional Specialist SAP S/4 HANA
Our Client is a prestigious multinational automotive supplier company. As a global company is presented in 41 countries and has 23000 employees. Their Global Shared Service Center, located in Budapest and  Székesfehérvár, delivers IT, HR  and Financial Services to a number of Company sites.Would you like to shape the future as a member of a global IT team?We are looking for a new colleague in the following position:Functional Specialist SAP S/4 HANA Location: Budapest( district XI. ) or SzékesfehérvárAccording to your preference, you may choose our Budapest or Székesfehérvár office as your place of work.  
Role Purpose:
As a member of the Strategic ERP Services team, you will work with state-of-the-art SAP technologies and be a part of an international team, participating in global Company projects. You will closely cooperate with internal customers, IT professionals, business experts, managers, and engineers.
Responsibilities:
Taking part in SAP roll-out projects, working together with different  locationsBeing responsible for a specific module of SAP S/4 during the preparation/implementation phasesDesign business requirements and recommend a technical approach to meet the needsOversee development: performing configuration, testing, training, and implementation of the technical solutionMaintain the technical and project documentation (Functional Design Documents, Test Scripts, SAP templates, Training Materials..)Close cooperation with Global Solution Architects – ensure global template consistency
Requirements:
College or University Degree (IT /Business Degree or Equivalent)3-5 years of experience with SAP S/4 HANA or a very strong SAP business process knowledgeIn depth understanding of business functions and SAP and specific knowledge of one of the following modules: SD/LE or FI or CO or PP or QM or PM or MMFull lifecycle ERP experience in manufacturing environmentSAP implementation experience in multinational environmentFluent EnglishStructured, flexible and collaborative personality with an ability to work with clients, team members or individually
The company offers:
Working at the world’s only fully integrated aluminum and leading renewable energy companyDiverse, global teamsFlexible work environment/home office (2+3)We provide you the freedom to be creative and to learn from expertsPossibility to grow with the company, gain new certificatesAttractive benefit package (13th month salary, cafeteria, Christmas benefit, health care fund, pension fund and bonus)Professional development, challenging tasks and opportunities for internal career growth at a rapidly growing multinational company As a Functional Specialist, you will participate in SAP roll-outs requiring business travels. Outside project periods, we provide you the freedom to schedule your tasks and work from home. This position is an excellent opportunity to further your knowledge about SAP and the related business processes.

IT Operation, Telecommunication
