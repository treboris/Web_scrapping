Hello there! We’re Vodafone Intelligent Solutions (also known as _VOIS) and we’re looking for a
Test Automation Engineer
Your role in a nutshell:
The Senior Front-end Developer supports the Online Delivery department and develops web solutions for the website, e-commerce site and management portal of Vodafone Germany. This role will architect and develop websites and applications using different web technologies. Furthermore, the Senior Front-end Developer will focus on knowledge sharing and continuous improvement of the code base for the front-end developer community. 
What you’ll do:
Develop and optimise web solutions for online users together with experienced developers from Vodafone GermanyManage the development of the current website through the full lifecycle as part of an agile Scrum teamCoordinate all technical development activitiesDevelop solutions in close cooperation with product owners, UX, editorial, quality assurance and operations, from the initial idea to the launch of new functionalitiesSupport the development of an agile front-end delivery chain based on React in the context of DevOps, continuous deployment, and test automationTake ownership of the developed code and share knowledge with other developers during code reviews with a focus on clean code principles and continuous improvementCollaborate with tech leads on new architectures and test new technologies
What you’ll bring to the team:
5+ years of experience in HTML, CSS and JavaScriptStrong know-how on current web technologies (e.g. React, TypeScript, REST APIs, Node.js, Express.js, Cypress).Experience in delivering high-value projects, websites and presenting them to stakeholdersAgile-based software development, capability to write clean, well-documented codeStrong organisational skills, effective prioritisation, good deadline managementExcellent team management skills and educational capabilities, outstanding analytical and communication skillsFull proficiency in English, German is an advantage
In return we provide you with:
A dynamic environment where innovative ideas are always welcomeA collaborative community where your professional goals and work are supported by a diverse teamAccess to internal trainings through Vodafone University and external trainings via other providersA supportive internal coaching and mentoring cultureOpportunities to participate in company activities and Vodafone Foundation eventsCorporate assets including a laptop and mobile phone with Vodafone RED subscription (unlimited voice, text message, and data within the EU)Cafeteria packagePrivate Medical ServicesEmployee Assistance Program to support your wellbeing and mental healthLife and accident insuranceYou can work remotely overseas for 20 days a year16 weeks fully paid parental leave.
