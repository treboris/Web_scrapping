Project Manager for Automotive Electronics (Engineering Sensors Pressure)
Do you want beneficial technologies being shaped by your ideas? Whether in the areas of mobility solutions, consumer goods, industrial technology or energy and building technology – with us, you will have the chance to improve quality of life all across the globe.Welcome to Bosch.The Bosch Engineering Centre of Budapest has been established in 2001. Today, it is a major research and development hub within the Bosch world focusing on the big mobility trends: automated, electrified and connected mobility. More than 2100 engineers are working together on complex automotive products within various fields of engineering from software to mechanical and hardware product development. Your ideas will be part of products improving the life across the globe.
Task:
The Automotive Electronics (AE) division develops new electronic control units (e.g. for combustion engines, comfort functions, airbags) and a wide range of sensors (e.g. for battery charge state, inertial, yaw-rate, pressure, NOx) in close cooperation with manufacturing. These products are engineered to a state of the art level both in technology and reliability and are to be found in cars of a very large group of manufacturers.„Intel of micromechanics“ – Bosch, as a pioneer in the R&D of semiconductor based miniature sensor systems (MEMS) has been the No.1 automotive sensor supplier in the world for years now. We in our Hungarian team of 10 years professional history lead development projects of airbag sensors integrating many technologies and disciplines of engineering, from the birth of the specification set by car industry partners to the series production. Join us, let’s make driving safer together!Your contribution to something big►  responsible project management in an international, multidisciplinary team of engineers,► scheduling and coordination of the development process till the start of series production,► conducting discussions focused on technical content, sensor application with OEMs, Bosch-internal customers, supporting engineering departments and production plants,► leading, coordination and follow-up of product quality assessments,► representation of the sensor development in customer visits, plant audits,► change management of series products,► application of Bosch development processes and methodologies, process development with respect to Bosch-norms and customer needs
Expectation:
What distinguishes you►  finished B.Sc. graduation in engineering or similar studies,(studies in electrical engineering or mechatronics, M.Sc. is an advantage)►  outstanding organisational-coordinational skills, active task follow-up,►  open communication, good people skills,►  responsible conduct, agility and teamwork,►  openness towards getting knowledge of new technologies, technical solutions, disciplines,►  proficiency in English language (German is advantage).
