As one of the biggest telecommunications infrastructure services providers in the region CETIN Group is at the forefront of digital transformation. It is present with subsidiaries in four countries, Hungary, Bulgaria, Serbia and the Czech Republic offering cutting edge technology solutions. In total, the Group employs nearly 3,000 people. 
CETIN Hungary owns and operates one of the largest 2G/3G/4G telecommunications network infrastructure in Hungary. We are committed to provide high quality solutions reflecting the technological requirements of our customers. Our main focus is to develop top-quality telecommunication infrastructures and fulfill associated market demand. 
Join us and work with highly skilled, motivated and world-class experts. Apply and be part of our team!
Currently we are looking for an
IP Network Operations Engineer
Role overview:
The primary purpose of this position is to operate and maintain Enterprise, Data Center and Service Provider IP Network. The best candidate in this role will monitor performance, system logs and activity on whole network and devices, also execute system upgrades based on evaluation and testing.
MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES:
Operate, maintain the full IP Network and related servicesDeliver timely and efficiently provisioning tasks (integration of new network elements including commissioning and acceptance tests)Execute and coordinate preventive network maintenance tasks in domain of IP network (including acceptance of new software versions and testing of new functionalities)Execute corrective maintenance activities in IP network (troubleshooting, handling of emergency cases)Participate in planning and controlling of troubleshooting and performance reporting processesPrepare, coordinate and execute migration activities, create documentationsoperate and develop network management systems,Track vendor performance, measure and follow SLAsResponsible for continuous improvement of IP network qualityParticipate in performance monitoring of IP networkSupport NOC teamWith 1 year fixed term contract 
REQUIREMENTS:
University degree in telecommunications or computer engineeringMinimum 5 years of work experienceTheoretical understanding of main techniques that are used in transport and IP network systems, firewallsHands-on experience in operating IP network technologiesOpen to learn and work with wide scale of telecommunication and IT technologiesFluent in English, both written and oral and native Hungarian
WHAT WE OFFER:
Continuous learning possibilities (in-house and on-line LinkedIn trainings)Positive workplace and culture where you would receive all the support you need from your peers and management to achieve your personal and team goalsFlexible working hours with Home Office possibilityCompetitive benefit package - bonus: 10% of the annual gross salary, health insurance, Medicover healthcare services, laptop, optional smartphone up to net 150.000 HUF value, advanced mobile services with unlimited Internet and the Cafeteria amount is 700.000 HUF in gross / yearCompany buses from Budapest
