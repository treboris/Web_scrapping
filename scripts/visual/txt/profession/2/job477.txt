Built on trust, an attitude of supporting each other both internally and externally. Junior Business Analyst
Task:
As the Company’s Junior Business Analyst, you will play a major role in supporting the Company’s objectives in data-driven decision making. This role has a high exposure to company’s leaders and requires high collaboration and communication skills.The main duties and responsibilities:•    Take a leading role in complying, verifying all business data from internal and external sources.•    Prepare and update ad-hoc & scheduled reports answering the business requirements.•    Take the role of data analyst within the Sales and Marketing teams, helping them with various diagnostics and visualization.•    Owner and driver for report automation when it comes to data gathering and reporting to both internal and external stakeholders.•    Develop best practices for data collection and analyze current systems and processes to determine what can be improved and implemented in order to achieve the desired business outcomes.•    Lead the standardization of reporting among the business units, where applicable.•    Have knowledge and experience in Excel programming & Power BI platform.•    Be the intermediary between end-users and data professionals.•    Support defining and developing data governance concept within Primőr.
Expectation:
University degree, preferably an engineer or a degree in Computer Science/IT or a similar science degree like applied mathematics or statistics.Alternatively, data analysis background with work experience proving data mining and analysis skillsExcellent communication skills required.  Very good written and spoken English requiredVery good analytical skills and advanced knowledge of Excel required.Basic understanding of business KPIs, finance and accountingExcellent presentation skillsExcellent planning and time management skills
Other:
1-3 years of experience within other FMCG companies or E-commerce companies would be preferable, but not requiredTo have knowledge and experience in Power BI platform Place of work: Budapest
What we offer:
You can work in a cohesive and success-oriented team, with a competitive benefits package, in a modern environment in downtown Budapest, with home office facilities.Contact us to get to know each other better and become part of our team.
