Industry 4.0 Engineer (f/m/div)
Do you want to be part of a team where you will be able to challenge yourself? Do you love to work within global teams and collaborate with several stakeholders? We have the perfect opportunity for you - apply now to become part of our team in Cegléd!
Job Description
As a Industry 4.0 Engineer, you will be responsible for the scope of Load & Go processes and MES solutions enabling in the European back-end sites at Infineon.
In your new role you will:
Align and harmonize requirements among European and other back-end sitesBuild up the competency of the team and back-end in generalGuide the team in defining requirementsAct as a global expert to provide consultancy to segmentsDefine and govern standardsCooperate with segments to implement and monitor automation solutions
Your Profile
You share innovative ideas, demonstrate comprehensive technical knowledge, possess problem-solving abilities, and can create value. You have a good inter-cultural understanding and are able to communicate well across all levels of the corporate hierarchy.You are best equipped for this task if you have:A university degree in Mechanical Engineering, IT, Automation, or a similar field.At least 5 years of experience in Automation or System Integration positionsKnow-how in production work methods and their interactions with high automation systemsIT skills and knowledge of manufacturing integration systems for automationImplementation experience with Load & Go and Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES), preferably Camstar.Basic understanding of QM methods (e.g. sampling and quality gates)Fluency in English. Hungarian would be an advantage. Please send us your CV in English, via "Jelentkezem" button underneath.
Part of your life. Part of tomorrow.
Infineon is a world leader in semiconductor solutions that make life easier, safer, and greener. Our solutions for efficient energy management, smart mobility, and secure, seamless communications link the real and the digital world.The backend segments enable the development of comprehensive innovation across the entire value chain: starting with chip manufacturing, to wafer testing, preassembly, package development and development of new materials. The backend cluster combines the competencies for planning, productivity, innovation and quality, assembly and testing.
