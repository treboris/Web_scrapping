Senior Network Support Specialist
Your Role and Responsibilities
Provides technical support to field engineers, technicians, and product support personnel in diagnosing, troubleshooting, repairing and debugging complex computer systems, software, or networked and/or wireless systems.Provides technical support in the operation and maintenance of vendor products, services, and solutions.Involved in report design, solving reliability and maintenance problems or bugs, and design engineering/software engineering.May be involved in customer installation and training.Provides support to customer/users on highly technical or sophisticated products.Requires knowledge of one or more networking system technology areas.
Required Technical and Professional Expertise:
Site discovery and documentation of existing network infrastructure.Provide break and fix support for the network environment.Follow established change management process.Maintain inventory of the network equipment.Maintain an audit ready environment.Provide 24x7 support for On Call coverage following rotation schedule as needed.Providing 3rd level technical support for less complex problems in an operational environment.Recommending alternative solutions, priorities, systems hardware/software, design changes and improvements.Performing off-hours work to support customer changes.Managing problems to resolution using defined process flow.Implementing changes using defined change management protocol.Performing additional responsibilities as assigned.Configure network devices.Device activation and implementation.Network Performance Investigation.Perform WAN and LAN troubleshooting.Firewall and VPN troubleshooting.Monitor network devices and circuits.Maintain CMDB Inventory in Service-Now.5+ years extensive experience / knowledge of routing and switching.Cisco routers and switches.Experience with DMVP and MPLS networks.
Preferred Technical and Professional Experience:
Cisco ISE infrastructure configuration and support which include:Configure and maintain TACACS.Configure and maintain RADIUS.Analyze logs to troubleshoot account authentications issues.Cisco wireless networks.Aerohive wireless networks.Aerohive branch office solutions.In depth knowledge of Aerohive HMOL and NG Cloud environments which include.Troubleshooting issue.Monitoring and reportingCisco Prime Infrastructure configuration and support which include: Analyze logs to troubleshoot issues.Configure wireless policies.Cisco CCNA wireless/routing and switching a plus NON-TECHNICAL SKILLS: Strong teamwork skills balanced with ability to work alone.Positive attitude.Strong written and oral communication skills.Ability to quickly assimilate technical and non-technical information.Ability to manage time and workload.see the full description on the company website

Competitive salary and benefits: Private Life & Health Insurance, Voluntary Pension Fund contribution and monthly benefit allowance to SZÉP card. Internal rewards and recognition programs. Ongoing wellbeing initiatives (including mental health support), team outings and seasonal gifts. Commuting and relocation support for local, EU or overseas candidates and their *spouses and children (*provided certain conditions are met). Annual profit-sharing bonus subject to company performance. Personal and professional development both in-person and online (certified trainings, on-the-job coaching & mentoring, career progression support); we also nurture new talent and 'career changers' through our comprehensive education programs and exclusive accreditations. Flexible working opportunities (part-time and home office) for a better work-life balance. Annual 1-day paid leave for volunteering. An open, diverse,  inclusive, and empathetic culture that supports learning and encourages collaboration over competition. As the proud owner of the Family Friendly Workplace certification, we provide great benefits for working parents with fair maternity and paternity leave policies such as additional bonding leave for dads and same-sex domestic partners at the birth or adoption of a child, accommodating working parents based on needs, organizing family events and many more.
