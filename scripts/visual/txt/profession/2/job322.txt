About the company:
Getronics is an ICT Services group with an extensive history that extends over 130 years, and we have a large team with 4000+ employees in 22 countries. Our Hungarian operation is a team of 200+ employees and is undergoing rapid growth. Our services portfolio span across Cloud Services, Smart Spaces, Business Applications, Security & Compliance, Field&On-site Support, Service Desk and Network Services.Getronics is full of diverse people focused on a common goal of reimagining the digital experience.
