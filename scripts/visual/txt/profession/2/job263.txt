Hello there! We’re Vodafone Intelligent Solutions (also known as _VOIS) and we’re looking for a
Senior DevOps Engineer
Your role in a nutshell:
The Senior DevOps Engineer supports the Online Delivery department and develops the website, e-commerce site and management portal for Vodafone Germany. This role will introduce and maintain processes, tools and methodologies throughout the software development lifecycle, from coding and deployment to maintenance and updates in order to deliver the best applications and services.
What you’ll do:
You’ll be responsible for the implementation of new digital services with an emphasis on infrastructureAble to act as an interface between technology and business unitsAct as a technical advisor and take on management tasks as wellDefine development, test and operating standards Represent your area in technical committees and with internal customers
What you’ll bring to the team:
5+ years of experience in IT solution design, software app development, IT operations and/or IT project management3+ years of experience in DevOps platform management in larger projectsKubernetes and AWS knowledgeCloud Native, Microservices and Infrastructure as Code knowledgeExtensive experience with 2+ programming languages (e.g. JavaScript, Java), virtualisation (e.g. Docker), database technologies (e.g. MySQL, NoSQL) and other software supply chain tools (e.g. Jenkins, Terraform, GIT)A degree in computer science, engineering or mathematicsOutstanding conceptual skills, analytical and strategic thinking, methodological, social, communication and leadership skillsFull proficiency in English, German is an advantageSolid experience in working with Agile, DevOps methodologies and continuous delivery methods
In return we provide you with:
A dynamic environment where innovative ideas are always welcomeA collaborative community where your professional goals and work are supported by a diverse teamAccess to internal trainings through Vodafone University and external trainings via other providersA supportive internal coaching and mentoring cultureOpportunities to participate in company activities and Vodafone Foundation eventsCorporate assets including a laptop and mobile phone with Vodafone RED subscription (unlimited voice, text message, and data within the EU)Cafeteria packagePrivate Medical ServicesEmployee Assistance Program to support your wellbeing and mental healthLife and accident insuranceYou can work remotely overseas for 20 days a year16 weeks fully paid parental leave.
