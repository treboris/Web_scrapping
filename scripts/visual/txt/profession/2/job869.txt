Environment & Security Manager (ServiceNow)
Looking for an Environments & Security Manager who will work with the Platform Lead, Release Manager, Test Manager and development team to plan, manage, and deliver ServiceNow releases and upgrades. and to account for security application and software development cycle. This role entails increasing the visibility of the security infrastructure for stronger security practices. The candidate will also ensure that management has insights at all levels to create a roadmap to increase and improve the organisation’s security.
Key responsibilities will be:
Single point of contact with knowledge of every aspect of ServiceNow environmentsProvides direction and oversight to Platform Team resources on environmentsMakes sure the environments are kept aligned and up to date to support the platform and continuous delivery release pipelineTakes ownership of platform environment issues and works to resolutionUpdate and align the platform cloning policyManages risks and opportunitiesSeeks to improve the environments management looking at ways to continuously innovateUser access to the pre-production environments (who and when)Involved in upgrades and patchingReview ServiceNow environments in the support portalMaintain Environments calendar and share with the teamProvide informed and governed response on security operations. Ensure the correct prioritisation of operational events, security incidents and vulnerabilities is applied automatically using the ServiceNow platform Apply sound knowledge of utilizing threat intelligence machine learning and AIOps Visualize operational health and security posture within ServiceNow platform Employ extensive knowledge of the integration of Monitoring Security Tools in ServiceNow Identify, prioritize, and remediate misconfigured assets with the Configuration Compliance Application in ServiceNow 
Required Technical and Professional Expertise:
3 to 5 years’ experience in:Highly organized, detail-oriented, and an inherent ability to multi-taskTeam leader and consensus builder Involved In:Test ManagementRelease ManagementService Portfolio and Service ManagementDelivery MethodologyPlatform operations and ManagementUpgradesHealth ScansScrum Ceremonies Excellent communicatorAbility to rapidly identify and resolve issuesPossesses a sound technical foundation and working knowledge of the ServiceNow platform and applicationsPossess good knowledge of every aspect of ServiceNow cloning, patching and upgrades
