ServiceNow Architect
Hydro Global Business Services (GBS) is an organization that operates as an internal service provider for the Hydro group. It`s ultimate purpose is to deliver relevant IT, financial and HR business services to all business areas within the company.
ServiceNow is a platform used globally in Hydro and is key for the delivery of GBS services within IT and Finance. Hydro is currently using ServiceNow functionality within ITSM, ITOM, Strategic Portfolio Management, Integrated Risk Management and capabilities from Core Services and Now Intelligence. The ServiceNow Enterprise Service Management team is responsible for application management for the ServiceNow platform.
Who are you?
You are passionate about staying ahead of new features within ServiceNow and you have a solid understanding of module interdependencies. You have a background in solution design, and you would like to contribute to technical decisions enabling our internal customers to get the highest possible value from solutions in ServiceNow.
You are a team player with strong interpersonal skills and a collaborative mindset both within and outside the immediate team. You like to take initiative and drive activities. Others perceive you as proactive, collaborative, result-oriented and structured.
Role purpose:
Our new ServiceNow Architect will be a trusted technical advisor and has a key role in defining ServiceNow related architecture and standards as well actual solution design. You will be assisting with design and implementation of Servicenow modules, applications, and custom functionality in addition to playing a key role in the implementation of Servicenow projects and enhancement requests.The position offers great opportunities for professional learning, development, and exposure.You will be a part of a highly competent, dedicated, and inclusive team, and we will offer you a competitive salary, as well as attractive pension conditions.
