Junior Project Manager position
Job Description
We are looking for a Junior Project Manager colleague who could help on various projects at our company: keeping accurate track of progress, understanding client needs and requirements, providing project administration.Our office is based in Budapest Hungary, most of our clients are from Western Europe (Germany, England). The language of the development team is Hungarian, but the whole team uses English.We presume excellent communication skills in English and Hungarian, positive attitude, along with high level of commitment and responsibility, to be agile and dedicated to deliver high quality work and also motivate teams to reach for higher levels of service.The candidate should have at least 1-2 years experience in Project Management preferably related to IT domain.
Education
BSc / MSc degree in Economy or Computer Science (or a related subject or equivalent experience)Good written and excellent verbal communication skills in English (Intermediate level) Experiences1-2 years of experience in Project Management Knowledge / SkillsExperience in using Project Management tools (Jira, Monday, etc)Self-confident in client conversationsStrong knowledge of Word and ExcelBasic knowledge of invoicing proceduresBasic knowledge of Software testing / quality assurance (ISTQB) Soft skillsOpen for new technologies and willingness to learnCreativity, ability to make decisions and taking on responsibilityProactivity & analytical thinkingExcellent time management and problem solving skillsGet the job done attitude
Responsibilities
Project administration (contracts, invoices, regular reports)Organizing agile ceremonies, project workshopsLeading discussions with developer teamDocument managementOrchestrating collaboration of members with different roleRequirement management (regular discussions with client and internal team members)Change managementSupporting quality assurance and testing
What we offer
Long term employmentTask varietySupportive backgroundRoom to growProfessional development opportunities (e.g. trainings)Free English courseRegular team building activitiesDaily lunch allowance, unlimited free coffeeFriendly working environment with a green, quiet surroundings (Budapest III. district)
