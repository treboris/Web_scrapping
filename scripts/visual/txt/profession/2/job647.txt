IT Development Team Leader
Through the collective strength of a global workforce across 30 countries and responsible, sustainable operations, Flex delivers technology innovation, supply chain, and manufacturing solutions to various industries and end markets.
In Hungary Flex operates in 7 cities – Budapest, Gyál, Üllő, Nyíregyháza, Sárvár, Tab, Zalaegerszeg – with around 10 000 employees.
If you would like to know more about us, please visit flex.com! 
To support our extraordinary teams who build great products and contribute to our growth, we’re looking to add a  IT Development Team Leader located in Sárvár.
Reporting to the Information Technology Manager, the IT Lead Developer role involves the responsibility for leading the developer team as well as analyzing end-user requirements and/ or architects, designs and develops programs that automate specific business processes.
What a typical day looks like:
Lead the developer team, develop solutions considering Flex corporate MES (Manufacturing Engineering System) standardsImplement IT part of production systems, adapting to local environment (Integrated MES system, interfaces, reports, APIs, etc.)Working together with other IT technical staff, problem handling, solution development/ deploymentInvestigate and resolve application functionality related issues and provide support and troubleshootingIdentify opportunities that can improve efficiency of IT processesParticipate in meetings with the IT MES team to discuss progress and issues to be resolved, and report status periodically to IT managerDesign application architectures
The experience we’re looking to add to our team:
University or College degree preferably in IT fieldAt least three years of programming and/ or system analysis experienceMinimum one year of people management experienceKnowledge of web technologies (React, Angular)Advanced knowledge in databases (MS SQL)Good programming capability (e.g. asp.net core, C#)Good communication skills in English and Hungarian
What you’ll receive for the great work you provide:
Fringe benefits: semi-annual bonus, CafeteriaUnrestricted work schedule2 days/week Home Office opportunityStable, dynamically developing multinational corporate backgroundUnique, forward-looking manufacturing technologies, professional work environmentOpportunity for continuous professional development provided by various and challenging tasks
