Sr. Compensation Analyst ( Global Sales)
What success looks like in this role:
Provide support in the annual quota setting process, ensuring that associate data is correctly implemented within the Incentive Compensation Management tool and aligned with the annual operating planPartner with other sales compensation team member to process sales associates results and calculate eligible payments based on the approved plan designDevelop reporting to provide insight on associate and plan level attainmentProvide KPI analysis on all areas of sales compensationSupport Incentive Compensation Management system data integration, system design, and testingManage data provided from various internal systems to facilitate results and payment processing
You will be successful in this role if you have:
3-5 years of related experienceBachelor’s degree in Finance, HR or another business areaAnalytical thinker who can translate data into actionable insightsAttention to detail and ability to work with a sense of urgency and ownershipExcel expert, including Pivot Tables; Macros a plusStrong financial and business acumenExperience developing and implementing process improvementsSystem / relational database development; Varicent experience a plus
Offer:
Exchange loss stop - on top of your monthly salary, each month you will receive an additional compensationMonthly gross 35.000 HUF cafeteria - regardless of your weekly working hoursPrivate Health Insurance - Generali Premium Package provided for all Unisys associatesHome office allowanceLast and foremost, a great team, also a lot of learning and training opportunities
