Senior Product Data Technician, Székesfehérvár, On-site
Maternity cover, 2,5 year employment contract
What is the job about?
Provide support to product owners and product specialist in the design and development of new and existing product information for our online sales platforms. Availability of correct product & material master data has always been key for doing business in the professional pump market, and now also to support the digital transformation there is a need for highly skilled Senior Product Data Technicians to:Build, validate and verify or document new and existing products by handling all relevant Product & Material Master Data, -structures and -documents within an agreed area according to Grundfos Group Standards and Processes to ensure efficient data flow towards our online customer touchpoint. Understand the importance of correct data & information and act as the gatekeeper and ambassador for data quality within own areaContinuously improve data quality within own areaSuggest process and tool improvementsSecure that Product Master Data & Information are in place for Gate passes Participate in relevant CoP’s (Communities of Passion/Practice) to contribute to development of processes and toolsReceive task/issue from product owner and solve it in dialogue and according to own knowledge/best practice.Follow relevant procedures and instructions.Document results and provide advice to solutions. Your main responsibilities:Handling all data & structure activities within the agreed task according to related Processes & StandardsSuggest improvements to data maintenance processesContinuously live up to and improve data quality within own areaCommunicate to stakeholders outside the project on status and progressActivity execution within the Agile framework
Your background:
We imagine that you have: Assistant Engineer / EngineerExperience implementing well-defined processes within product development.Ability to coordinate minor processes or projects.Ability to establish a well-defined business process within product development..Most likely to have 5-6 years of relevant work experience before entering level besides formal education.Ability influence others and follow processes.Some experience supporting key business initiatives.Knowledge of best practices and relevant practices, systems in product development.Significant knowledge of operational systems e.g., SAPSignificant knowledge of Product Master Data Processes & StandardsUnderstand the master data within the area and it’s impact
What’s in it for you?
Whether it’s developing leadership skills or advancing your expertise even further, we’ll support you with continuous learning and development opportunities, to help discuss and steer your long-term Grundfos career path. You’ll be welcomed from day one into an inclusive, trusting environment guided by six core values.In addition, your day-to-day benefits include:You are always welcome to our offices where you can engage, learn, and have fun with colleaguesOn top of it, flexible working hours and up to 3 days’ additional paid leave for volunteering of your choiceAnnual bonuses, health insurance, parental support, internal well-being consultants and programmesAccess to the modern Grundfos Academy to pursue further both personal and professional developmentDiverse, inclusive environment with employee-led forums offering colleagues a safe place to connect and share openly
