Bit Soft HU Kft.
Customer Support Manager
Bit Soft is a market leader in helping companies in the hospitality industry thrive. We offer consultancy, software, and integrated IT solutions for our clients to help them achieve efficiency, control, and flexibility. With our team of experts, we have installed thousands of terminals and managed highly complex projects in the last 26 years. We are the strategic partner for multiple restaurant and hotel chains across Romania, Hungary, Bulgaria, and France such as City Grill, KFC, Burger King, Starbucks, Radisson, JW Marriot, and Ramada. At Bit Soft, we are a young and dynamic team that aims to expand to other European markets. We value teamwork and positive work culture and don't shy away from having fun together.We are looking for a motivated and experienced Customer Support Manager to join our team. As our Customer Support Manager, you will play a critical role in ensuring our clients receive exceptional support and service while managing a team of Technical Support Engineers.
Task:
Ensure that SLAs are met and that the support line operates at its best.Manage resources and maintain the support schedule to ensure 24/7 coverage.Assign and manage customer queries and issues to Technical Support Engineers.Handle all customer escalations related to support issues to ensure customer satisfaction.Conduct regular customer satisfaction surveys and formulate action plans for improvement.Ensure process compliance and continuous improvement.
Expectation:
Excellent communication, organizational, and prioritization skills.Ability to manage stressful situations and take ownership and responsibility.Energetic personality with a positive attitude and openness to feedback and professional development.Experience in a support line or technical support role.Proficiency in English and Hungarian.… and above all high level of professionalism!
What we offer:
Competitive salary package.Opportunity to work with cutting-edge technologies.Collaborative and friendly work environment.Flexible work hours between 7 AM and 7 PM.Home office opportunity up to 3 days a week.Training and development programs.
