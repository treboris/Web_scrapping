Presales Solution Architect
Position Accountabilities & Major Tasks:
The Solution Architect has a strong understanding of portfolio offerings, is capable of supporting the assessment of client requirements, and robustly developing the costing and related documentation. All in order to create a high-value service solution for the client.The successful candidate has experience as a technical and personnel leader supporting organization goals, needs and objectives through operational and strategic oversight/solution development, for Digital Workplace Services including at least one but preferably two of the following:Transformational Services, Collaboration, Workplace as a Service, Modern Device Management including mobile devices, Intelligent Workplace Services (Service Desk, Field Services & Asset Management), and Proactive experience Management.Ensures that Unisys’ standard solutions are maximized and that Global Sourcing is used in the solution. In addition, ensures that Unisys products are considered and that the standard solution methodologies for Outsourcing are used in developing the solutionProvides and supports input for internal (e.g. Deal Reviews) and external (e.g. executive summary) documentation
Essential:
Strong English language skills are necessary.    Relevant presales and technical experience in the IT industry – selling/procuring outsourcing/infrastructure services solutions.Knowledge of the business, industry, and market, as well as sales strategies, account/program management, service delivery, and financial analysis.Requires knowledge of technical services, outsourcing and operational support in the enterprise and digital workplace environment.Experience in tailoring presentations to client needs and varying levels of technical understanding.Excellent understanding of client service delivery and customer-driven IT infrastructure services environment.Ability to adapt and perform in an environment that is continually changing and evolving.Excellent client relationship skills and the ability to work collaboratively in a matrix management structureSound knowledge of ITIL principles
Desirable:
Fluency in German or FrenchBachelor’s degree or equivalent work experienceFormal ITIL v3 qualifications at the Foundation level or higherKnowledge of IT service management technologies and architecturesC-Suite consulting experience
