Egészségbiztosítás
MOL Group’s Downstream division consists of different business activities that are part of an integrated value chain. This value chain turns crude oil into a range of refined products, which are moved and marketed for household, industrial and transport use. The products include, among others, gasoline, diesel, heating oil, aviation fuel, lubricants, bitumen, sulphur and liquefied petroleum gas (LPG). In addition, the company produces and sells petrochemicals worldwide and holds a leading position in the petrochemical sector in the Central Eastern Europe region.
Our Downstream Digital Development organization is looking for a
Digital Support Associate
who will support MOL Group Downstream Digital Development team in various topics including managing stagegate process of digital projects and digital CAPEX/OPEX planning process within Downstream. Contributes to the measurement of business value delivered by digital to have a clear understanding of the result of Digital Programs.
Your main tasks are:
To contribute MOL Group Downstream digital ambitions' maintenance by supporting continuous idea/demand managementTo support the measurement/evaluation of business value delivered by digitalTo develop standard reports & dashboards, collect project status reports, acts as a focal point for new initiativesTo coordinate the management and development of Digital CAPEX/OPEX planning processTo prepare the process of Total Cost of OwnershipTo contribute the end user support on Downstream level
What is required to fill the position:
University degree (business, management, engineering, IT)Change management certification is an advantageMinimum 1 years’ in similar area or within the organizationFluent english knowledgeStakeholder managementGeneral literacy in Digital technologiesGeneral understanding of Downstream processes and technologies
What makes you stand out?
Strong organizational, communication and interpersonal skills with large agility and flexibilityGeneral analytical skillset

Extremely innovative work environment in our HQ - MOL CampusCompetitive compensation package including cafeteria and private health insuranceUnique opportunity to take part in the improvement of our Downstream division at one of the most relevant company, with a stable background in CEE-regionProfessional trainings and career opportunities within MOL GroupDuring the various multinational projects, that you will be working on, you will develop quicklyBe part of a competent, diverse and highly motivated multi-cultural team
