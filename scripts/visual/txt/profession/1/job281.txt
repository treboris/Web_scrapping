Commsignia Kft.
System administrator
Commsignia is a leading software and hardware provider on the emerging Vehicle-to-Vehicle and Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2X) communication market, with a mission to increase traffic safety and efficiency on the road.  As a System Administrator, you will support the growth of a fast-growing company, that develops embedded software and hardware products for the Automotive and Smart City Industry. Our IT department needs to be agile and able to support a wide variety of requests from simple desktop support to complex network and DevOps projects. Your job would be to take ownership of the desktop and overall office-related tasks that include onboarding new colleagues and maintaining documentation and order within the IT department. 
Task:
Windows and office infrastructure management including servers and connected servicesResponsibility for internal services' availability (bug fixing and issue resolution)End-to-end responsibility for user management (onboarding, equipment management)Preparing up-to-date installer files and images for deployments (Windows and Linux)Linux Server and Desktop administration Office infrastructure software and hardware maintenance (eg. printers, scanners, network, cabling, meeting rooms)Network infrastructure management (troubleshooting and optimization)Backup system maintenance and troubleshootingKeeping up-to-date infrastructure monitoring systemManaging Virtual machines Server room management (cabling, organization)IT inventory management (IT equipment administration, software license management, expiration monitoring, warranty management)Internal IT-related training and communication
Expectation:
3+ years experience in the area of IT systems administrationConfident knowledge of Windows servers and services (2016, 2019, 2022)Basic knowledge of Linux distributions (Debian and RedHat)Solid knowledge of networking software, hardware, and networking protocols, CCNA or equivalentFamiliarity with virtualization hypervisors (Vmware, Proxmox, Hyper-V etc.)Strong documentation skillsGood communication skills in both Hungarian and English
Other:
Good organizational and problem solver skillsUser-friendly attitude, willing to help to the colleaguesProactivity (it's ok to be a beginner in certain topics, but hungry for knowledge is a big plus)Interested in information technology and open to learning new thingsKnows the hardware and likes to deal with it (Laptops, PC -s, smartphones, gadgets, etc)
What we offer:
Market and trends overview of the automotive industry, Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS), autonomous cars, and the technologies that will be built in every vehicle in the following years Opportunity to grow professionally in a dynamic working environment A long-term career opportunity in building a Budapest-based tech company rapidly growing to the global markets (EU, US, Asia) Flexible working hours Steady work in a cooperative and friendly team Cafeteria and Private health insurance
