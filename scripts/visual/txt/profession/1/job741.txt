Senior Analyst, IT Risk and Compliance
Budapest/Hungary
About the Role
Responsible for managing Digital Cyber Security Engineering activities, incorporating best practices across the entire company – as a member of the Global Digital Security team. The team is responsible for the strategic layout of IT security and compliance (not operation).You are going to report to the Senior Manager, not going to have direct reports, but there is a clear opportunity, or even an expectation for growth. You are going to have the opportunity to work in a very international environment on various global projects.
What You Will Do
Responsible of Digital Security - with your team - across the company globallyContinuously improve and optimize Cyber Security environment across the company, especially in the Cloud environmentsManage and maintain the present Security ToolsEvaluate risks and appropriate processes and technology to mitigate these risksEstablish and foster a corporate information security organizationDevelop a sustainable risk governance process around information security, including KPIs and reportingPerform risk assessments of individual projects as well as holistic companywide assessments
What You Need
Ideally Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Software Engineering, or a related technical discipline.5+ years of experience on Cyber Security in a global footprintSolid experience in Risk Governance and Risk ManagementSolid experience on End Point Security, Cloud Security, Application Security and Network SecurityCISSP certification is a mustStrong English language knowledge (reading, writing and speaking)Ability to prioritize and execute tasks in a structured and analytical wayVery good communication in a team environmentProject Management Experience
What is Nice to Have
Experience with Scrum in an Agile Development environment and familiarity with relevant tools.Experience in developing cross-platform applications for the cloud, desktop, and mobile.Experience working with global cross-functional teams.
What Makes You Eligible
Willingness to travel maximum 5% of the time, domestic and possibly international.
Some office days/month in our Budapest (maybe Pécs/Székesfehérvár) office are preferred to collaborate with Haman teams
What We Offer
Access to employee discounts on world-class HARMAN/Samsung products (JBL, Harman Kardon, AKG etc.)Flexible working from home scheduleRelocation support as in our policiesInclusive and diverse work environment that fosters and encourages career development opportunitiesProfessional development opportunities through HARMAN University’s business and leadership academies​“Be Brilliant” employee recognition and rewards program
