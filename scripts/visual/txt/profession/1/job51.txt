JOIN US AS A
LEAD SYSTEM VERIFICATION ENGINEER
YOUR TASKS:
You will have interesting, diverse and varied tasks in our innovative projects with architecture design: support development of complete system architecture in coordination of HW, SW, and mechanical engineering design teamsYou will lead and coach a 3-4 members team of verification engineers to analyze electromechanical systems requirements and decompose into logical segments in preparation of test cases designYou will manage creation and maintenance of system qualification and integration test cases with the appropriate test tools, document and link them to the specific System requirementsYou will manage execution of System test cases with appropriate manual or HIL setup, analyze results, generate test reports and share with internal/external stakeholdersYour main focus will be to create and maintain a comprehensive test strategy for each project, in accordance with ASPICEOn an international level you will be in contact with our teams in Germany and in the US
YOUR PROFILE:
You have at least a BSc degree in electrical engineering, software engineering, or Computer ScienceYou have at least 5+ years of with CANoe/CANalyzer/CANape to create test panels and test automation scripts using CAPLYou have 5+ years of experience with requirements management, verification, issues/ tasks reporting and resolution with tools, and able to prepare complex written reports, HIL system diagrams and other documents3+ years of experience guiding team of software/system verification engineersIf you have experience with VT Test Studio software we consider it as a big plusYour English language skills are conversational, you will use them on a daily basisWe consider as an advantage if you have worked in Agile environment
WHAT WE OFFER:
We specifically pay attention to your development and support it,You can work with the latest technologies as part of our international teamVery flexible work schedule, home office per your request (up to 100%),Annual bonus, cafeteria, housing/travel allowance,Health screenings, sports support, discounted canteen,Exciting corporate events. Join a multinational team, where we care about You, your individual development and teams collaboration.  As a dynamically growing company with unique products and the Global Leader in Comfort and Climate Seat Technologywe are a continuously growing by developing exceptional automotive comfort and wellness products. Be part of our inspirating future! Our main customers are: Tesla, BMW, Audi, Ferrari, Porsche, Maserati, Bentley, Mercedes, VW, Ford, GM and Renault.
