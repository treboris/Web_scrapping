Currently we are looking for
SD WAN NETWORK ENGINEER (LJD)
Location: Budapest, hybrid (office and home office)
Salary: 1.200.0000 - 1.800.000 gross
Type of employment: employee
Start: ASAP
About the position:
We are looking for a Network Engineer (SDWAN) to join our parter's team embedded within their client a global O&G provider located in Budapest.You will join a Network team responsible for maintaining and developing the entire Europe network of our client that includes office locations, plants, refineries, retail locations or offshore oil platforms, the most critical IT backbone of the company.
Responsibilities:
Develop and perform implementation activities for all service and customer projects (based on Engineering deliverables)Perform Quality Assurance Utilize industry standard solutions and manufacturers with the possibility of continuous training to obtaining the latest certificates needed for your jobPrimary contact for vendors.Develop and maintain Technical Support process and deliverables and hand-over of technology and service to operationsDevelop and maintain Network Service Support Models and workflow automationAssist with both development and maintenance
Required skills:
5-7 or more years of Network Engineering experienceExpert hands-on experience in different network technologies like Cisco Routing and Switching, Network Security, Network Automation – CCNP-equivalent or above. Troubleshooting/operational background is also preferred
Advantages:
Hands-on experience and candid interest in SDWAN technologies (Versa is preferred)Equal to Versa SD-WAN Administrator Specialist/professional or Cisco ENSDWI concentration exam level (done with ENCOR)Open for occasional on-call or after-hours supportAutomation skills are also great plus – Python, Ansible, Nautobot, etc.
