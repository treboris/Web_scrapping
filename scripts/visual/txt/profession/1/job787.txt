ABOUT EMPLOYER
PPD Hungária Kft. is a part of the regional energy entity – the PPD group established in 2001. PPD Hungária Kft. was established in 2014 as a strategic entity on the Hungarian gas market. We are a key and reliable partner for our customers as a professional and respected company on the Hungarian natural gas market. We are focused on excellence, the ongoing acquisition of knowledge and strengthening of skills are essential for us. We are determined to continuously ensure the sustainability of the overall business system through constant development and carefully considered strategies.
