Technology Assurance Consultant (Medior/Senior)
We help our clients with industry-specific services to create value and manage risks. Our wide-ranging client portfolio enables you to gain real-life experience in industries such as telecommunications, energy, IT, finance or sports, and to work on ground-breaking projects.
What we offer:
Opportunity to work in an international team,Possibility to enhance your theoretical and practical knowledge,Valuable experience in an international environment,Attractive and competitive benefit package,Flexible working options - most of the time we work at home, but you can work from the office at anytime if you want, over the mandatory 2 day/week office day.Internal and external training opportunities that fit your personal development needs,Long term career opportunity.
What You would do at KPMG:
Work as a medior or senior Technology Assurance expert on financial audit engagements primary locally and potencially the CEE region,Act as lead auditor on IT risk advisory projects by coordinating the work of junior colleagues under the supervision of managers,Work as an expert team member of the Technology Assurance team, with the possibility to work on IT audit, compliance and IT risk advisory projects,Contribute to financial audits performed by KPMG, by auditing accounting systems and ERP systems (IT, financial and accounting knowledge is advantageous).
What brings you aboard:
Minimum 2 years of relevant professional experience, preferably in IT audit or IT risk advisory,University or college degree (M.Sc. or B.Sc.),Fluent written and spoken English is a must, knowledge of another CEE language is an advantage,Hands-on MS Office skills,Outstanding analytical skills, good communication and interpersonal skills,Ability and willingness to learn and deliver in a challenging environment,Efficient team player and client-oriented thinking.
