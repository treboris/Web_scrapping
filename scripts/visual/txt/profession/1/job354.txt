A Snapshot of Your Day
As a Finance & Resource Specialist, you are responsible for supporting team members focused on assigned projects by performing administrative tasks. You clarify several tool-related topics with the team, and alert team members of upcoming deadlines. You keep track of purchase receipts and working hours and input those numbers into an accounting software program and spreadsheet.You regularly look for and recognize opportunities for process automation and have a comprehensive understanding of all related processes.As this role has both global and local touchpoints, you will collaborate with various team members in the Controls and Digitalization division. How You’ll Make an ImpactSupervise and support the approval process for monthly time sheets and post hours on projectsDrive indirect material and service orders (e.g., IT equipment, trainings)Support the project team with travel expenses, daily allowancesPrepare and track contracts, including back-up documentations across the business unitLaunch new delegations of employees and coordinate the process across different stakeholders and partnersPrepare cost settlements, drive charges towards our customersMaintain daily contact with our engineers and other central departmentsDrive purchasing of different services and materials

About the Team
Our Controls and Digitalization teams have a global presence with almost 2,000 employees worldwide. The team focuses on providing value-added services and solutions to our customers in the areas of controls, digitization, automation and electrical equipment. Who is Siemens Energy?At Siemens Energy, we are more than just an energy technology company. We meet the growing energy demand across 90+ countries while ensuring our climate is protected. With more than 92,000 dedicated employees, we not only generate electricity for over 16% of the global community, but we’re also using our technology to help protect people and the environment. Our global team is committed to making sustainable, reliable, and affordable energy a reality by pushing the boundaries of what is possible. We uphold a 150-year legacy of innovation that encourages our search for people who will support our focus on decarbonization, new technologies, and energy transformation.Our Commitment to DiversityLucky for us, we are not all the same. Through diversity we generate power. We run on inclusion and our combined creative energy is fueled by over 130 nationalities. Siemens Energy celebrates character – no matter what ethnic background, gender, age, religion, identity, or disability. We energize society, all of society, and we do not discriminate based on our differences.
