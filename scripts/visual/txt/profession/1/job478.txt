What success looks like in this role:
This is a key position in The DWS Business Units (BU) Go-To-Market (GTM) Team. 
Working with Sales, Account Management, Business Partners, Service Delivery, Legal and Finance you apply your presales outsourcing experience, business expertise, and commercial acumen to develop leading outsourcing solutions in response to proposal requests and across global clients.
This critical role is focused on Solution development and supports the BU by driving increased win rates for new clients and improving portfolio adoption across existing accounts. Within this role, the Solution Architect creates unique value for Unisys Outsourcing clients through the oversight and support of solutions that utilize Unisys standard portfolio offerings while applying a consistent solution architecture methodology to drive requirements based solution approach.
Position Accountabilities & Major Tasks
The Solution Architect has a strong understanding of portfolio offerings, is capable of supporting the assessment of client requirements, and robustly developing the costing and related documentation. All in order to create a high-value service solution for the client.The successful candidate has experience as a technical and personnel leader supporting organization goals, needs and objectives through operational and strategic oversight/solution development, for Digital Workplace Services including at least one but preferably two of the following:Transformational Services, Collaboration, Workplace as a Service, Modern Device Management including mobile devices, Intelligent Workplace Services (Service Desk, Field Services & Asset Management), and Proactive experience Management.Ensures that Unisys’ standard solutions are maximized and that Global Sourcing is used in the solution. In addition, ensures that Unisys products are considered and that the standard solution methodologies for Outsourcing are used in developing the solutionProvides and supports input for internal (e.g. Deal Reviews) and external (e.g. executive summary) documentation
You will be successful in this role if you have:
Essential:Strong English language skills are necessary.    Relevant presales and technical experience in the IT industry – selling/procuring outsourcing/infrastructure services solutions.Knowledge of the business, industry, and market, as well as sales strategies, account/program management, service delivery, and financial analysis.Requires knowledge of technical services, outsourcing and operational support in the enterprise and digital workplace environment.Experience in tailoring presentations to client needs and varying levels of technical understanding.Excellent understanding of client service delivery and customer-driven IT infrastructure services environment.Ability to adapt and perform in an environment that is continually changing and evolving.Excellent client relationship skills and the ability to work collaboratively in a matrix management structureSound knowledge of ITIL principles Desirable:Fluency in German or FrenchBachelor’s degree or equivalent work experienceFormal ITIL v3 qualifications at the Foundation level or higherKnowledge of IT service management technologies and architecturesC-Suite consulting experience Key SkillsLoyalty and passionAnalytical, diagnostic, project management and problem-solving skills.Excellent proposal writing skills.Excellent interpersonal skills.Excellent presentation and communication skills with the ability to work effectively with senior management and service delivery teams.
