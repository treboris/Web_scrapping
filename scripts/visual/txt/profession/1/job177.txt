Currently we are looking for a
SENIOR PMO SPECIALIST - CONTRACTOR (KI)
Location: Budapest
Salary: competitive
Working language: English 
Type of work: contractor, 6 months, part-time (average 50%)
About the position:
The Senior PMO Specialist works with Geneva and Budapest based resources to understand the impact of the coming changes, propose innovations and enhancements to the processes and systems in place and implement the new solution. 
Main tasks/goals:
critically review, innovate and update the current project management policy to newly agreed governance of the organization digital transformation.develop the Project Management methodology that supports the new Project Management policy.Adapt the project management systems (processes and tools) to the future state of the Digital Transformation Department (DTD 2.0).
Requirements:
5 to 10 years of experience as a member of a PMO.Experience in a decentralized PMO where project managers are based in different countries.Project Management certification, such as PMP, PRINCE 2.Ability to challenge the status quo, proposing innovative new Ways of Working to increase the PMO’s efficiency and effectiveness, and the project managers’ and others’ experience working with the PMO Good verbal and oral communication skills. Excellent documentation skills.Fluent in English
Sector(s):
IT Development
Project Management
IT Operation, Telecommunication
Project Management
Additional information:
The Senior PMO Specialist reports to the Manager, Information Technology Global IT Planning and Project Management.Will work closely with: members of the PMO and the Enterprise Architect.
