Network Security Analyst
Smartphones, computers, networks, self-driving cars, intelligent machines, the latest medical advances, renewable energy the digital transformation makes our lives easier, safer, and more comfortable. And we are at the center of it all: The world's leading technology companies with innovative solutions from ASMPT.With our SIPLACE SMT placement machines and DEK stencil printers we support electronics manufacturers all over the world on their path to the Smart #1 SMT Factory based on Industry 4.0 concepts. As the industry’s innovation and technology leader, our product portfolio stands for top quality, process integration and flexible best-in-class solutions. Our vision: We enable the digital world!
Task:
Analysis, development and implementation of the Secure Network programme, which includes:Further strengthening of the corporate network border with external connections.Creating office level network security strategies, so that each office “is its own fortress.”Implementation of network VLAN level zoning and segmentation, based on the Zero Trust Network Access (ZTNA) methodology.Implementing user access controls into the firewall rule schemas.Integration with WAN, LAN and WIFI technologiesProvide day to day operational support on Zero Trust Network Access related issues including:Swift analysis and response to operational access incidents, while balancing the need to maintain operational network security.Troubleshooting network security related problems and providing deployments and changes to remedy.Identifying and responding to security incidents in a timely manner.Define with the IT Director the enterprise architecture to support the utility and warranty of the Network Security Service to the global segment:Maintain ongoing technology reviews with core vendors supporting the service to ensure that replacement cycles reflect the needs of the business today and tomorrow and that the IT infrastructure portfolio is focused on core technologies and vendors. Provide support, analysis and response to internal and external security audits.Further support wider ranging projects from other IT groups including:Support the Server Micro-segmentation project.Support the Network Access Control project.
Expectation:
You are qualified, or have IT related degree or HND or equivalent level, preferably in an IT or computer related discipline, or 2+ years of working experiences within an enterprise IT environmentYou have a good understanding of network technologies. You are able to work on own initiative, address and resolve problems in a pressurised environmentYou are able to apply a logical and systematic approach when solving relatively complex problems You are a native speaker in Hungarian, and you have excellent English language skills (oral and written)You have a good communication skills to work with the businessYou are a strong team member, so you are collaborative, supportive and openYou are able to work additional hours and be on-call duty from time to time as a result of a key operational requirement.Qualified to at least ITIL foundation levelWillingness to travel to global locations (up to 10%).
