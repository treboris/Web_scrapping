Egészségbiztosítás
KBC Bank, the parent company of the Hungarian K&H Bank, provides banking services in 5 European countries.
The international IT solutions of the banking group, covering several European countries, takes place with Hungarian and Belgian colleagues in Hungary and Belgium, therefore not only our services are international, but also our teams.
Our responsibilities range from day-to-day operation to application development and its operation.
We are constantly expanding the Hungarian side of the team, which is why we are looking for enthusiastic, motivated new employees with junior, medior or possibly senior work experience.
SYSTEM ENGINEER (TAM)
The TAM (Technical Application Management) teams is enabling actual business applications to be hosted on cloud platforms. This means setting up servers, maintaining them to adhere to supported OS/software level & secure configuration baselines, deploying business application onto them, setting up monitoring & integration assets, automating/scripting recurring tasks/actions.
As the TAM teams are also maintaining business critical ‘24/7’ applications, some aspects of the work need to happen outside business hours or during the weekend and there is also a 24/7 on-call duty to which you will be added once you have reached the required expertise/environmental knowledge levels to operate more independently.
