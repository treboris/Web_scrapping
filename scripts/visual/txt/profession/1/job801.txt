Diákmunka - Buyer Assistant Intern - Data Excellence & Digital
Feladatok:
This internship is excellent opportunity, if you are interested to discover procurement environment focusing on data management within an international company & multinational team.As a Data management & Analyst (Buyer assistant Intern), you will be in charge of supporting the Plastic raw material procurement team around 3 aspects: •1/ Digital data management (data quality, updates, tools…): Support the buyer in data collection, animation and maintenance •2/ Support buyers in administrative tasks elaborating cost analysis and in implementing data.•3/ Management of “small” suppliers’ relationship. You will be interacting with different entities: Finance, plants, buyers from other categories, suppliers. The scope of the job is Europe. Main missions Supplier Management for low spend Execute the process for changing from excel to digital toolsData improvement (To match part numbers with the related raw material designations for direct business)Check the data accuracy and take the necessary actions to correct Support the negotiation preparation and RFQ processMaintain and update data tools between plastic procurement category teams ( PaP update based on cost models – especially historically data)
Elvárások:
•Digital Mindset•Lean Data Management skills•Good communication skills•Advanced English•Able to work 20-30 hours / week•Procurement motivation•Analytic thinking skills to find the root cause of the issues and take proper actions•Advanced level of Ms Office applications•Ongoing university studies in Supply Chain, Logistics, Data management, Management, Economics, or any related field•Be autonomous as your team around Europe, entire team will be connected to you through TEAMS! •It is a plus, if you have already gained some work experience in this field, but don’t worry, if you don’t have any - apply and gain your first experiences here, at Schneider Electric!
What we offer:
Others•Be able to work at least 24 hours/week, flexible working hours•Gross salary: 1800/hour•Internship duration for minimum 1 year•We provide a laptop/cell phone for your work•You can start as soon as possible: we are looking forward for you to join our team!Bérezés:1800 Ft/óra
