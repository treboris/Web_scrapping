Since 2015, we, the Laba Group team, have been creating those ourselves.Since 2015 we have:→ launched more than 395 courses and trained 90,000 students→ created Skvot, a creative skills school, robot_dreams IT school and our first online school |elvtr| in the USA→ opened Laba in Ukraine, Hungary, Poland, Turkey, Romania and the Czech RepublicWhat comes next:→ in 2023, we will open offices in Germany, Spain, South Korea, Latin America and the UAE→ in 2024, we will become the largest educational project in Europe→ by 2025, we will increase the value of the company up to $1 billion and become a unicornTo make everything happen ????, we need a team of the best and currently looking for a B2B Project Manager to help us build all the processes from scratch, implement new ideas (no matter how crazy they are:), and adopt the project to the Hungarian market.Your sense of absolute ownership of your courses and your creativity will be what drives the team to success.
Task:
What you’ll be doing at Laba Hungary:Building up the processes between external stakeholders, speakers and our production and sales teamsInteraction with all the parties involved (speakers, sourcers, recruiters, project managers, content producers, etc)Reaching out to speakers and creating a pool of potential onesKeeping in touch with our speakersArranging and monitoring the process of signing the dealsResolving any misunderstandings along the wayUpdating the already existing processes and making them more efficient
Expectation:
We are going the same way if you have:Experience in process building/developmentStrong written and verbal communication skills with professional confidence and credibility, so that you can effectively engage and interact with senior and executive-level external stakeholders and speakersStrong business acumen combined with marketing or sales or key account management background, with the ability to quickly understand and communicate course ideas and their value to a wide range of audiencesHuge leadership potential1+ years of experience in project management managing moderately sized projects and programs from start to finishProven experience of managing and cooperating with the teams in fast-paced, change-intensive environmentsAbility to manage project schedules and associated dependencies, identify possible issues, risks, and actions, and work with key stakeholders to mitigate themKPI-driven mindsetAbility to manage multiple priorities effectively and thrive in an environment of uncertainty and changeOutgoing, energetic, upbeat, and fun personalityNative proficiency in Hungarian and basing in BudapestCool if you also have some of these:Experience in online education is a significant advantageKey account management experienceWe recognize our people by the following values:constant growth and goal achievement give you driveyou have courage to discuss problems and can find out-of-the-box ideas not only for your tasksyou don't separate your work from your life, you’re truly passionate about your work insteadyou want to build an international career and exchange experience with colleagues worldwideyou believe in online education and want to make it really useful all over the worldyou appreciate both the freedom of a startup and the systematicity of corporationsyou realize that the world is changing quickly, and you want to keep up with it
Other:
We offer:full-time result-oriented schedule (you are your own time manager, not the company time tracker;))work format — hybrid (3/2 or 2/3 on-site in Budapest and remote)In addition, you can expect:a team with a common and inspiring goal (we support each other and share experiences) challenging tasks that reveal your hidden talentsthe ability to shape the product and improve it (optimization is a continuous process)absence of rigid frameworks (you can offer rogue ideas, and together we will figure out how to implement them)skills development and Western markets growth (expanding the area of responsibility and promoting ourselves as professionals with diverse experience)free access to all our courses.
