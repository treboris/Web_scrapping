Allianz is one of the world's largest global insurance and financial service companies. We operate in more than 70 countries with over 150,000 employees. We have close to 100 million customers.
As a member of the international Allianz Group, Allianz Hungária Zrt. is the market leader in the Hungarian insurance market. Various professional awards are presenting the highest standard of work we do for our clients.
We are announcing an opening for the following position in our Budapest Office:
Junior Data Scientist
Main responsibilities:
You will participate in the analysis and solution of differentiated business problems by the following means:By leveraging ETL and data-transformation tools or scripts (e.g., SQL, Python), you will merge various internal and external data tables and sources, perform data quality checks, and build up complex data modelsYou will help create analytical solutions that range from designing, developing, and delivering code and models, to exploring meaningful insights, and translating them into actionable recommendationsYou’ll deal with big data sets across a variety of functions and geographiesYou will have the opportunity to get in-depth exposure to emerging methodologies and programming languages
Expectations:
M.Sc or B.Sc in a related field (analysis, actuarial, mathematics, physics etc.) – fresh graduates are welcomeKnowledge of SQL and PythonFluency in Hungarian and EnglishWillingness to learn about business processes and how they are translated into analytical tasksSolution and process-oriented focus, practical mindset Allianz Group is one of the most trusted insurance and asset management companies in the world.
