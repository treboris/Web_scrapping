At Ford Motor Company, we believe freedom of movement drives human progress. And as you start your career journey, we also believe in providing you with the freedom to define and realize your dreams. With all our incredible plans for the future, there’s no better place to jump start an amazing career, and access a better tomorrow, today.
Ford is one of the largest US-based automotive employers in Hungary. Operating in two locations - Budapest and Szentendre - with more than 600 highly skilled professionals on board, our team is responsible for leading and implementing Ford's business solutions strategy in Europe.
Ford Business Services is looking for a
Dashboard Coordinator
to join our National Sales Company Operating System team in Szentendre
As a Dashboard coordinator you will Contribute to the development of the business models with our dealerships across Europe. Suggest creative solutions, present it to the European top management while improving your business acumen and analytical skills. The main focus is on the communication with Business Owners, measurement of dealer KPIs and automation of business processes.
What you’ll love about us:
A great opportunity to get a comprehensive insight into how business processes work at one of the biggest car manufacturing company in the worldHybrid working modelNow you have a chance to create the footprint of the newest foundation of Ford. Your ideas will always be welcomeTest driving days with our most recent new products so you can see your daily job coming aliveAs an employee you can use your discount for new and used car purchase as well besides other benefits like cafeteriaShuttle bus service between local train station and the office
What will be your roles and responsibilities in the team?
Model E certification process and Excel reportingAlign operational details of Key Performance Indicator measurement with business ownersSupport Excel reporting setup, develop and maintain desk proceduresIdentify possible interfaces and seek automation opportunitiesSupport the scorecard setup process and governance Variable Dealer Margin reportingSupport Business owner request to harmonize and optimize workflows among dashboards and back-end database operated by the teamSet up user interface platform (HERMES) templates in connection with variable dealer margin reportingIdentify touch points between Model E compliance implementation to target leaner process execution Dealer Information Database (DID) and Dealer attestation processFamiliarize with Dealer Information Database functionality, maintain special attention to activity-based featuresSupport Business owner requests to change business logic in DID structureSet up and maintain dealer attestation process, collect and store Field Service Actions, consider future development and hosting solutions for this process Dealer Communication Center – Digital Case managementUnderstand market and user’s needs and collect development ideasWork together with the process reengineering team to identify a new application for this tool and find a new hosting solutionRun standard reporting and send to markets about main KPIs.Revisit training materials and provide training upon market requests
Professional experience and soft-skills that will help you in your everyday job:
Degree in economics, business informatics, marketing or similar fieldExcellent command of both written and spoken EnglishStrong analytical skill, advanced usage of Excel – reporting experienceExcellent communication skillsApplication of techniques to improve and automate processes – Alteryx knowledge is preferredSelf-motivated and ability to work independently – project management experience is preferredVery good problem solving skills
