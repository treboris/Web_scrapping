Customer Support Specialist
Have you ever contacted a call center in Hungary? Then it's likely your call passed through VCC Live's cloud contact center software. Our platform powers the majority of Hungary's call centers and is used in over 50+ countries worldwide.
On a busy day, VCC Live's multichannel platform handles millions of calls, text messages, and emails - and our channels continue to grow with the likes of WhatsApp, Messenger, and more. As we offer a highly customizable solution, we're always adding to our integration capabilities and our no-code configurability is a huge benefit for our clients who afford us with an NPS of 80+.
Ultimately, VCC Live presents a powerful, simple and straightforward experience for the thousands of contact center agents who are using nothing more than a laptop, headset, and an internet connection…
WHAT WILL YOU DO
Maintain contact with call center managers and supervisors, providing technical support and advice for customers by answering calls and emails.Help the development, and test new software features while taking into account our clients' points of view.Investigate customer-related technical issues along with our developer and operations team.Support other departments (sales, marketing, customer success management) to solve problems and identify major service issues.
WHO YOU ARE
You have outstanding IT skillsLogical thinkingCustomer-focused attitudeGood English speaking and writing skillsSpeak Hungarian on a native level
WHAT WE OFFER
The opportunity to work at a financially stable, growing company with a strong international presence and market position in Europe, America and East-Asia. A healthy work-life balance with flexible work options including plenty of home office days and additional vacation days.We commit to investing in your personal growth in the long run (attractive salary package, benefits and career opportunities). A multicultural work environment in the Budapest One office that was awarded “The Most Loveable Workplaces” prize.A company with regular team buildings, sport events, charity programs, birthday celebrations, sweets days! Mentorship and training opportunities from the start. We create your career path together to help you reach your maximum potential. Internal training sessions, school programs and language courses.
