Data Analyst
We are looking for a Data Analyst with a strong BI focus to join one of our leading Hungarian-based, innovative software development partners.
Task:
• identifies analytical needs of each department through one-to-one communications of key employees; duties specific to this function may include the assessment of the following: what decisions need to be made, why decisions need to be made, when decisions need to be made, and who will use the data and insights to make the decisions• design, implementation, and enhancement of reports, data models, data processes to support day-to-day business and strategical decisions• creating complex SQL queries for data extraction (from source systems) and appropriate transformation• produce analyses and interactive reports in Power BI, develop and operate quality assurance processes for the whole chain• negotiate new requirements with business users and data structures with source systems experts• support for building data strategy, data warehouse, reporting and dashboard systems• analyze data, deliver insights, and present them to the stakeholders• works closely together with the data engineers and specify development requests for them• assists users with data related tools, provide trainings if needed• communicates appropriately with business users to understand their needs• proceeds with appropriate actions that include requirements documentation, design and use• ensures that information is available as needed in a timely fashion and is accurate, valid and reliable
Expectation:
• 5+ years of data and analyses experience at a multi-national company• bachelor or higher degree from a university in Computer Science, Management Information Systems, Business, Management or Economics• experience in a business analytics/intelligence utilizing enterprise databases with large scale, complex data sets performing data mining and deep dive analysis• experience in Power BI, preferred Power BI Service in creating reports, dashboards, publishing datasets and setting up RLS• experience in building and transforming queries to different data sets using Power Query; data analysis, and ETL processes• relational database experience/SQL know-how and knowledge of data warehouse technologies• strong statistics and analytical skills with demonstrated proficiency in databases, query languages and data visualization tools• creation and maintenance of appropriate documentation to support the delivery of the service or project• business-oriented mindset, problem-solving skills, precise attention to detail, excellent client facing and stakeholder management skills• proficiency in English, communicative personality, insightfulness
