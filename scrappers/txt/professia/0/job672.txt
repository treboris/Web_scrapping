About us
SiemensWe are a technology company focused on industry, infrastructure, transport, and healthcare. By combining the real and the digital worlds, we empower our customers to transform their industries and markets, helping them to transform the everyday for billions of people.Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are looking for a senior front-end developer for a project creating cloud based repository of 3D building architecture plans. You will be working with cutting edge technologies in a scrum team which closely collaborates with teams in Switzerland and Germany on a solution intended to manage portfolio of architectural plans of buildings equipped with Siemens devices. Our goal is to implement highly scalable solution with attractive look & feel which will be used across the world by our external customers – building developers and our colleagues at Siemens.https://youtu.be/Kghi3uW9wXgIn this role you will be responsible for:• Analysis, design and implementation of user interface using JavaScript framework• Implementation of unit tests for your code• Document your design and code• Participate on scrum events – daily stand-up, sprint review, demo, retrospective• Communicating with product management and team members from other teams about interfaces and dependenciesMandatory skills:• Object oriented analysis, design and implementation• Knowledge of design patterns• Experience with JS frameworks (Angular 2 or higher)• Experience with unit testing JavaScript applications to ensure application qualityAt Siemens we are always challenging ourselves to build a better future.
