SAP Analytics Consultant
        For our growing SAP practice in IBM Slovakia, we are searching for SAP consultants with good skills in analytic areas such as BW, BO, and SAC. Depending on the project type, the role should cover the whole process, or just the particular phases of analysis, implementation, testing, and support. Whether you are junior or senior, we are expecting someone who is fair, who has good drive, is communicative - especially towards the client, and has willing to work on assigned tasks.
The position is located in Bratislava, Kosice, and Banska Bystrica.
Do you have some of these skills?
•    SAP BW, BW on HANA, BW4HANA
•    BW Modeling Tools (Eclipse)
•    Query Designer
•    Routines - (ABAP, AMDP)
•    HANA DB + Calculation Views, SDI, SDA
•    CDS Views
•    OData
•    SAP Data Intelligence + Python
•    SAP Business Objects + SQL
•    SAP Analytics Cloud
•    SAP Data Warehouse Cloud
•    English language (mandatory)
•    German language (advantage)
Are you?
•    Team player
•    Good in communication
•    Willing to travel (time-to-time)
•    Open to progress and learn
•    Coming up with new ideas
•    Not afraid to solve problems
What can we offer?
•    Salary starting from 2000 - 5000Eur/gross, based on your skill level
•    International project, that fits your experience and needs
•    Good vibe in the team
•    Opportunity to grow-up
•    Home office and various benefits listed here ibm.biz/benefits2021
 Please, do not miss the chance to get in touch with us, just because you have missed some of the criteria.
