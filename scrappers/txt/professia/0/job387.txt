Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesAre you looking for your first Software Developer of Testing position?Are you able to work full-time?Then keep reading about our Top-Level Trainee position!At PANTHEON.tech, we are looking for Java / Python / UI / Testing/GO/DevOps Trainees.Why is this job unique?It involves working on prototype projects, on something that no man has done before. Each and every task is a challenge.What will be your tasks?Writing code, of course, and learning a lot! We believe in small teams, but if you end up with a simple task, it might be completely up to you - the analysis, design, coding, and testing. More complex tasks are always assigned to teams.What do we expect from you?Independence is the key. We will not spoon-feed you - this is a job for the kind of people who do not like others looking over their shoulders. Also, we require a bit of perfectionism – since we do many open source projects, anybody can have a look at your code, so make sure that you have something to be proud of. But of course, you will have all the support and a buddy that will lead you.How high can you climb?To be a Trainee is just the beginning. In our company, career growth is available for everybody willing to constantly improve himself. Through regular employee assessments, there is a high possibility of promotion from Trainee through Junior, Semi-Senior, and Senior to an Expert. Since we are a family-based company, we will be glad to hear anybody´s ideas and we will happily support good projects. Our seniors are some of the best in the industry and they can teach you a lot. Employee perks, benefitsWe rely on an informal approach and a friendly atmosphere among all employees, regardless of position.•	Personal Day, Birthday Holiday•	Flexible working hours, Off-site work after the Academy•	Sick leave or TCFM leave cover•	Financial support for public transport & trainings•	Regular teambuildings, family environmentYou can choose from one of our three branches: Bratislava, Banská Bystrica or Žilina! Information about the selection processPantheon Academy is an excellent way to start your career as a Developer or Tester. It is available in two options, depending on the level of your current skills.The longer program called the Pantheon Academy Trainee program lasts for 6 months. Your salary starts at 950 euros.For more experienced beginners, we have the shorter program called the Advanced Pantheon Academy Trainee program, which lasts for only 3 months and the salary starts at 1,100 euros.In both options, your salary grows together with increasing experience, by 50 or 100 euros every month. In both options, the contract is limited to 6 months.By the end of our Academy, you will be ready to be promoted to Software Developer/Software Developer in Test.
