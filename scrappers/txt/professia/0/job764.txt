By combining the real and the digital worlds, we empower our customers to transform their industries and markets, helping them to transform the everyday for billions of people.Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are looking for a senior Java developer for a project aimed at developing a new generation of a fire safety system. Our goal is to implement highly scalable distributed system which will be used across the world.In this role you will be responsible for:• Analysis, design and implementation using Java• Documenting your design and code• Implementation of unit tests for your code• Coaching of junior team membersWe expect:• 5+ years of programming in Java SE• Expertise in object oriented analysis, design and implementation• Knowledge of design patterns• Experience with system architecture (framework) development• Knowledge of UML• Good analytical and problem solving skills• Good knowledge of EnglishNice to have skills:• SWT/Jface• JS frameworks (Angular 2 or higher)• Java for Embedded Systems• German
