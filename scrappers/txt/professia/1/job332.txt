SAP R/3 Finance (FI) Consultant
Place of work

full-time
Salary
2 500 EUR/monthSalary from 2 500 €  brutto /month + variable bonus
We are required by law to disclose basic wage component (minimum salary) for the advertised positions. This salary is for junior candidate. We carefully consider your professional qualifications and experience in our compensation package and/or when offer
About us
SiemensWe are a technology company focused on industry, infrastructure, transport, and healthcare. By combining the real and the digital worlds, we empower our customers to transform their industries and markets, helping them to transform the everyday for billions of people.Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are looking for an SAP FI consultant with experience working on related SAP area. You will work with integration with other SAP modules preferably CO & SD & MM.In addition it will be desirable to have also the following knowledge and experience: Project business requirements SAP Fiori Agile MethodologyNice to have: Knowledge and experience in Spiridon Finance processes (General Ledger & Account Receivable and Account Payable & Bank Accounts and Treasury & Fixed Assets & Closing and Reporting, ...) Knowledge and experience in Spiridon Finance specific funtionalities (Intra-Comapny business, Taxes, Account Determination, Credit Control, EDI, ... )Other prerequisites: Fluent English (written & spoken) is mandatory. German (written & spoken) is recommendable Management capability. Problem resolution capability. Team work.
Required education, skills and personality requirements
Required educationUniversity education (Bachelor's degree)Language skillsEnglish - Upper intermediate (B2)Other knowledgeSAP R/3 - Advanced
