About us
SiemensWe are a technology company focused on industry, infrastructure, transport, and healthcare. By combining the real and the digital worlds, we empower our customers to transform their industries and markets, helping them to transform the everyday for billions of people.Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are looking for an experienced Oracle administrator, who will help us with our long-time projects focused on delivering and integrating complex control center software systems to big international customers in energy field (energy generation, transport and distribution companies).Your responsibility will be database administration and optimalization (configuration, installation, backup strategy, upgrades, restores, tuning, planning, scripting)We expect:• 3+ years of Oracle database administration • experience on UNIX/Linux• strong knowledge of SQL, OEM, SQL optimalization, SQL trace• experience with RHEL (7) administration• advanced experience with KSH/Bash scriptingNice to have:• experience with Java, Perl and Python are welcome• big plus experience with Qt, GDB
