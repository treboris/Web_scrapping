Senior React Developer for brand new e-shop frontendGymBeam s.r.o.
Place of work
Remote work
Contract type
full-time, trade licence
Start date
ASAP
Wage (gross)
2 800 - 3 800 EUR/monthContract from 188 to 255 € per day
Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are  looking for a highly skilled and experienced Senior React Developer to design and implement a brand new storefront for the biggest e-commerce platform for 160 million fitness enthusiasts in the CEE region:
-   we are the fastest growing fitness company in Europe;
-   our e-shop serves 2 000 000 customers in 14 countries;
-   it had 250 000 000 pageviews last year;
-   we expect 3 000 000 orders on our e-shop this year;
-   a brand new storefront is GymBeam's goal for this year, and you are going to be a part of a fast-moving senior team.
The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of modern web technologies and frameworks, and will be responsible for the technology stack, improving the infrastructure, and ensuring the storefront is highly-performing, secure, and optimized for SEO.
GYMBEAM’S TECH TEAM OVERVIEW:
-   34 people in 7 teams working in IT;
-   Software Developers, QA Engineers, Data Engineers, Product Owners, Agile Delivery Manager;
-   Essential technologies: PHP, Python, TypeScript;
-   Own QA and Product teams.
WHAT WILL BE YOUR JOB:
-   designing and implementing a new storefront for our e-commerce project using React and and TypeScript;
-   developing and maintain front-end features, server-side rendering components and business logic consuming GraphQL APIs;
-   building reusable components and optimizing them for maximum performance;
-   proposing technical solutions to boost SEO and levy edge computing;
-   writing clean, efficient, and testable code that conforms to best practices;
-   improving our CI/CD pipeline and DevOps practices;
-   staying up-to-date with the latest developments in frontend technologies;
-   participating in code reviews, testing, and debugging to ensure high-quality code;
-   helping other people on your team to be their best.
TECH STACK WE USE ON THIS TEAM:
-   React and TypeScript;
-   Next.js, Vite and Tailwind;
-   GraphQL, Elasticsearch and Cloudflare;
-   Docker and Kubernetes;
-   Cypress for test automation;
-   Git. Employee perks, benefits- work-life balance with flexible schedules and remote work options;
- all overtimes paid out automatically;
- shopping at our e-shop at cost prices and discounts for family and friends;
- e-learning courses at LinkedIn and Udemy;
- online certifications, trainings, conferences and elaborate personal development plan;
- your own work computer (windows OS/mac) and additional IT accessories;
- team and company-wide events as well as coworking spaces at Brno, Praha and Budapest;
- gyms and recreational facilities memberships (Multisport card);
- open-air rooftop gym and chillout areas in our Kosice office;
- lunches for 1 € twice a day in our Kosice office or compensation otherwise;
- extra week of vacation when your baby is born. Requirements for the employee Candidates with education suit the positionSecondary with school-leaving examination
Follow-up/Higher Professional Education
University education (Bachelor's degree)
University education (Master's degree)Language skillsEnglish - Upper intermediate (B2)Number of years of experience4Personality requirements and skills-   you are a passionate Senior React Developer who is looking for an opportunity to work on an exciting e-commerce project;
-   at least 4 years of experience in React development, preferably in the e-commerce space;
-   strong understanding of modern web technologies and frameworks;
-   thorough understanding of React.js and its core principles;
-   experience with popular React.js workflows (such as Flux or Redux), data structure libraries (e.g., Immutable.js), and common front-end development tools (such as Webpack, NPM, or Vite);
-   knowledge of SEO best practices and experience in implementing them in web applications;
-   familiarity with modern front-end build pipelines and tools;
-   familiarity with state management libraries;
-   a knack for benchmarking and optimization;
-   preferably experience with high-performing web applications and edge-computing;
-   you prize learning and personal growth;
-   you like moving fast, getting things done, and delivering value to users. Advertiser Brief description of the companyGymBeam is an international company from its very beginning. Currently operating in 14 markets in Europe, GymBeam is one of the fastest-growing e-commerce projects in the CEE region. We are experts in sports nutrition, healthy functional food and sports accessories. We are the producer of our 6 brands and another 700+ products. Our e-shop also resells more than 9000 products of 120 brands. We serve over 5 million customers and this base is growing exponentially. To respond to our ultra-fast growth we are hiring across the whole company and in almost all departments. We consider all teams as a key to strengthen our continued growth in the future!
