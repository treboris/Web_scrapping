TRANSFORMATION NEEDS TECHNOLOGY AND TALENTS
SAP Junior Developer
                                Location: 
                            Bratislava, Slovakia (Job with occasional home office)
                                Minimum salary: 
1 700 EUR/month
                                Contract type: 
                            full-time, part-time, agreement-based (Temporary jobs)
Your roleDuring your initial training, you will learn SAP’s programming language ABAP and you will be introduced into our business.After the initial training you will continue to learn new SAP-related skills while in parallel working on development projects. On these projects you will start with programming according to specification and design papers. Depending on your skills, you will participate on creating the design papers and even create software designs yourself.In our development team you will be responsible for maintaining and extending our software portfolio. We develop software as the basis for our tool-based consulting services, which we offer to SAP and SAP customers. Our consultants/ customers use these tools for SAP system analysis, system consolidations, data migrations and conversions.Details of your tasks regarding the job will vary based on your skills, your experience and competencies. Your skills You are a graduate of a technical/IT- oriented university, or you are in the last two semesters of studying and just about to graduate You are motivated and willing to work as a part of a young team and learn new things You have intermediate skills in English language You know at least one programming language like Delphi, Java/J2EE, C/C++, or Visual Basic Knowledge of one of programming languages as JavaScript, HTML and operating systems UNIX/Linux on basic level is beneficial You know the basics of SQL and database systems SAP‘s programming language ABAP is not required, but beneficial Benefits Being part of a dedicated, young and dynamic team Working in an international environment Flexible work times Extensive product and process training and knowledge transfer Growth opportunities and chances of long-term employment Possibility to improve your language skills Possibility to travel abroad Additional company benefits Apply Now
