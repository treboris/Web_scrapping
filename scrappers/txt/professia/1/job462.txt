React Developer for TelematicsSygic, a. s.
Place of work
Sygic, Mlynské nivy, Bratislava, Slovensko (Job with occasional home office)
Contract type
full-time
Wage (gross)
2 000 - 4 000 EUR/monthThe minimum salary is 2000 - 4000 EUR/ brutto. The final salary is upon agreement.
Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesThe job is to develop web applications (NodeJS + React) for vehicle monitoring, fleet management, or transport management. You will create a new pan-European fleet management system for 500,000 vehicles. We currently run www.dispencer. Sk, www.webdispecink.cz and telematics.eurowag.com and the Europe-wide transport management system - Transport manager. This is working on web portals with thousands of users daily. We use the latest technology. We are looking for a relaxed person who finds working on complex challenging tasks.
