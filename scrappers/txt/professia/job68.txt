Information about the position Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are looking for experienced Developers for SharePoint / M365 / Power Platform (PowerApps, Power Automate, MS Flow). As a member of an international team, you will work mostly for customers from DACH region.

• Conception and creation of interfaces
• Requirements gathering, functional design, implementation, configuration, testing and deployment
• Maintenance of existing applications based on PowerApps and Microsoft Forms
• Independent implementation of projects after coordination of the requirements with the business users
• Active communication with the customers, developers, and other team members Employee perks, benefits• Familiarity of a small sized firm and security and possibilities of a large company
