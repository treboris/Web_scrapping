Job description, responsibilities and dutiesWe are looking for a student interested in SW development who is willing to get an experience in SW development for various healthcare products. We want to involve you in our student’s mentoring program, learning and experimenting.Brief expectations:• At least intermediate knowledqe of C#, C++, python• Testing skillsBasic salary component (gross) and other remuneration: The offered remuneration is from 6€ gross / hour.
