Our client is an international company in the automotive industry who are looking for a software project manager who will take part in the company's development in figuring out new and innovative solutions.
Direct and manage project development, coordinate the software development activities
Plan the software project in collaboration with the project team
Manage issues, participate and lead problem solving which are relevant for the software
Create and maintain project related documentation, report the project state to the management
Managing escalation situations
Ensure proper communication to the project team and to the customer
Coordinate release activities to be able to achieve project milestones
