Frontend Developer 
Your new company
Do you want beneficial technologies being shaped by your ideas? Whether in the areas of mobility solutions, consumer goods, industrial technology or energy and building technology – with our Partner, you will have the chance to improve quality of life all across the globe.
Your new role
You will be part of our Partner's full-stack (Java, Angular, Flutter) agile software developer team, actively contributing to guide and execute our Partner's vision with a cross-functional logistics and transport solution  used world-wide at our Partner with thousands of users.
You will develop production ready and stable web-frontend communicating with a cloud-hosted (Azure), microservices based API
You will participate in project architecture planning, building our Partner's vision
You will use your creativity creating the UI and researching new technologies
Based on your interests you can participate in UX planning of web and mobile frontends
What you'll need to succeed
Education: Bachelor’s / Master's degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Information Systems or comparable working experience
► Personality and Working Practice: team player, good communication skills, analytical thinking, structured way of working, agile mindset, “can-do” mentality, sincere interest in new trends and technologies
► Hands-on Experience and Knowledge: Solid experience in Angular 14, TypeScript, JavaScript, HTML, CSS/SCSS, experienced with working with REST APIs. Familiar with test automation and clean code principles.
► Languages: fluent command of English
What you'll get in return
► Competitive compensation package incl: salary, annual & performance bonus, cafeteria
► Flexible work arrangements:
With high % of home-office opportunity, that remains after the pandemic
Flexibility in location & timing – in accordance with team’s target & alignment
► Bleeding edge technologies in our  Partner's projects
► International, diverse, inclusive company culture supported with transformational leadership mindset
► Continuous learning & growth opportunity –  Our Partner offers wide range of development programs and trainings to ensure that you’ re ready for the next challenge
► Wellbeing programs including Medical & Mental support
► Organized events in various theme such as Sport, Cultural & Educational also, many self-organized activities supported by the company
► Childcare opportunity
What you need to do now
If you're interested in this role, click 'apply now' to forward an up-to-date copy of your CV, or call us now.
If this job isn't quite right for you but you are looking for a new position, please contact us for a confidential discussion on your career.
