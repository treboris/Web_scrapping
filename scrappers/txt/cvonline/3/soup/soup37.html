Cloud Analyzer Specialit

Your new company

Mobile networks are the core to accelerating digital transformation and building our connected IoT society where everything that can be connected will be connected. These networks not only handle huge amounts of data but also generate tremendous performance and log data that allows advanced ML automation techniques to optimize their complex operation. It’s mission is pursuing data-driven operations boosted by AI on the latest Cloud technologies to improve customer experience. In our unit, we collect, ingest, and archive data from it's customers' networks all over the world. For more information on our presence and activities in Hungary, please visit Areas in Hungary.

Your new role

  • Work for the Network Support Service, taking an active role in data-driven fault isolation in Cloud environments.
  • Integrate new data management technologies and software engineering tools into existing structures for localizing errors and incidents in Cloud-based networks
  • Support/assist the customers with initial Network Support Service deployment using the combination of product knowledge, solution-level expertise, and data-driven support capabilities.
  • Work in a DevOps team: support engineers in troubleshooting and creating new rules in the fault isolation expert system
  • Collaborate, and communicate in an international environment, both with colleagues internally as well as customers and suppliers of the company.
  • You may have the opportunity to travel internationally and work in different cultural environments.

What you'll need to succeed

  • Deep knowledge of Unix/Linux and IP Technologies
  • Experience with Docker/Kubernetes
  • Scripting capabilities - as a minimum bash(shell) and Python.
  • Practical knowledge of Cloud platforms (RH, Azure, AWS), willingness to gain a deeper understanding of the field
  • Communications/presentation skills
  • A good sense for solving problems and an analytical approach
  • Customer handling attitude

What you need to do now

If this job isn't quite right for you but you are looking for a new position, please contact us for a confidential discussion on your career.
