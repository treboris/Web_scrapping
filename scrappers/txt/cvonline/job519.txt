ÁLLÁSHIRDETÉS FELADÁS
CAD and Application Engineer 
Let’s be successful together!
Our multinational Partner is looking for an experienced colleague for the position below:
Receiving and transmitting the CAD files among internal and external partners;
Organizing prototyping builds, testing & CAD design iterations;
Designing and modeling of the new products, existing products and its test fixtures;
Draw 2D drawings according to the standards in place;
Realize and document the 3D models and the 2D drawings (with dimensioning and tolerancing) by keeping the requirements expressed and respect the deadlines by the requestor;
Manages the computer (workstation) equipped with CAD software;
Manages the CAD softwares, its licences in order to keep it up-to-date and working;
Know how to use CAD environments, receiving / transmitting CAD documents via different systems (Creo / Catia);
Support Technical Development Managers during the industrialization of the design;
Contact suppliers to clarify technical details, feasibility;
Support customer and program needs through customer and plant visits;
Establish and maintain positive customer relations, through analysis and resolutions of customer issues;
Communicate customer expectations and technological trends to group engineering, sales, and production plants;
Coordinate customer responses, relying on effective communication with Advanced Engineering, Sales, and the production plants;
New project handover to production and support also serial production;
Lead and Coordinate Change Management projects.The registration No. of Hire-One Ltds. is this: 3376/2008.
Elvárások: University degree in Mechanical or Automotive Engineering;
Knowledge of technical designing, sizing and tolerancing acc.to ISO standards (GD&T);
Knowledge of material forming techniques (stamping, bending, laser cutting);
CAD software knowledge of Creo and/or CATIA or Inventor;
Min. 3 years of experience in Automotive Industry (as CAD, product, process or project engineer);
Experience in project management and APQP, IATF16949; advanced PC skills;
Language skills: fluency in English.
Amit nyújtunk: Work in young motivated multinational team
Learn from experienced team members (in house and abroad)
Continuous developement through new projects and related technology
Opportunity to visit other plants in France, Germany, and North America for training
Opportunity to get all necessary trainings (professional and soft skill)
Competitive compensation package including SZÉP Card benefit support, and fitness ticket support
Előnyök: German language knowledge
Metallurgical knowledge
Knowledge in combustion engine and E-mobility  Strong Analytical, problem solving and inter-personal skills
Superior communication skills, self-starter, and results-orientated personality
