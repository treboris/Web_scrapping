Senior Azure DevOps Engineer 
Country: Hungary (+36) • Budapest
Description:
We are looking for a Senior Azure DevOps Engineer for a new venture of one of the region's most established multinational company.
Duties:
•- Support the Capability Maturity Model development and roadmap execution of the DevOps Domain
•- Setup the DevOps Infrastructure, Environments, Tooling & Utilities
•- Support the Release & Configuration Management of the core Product on normal and hotfix release tracks
•- Work closely with Azure Cloud Architects, Security Architect, QA Engineers, Full Stack Developers for the successful development and operations of the DevOps capabilities
Requirements and skills:
•- Strong understanding of Continuous Integration, Delivery and Deployment conceptsx
•- Have at least 3 years of professional experience (min. 7 years preferred) as a DevOps engineer, any experience in RunOps, SysOps or SecOps is a plus
•- Have hands on with following DevOps Technologies: Microsoft Azure, Azure DevOps Pipelines, Docker, Kubernetes / OpenShift, Atlassian JIRA, Confluence, Ansible, Terraform, Helm, Gitlab, GitHub, Bitbucket, Jenkins, SonarQube
•- Have Delivery Experience in Agile Development, Scrum, Kanban Delivery Methodologies
Command of languages:
Good command of English
Client company profile:
Our client company is a new venture for one of the region's most established multinational company. Strong backing and commitment from the Shareholders
What they offer:
• Competitive Compensation Package and Incentive System (13th and 14th Month salary bonus + Annual financial bonus based on company goals)
• 3 days home office/week, flexible working hours
• Less Regulation, Fast Moving Agile Organization
• State of the Art Technologies and Capabilities relying on in-house developed Products and 3rd Party Cloud SaaS / PaaS
• Lots of room for career growth
• Efficient Business Operations, lean Processes and low level of Bureaucracy
• Regional ambition, potential international expansion ahead
