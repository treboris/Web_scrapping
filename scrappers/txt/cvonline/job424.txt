ÁLLÁSHIRDETÉS FELADÁS
Vulnerability Management Specialist 
About this opportunity 
Customer Support newly established a team that is responsible for analyzing and answering to the customers’ security and vulnerability management related questions on products. 
What you will do   
Perform analysis of customer security scans on products 
Investigate suspected vulnerabilities in deployed products / network parts 
Use Customer Support knowledge base, Ericsson Vulnerability Management Service (EVMS) and security advisories when analyzing the Customer security queries 
Write Technical Answers to the customers, and advice on the fixes, mitigations, and configurations 
Maintain Customer Support knowledge base on security related topics 
You will bring   
Bachelor’s degree and/or equivalent experience 
Basic understanding and interest on Security, Risk and Vulnerability Management 
Ability to communicate and collaborate efficiently 
Strong problem-solving skills, results-oriented, and a good teammate 
Friendly and service minded orientation 
Proficient in English (verbal and written) 
It's an advantage if you have   
Knowledge of common vulnerability databases (MITRE, NVD) 
Knowledge of Information Security related standards and regulation, including ISO/IEC 27001, Data Privacy Legislation 
Experience with vulnerability scanning tools and in cloud security 
Good understanding of Linux 
Writing Python scripts 
Knowledge of security standards or some security certificate

If this job isn't quite right for you but you are looking for a new position, please contact us for a confidential discussion on your career.
