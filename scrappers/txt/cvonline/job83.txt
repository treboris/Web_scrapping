ENGINEERING SUPPORT (DEBRECEN) 
At Diehl: The future is in your hands!
More than 17,000 employees are currently shaping the engineering of the future in our five Corporate Divisions Metall, Controls, Defence, Aviation and Metering. At Diehl Aviation, an efficient and dedicated network of aviation experts constantly develops and produces the state of the art in avionics systems, cabin elements and cabin lighting – from high-quality composite components, cabin fittings and electronics in commercial aircraft to highly integrated bathrooms and galleys. Apply at Diehl and join hands with fascinating technologies and a unique corporate tradition of stability and trust.
Engineering Support (Debrecen)
Your tasks would be:
Delivering/converting 2D/3D data for internal and external partners.
Ensuring incoming requests are processed precisely and in a timely manner.
Teamwork with other Data Exchange colleagues, giving feedback on operational issues.
Requirements of the application:
IT affinity (advanced use of MS Windows 7 /10 , CITRIX environments, knowledge of file extension meaning and correlation)
affinity to work in a heterogeneous cross-company IT environments
ability to read, understand and create technical documentation in English (German is an advantage)
willingness to work within highly reactive issue-reporting-processes
multitasking ability
the ability to work independently and precisely
the ability to cope with repetitive tasks
the ability to communicate in an effective and assertive way
