Pozíció leírása / Job description
Our Client is one of the leading investment banks in the world, and for their growing professional team we are looking for an Senior Automation Developer.
Developing and encouraging the adoption of standard approaches that:
improve testing processes (unit testing, code review process)
improve current quality control processes
help create automation workflows in a resource effective way
Identify recurring error sources to decrease the number of issues emerging after handover to Business users
Assure the continuity of business for stakeholders that utilize the automation solutions developed by the entire digital team by:
Handling incidents, urgent change requests and urgent fixes in connection with workflows that are in production
Assuring that maintenance development artefacts adhere to internal standards and policies
Monitoring and assessing the risk of outdated standard components applied in workflows that are in production
Identifying, recommending and implementing process improvements to enhance testing strategies
Creating proposals for workflow retrofit with the involvement of technology and reporting teams
Driving the retrofitting process of workflows that contain outdated standard components.
Developing automated solutions in KNIME data transformation and automation tool
Incidents may need to be addressed on bank holidays and after-hours occasionally, very rarely on weekends.
                Elvárások / Requirements
Knowledge and Experience
Experience in full software development lifecycle (design, development, testing, maintenance) and QA methodologies
Experience with a data transformation tool (e.g. KNIME, Alteryx, Qliksense, ETL tools)
Knowledge of scripting languages (e.g. Python, Javascript, VBA)
Excellent command of the English language (both written and spoken)
Experience in financial sector is an advantage
Familiarity with software testing methodologies is an advantage
Experience with software quality control is a plus
Skills
Excellent analytical skills
Strong presentation and visual story telling skills
Ability to deal with a range of senior/junior staff across a number of disciplines
Ability to work autonomously
Excellent ability to pay attention to details and work under pressure
Confident ability to achieve objectives via working with and influencing others
Qualifications
University / College degree or equivalent in Computer Science or related Information Technology, Business Informatics or Finance field
5+ years of relevant work experience
Competencies
Resourceful and creative in solving problems and overcoming obstacles
Motivated to create sustainable and elegant modular solutions
Self-starter approach
Enthusiastic team player
