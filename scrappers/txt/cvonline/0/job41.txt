MLOps Engineer 
Your new company
International Telco company is looking for candidates to expand the MLOps team, and develop an AI platform using cutting-edge infrastructure technologies.
Your new role
- Ensure that scalable IT infrastructures are created in the cloud.
- Build robust data pipelines using data from our customers. 
- You will integrate ML models into ML infrastructure and ensure that they function correctly. 
- Implementation of AI and ML automation 
- You are responsible for different CI/CD pipelines - private and public cloud providers (e.g. AWS) 
- Continuous Deployment (automation, and SW quality aspects) 
- Gitlab Pipelines
What you'll need to succeed
- Container architectures (K8S Docker) 
- Ingress Control 
- Templating of k8s manifests 
- Experience in programming (ideally Python) 
- Exposure to public cloud providers (ideally AWS) 
- Experience in logging and monitoring (ELK) 
- Experience in developing/consuming RESTful API 
- Docker Security 
- Linux experience
What you'll get in return
- Flexible home office (full remote if preferred)
- Annual bonus
- Comprehensive benefit system
- Internal and external training
What you need to do now
If you're interested in this role, click 'apply now' to forward an up-to-date copy of your CV, or call us now.
If this job isn't quite right for you but you are looking for a new position, please contact us for a confidential discussion on your career.
