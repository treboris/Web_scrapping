Controlling business partner - REF1538A 
 Description 
Responsible for delivering normal Business Unit’s controlling tasks with full P&L responsibility (Revenue + Cost).
Participation in forecasting and periodic (monthly, quarterly, annual) closings.
CO Business Partner for the given unit(s).
Profitability analysis in various dimensions, management decision support.
Support the business and management with ad-hoc analyses, tasks.
Participation in various internal and business unit related projects
Work with leading financial and reporting systems: MS PowerBI, SAP, SQL.
  Qualifications 
3-5 years of working experience in the field of controlling.
MSc or BSc preferably in Finance/Accounting.
Active, minimum of intermediate level of English knowledge.
Strong MS Office skills, specially Excel and Power Point (incl. waterfall charts, diagramms) (Excel will be tested in the selection process)
Experience with SAP, SQL or MS PowerBI is an advantage.
Accurate and proactive personality with strong communication skills.
Excellent analytical and problem solving skills.
Business oriented approach, openness for new technical solutions.
Team-player but ability to work individually, taking responsibility over his/her work.
Additional Information
* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
