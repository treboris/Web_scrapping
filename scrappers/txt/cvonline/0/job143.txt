Our partner is a global supplier of software engineering and IT outsourcing services, and they are looking for a Java Developer. You’ll be part of a team of professionals who are ready to find the best tailor-made IT solutions for their multinational clients in various industries and solve complex problems.
 What you will do in the first three months:

Practical study of new material;
Complete internship involving real projects with the application of the studied technologies

Knowledge of Spring and Hibernate frameworks;Knowledge of JavaScript and basic frameworks such as Angular or React
