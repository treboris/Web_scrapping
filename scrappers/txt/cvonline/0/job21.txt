C++ developer 
Job description
For 35 years our partner has been offering software solutions which are specifically created for infrastructure and civil engineering. Mensura, their program is an infrastructure design CAD software, with more than 12,000 licenses installed. It offers an entire production chain from processing the survey points to CAD design and 3D modelling. It is a civil engineering design and drafting software, used by civil engineers, land surveyors, construction contractors and draftsmen. Their company is a pure software, cloud and mobile solutions provider for the construction sector and has driven the digitalization of its ecosystem over two decades. They support every stakeholder (e.g. civil engineers, land surveyors, contractors, network owners, engineering design offices and public authorities) involved in the infrastructure, building sites and urban resource management ecosystems, offering an innovative and efficient approach to digital transition, building information and city information modelling.The ideal candidate will be responsible for developing our flagship product, the above mentioned Mensura.
Responsibilities
Developing 3D visualization parts of Mensura
Participate in all stages of software development (analysis, design, implementation, testing, deployment, maintenance)
Write well-designed, highly scalable and testable code
Analyze and maintain existing software applications, implement change requirements
Determine and fix programming bugs
Participation in international projects
Qualifications
Strong C++ programming skills and experience with working in large codebases
Knowledge of MS Visual Studio
Analytical approach with an emphasis on accuracy
Excellent problem-solving abilities with strong attention to details
Ability to work independently, proactivity
Up-to-date English knowledge for correspondence and processing technical documents
Benefits
High standard working conditions
Flexible working hours arrangements and home office possibility
Competitive salary and compensation package Location: Budapest, XII. district
