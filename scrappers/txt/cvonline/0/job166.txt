Pozíció leírása / Job description
Our partner company is a worldwide leading supplier of lottery products. With more than 8,500 employees globally and currently serves more than 400 customers on six continents today.
Works closely with our customer, functional business units, and software developers to administer the customer’s system to accomplish the operation 
There is a shift from 22:00 PM-02:00 AM. But the candidate has to do work actively when there is an actual task
During the other period, and on the weekends, there is an on-call, which is normally at 3 days/week
Provision and operation of customer’s system 
Current upkeep, care, maintenance, and upgrades 
Analysis and removal of occurring problems according to documentation 
Performing backups and restores 
Constant communication with involved units and partners 
Reporting 
                Elvárások / Requirements
 Good knowledge of Linux systems is mandatory, Oracle and Application Servers are desirable 
 IP networking, virtualization, and backup systems experience is required 
 Basic knowledge of SAN, FC, and storage systems 
Work experience with server hardware 
Excellent English (verbal & written) is mandatory, and good German is desirable 
Stress-resistant, team player, ready to work in shifts 
Previous experience in working according to ITIL-based processes and functions is desirable 
Minimum of 3 year’s relevant experience 
A bachelor degree is an advantage 
                Amit nyújtunk / Benefits
Economically stable and growing enterprise in a high-tech environment 
Excellent working atmosphere 
Competitive remuneration package
