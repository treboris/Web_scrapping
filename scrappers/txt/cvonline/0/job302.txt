Pozíció leírása / Job description
Our partner is a global supplier of software engineering and IT outsourcing services. As a DevOps Engineer, you’ll be part of a team of professionals who are ready to find the best tailor-made IT solutions for their multinational clients in various industries and solve complex problems.
Your responsibilities will be: 
Deploying and configuring microservices applications on AWS;CI/CD process development and support;Support and administration of infrastructures on AWS;Active participation in automating processes, and implementing new systems;Making sure the systems are available, well-optimized and well-maintained to meet clients’ expectations;Keeping contacts and coordinating with various departments
                Elvárások / Requirements
Experience in administering Linux/UNIX and Windows systems (must be practical, at least two years);Experience with web servers and deployment of web applications;Experience with AWS cloud systems;Experience with Docker and Kubernetes;Experience with MongoDB, MySQL;Practical CI/CD automation and organization skills (Jenkins, CodeBuild, CodeDeploy, CodePipeline);Knowledge of bash/shell;Ansible automation experience is desirable;Knowledge of Python is desirable;Knowledge of English at the intermediate level is required 
                Amit nyújtunk / Benefits
Stable long-term work environment;Competitive salary with a flexible system of revisions;Benefits and social packages;Comfortable A+ office in Budapest;Every necessary tool and device in the office will be provided to comfortably perform all project tasks: computers, meeting rooms, spacious modern kitchens with professional coffee machines, comfortable recreation areas with game consoles, board games, and a selection of literature for every taste;Paid English courses and conversation clubs;Opportunities for professional and personal growth;Mentoring program, internal and external professional training programs;Support and care from our client's friendly team
