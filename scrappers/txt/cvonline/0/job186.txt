ÁLLÁSHIRDETÉS FELADÁS
DevOps Engineer 
Your new role
Actively further developing our DevOps toolchain and implementing new test and deployment specifications
Conducting configuration and package management
Ensuring that any operational requirements are validated by the DevOps toolchain before going live
Ensuring that our development, test and operating environments are set up in a standardised and automated manner
Actively managing stakeholders and continuously enhancing our DevOps culture
Coordinating regular platform releases
What you'll need to succeed
Degree or similar qualification (apprenticeship) with a focus on IT
Knowledge of agile software development using scripting languages (e.g. PHP) and agile methods
Experience with modern public cloud environments (preferably AWS) and containerised applications
Experience with the automation of software releases using CI/CD pipelines as well as conducting automated test procedures and using programs to create and evaluate code metrics
Experience using automation tools for orchestration, configuration and administration and with using infrastructure-as-code technologies
Strong communication skills and team spirit as well as fluency in English
What you'll get in return
Possibility to improve yourself in a constantly growing organization
Secure, long-term job opportunity at a stable company
An above average salary
Intensive, individual, and practical training
Varied and interesting responsibilities
Modern and comfortable working environment (adjustable desk and chair, sports facilities)
Home office possibility
What you need to do now
If you're interested in this role, click 'apply now' to forward an up-to-date copy of your CV, or call us now.
If this job isn't quite right for you but you are looking for a new position, please contact us for a confidential discussion on your career.
