Your role:
DELMIA Quintiq, as part of Dassault Systèmes, is looking for an ambitioned newcomer or experienced Infrastructure Consultant (m/f/d) to join the Services department and participate in designing, delivering, and deploying state-of-the-art software solutions for our customers’ supply chain puzzles in manufacturing, logistics, and many other industries. Our suite of applications provides world-class optimization and decision support for everything from demand forecasting and capacity planning to detailed scheduling of delivery routes, workforce, and production lines. Your responsibility is to define the technical architecture, the integration design, and the deployment in our projects being in touch with Kafka, Azur, Cloud, and other tools.
Your responsibilities:
Design technical architecture concepts and support the functional targets of the planning solution by working closely together with other specialists from Dassault Systèmes and the customer team
Work with the “big names” of the market and drive innovative solutions
Lead the technical integration design and drive the hardware/network/infrastructure assessment
Deploy, operate and maintain DS products on the customer's site
Ensure that the design and the deployment of the technical solution are aligned with the security, performance, scalability, and capacity expectations of the customer
Tuning of DS products and configuration of a related key software component such as Operating System, web servers, and integration components (SAML, databases, middleware etc.)
Your qualifications:
Obtained a degree in software engineering (or equivalent) specialized in hardware or system architecture
Relevant background and demonstrated experience in infrastructure consulting, preferably in a supply chain or production planning context
Knowledge of mainstream integration patterns, technologies, and systems such as ODBC, database systems, Kafka, SOAP, REST, and JMS
Diligence, an eye for detail, and a clear head when managing deadlines and multiple stakeholders
Good communications skills, customer-oriented, flexible mindset, technology passionate
Excellent English skills are mandatory and German skills are desired, as well as willingness to travel as needed within the DACH region
What's in it for you?
A permanent job in a sustainable, innovative, science-oriented international company 
An open, creative, dynamic work environment in a motivated team  
Varied challenging and rewarding tasks 
Attractive training opportunities, supportive colleagues
