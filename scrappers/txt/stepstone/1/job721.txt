Gruppenbeschreibung
Magna's ADAS technologies are shaping an autonomous future and improving lives. Full autonomy is coming. But to create the technology which will bring it into view, you need the desire and ability to adapt complete ADAS systems or modular solutions to any vehicle, for any mobility need. Magna's electronics solutions connect OEMs to their customers with features that improve their lives. It's our expertise across the entire vehicle, key strategic partnerships and culture of innovation that allow us to make our industry-leading technologies road-ready. And it is how we'll continue to build a safer, smarter and more connected mobility experience for all people.
Stellenbeschreibung
Key Responsibilities
Review Customer specifications and needs on various ADAS product lines
Align with customer on expected solutions and target price
Align with Product Line Management and Core Engineering on technical concepts and reuse potential
Define technical scope and boundaries of customer quotation
Create technical solutions and system architecture to satisfy customer needs and align with all engineering disciplines (SW, Functions, Safety, Security, Mechanical, Electrical, testing)
Present technical solutions to internal management as well as to customer
Ensure technical solutions are technically advanced, cost efficient and align with customer needs to ensure Magna's market position remain superior to competitors
Support Engineering teams to evaluate and estimate solutions and effort
Key Qualifications / Requirements
Bachelor's degree in computer engineering / software engineering / electrical engineering / computer science or equivalent (Master's preferred)
Minimum 5-year experience in the ADAS and AD automotive domain with experience in key ADAS technologies in Camera, radar, lidar and ultrasonic systems including software
Multiple years relevant experience regarding the responsibility / activities (listed above) for automotive electronics serial development projects (mandatory)
Experience with systems engineering, system architecture development as well as with Functional Safety and Cyber Security
Knowledge of analysis and engineering tools such as version control/configuration management, UML tools, static code check tools
Experience with ALM tools (PTC Integrity), Requirements management tools and task management tools necessary
Experience with ADAS simulation tools such as carmaker
Experience with multiple SoCs and Microprocessors
Experience with internal (inside ECU) communication protocols as well as external communication protocols (CAN, Ethernet,,,etc.)
Comprehensive knowledge of PTC Integrity, Enterprise Architect, DOORS, Rhapsody, MATLAB-Simulink and signal processing toolbox (or similar)
Comprehensive knowledge of English (verbal and written)
