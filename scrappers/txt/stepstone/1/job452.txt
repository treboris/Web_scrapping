Ihre Rolle
Build up strong domain expertise in industrial applications and drive the design and implementation of software products for our customers in the manufacturing industries, using a state-of-the-art software stack  
Engage with customers, product management and your team in understanding customer requirements, and deriving robust and future-proof implementations 
Identify DevOps requirements to make sure that product lifecycle management and operational availability is guaranteed for our customers 
Drive engineering initiatives to address DevOps requirements and contribute to improving the overall development process 
Participate in cross-functional tasks such as customer research, product design and recruiting case interviews
Ihr Profil
Prior software development experience in large software applications, preferably in typical enterprise software frameworks based on .NET, C# or Java  
Strong experience with a leading cloud product offering in a software product context, preferably from an operations perspective. 
Hands-On experience with Microsoft Azure services, security concepts and administration 
Excellent communication skills in written and verbal English 
Willingness to shape the role of software in an established industrial company, a mindset to continuously drive improvement throughout our organization and high motivation to work in a distributed, international team
