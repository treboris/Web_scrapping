Welcome to the FUCHS Group, the world's number one independent manufacturer of lubricants and related specialties. As a global company with roots in Germany, we expand the possibilities of whole industries, such as the automotive and aerospace sectors. Over 6,000 employees look closer, dig deeper and think further to create innovations and develop markets. That is what drives and connects us. We encourage our employees to expand their potential and grow together with us.
Your responsibilities:
Your areas of responsibilities:
Key partner for IT/SAP from the business side and owner of the relevant sales processes
Coordinate the current IT driven sales activities (CRM, Business Warehouse) and collaborate closely with Global Sales Organization
Set global standards for our sales processes together with the national companies and support the transition to S4/HANA on the process side
Define and prioritize new requirements regarding global sales processes for the ERP and CRM systems
Define specification of processes for the further development of our platforms and solutions, incl. change requests, testing and release of the change requests after implementation
Be in charge of the documentation and introduction of processes as well as initial training of key users
Qualifications we are looking for:
Successfully completed studies (Master's or diploma)
Several years of experience in sales, in a technical department preferably in the B2B sector
Strong experience in process management, with project management methods and with several platforms (CRM)
Collaborative personality with excellent communication skills and persuasiveness through different hierarchical levels to balance different interests in international matrix organizations
International project experience and experience abroad would be an advantage
High IT affinity and business fluent English skill
These are your benefits:
An interesting working environment in a dynamic, worldwide operating company
A secure future perspective and a good work-life balance as well as individual further education opportunities and training
Possibility to work partially from home
A very good working atmosphere with flat hierarchies
Opportunities to participate in various health and fitness activities, such as health days, sports groups and cooperation with fitness studios
