Einleitung
Statista is one of the world’s leading statistics portals.
As a multiple award-winning innovative digital company, Statista provides customers such as Google, Bloomberg, Forbes, Procter & Gamble, or Porsche with high-quality research & analysis, data intelligence, and content marketing products and services.
With 1100 employees from over 70 nations and 13 subsidiaries in Europe, North America, and Asia, Statista derives its innovative energy from its internationality and diversity.
Statista provides a company culture based around learning by doing and entrepreneurship, offers great career opportunities, and combines the pioneering spirit of a start-up with the stability of an established company.
Your Team
In our Editorial Research Intelligence Department, approximately 200 researchers work on the statistics and topic pages for our different national platforms. Each day, while focusing on their specific fields, our experts research and prepare new data from the world’s most significant sources for our platforms: https://www.statista.com/. 
Ihre Aufgaben
Assisting our editors in creating online statistical content, covering key industries within a variety of regional focuses
Processing and publishing data and editorial texts in English for the Statista.com platform, following our editorial style
Managing web content using Statista’s own content management system (CMS)
Developing a better understanding of data and quantitative research, as well as gaining SEO-optimized writing skills, in order to produce text with a statistical focus
Ihr Profil
Enrolled as a full-time student anywhere in Germany – bachelor’s or master’s programs (this condition is mandatory) – please inform us how much longer you will be studying
Interested in any of these industries: Media, Health, Pharma and Medtech, Transportation and Logistics, E-Commerce,  Consumer Goods (Food and Non-Food), Technology or Energy 
Exceptional editorial English skills - fluency in other languages such as French, Spanish, and Nordic languages is always a plus (please send a cover letter in English). 
Ability to describe complicated data in a concise and understandable manner, displaying attention to quality and accuracy
Prior writing experience (please send us samples) and interest in developing in this area
As this is an editorial position, please note that we can only consider candidates who provide us with their cover letters.

Dynamic, open and international team
