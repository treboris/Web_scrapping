We are Uniper: an international energy company with about 11,000 employees.
We combine a balanced portfolio of technologically advanced large-scale assets with outstanding technical and commercial expertise.
These assets and capabilities enable us to deliver flexible, bespoke, competitively priced energy products and services with agility, precision and speed.
Your tasks
Our Operations & IT team in Düsseldorf is looking for YOU!
You will play an active role in making the following tasks work robustly and smoothly:
Service Operations - Ensuring effective and efficient IT-Service delivery of Uniper IT-Infrastructure and applications within the given service levels
Service Design - Designing new or changed IT-Services based on business needs and ensuring all areas are following the specified process(es)
Multi Supplier Integrator - Provision of the assigned ITSM-Processes using an external team
Supplier integration - development and maintenance of interfaces to external and internal suppliers
As an IT-Service Manager (ITSM), you are responsible for one or more central ITSM-Processes (ITIL/SIAM) and you fulfill an important part in ensuring that Uniper IT runs smoothly. Your tasks include:
The communication of the ITSM-Processes to the departments and the business stakeholders
Ensuring the maintenance and use of the central ITSM-Processes
Willingness to provide 24/7 on-call support to quickly resolve critical IT-Incidents
Ensuring that global rollouts, applications and cloud-based services are taken into account in the central ITSM-Processes
Analysis and reporting to identify improvements required, and definition of implementation plans and strategies
Supporting IT improvement initiatives, particularly in the area of core ITSM-Processes
To form the interface between the technical IT-Teams and the business stakeholders
Provide detailed feedback to IT-Application and -Service-Owners regarding the status of their services and improvements
Support for the introduction of existing standard processes in new areas
Responsibility for the requirements, the inquiries, the escalations and the integrated reporting of the central ITSM-Processes
Participation in internal and external audits and the timely provision of all evidence
Your profile
A university degree or master's degree in information technology, business administration or equivalent professional qualification
Several years of professional experience in the development and operation of dynamic IT-Service and support processes (ITIL & SIAM) and/or large IT-Projects in an international team
Smart communication and presentation skills with which you win over colleagues and stakeholders
Excellent knowledge of the definition of processes and procedures in a business area
Entrepreneurial thinking and acting, with which you are always on the lookout for new opportunities to create added value
Implementation strength and hands-on - without losing sight of the medium-term perspectives
Business fluent English completes your profile
