Ihre Rolle
As part of the digitalization initiative of Carl Zeiss, be responsible for defining, aligning and communicating the architecture of an agile release train to help ensure the digital platform under development fits the business purposes.
Additionally, you will:
define subsystems and their interfaces, allocate responsibilities to subsystems and understand solution deployment
define non-functional requirements for the solution and ensure its implementation
provide guidance and coaching to the technical team members in the release train in terms of architecture and technology to ensure that the solution complies with the overall architecture and goals
be responsible for defining and aligning the security concept of the solution, and for its compliance to the applicable laws in terms of data privacy, data security and cybersecurity
be responsible for the documentation of the architecture of the solution, in accordance with the applicable guidelines and norms. Support the teams in the documentation of the architecture of the different parts of the solution
participate in the development of technical standards for the area of business
work closely together with the Product Manager and Release Train Engineer to ensure success of the release train, steered through the concept of Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe)
interface with ZEISSâ€™ IT departments to ensure smooth integration of solution into ZEISS infrastructure
take ownership of the end to end implementation working with multiple members in a cross functional team

You have:
