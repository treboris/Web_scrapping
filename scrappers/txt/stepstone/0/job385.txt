Olympus is passionate about creating customer-driven solutions for the medical, life sciences and industrial equipment industries. For more than 100 years, Olympus has focused on making people’s lives healthier, safer and more fulfilling by helping to detect, prevent and treat disease, furthering scientific research and ensuring public safety. Olympus is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, with more than 30,000 employees worldwide in nearly 40 countries. Olympus Europa, headquartered in Hamburg, Germany, serves the EMEA (Europe, Middle East & Africa) region and employs more than 7,800 people in 21 countries. Apply now!
Your Responsibilities
Participate in the development of a regional/global software/provider strategy and sourcing standards in alignment with Olympus’ broader commercial framework
Gather data to analyze and report on the (regional) usage of software/provider licenses
Identify opportunities to improve the usage of licenses/contracts
Inform relevant stakeholders about necessary changes/updates in the software/provider or license arrangements
Monitor various licenses and contracts currently held by Olympus
Monitor the corresponding usage of software and work with key stakeholders to adjust the allocation of software licenses based on usage pattern
Manage software requests from stakeholders and research for possible software and also ensure to negotiate best possible contracts for new/existing software for Olympus
Select software and providers licenses according to Olympus’ standards
Manage the relationships with software and provider suppliers from a contractual perspective
Conduct all day-to-day engagements and operational activities with suppliers and within the organization (e.g., managing software requests and granting access)
Your Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Supply Chain Management, Business Administration or equivalent and ideally related experience
Several years of experience as a Software License Manager / Supplier Relationship Manager or in a similar role within the IT field
Experience in the Medical industry, either in a consulting company or directly in a life sciences health care company preferred
Experience of managing IT applications
