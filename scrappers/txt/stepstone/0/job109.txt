We have a clear commitment: putting best-possible solutions in the hands of medical professionals, enabling them to find the right answer to the challenge they face. Fresenius Kabi is a driver of healthcare for the coming decade. We truly live diversity and value different backgrounds and motivations. Around the world, all of us are committed to our shared endeavor: caring for life.
#careerswithapurpose at Fresenius 
Over 300,000 people across the globe work at Fresenius to provide better medicine for more people. Our independent business segments Fresenius Medical Care, Fresenius Kabi, Fresenius Helios and Fresenius Vamed offer a wide spectrum of products and services in the health sector. We offer you the opportunity to make a difference with your career – and to become part of our impressive growth story.
Fresenius Kabi is a global healthcare company that specializes in lifesaving medicines and technologies for infusion, transfusion and clinical nutrition. Our products and services are used to help care for critically and chronically ill patients.
Your assignments
Project management for the implementation of applications at international Fresenius Kabi sites according to the "Vision 2026" deployment plan
Definition, scheduling of project milestones and their consistent implementation through demand-oriented and cross-team resource management
Planning of global and local resource requirements and coordination of project resources with the aim of achieving project milestones on time
Lead of cross-site workshops to conceptualize the project plan, schedule project milestones and develop the project management methodology with the project team
Prioritization and coordination of the implementation activities
Creation and tracking of project plans in the global project management software
Management of user requirements, prioritization in the implementation and later coordination of the timely creation of technical solutions with internal and external project resources
Planning and scoping of qualification in coordination with the validation department with associated creation of change control events in KabiTrack
Identification of application-related project risks and escalation by initiating appropriate countermeasures
Your profile
Successfully completed university degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field with at least 3 years of relevant experience:
At least 3 years of experience working with manufacturing IT-applications
At least 3 years of experience in the production or quality environment
Experience in leading projects
Familiar with concepts and principles of business process design with a focus on digitalization and Industry 4.0
Knowledge of Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP)
Knowledge of Good Automated Manufacturing Practice (GAMP)
Knowledge of computer system validation (CSV)
Ability to lead international cross-functional project team members in a matrix organization
Independent, process- & result-oriented, balanced way of working
Proactive and responsible working style
Excellent conflict and interface management
High intercultural competencies and excellent emphatic assertiveness
Excellent knowledge of English in speaking and writing, German or other languages would be an advantage
Willingness to travel to participate in workshops at international locations
