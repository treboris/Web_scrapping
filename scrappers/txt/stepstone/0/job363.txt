ALLES.AUTO is the new digital platform around the topics of car buying and car ownership.
We are the digital car dealership of the future with our own software-as-a-service solution developed by the trade for the trade. As a cross-brand e-commerce marketplace, we map all the offers of the stationary car trade online: New and used vehicles, workshop services, accessories as well as all relevant services in the area of payment, leasing, financing and advice.
ALLES.AUTO was launched under the leadership of AVAG Holding SE (one of the largest automobile trading groups in Europe) and converted into a public limited company in autumn 2022. As a joint venture of several large automobile trading groups and other players in the automotive industry, ALLES.AUTO AG operates independently on the market and continuously strives to expand its shareholder base.
Our platform www.alles.auto has been live since December 2022!
Therefore, we are expanding our team and are looking for reinforcement for the following position:
React Lead Developer 
Aufgaben:
Developing and maintaining the frontend of the application: Your primary responsibility is to lead the development of the frontend of the application using React. This includes designing, coding, testing, and debugging the frontend codebase
Ensuring the performance and quality of the frontend code: You should ensure that the frontend codebase is performant, scalable, and adheres to the best practices for quality. This involves conducting code reviews, optimizing the codebase, and implementing appropriate testing strategies
Providing technical leadership: You are expected to provide technical leadership to the frontend development team, including mentoring junior developers, reviewing code, and ensuring best practices are followed
Collaborating with other teams: You are responsible for collaborating with other teams such as backend developers, UX designers, and project managers to ensure the frontend development aligns with the overall project objectives
Identifying and resolving technical issues: You must be able to identify and resolve technical issues that arise during the development process. This requires strong problem-solving skills and the ability to work collaboratively with other developers
Staying up-to-date with emerging trends and technologies: You should stay up-to-date with emerging trends and technologies in frontend development, particularly with React 
Persönliche Anforderungen:
You have a strong proficiency in React, including expertise in React's core principles, lifecycle methods, and component architecture. You are also experienced in using React libraries and tools, such as Redux, React Router, and Next.js
You have expertise in frontend web technologies, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You are also proficient in using frontend web development tools, such as Git, npm, and webpack
You have a strong understanding of design principles, including typography, color theory, and user experience (UX) design. You are able to translate design concepts into functional and responsive web interfaces
You have experience working with agile methodologies, including Scrum and Kanban. You are able to participate in agile ceremonies, such as sprint planning, daily stand-ups, and retrospectives
You have strong leadership and communication skills, including the ability to manage a team, provide constructive feedback, and communicate effectively with stakeholders. You are also be able to collaborate effectively with other departments, such as design and backend development teams 
Wir bieten
We promote you: Personal and professional development opportunities are guaranteed by our strong and long-term growth. You can expect challenging and interesting tasks in an exciting work environment.
Your opinion is important to us: work independently and contribute your ideas. Flat hierarchies and short decision processes are self-evident to us.
We offer you: a permanent contract, a fair salary and 30 days of vacation. As long as it is not necessary due to a project or if you coordinate with your colleagues, we offer flexible working hours.
Here you can feel comfortable: Not only because you get everything you need, but also because we have an office dog, plants in the office and other classics. You can look forward to a creative working atmosphere and plenty of room for innovation.
