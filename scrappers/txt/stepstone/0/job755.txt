Ihre Rolle
You will play an active role in shaping the future of Data Governance on enterprise level and actively contribute to the success of the digital transformation at ZEISS
As a Data Governance/ Data Standard Expert (m/f/x) at ZEISS Digital Partners, you are part of our Data & Analytics team and will join the vibrant ZEISS data community
In this function, you will drive the development of Data Standards aligned on company's roadmap priorities, with the overall objective to position data as a strategic asset at ZEISS
You will support the Data Quality Program @ ZEISS to create a global Enterprise Data Quality framework aligned with the enterprise data strategy
With your team, you will define and implement global data quality management methodology, standards, processes, and tools
You will monitor achievement of goals and objectives for data quality management, implement and consolidate DQ KPIs & SLAs as well as provide cross-functional scorecards and dashboards
As a Data Governance / Data Standard Expert (m/f/x) you will closely monitor internal Data Standardization initiatives and provide moderation of activities in compliance with enterprise governance principles when requested
You will also represent Zeiss in x-business relevant external data standardization working groups
Ihr Profil
An excellent university degree in computer science, business informatics, business administration, engineering, or mathematics, or in a related field
At least 3 years of hands-on experience with Data Standardization in a large organization
Experience in working with standard development organizations or industry associations is a plus
Experience in defining, documenting, and implementing Data Quality best practices, standards & procedures in a large organization or in data quality initiatives, analysis, and reporting (data integrity, data profiling, KPI reporting and advanced analytics)
Knowledge of major business IT applications like SAP ERP/SAP S4/HANA, SAP CRM or any other CRM solution, SAP MDG, Adobe Marketo, Cloud Platforms (Azure and/or AWS/ GCP), Data Cataloging incl. Business Glossary and Data Quality technical solutions
Excellent communication skills and ability to bring various stakeholders to consensus-based decisions
Understanding of the impact of data quality on the operational business processes, steering & management decisions
Excellent analytical, problem-solving skills, and conceptional thinking paired with the ability to understand multiple interdependencies
Excellent English skills, German is recommended
