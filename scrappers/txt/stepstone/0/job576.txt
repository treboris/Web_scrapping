Tasks
Since 2016, Mercedes-Benz Connectivity Services GmbH has been connecting the automotive and digital world around the connected vehicle: With innovative data-based solutions, the subsidiary of Mercedes-Benz AG enables its B2B customers to make business processes more efficient, improve customer experiences or develop new business models.
Mercedes-Benz Connectivity Services creates digital solutions of the future that help B2B customers to increase their process quality and efficiency, improve customer experiences, act more sustainably or develop new business models. It is based on real-time data from more than 100 measuring points and signals inside and outside of more than 14 million connected Mercedes-Benz vehicles worldwide. This data is used to generate added value for products and processes in companies from a wide range of industries. The Stuttgart-based company has about 60 employees working on the development, operation and sales of digital data-based products.
We are the Engineering & Technology Team and we are looking for a Site Reliability Engineer (m/f/d). We are an experienced and passionate team being in charge to develop and operate data-driven, award-winning digital products and innovative IOT services with cloud native microservices for business customers of Mercedes-Benz. We are working agile, delivering new features to our customers with every sprint using our automated CI/CD pipeline.
These exciting tasks are waiting for you:
Assure high availability of our digital products for our customers
Run and monitor our digital products in the cloud
Maintain and enhance our CI/CD pipeline
Shape and enhance the platform base of our digital products
Analyse and solve problems arising during development and production
Contribute to reviews and share your knowledge with the team
The position is to be filled several times as soon as possible on a full-time basis.
Qualifications
Your profile:
You are breathing an agile, cloud native and DevOps mindset
You have a good IT Security and cryptography understanding
You know how to run solutions 24/7
You are familiar with microservice and event driven architectures as well as distributed systems
You have experience with Azure, Kubernetes, K8s Operators and Containers
You are familiar with modern technologies, such as Flink, Nifi and Kafka, and you know how to use and tweak tools like Grafana, Kibana, Prometheus, Opsgenie and Datadog
You have experience in Gitops and running a CI/CD pipeline
You are eager to learn new methods and technologies
You are fluent in English, German is a plus
