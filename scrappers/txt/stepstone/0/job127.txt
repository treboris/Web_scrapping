AVIANET GmbH is a technology subsidiary of AVIAREPS AG with more than 25 years’ experience in providing professional Passenger GSA services and tourism marketing to clients around the globe. As the world’s leading GSA and Tourism representation organization with 65 offices in 59 countries, AVIAREPS represents airlines as well as destinations, hotels, attractions, airports, cars, trains, cruise lines, retailers, and import/export organizations, and offers a full suite of B2B and B2C services which includes sales & reservations, marketing, digital, and PR programs. AVIANET provides management and support for the global IT infrastructures under our Managed IT services and solutions to numerous clients worldwide.
We are looking for mid-career and target driven
IT Support Engineer
based at our Munich office
The successful candidate will ...
Work in the team of IT Engineers responsible for maintaining and developing LAN/WAN IT infrastructure of the company
Take part in IT projects for external clients
Implement, manage, and troubleshoot a variety of software applications and hardware
Be responsible for the end-user support
Monitor performance, diagnose and resolve users’ IT problems on daily basis
Provide day to day IT operational support for business users
Install, configure, troubleshoot, and repair PC and LAN equipment
Provide user support (MS Office, Win10-Win11, softphones, mobile phones, printers, scanners, etc.)
Perform installation and support of Microsoft Office products including MS Office
Develop documentation, including applications guides and user training manuals
Take over project work as assigned
Your qualifications
1 to 2 years experience and working knowledge of the following technologies is required:
Windows Active Directory structure based on latest Windows Operating Systems
Microsoft Exchange Servers support
Windows 10 & 11 Professional support
Symantec Enterprise End Point Protection and Backup Services
HP Servers, Desktops, Laptops Hardware support
Good English and/or German communication skills
Excellent team player
Strong analytical and troubleshooting skills
Willing to travel
High Flexibility and dedication
Friendly attitude
Our offer
The opportunity to bring in your personality and ideas, and to quickly take on personal responsibility
An attractive location in the center of Munich, with excellent transportation links and a panoramic view
A modern workplace with an open-door policy and short communication channels
An open-minded, dynamic, and helpful team
Option to work partly remote
Travel industry benefits: DRV card, access to PEP offers
A subsidy for an Urban Sports Club membership
