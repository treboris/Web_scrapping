Your working environment
The Volkswagen Group is one of the leading manufactures of automobiles and commercial vehicles worldwide.
We – the supplier management organization – are the technical part within the purchasing organization. We are responsible for risk identification and mitigation for the full supply chain.
Our software team is a growing team with passion for all kinds of automotive software, making sure to have the best-in-class software supply chain to ensure and safeguard the maturity and on-time-delivery of software from our suppliers.
We are using agile methods were appropriated understanding that it´s not always the best method to choose.
Your tasks
Conduct risk analysis of different software supplies together with all necessary internal stakeholders during the whole software development life cycle
Develop risk mitigation strategies in close cooperation with software suppliers and internal stakeholders
Safeguard all supplier software releases according to the agreed mile stones
Regular status reviews and continuous tracking and reporting (escalation if needed)
Support of the purchasing project organization as well as task forces
Your qualifications
Successfully completed degree in computer science / engineering / physics or similar qualification
Fun in technology and science of any kind
Several years of professional experience in software development (preferably automotive or embedded software)
Strong skills in automotive software development processes and project management
Strong problem solving abilities
Good communication, interpersonal and influencing skills
Fluent German and English, spoken and written
Willingness to travel
