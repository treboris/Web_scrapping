About Heraeus
Solutions from the Heraeus Group provide faster internet, rid water of germs, and keep hearts beating in time. As a broadly diversified and globally leading family-owned technology company, we combine materials expertise and technological leadership to create the most diverse products, all with one thing in common: World-class quality. Working with customers around the world, we shape the future and provide vital innovations in the areas of environment, electronics, health, and industrial applications. Heraeus is one of the top 10 family-owned companies in Germany and has a team of around 16,200 employees in 40 countries.
The following challenges await you:
As Solution Architect E-Commerce (m/f/d) you are responsible, together with our Dev-Team, for the technological further development of the e-commerce system landscape.
You will lead the development by guiding, supporting and challenging our development team with regular code reviews and support of the branching concept
You will ensure that our e-commerce system landscape remains flexible, modern and maintainable and drive the further development and modernization of the e-commerce architecture
Due to your hands-on mentality, you not only lead the way, but also get involved yourself
By providing targeted advice to your stakeholders (Product Owners, Management), you enable them to do their work in the e-commerce environment in the best possible way and to make sustainable decisions
You work closely with the product owners and external service providers to make our e-commerce even better
Your profile:
You have successfully completed vocational training (e.g. IT specialist for application development) or a degree in computer science
You have several years of professional experience as a solution architect (m/f/d) or in a comparable role in the e-commerce sector and have already played a leading role in the design of complex system landscapes in the e-commerce context
You also have experience in connecting e-commerce systems with 3rd party systems (ERP or others)
You have experience in operating an e-commerce system with the analyzing incoming incidents
You are used to running regular deployments from staging to productive environments
Experience in Java 8, relational databases (MSSQL / Oracle / MySQL), GIT (Bitbucket, gitlab, Azure Devops) required
What we offer:
Strong salary package – attractive basic salary plus variable profit-sharing (up to 150 % target achievement possible) and support for private pension plan
Modern working time arrangements – time sovereignty without time recording, focus on results-oriented work 
Regular time off – 30 days annual leave plus up to 6 additional days off (company closures on bridge days and between years), special leave for special occasions, life phase account for paid time off (e.g. sabbatical, earlier retirement)
Additional benefits – subsidized public transport, discounts in many (online) stores, company medical service with comprehensive preventive medical check-ups, sports facilities and employee canteen with freshly prepared meals every day
Sustainability and social commitment – sustainability as part of the corporate strategy and targeted support for initiatives in the areas of education, family and science through our own foundations
