A vacancy for an IT Systems Engineer (d/f/m) has arisen within Airbus Defence and Space in Manching.
In Manching we develop high fidelity Aircraft Simulators. Our simulations are basically distributed realtime systems. For the development and operation we rely on a powerful, Linux based, self managed IT infrastructure. Recently started programs like Eurodrone and FCAS ask for the next generation of Simulators. A significant enabler is the underlying IT Infrastructure. We are therefore looking for an experienced IT Systems Engineer ready to take the IT Infrastructure to the next level!
Your location
Located about an hour's drive north of Munich, Manching is an up-and-coming market town that offers a wide range of  leisure and cultural activities. Here, you can enjoy the quality of life in the countryside while the pleasures of near-by cities are still within easy reach.
Your benefits
- Attractive salary including holiday pay, Christmas bonus and profit sharing
- 30 days holidays and extra days-off for special occasions
- Excellent upskilling opportunities and great development prospects
- Special benefits: employer-funded pension, employee stock options, discounted car leasing, special conditions for insurances, public transport subsidy, discounts at local businesses
- On-site-facilities: Medical officer for check-ups and other health-related services, canteen and cafeteria, kindergarten nearby
Your tasks consists of
- Capture the infrastructure needs and requirements from our Customers, Developers and DevOps Engineers
- Design and implement IT infrastructure architectural blueprints for our products
- Design needed Development, Staging and Testing Infrastructure, making using use elastic computing (Virtualization & Containers)
- Support the IT Administration team in implementing the Architectures
- Coordinate closely with corporate IT stakeholders like ITSec and Networking
We are looking for a candidate with the following skills and mindset:
- Bachelor degree (or equivalent) in Computer Science or a related discipline
- At least 5-8 years of professional experience in the design and operation of Enterprise IT Infrastructure
- A deep understanding and experince with the Infrastructure-as-Code paradigm
- A good understanding which High-Availibility, Scalability and Fault tolerance mean
- Experience with dynamic Povisioning and Immutable Systems
- The urge to automate as much as possible
- Experience with the Atlassian tool stack (Jira, Confluence)
- Excellent English (negotiation level)
- Proactivity is key
Related Technologies
- Virtualization (Proxmox, oVirt, KVM)
- Infrastructure provisioning Tools (Ansible, Puppet)
- The Hashicorp Toolstack (Terraform, Packer, Vault)
- Container based Infrastucture (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Storage Solutions (Ceph, GlusterFS, ZFS, Samba)
- Monitoring (checkMK, Icinga, Prometheus)
- Webserver (Apache, Nginx, HAProxy / Taefik)
- Continuous Integration (Jenkins)
- Authentication (LDAP, Active Directory, Kerberos)
- Networking (VLANs, Cisco IOS, DNS, DHCP, PXE)
- Version Control Management (Git)
