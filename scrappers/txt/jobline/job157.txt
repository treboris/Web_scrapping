The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH)is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Change Manager (REF1395D) - German and English Speaking
If you want to be part of an energizing and inspiring team, where you can freely develop your skills, this is the right place for you. 
Your task:
Change opening
Change coordination which consists of planning and scheduling, task chasing, interfacing with technical teams
Change Management tasks where the main responsibilities are gathering approvals, attending CAB-s, release and closure of change tickets
Requirement:
General enterprise IT understanding
At least 3-5 years’ experience in a Customer Support team
Proactive with a high sense of responsibility
Independent team player with very good communication skills 
Confident German and English knowledge (at least level B2)
High resilience and ability to steer complex topics and simultaneous tasks
Resolution-focused mindset
Experienced
