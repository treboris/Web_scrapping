thyssenkrupp is an international group of companies comprising largely independent industrial and technology businesses and employing 96,000 people. Across 48 countries it generated sales of €41 billion in fiscal year 2021/22. Backed by extensive technological know-how, the businesses develop cost-effective and resource-friendly solutions to the challenges of the future, especially in the fields of climate protection, the energy transition, digital transformation in the industry and mobility of the future.
In the Hungarian development center with our engineering team of 1000 we are present as an innovative and significant partner in one of the fastest growing and most exciting segment of the automotive industry, in the field of electromechanical steering systems. With outstanding quality and high-tech developments we meet the needs of renowned car manufacturers and our developments can be found in 25 % of the manufactured cars all over the world. Our engineers work on new challenges that can change the future of automotive.
Embedded Penetration Tester (Ethical Hacker)
Tasks:
Design and support the design of test framework systems implementing product cybersecurity
Perform cybersecurity attacks on the product to evaluate the security level of the system.
Perform penetration tests on hardware level (communication protocols, debug interfaces, memories, Hardware Security Modules)
Validation of cybersecurity goals and vulnerabilities exploration
Support of risk assessment
Support risk and vulnerability management
Working with management to implement cybersecurity in the product
Carrying out and attaining the mission and the goals of the business unit managed
Continuous self-development
Communicate regularly with other stakeholders
KPI and periodic reports about the status of the cybersecurity test projects
Establish and maintain relevant controls and feedback systems to monitor the operation of the product cybersecurity project team
Requirements:
University degree (BSc, MSc) in a technical field: Electrical Engineering, Computer Science
4+ years of professional experience
Familiarity with embedded systems.
Advanced knowledge of C and assembly programming language
Out of the box mindset
Ready to learn about new technologies
Familiarity with measurement and debugging tools
Experience in the field of information security / cybersecurity
Ability for teamwork
Self-driven to improve continuously
The ability to present the work outcome according to the audience
Critical thinking
Advantages:
Experience in the automotive industry
Experience in Reverse Engineering
Experience in embedded hardware security
Python development
Following certificates: CEH, OSCP, OSCE, CISSP
ITIL Foundation, PMP certification

You can be part of a successful and growing company in the field of technology
We support you with flexible working hours and home office opportunity (60 % of the working time in many of our positions)
Competitive salary and wide range of other benefits (cafeteria & bonus) are provided to you
You can learn new languages in small groups, selecting from 5 languages
