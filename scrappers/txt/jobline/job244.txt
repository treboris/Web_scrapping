Present in the market under the Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions brand name, IT-Services Hungary (ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group and is the largest ICT employer in Hungary. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 4500 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Senior Devops Engineer (REF214E)
Our group is responsible for the integration, validation and production of test results ("System Under Test") in accordance with the requirements of the Deutsche Telekom AG Platform, as measured by the diversity of the group. The work is carried out in several virtual squads within the group, mainly with german but also international colleagues. In one of these squads, called NNA_AAC , we are looking for a Senior DevOps Engineer colleague.
Tasks
Scope of NNA_AAC Squad: Development of automation use cases based on the automation tool NDO (Network and Domain Orchestrator) as part of the project "[email protected]", test automation.
System management, automation and integration of network related systems within IPC
Realization and implementation of automation solutions IP Core by means of SDN Orchestrator (NDO)
Test automation (CI/CD)
Requirements
Network knowledge and experience Cisco CCNP level or similar level in Juniper
Experience with network automation and Netconf
Intermediate Python skills with min 1-2 years experience
Data modeling (ideally using YANG, but XSD experience translates easily)
Experience with REST, JSON, YAML, Linux, Git/Gitlab, CI/CD, Docker
Ideally, experience with Kubernetes
