The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Security Solution Administrator- OT-Security (REF805P)
In this position you will be responsible for the platform operation and application administration of the industrial cyber security solution in Deutsche Telekom Security.
Your tasks:
You will be responsible for the administration of the OT-SOC system infrastructure and the OpenBSD / Linux servers
Administration of OT (Operational Technology) monitoring system and management of the connection to the central infrastructure
Basic administration of public clouds (Open Telekom Cloud) for components deployed on cloud infrastructure
Maintain the application, documentation and systems. Optimize the service: reduce downtimes, reduce system troubles. Check and monitor: availability, system health and activities/changes for all components
2nd level support incl. Incident-, Problem-, Patch-, and Change Management and tracking the incidents
Skills:
At least intermediate English language skills
Higher level (specialization) qualifications or relevant professional experience
Experience with Linux/Unix (Ubuntu, OpenBSD) operating systems
Experience with Shell Scripting (bash)
Experience with virtualization (VMVare)
Basic knowledge with the administration and deploying solutions in cloud
Basic knowledge in IT-Security, Network Security and Network-technologies (OSI Modell, IP, TCP, UDP)
Customer-oriented approach. Quick understanding of complex problems. Good team player attitude. Good communication ability.
Familiar with ITIL processes
Able to provide 24/7 OnCall Support
Advantages
Experience with Genua GmbH Genu Servicebox, with Genua GmbH Genu Rendezvous Server and with Genua GmbH Genucenter or with similar products from different vendor
Experience with Microsoft Defender for IoT (CyberX Central Manager Appliances and CyberX Sensor Appliances)
German language skills
Experienced
* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
