The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group and Germany-based Deutsche Telekom IT. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Experienced Scrum Master (REF1344U)
Our group VNI is responsible for the integration, validation and production of test results ("System Under Test") in accordance with the requirements of the Deutsche Telekom AG Platform. Daily tasks carried out in several virtual squads with German and International colleagues.
You will be part of an innovative and agile work surrounding, supporting future oriented software and network solutions  within Telekom Deutschland.
You will be responsible for supporting agile teams , organizing and leading agile ceremonies, keep close contact with Product Owner and agile team members.
Guiding and coaching the agile team and organization on how to use Agile/Scrum practices and values. Advice and coaching of agile teams and managers in the complex support area  in the introduction and application of agile methodologies.
Communication of agile values, principles and practices in the team as well as the ability to continuously develop agile skills, coaching the team to higher levels of agile maturity. 
Skills:
Certified Scrum Master – SAFe preferred is an advantage. (Proven experience is also acceptable)
At least 2 years experience in relevant field is needed
Awareness of agile practices and ability to effectively manage and mentor development teams
Excellent interpersonal, project management, and problem-solving skills,
Knowledge of agile administration tools (eg. JIRA, Confluence, Kanban...)
German and English language is a must have 
You are analytical and solution oriented
Strong awareness of data protection and security in general and specifically in the field of automation
You are driven by a willingness to grow and are constantly pushing yourself and your team to try innovative ideas
* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
