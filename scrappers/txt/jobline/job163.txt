The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Machine Learning Operations (MLOps) Engineer (REF692A)
We are searching for a Senior Software Engineer to join our highly skilled team in the area of AI and ML with the respective skill set in the area of CI/CD and Kubernetes on automated infrastructures (e.g. AWS). The candidate will have the chance to grow into emerging technologies of AI as well.
Your tasks will be:
Ensure that scalable IT infrastructures are created in the cloud.
Build robust data pipelines using data from our customers.
You will integrate ML models into ML infrastructure and ensure that they function correctly.
Implementation of AI and ML automation
You are responsible for different CI/CD pipelines -  private and public cloud providers (e.g. AWS)
Continuous Deployment (automation, and SW quality aspects)
Gitlab Pipelines
Skills:
Container architectures (K8S Docker)
Ingress Control
Templating of k8s manifests
Experience in programming (ideally Python
Exposure to public cloud providers (ideally AWS) 
Experience in logging and monitoring (ELK)
Experience in developing/consuming RESTful API
Docker Security
Linux experience 
Optional skills which can provide a +:
Apache/Cloudera
Basic Frontend experience in order to adapt config interfaces
Kubeflow
* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
