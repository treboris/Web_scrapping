As an internationally positioned IT team, we support the Client's product development by providing applications and solutions for mechanical and software development and for the management of development data. In this processes, we count more than 6,000 users among our internal customers.
We operate in a very heterogeneous environment of applications, such as CATIA Mechanical Design, MS Project Server, JIRA, GIT Software Management, Doors NG Requirements Management, and our PLM system CIM Database.
To strengthen our team we are looking for a qualified and motivated IT specialist at our location in Budapest.
We offer an exciting work environment in a globally operating enterprise with many various opportunities for your own personal and professional growth.
IT Asset Manager Specialist (SW)
Tasks
Manages the entire software leasing with regard to the product lifecycle (leasing, deployment, maintenance, utilization, and disposal) including accounting
Establishes and maintains and monitors software inventory database to ensure compliance 
Devise licensing/leasing strategy in cooperation with headquarter
Stays in contact with external Service providers for Workplace Systems. 
Provide commercial support for the negotiation of new or renewed (leasing) contracts and identifies savings opportunities for renewals, software licensing
Ensure invoices are consistent with the terms of the contract; track and renew software maintenance agreements and warranties 
Procurement, receipt, inventory and provisioning of software 
Review and revise standard equipment configuration policies 
Meter and review software utilization and make recommendations on requirements 
Interacts with Finance as appropriate
Requirements
Minimum three years of experience and knowledge with IT software asset management; 
Experience with interpretation of software licensing contract terms and conditions;
Understanding of complex software agreements;
Knowledge of licensing agreements involving software, including Microsoft, SAP, Adobe, and Oracle;
Well-versed vendor management and negotiation skills;
Significant knowledge of IT Service Management processes;
Strong analytical skills and attention to detail;
Solid problem-solving and consultative skills;
