Sales AND IT business analyst
Responsibilities:
Participation in the development of complex ICT projects abroad; Participation in the creation of comprehensive commercial and technical proposals;Analyzing potential customers' requirements; Making market research, analysis of competition; Maintaining contact with potential customers and suppliers;Creating a budget (CAPEX and OPEX),Organizing business trips: accommodation, plane tickets, transfer, insurance.
Requirements:
Either a business user with a strong interest in IT technologies, software development, and server infrastructure OR a technical (IT) user with a strong interest in topics like business development, international procurement, and international relations.Excellent analytic skills, Collect and process huge amount of information and data, then summarize and draw a clear conclusion.Speaks fluent English, which is fit for negotiation. Can travel abroad once per month (2-3 nights per trip).An advanced user of Excel.Experience gained in the field of IT consultancy
What our client offers:
You will have the opportunity to learn from professionally prepared colleagues and expand your knowledge during training sessions.Exciting and challenging tasks related to ICT, international relations, and business development.A unique opportunity to meet and work directly with well-known businesswomen, businessmen, politicians, and technical gurus across Eurasia.We know how important work-life balance is, so we support hybrid working (1-2 days a week home office option is provided).We provide our employees with a free vacation in the company's Balaton resort.A good mood is guaranteed, as it gives the power to your day.
Advantages: 
Knowledge of Russian or any Slavic or Arab languaBasic knowledge of MS Project and MS VISIOAdvanced user in MS PowerPoint,Knowledge of agile software development methodologies
