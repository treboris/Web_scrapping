The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
System Engineer - experienced (REF1324E)
Tasks:
Implementation of concepts and scripting standards incl. management of optimization measures within the scope of cloud management.
Coordinating and controlling the processes according to valid standards, in order to increase the efficiency of the service activity and the customer satisfaction.
Resolving incidents, reverse engineering of MF and Zabbix Monitoring Tools, implementing new features. 
Participates at scripting in case of automation projects.
Supporting the development team in upcomming projects.
Deploys and provides support for new scripts in live production environments.
Design / conception / evaluation of IT infrastructures, cloud platforms.
Skills:
Postgres, MySQL, Linux/Windows system management
Basic Network Knowledge
Scripting in multiple coding languages
Good analytical and problem-solving skills, solid attention to detail and good documentation skills
Agile/DevOps mindset
Good English written and oral skills, German is nice to have
Experienced
