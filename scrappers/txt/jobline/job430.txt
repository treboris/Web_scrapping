Test Automation Engineer
Our new test engineer's focus will be:
Creating automated tests on all levels of the testing pyramid
Working together with a cross-functional team to implement test development
Understanding and validating requirements from a testing perspective
Contributing creative ideas to help evolve our testing methodologies
Occasionally mentoring colleagues with less experience
Our international client is looking for an experienced test automation engineer for their team based in Budapest. By joining the team, you'll be involved in the development and testing of one of their products, which optimizes the daily utilization of flights, increases user productivity and improves airline management when there are deviations from the regular schedule.
The requirements you should fulfill:
3+ years of experience in test automation (or software development with a focus on QA)
Excellent English knowledge (spoken and written)
Knowledge of Java and JavaScript/TypeScript
Experience with browser-based automation tools
Experience in API testing
Familiarity with agile practises and CI/CD
Familiarity with version control (preferably Git) and issue tracking systems (preferably Jira)
It's an advantage if you also have:
Familiarity with Docker and Kubernetes
Experience in SQL/NoSQL databases, cloud (preferably Azure or GCP), Linux/Unix
University degree in Computer Science
What our partner offers:
Flexible working hours, remote work opportunity
Training opportunities for professional and soft skills
Supportive, professional and multinational team
Discount on company flights
Agile (scrum) environment
Working part-time is also possible
