The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Technical Process Specialist - VDS Coordinator (REF704I)
Tasks:
Drive and steer the implementation of selected applications and processes in VDS environment for partners
Evaluates and determines the dependencies between the company and partners
Creating and developing the monthly reports
Implements ad hoc tasks that could have an impact on the partner organization, e.g. introduction of new systems or company guidelines 
Requirements:
At least 5 years experience in multinational company 
Basic knowledge in Reporting and application management
Analytical ability to describe processes for stakeholders
Basic knowledge of project management
High communication skills
Solid organizational skills including attention to detail and multitasking skills
Proficiency in Excel
Good English and German knowledge
Advantages:
Good knowledge of reporting tools 
Presentation skills
Analytical skills to process large amounts of data
Experienced
