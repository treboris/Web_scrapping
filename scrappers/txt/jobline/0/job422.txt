Quantitative Developer
About our Partner:
Our partner has become a key player in the RegTech industry in the past few years and they are providing solutions for a variety of clients from the insurance and asset management sectors. This decision created a high demand for talented and motivated individuals to participate in them success story and be a key-player in the years to come. They aim to be the market leader, by utilizing best-inclass financial expertise and industry-leading technology.
Tasks:
Working on different client projects related to regulatory client reporting, risk management, and asset pricing;
Developing our internal libraries (regulatory reporting and risk management framework);
Understanding different third-party data structures and integrating it to the already existing codebase;
Supporting the Reporting Operations Team.
Requirements:
MSc or PhD in a quantitative subject (Quantitative Finance, Actuarial Sciences, Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science);
Multiple years of experience in a similar role;
Interest and knowledge of quantitative finance, financial market instruments and portfolios;
Strong programming knowledge in Python (familiarity with Numpy/Pandas/Quantlib packages is an advantage); alternatively, knowledge of a high-level computing language (Matlab or R) and an object-oriented language (e.g. Java/C#/C++) and willingness to learn Python;
Strong written and oral proficiency in English;
Entrepreneurial spirit, self-motivated and great team player.
