The Boschung Group is a world leader in the development and production of solutions for the efficiency and safety of traffic surfaces for the world’s largest airports, busiest highways and most visited cities and municipalities across the globe. 
In our Boschung Technology Center, headquarter of the group based in Payerne, Switzerland and our numerous other locations around the world, everything we accomplish is a result of the strong partnership we have with the people in our team. Looking to strengthen the R&D’s team right away or by agreement, BOSCHUNG MECATRONIC is recruiting an:
Full Stack Software Engineer 100%
(Remote work & periodic on-site visits. English required; Workplace in Hungary)
Your tasks
Conception and development of API-based solutions and microservices using frameworks like Spring or SpringBoot
Development and maintenance of frontend applications using frameworks like React, Vue or Angular
Contribution to develop, implement and maintain workflows as CI/CD pipelines in collaboration with other teams.
Conception, development of new features and maintenance of legacy products
Active participation on code reviews and bug fix activities
Your profile 
You have a Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering, Computer Science or related field and 4+ years of professional experience
Solid foundations of the JAVA 8+ ecosystem and Spring/Spring Boot framework
Experience in relational (PostgreSQL, MySQL) and non-relational (Cassandra) database design
You are interested in Kubernetes, Docker, RabbitMQ and MQTT technologies and ideally you already have expertise in these areas
Familiar with Mobile Apps development is an asset
Fluent in English, German or French is an asset
Knowledge of Agile methodologies is an asset
You are motivated, goal-oriented and pro-active team player which is comfortable with remote cross-team collaboration and communication
Willing to attend PI planning sessions in the headquarters every two month

You will be able to carry out a variety of activities in a modern infrastructure and a prosperous business
You will be part of our initiative to expand our Digital Services!
You will be familiarised with your new task with the support of a talented and motivated team, working with the latest technologies!
