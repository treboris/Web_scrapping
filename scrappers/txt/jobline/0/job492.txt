IT Auditor and Analyst
Our Client is a global technology leader in the production of speciality materials and chemical products. We are looking for an IT Auditor and Analyst to their growing Regional Operations Centre in Budapest.
Responsibilities:
Participate in strategic, operational and compliance audit projects,
Handle audit IT control processes and create documentations,
Provision of assistance in integrated audit projects by working with the business audit team closely
Review third-party assurance using recognized standards (SOC 1 Type 2, SOC 2)
Provide annual tests of ITGCs over financial reporting required under US SOX
Participate in assurance reviews and investigations
Build relationships with IT organizations
Requirements:
At least 1-3 years relevant experience in Internal Audit, Big 4, IT or related fields
Bachelor/Master degree in Management Information Systems, Computer Science, Mathematics, Accounting, Business, Finance or related disciplines
Experience in testing IT General Controls (ITGCs), Key Reports (IPEs), and/or Application Controls
Proficient in Office 365 applications
Understanding of processes, risks, and controls
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills and ability to pay close attention to details
Excellent English verbal and written communication skills
Ability to work in cross-functional and multi-cultural teams
Highly motivated, reliable, accurate and able to work independently
What our client offers:
Competitive salary and benefits (Cafeteria, Private Health Care Insurance)
Flexible working arrangements and home office possibilities
Structured onboarding support
Diverse career paths (e.g. people management, subject matter expert)
Development professional opportunities (language courses, online learning courses)
Company and team events and possibility to join social groups
Advantages:
SAP experience and Root cause analysis skils
Willing to acquire professional certifications such as Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), Certified Information Security Professional (CISSP), and/or Certified Internal Auditor (CIA)
Eager to stay current with Cybersecurity and IT Governance trends
Understanding of internal control frameworks (e.g. COBIT, ISO 27001, NIST Cybersecurity Framework)
Discover areas for improvement including simplification and automation of controls or audit processes
Experience with Data Visualization Software and data extraction
