Data Analyst Expert
Our partner is one of the world's biggest financial providers, which has a regional IT and financial center located in Budapest. They are looking for a new colleague, who would like to gain knowledge in a cutting-edge data analytics, reporting and integration platform called KNIME. The new member of the team will actively take part in the finance regulatory reporting workflows by transforming data and automating processes using complex data structures. Stable and truly multicultural corporate environment, work-life balance, further career opportunities, competitive salary and attractive benefit package are waiting for the new colleague.
About this position
Take part in keeping close contact and maintaining effective communication with technology teams and with stakeholders in the Financial Regulatory
Reporting departments and other business units
Gather and understand business needs regarding the regulatory reporting processes and workflows, and identify possible technical solutions
Analyze, transform and automate complex and diverse data structures, create workflows using KNIME and establish user testing for the optimal usage
Participate in setting strategic direction for automation of reports, integrating the special area know-how with an in-depth understanding of industry standards and best-practices in data transformation
Mentor and train junior team members, support non-technical colleagues, facilitate handover of the final solution
Requirements
Min. 3-4 years of experience in reporting roles, where automation and transformation functions were also necessary
A background in finance and/or computer science
Relevant higher-education degree
Experience in the field of banking is beneficial
Advanced MS Excel knowledge
Experience with any of the following data transformation, automation, visualization tools: KNIME, Alteryx, Talend, Tableau, PowerBI, VBA, Qlik, Python etc.
Fluent English both verbally and in writing
High level of analytical skills, structured mindset, ability to work with complex and diverse data structures
Precision, need for clean codes and scripts
Good interpersonal skills, ability to manage stakeholder and partner relationships across large, matrixed organizations
Ability to work independently, actively put forward ideas and solutions to help the organization operate more efficiently
High degree of curiosity, also be able to follow industry specific guidelines and SOPs
Offer
A visible impact on data transformation and automation projects on a higher level as well
Work-life balance, no overtime
Attractive benefits (health insurance/all you can move/ private pension/ community events)
Multicultural atmosphere
Home office opportunity
Flexible working hours
