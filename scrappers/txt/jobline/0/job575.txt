Senior Business Analyst
Your new company
E-commerce start-up on a quest to develop a market leading e-commerce platform from ground zero
Your new role
 Gathering, validating, analysing and documenting business requirements
o Conducting meetings and presentations to share ideas and findings
o Modelling business processes and identifying opportunities for process improvements
o Identifying issues, risks and benefits of existing and proposed solutions and outlining business impacts
o Creating functional specifications for solu ons
o Estimating costs and identifying business savings
o Supporting business transition and helping to establish change
o Effectively communicating your insights and plans to cross-functional team members and management
o Gathering critical information from meetings with various stakeholders and producing useful reports
o Ensuring solutions meet business needs and requirements
o Prioritizing initiatives based on business needs and requirements
What you'll need to succeed
o A minimum of 5 years of experience in business analysis or a related field
o Exceptional analytical and conceptual thinking skills
o Advanced technical skills
o Excellent documentation skills
o Excellent planning, organizational, and time management skills
o A history of leading and supporting successful projects
o Fundamental analytical and conceptual thinking skills
o Competency in Microsoft applications including Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook
o Experience with Agile Methodologies, Scrum, Kanban
o Experience working closely with Product Owner
o Experience with Miro, Figma and Visio is a plus
o Experience with UML 2.0, BPMN 2.0 is a plus
o Experience in eCommerce or Retail Commerce Domain is a plus
o Project Management / Assisstant skills is a plus
o Test Management, Manual Testing, UAT Testing experience is a plus
What you'll get in return
o Competitive Salary
o Annual bonus
o Flexible home office
