Power Platform Expert
About Our Partner: 
They are a multinational company with a purpose to create financial security and stability for their clients. Our Partner is looking for is Power Platform Expert who will be responsible for building and operating governance technical framework around Microsoft Power Platform services, focusing on Power BI (premium) and Power Apps, to support our employees in self-service data access and data management as an important element of our digitalization strategy. 
Tasks:
Design, implement and support operation of a governance framework and toolset, integrated with our Azure based Modern Data Platform, 
Support building knowledge for the Power Platform community to be prepared of utilizing on Power Platform capabilities, 
Be the first point of contact with local and company-wide architects, also to Microsoft in case of support or request must be escalated,                                                          
Be the owner over the common and sharable assets of the Power Platform services/BI products and build & educate the company’s community to prepare for self-service BI, 
Continuously follow and learn new capabilities of the Power Platform and adjust governance method, tools or processes corresponding to expected changes 
Requirements: 
2-4 years of experience in a similar position/field, 
University/college degree in the field of IT/business, 
Professional MS Power BI, MS SharePoint, 
Minimum intermediate MS PowerApps and MS Power Automate knowledge, 
Openness to acquire or deepen Python knowledge, 
Cooperative attitude 
Advantages: 
Power pivot knowledge, 
DevOps and SQL knowledge, 
Power BI CI/CD deployment process knowledge, 
Power pages understanding
