engineering team leader
The new team leader will be responsible for:
Technical and disciplinary leadership of gasoline and transmission calibration teams
Responsible for know-how, budget and workload of the department
Monitoring and development of calibration processes
Recruitment according growth strategy and employee development
Responsibility for the calibration projects
Supporting sales
Our partner is the biggest independent company who develops engines (combustion, electrical and hybrid as well), electrical powertrains, batteries and softwares for the partners in the automotive industry. Moreover our partner also participates in the process of simulation, testing and calibration. You can find their products in various vehicles like cars, trucks and self-driving cars. We are looking for an engineering team leader in the field of vehicle calibration to join their team. 
The ideal candidate has:
Minimum 5 years of experience in calibration and/or thermodynamics 
Excellent knowledge of combustion engines and/or transmission
Experience of leading projects and teams
Excellent computer skills (MS Office and base of application software)
Good English knowledge (advantage: German language knowledge) 
Business and service-oriented attitude
Comprehensive management knowledge
Commitment to continuous improvement and personal development
Assertiveness and self-management skills
What our partner can offer:
Private health service, life and accident insurance
Flexible working time, possibility to work from home
Travel reimbursement
Relax and fitness room
Shuttle buses between Budapest and Érd
