IT Business Analyst
About Our Partner: 
Our partner is a global leader focused on the design, manufacture and distribution of agricultural machinery and infrastructure. Now they are looking for a passionate and talented Business Analyst who would be an integral part of the Product Teams in the Agile Software Delivery Process. The DPE enriches the team in the areas of Requirements Engineering, Continuous Testing and Production Support. 
Tasks: 
Refinement of User Stories and Features, 
Methodical support in generating ideas with Product Owners and Subject Matter Experts, 
Determination, documentation, checking & coordination and management of requirements, 
Definition of acceptance criteria, 
Definition of the integration points with other systems and communication of the effects of possible system changes in cooperation with other teams, 
Contribution to the definition of wireframes and visual designs, 
Support of the global development teams in clarifying requirements, 
Visual representation of business processes, 
Support in estimating efforts for new developments and improvements, 
Knowledge transfer for new and changed functions for the change management process, 
Quality assurance of the implementation of technical and functional requirements, 
Planning and execution of tests, 
Definition of test conditions and scripts, 
Support in setting up test automation, 
Analysis of test results, 
Successful processing of customer and dealer inquiries for troubleshooting and changes as part of The Company’s IT incident process, 
Error analysis and support of the development team in troubleshooting, 
Monitoring of the system uptime, 
Active participation and preparation for agile ceremonies such as Product Increment Planning, Sprint Planning, Refinements, Sprint Reviews and Retrospectives, 
You may need to travel to European countries, to the USA or to India 1-2 times a year,
Responsibility for compliance with all Company guidelines by all employees and teams, as well as compliance with all control processes and standards. 
Requirements: 
Bachelor`s / Master`s degree in computer science, IT, engineering or equivalent education, 
Several years of professional experience (approx. 3-7 years), 
Relevant experience in software development, 
Experience in requirements engineering, testing and production support is desirable, 
Previous Hybris (SAP Commerce) platform experience is advantageous, 
Hands on experience, working with B2B and B2C eCommerce solutions, 
Experience working on an Omni-channel implementation, 
Understanding of ecommerce business processes for order management systems, payment  
gateways, baskets, delivery & fulfilment solutions, 
Understanding of DAM and PIM, Content Management solutions, 
Analytical comprehensive thinking, 
Good communication skills in spoken and written English (German is an advantage), 
Successfully influencing different groups and teams in a complex environment, 
This opportunity requires flexibility as you would become part of an international team with colleagues in different time zones,
​​​​​​​Act as an Ecommerce evangelist within the organisation, helping to create broader understanding of the benefits and opportunities the digital customer experience channels provide. 
Advantage: 
Knowledge of agile methods (e.g. SAFe)
