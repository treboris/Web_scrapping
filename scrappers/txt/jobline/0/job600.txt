Lead Developer IoT Cloud
Your Tasks will be:
Responsibility for the architecture and interface design, requirements analysis and subsequent implementation for an end-to-end IoT cloud
Responsibility for conceptual tasks in backend development with high demands on data security and data protection.
Proactive source of ideas for new technologies, systems, processes and optimizations
Analysis, conception, implementation and testing of new customer-specific applications and modules.
Support for the requirements survey, presentation of the results to the customer and technical and technological advice.
Coaching and technical management of 10 developers in the team
The applications that you are responsible for implementing are based on Java, JavaScript, Python and Go. Other programming languages can also be integrated. The deployment infrastructure is in the cloud.
Our Partner is a global technology company supplying systems for passenger cars, commercial vehicles and industrial technology, enabling the next generation of mobility. We are looking for a Lead developer to be part of their team. 
Requirements:
Successfully completed studies in the field of (business) computer science, business (engineering) or a comparable subject
Very experienced in developing IT architectures
Several years of professional experience in the field of software development / full stack, cloud, microservices and knowledge of infrastructure and operations
Systematic application of methods for requirements analysis and agile software development (Scrum, Kanban, UX) as well as systematic application of standards in IT service management (IT-PEP, ITIL, DevOps)
Technology stack: extensive knowledge of Java and JavaScript, Docker, MS Azure and databases including graph databases
Fluency in written and spoken German and English
