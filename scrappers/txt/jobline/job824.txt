Cloud Hosting Engineer
About Our Partner:
The company’s main focus is on the evolution of future navigation and infotainment systems and their online service. Now, they are looking for a talented Cloud Hosting Engineer who will join their success story.
Tasks:
Operation and development of the service infrastructure and services
Proactive monitoring of the systems
Discovering the root causes of problems, developing solution proposals
Creating solutions that automate/support troubleshooting and prevention
Optimizing the systems
Developing and introducing new solutions
Cooperation with the development and operating teams, representing the interests of the services
Supporting iGO developers and testers in the use of online services
Requirements:
Experience gained in System Operation
Deep knowledge of Linux systems
Automation knowledge
Experience in operating virtual systems
Knowledge of some script and/or programming language (e.g. shell, Awk, Perl, Python, Java, C/C++)
Good command of English
Advantages:
Knowledge of (Postgre) SQL language, experience in database server operation
Knowledge of (Apache) Webserver
Knowledge of Middleware (Glassfish)
Usage of regular expressions
Experience in operating cloud environments
Experience in classic infrastructure operation and in DevOps
Munkavégzés helye
