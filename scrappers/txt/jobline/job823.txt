German Master Data Specialist 
Our stable and prestigious multinational partner is currently building up a
new operation and
looking for a master data specialist to join their them for an exciting
transition project in Budapest.
About the job
As a German speaking specialist you will responsible for keeping contact
with the streams abroad and maintain MDM attributes. Ensure data quality,
enrichment and integrity, resolve any queries and issues. Assist
integration of the data for customers and vendors and assure that
procedures are compliant.
What's in it for you
You can join a new operation and grow with the new team
Vast range of career opportunities
Competitive salary and benefit package
Home office opportunity
Employee-focused culture
The ideal candidate
Has at least 2 years experience in a multinational/SSC environment from
a similar field (preferably from Accounting or Master Data)
Has at least an intermediate English and German language knowledge
Customer focused mindset
Precise and can multitask well
Has logical thinking and good organizational skills
