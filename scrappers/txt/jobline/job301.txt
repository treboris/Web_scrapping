The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Senior Software Engineer (Data & Analytics)REF1182D
In the Digital Hub Network Data Intelligence (NDI) we will in future bundle all relevant activities & teams for analytical support in solving technical problems of our customers. In addition, we are developing a series of proactive measures to prevent errors and malfunctions from occurring in the first place, e.g. through AI-based anomaly detection in our network infrastructure.
We support Data-Scientist teams from Telekom Service and Technik by providing network infrastructure and ticket data on our Big Data platform. We prepare this data in interdisciplinary teams in such a way that we provide a digital representation of the Deutsche Telekom access network, the relevant parts of our customers' home network and the technically relevant parts of the contract.
As a senior software engineer in the area of Data & Analytics, you develop in agile teams complex software solutions. You are responsible for the development of complex products and services covering the whole software lifecycle.
You're a good match if you have a profound experience in the area of Big Data covering:
Expert know how in technologies and methods: Streaming processing, ideally with Cloudera and practical experience with Kafka, NiFi and REST
Expert know how in (object oriented) programming languages: Java, Python, Perl
Know How and experience in classical BI/DWH environment: SQL, DB2 (Unix)
Experience in hardware sizing and in the implementation of security requirements and software engineering know how using agile Methods on Design, Architecture, Development and Test
Practical experience on tools for change- and configuration- management like GIT and JIRA
Experience on agile methods like Scrum and SAFE
Following technologies are in scope:
Cloudera, Kafka, NiFi, Flink, Spark, Informatica, Unix/Linux, Java, REST, SOAP, PL SQL, DB2 (Unix), Perl, Scala, Python.
