The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Senior Security Consultant (REF1365Q)
Consulting CU Shell tribes, projects and client with security & compliance expertise on request. Security Consultant will often be engaged on more than one project or assignment at any one time. Security engagements will vary in budget and duration.Security Consultant will be accountable to deliver the agreed scope  within budgets  and should be fully chargeable to projects. Key Accountabilities:Providing and maintaining methods and processes according to ITIL to support the  IT Risk Management programs and fulfilling the customers’ expectations in Compliance, Security and Operations. Providing technical security end-to-end advice and consultancy to internal and external customers of the CU Shell in line with Shell and T-Systems Project Excellence standards and processes.Ensuring that all customer security and compliance requirements and expectations are met in line with all applicable T-Systems security standards.(PSA / ESARIS)Identification of security requirements within the scope of a proposal or project. 
General:Bachelor's Degree with a focus in IT Systems / Computer Science / Information Security.+5 years of experience in the field of IT Security.Cloud domain expertiseProfessional certification CISA / CISSP is required  CISM / CGEIT is a plus Competences and experience:Security Requirement analysis- Uses broad knowledge of IT Security practices and regulations to find effective and practical solutions to security and compliance requirements.Being able to comprehend architectural concepts.Relationship management (with Shell IRM)- Cultivates working relations and partners within T-Systems and to customer business functions to achieve superior results.Ability to work under uncertainties. Proactive and suitable to work in agile environment- Provide consultative services to improve processes and minimize risk for both T-Systems and the customerFamiliarity with Shell/Client environment, tools, processes and people along with TSI internal security policies and procedures.Participates in the planning, scoping, designing and testing of IT controls for the customer.Summarizes and documents security and compliance objectives, processes, audits, and evidence for management reporting.Continually updates knowledge of common IT systems (Operating Systems, network devices, applications) and underlying controls frameworks (COBIT, ISO, PCI, etc.).Supports Legal and Sales teams to review contracts and identify commercial and operational implications to security and complianceTechnical skills:Attack techniques of information securityInformation security Audits and  ManagementRequirement analysisSolution architectureNetwork and internet securityCloud SecuritySAPVulnerability managementAdvantage:Professional certification CISA / CISSP is required , CISM / CGEIT is a plus PM and Agile experience and certification is a plusShell environment and/or oil & gas sector experience, background and knowledge
