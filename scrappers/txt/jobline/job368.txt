Senior Software Solution Designer for Open Sovereign Cloud - REF964D
Sovereign Cloud is T-Systems' cloud offering for clients with high sovereignty needs. Clients in regulated sectors such as healthcare and the public sector are put in complete charge of their data – on an open-source platform, hosted in Europe. Our focus is to build new product – Sovereign Cloud within T-systems international cloud portfolio. As a Software Developer in the Open Sovereign Cloud team (OSC) working on Gardener you will help us deliver the mission of helping customers run and use services based on containers with complete data sovereignty anywhere. At OSC, we are building a core set of functionalities that will allow our regulated and sectorial customers to create and use services based on Kubernetes at scale in their choice of regulated and compliant data centers, public clouds or hyperscalers. You will be part of an exceptional team moving the needle towards making containers as the next generation compute platform everywhere. This is an opportunity engineer system on a massive scale, and to gain top-notch experience in distributed systems and cloud computing. Senior Software Solution Designer for application development is responsible for end to end solutions for customer’s demand with respect to different options on market, available internal systems, and Deutsche Telekom standards. Holder of the position is responsible to be part of the scrum team as the highest priority to drive business in order to bring value for customer. Key accountabilities:Support and develop software on Open Sovereign Cloud as defined by product management and product ownersLead and work in SAFE methodologyDevelop software solutions by studying information needs; conferring with users; studying systems flow, data usage and work processes; investigating problem areas; following the software development lifecycleDevelop complex cloud secure solutions based on open source technologies (GoLang, Kubernetes) as specified in requirementsDocument and demonstrate solutions by developing documentation, flowcharts, layouts, diagrams, charts, code comments and clear codeImprove operations by conducting systems analysis; recommending changes in policies and proceduresAnalyze initial customer demand and existing system architectures to bring value for customerDefines architecture rules and specificationsDevelop solutions for complex systems with good operability and a good price/performance ratioIdentify different options for fulfillment of customer’s demand to find optimal solution with appropriate ratio effort, price, time, qualityEnsures compliance with rules and plansDesign integration of solution, application, to existing systemsPerform consulting to customer with respect to technical realization to mitigate differences in business vs technical requirementsCommunicates effectively as a designer with developers (coders, testers)Creates solution/architecture documentationDesign test driver, simulators & automation, logical test cases for test items, test infrastructure, test end & test abort criteria from release & test criteriaImplement solutionPerform lessons learned
Basic qualifications: 2+ years of non-internship professional software development experience3+ years of experience contributing to the architecture and design (architecture, design patterns, reliability, and scaling) of new and current systems.5+ years of non-internship professional software development experience.Programming experience with at least one modern language such as Golang, Java, C++, or C# including objectoriented design.4+ years of experience contributing to the architecture and design (architecture, design patterns, reliability, and scaling) of new and current systems
