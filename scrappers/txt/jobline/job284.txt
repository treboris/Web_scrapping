The largest ICT employerin Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH)is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. Junior Financial Performance Associate - MilesPlus Support - REF 1442J
In this junior position, you are expected to provide administrative support for the time writing service, provided to a large global customer, as a member of a wider Financial Management team.Time writing (work time administration) is the basis of financial management activities. The team handles the work time administration tool for the customer and provide full administrative support, including:Receiving requests via email (shared mailbox) or a sharepoint toolQuality checking requests, calrifying any missing/unclear informationAdministering/modifying time writing tool data, including user data and work time booking codes for different work elementsKeeping in touch with other support teams and management team members internationallyMonitoring and following up on requests, making sure that deadlines are keptAnswering user questions / handling issues and escalations via email or phoneProviding regular and ad-hoc reportsSupporting the team members with updating the process documentation and identifying and realizing process improvements
Requirements: Active, at least intermediate level of English knowledgeGood command on Microsoft Office tools (Excel, PPT)Ability to work in a multi-located virtual teamAble to cope with monotonous tasksWe are happy if you possess:University/college degree in Finance/Economics is an advantageInternational experience or customer service/ service management experience You are welcome if you have:Customer focusTeam play abilitiesExcellent communication and problem-solving skillsPrecise attitude and pays attention to detailInternational experience or customer service/ service management experience 
Junior
* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
