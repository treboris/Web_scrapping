Network Engineer (Firewall)
Your new project
The Service Hub NETS is looking for a Network Engineer to join the firewall team for the operation and further development of the current security solutions. Together we want to transform actively into our future network design and services based on mostly software defined networks and cloud services to support the business of our internal customers. This includes firewall, proxy, OSGD fields in agile working environments. 
Your new role
developing and supporting of the systems of firewall, proxy and OSGD
developing of new security functions, e.g. intrusion prevention, forwarding proxy, internet service database
transferring the existing Web Application Firewall Services to the new techniques
activities regarding modernization and innovation
migration of the existing system in direction of cloud
on-call duty
minimal operational tasks
Your day-to-day tasks will be based on the responsibilities, in co-operation with the service team.
What you'll need to succeed
hands-on knowledge of current firewall products (CISCO ASA/Firepower)
proxy knowledge
knowledge in intrusion detection and prevention, threat defense or malware detection
experience in setting up and designing c
German language
willingness of developing and learning
Nice to have:
English language
knowledge and usage of cloud technology
experience with working in virtualized environments 
traditional network knowledge based on virtualized/cloudified service farms
