The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
CMS Cloud Application Developer – Migration area (REF851R)
Our Automation Team is looking for enthusiastic team members, with passion to participate in global development projects. We are continuously creating easy-to-use, fast and reliable software automation solutions for customers over the world. Working within the Content Migration Services (CMS) Development Team on the development and evolution of our proprietary data migration software. The role involves working in a small, agile development team specialising in current Cloud and DevOps technologies to build solutions used by our delivery team to deliver scalable and repeatable data migration solutions for our customers
Tasks
Work within a small development team writing specifications, building, testing and documenting our software solutions
Help to define, build, and support a proprietary software platform, meeting both industry and T-Systems delivery standards and to satisfy the requirements of T-Systems customers
Manage and develop continuous development and integration of our data migration software solutions
Be part of a dynamic technical team, leading the way with the creation and implementation of configuration management and automation tools and processes.
Help to develop scaling strategies, automation scripts, and solutions to implement, streamline, and execute our data migration solutions
Conduct training sessions and knowledge share workshops with other team members
Influence current and future technology and design choices within the T-Systems Content Migration Services team
Experienced
Experience in the software development process with tools and languages such as the following (not all are essential):
.NET Platform (C# / ASP.Net / .Net Standard)
Continuous Integration (TeamCity / ADO etc.)
Version Control (Git)
Actor Model (Akka.Net)
Relational and NoSQL Databases (SQL Server / Oracle / Postgres) (RavenDB / MongoDB)
Python / IronPython
JSON, XML, XPath
Web Services and Protocols (REST / SOAP) (TCP / HTTP / SSL) 
Familiarity with the key components available in cloud platform solutions from Amazon, Google MS or T-Systems and knowledge of how to provision and manage cloud servers, queues, cache, databases and network components (Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform)
Experience of deploying scalable cloud solutions on virtual servers using distributed message queues, distributed cache and associated network components such as NAT gateways and VPC endpoints
Apply, if You are:
Experienced in working within a small, agile development team
A self-motivated, creative thinker with excellent communication skills
Keen to learn new skills and comfortable with conducting training sessions and knowledge share workshops with other team members
Able to contribute to and influence current and future technology and design choices
Up to date with current cloud technologies and have ample knowledge and experience in system automation, deployment and implementation
