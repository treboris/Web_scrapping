Present in the market under the Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions brand name, IT-Services Hungary (ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group and is the largest ICT employer in Hungary. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team. 
AWS Architect - REF341L
Tasks
Create Architectural designs for workloads migrating to AWS
Perform AWS Well-Architected Reviews
Create Infrastructure as Code for AWS services as per the Architecture designs
Create CI/CD Pipelines for automated deployments.
Develop Serverless architecture designs in AWS
Day-to-day AWS support to the customers
Produce Containerisation solutions on AWS
Produce documentation for technical implementations in AWS
Requirements
Demonstrate a strong understanding of the AWS Well-Architected Framework to validate solution/architecture
Deep expertise in AWS services across Security, Identity, Compliance, Management, Governance, and Networking
Cloud Security Architecture experience
Experience in implementing security and governance on AWS
Proven experience of Network architecture and design
Excellent ability to collaborate and manage the expectations of stakeholders
Strong experience in defining standards, setting technical roadmaps, designs, strategies and mentoring
Strong experience in architecting and migrating large scale applications to AWS, as well as Disaster Recovery solutions
Reliable and consistent decision maker
Working closely with DevOps teams to ensure security is a large focus and help to build a true DevSecOps culture
Working in an Agile environment with multi discipline teams to find threats and solutions swiftly and effectively
Development tools: GitHub, Python, Perl, Shell, JSON/YAML, Jenkins
Network: IPv4 CIDR, IPv6, BGP, DNS, VPN (IPsec)
Infrastructure as Code: CloudFormation, Terraform, Ansible, Chef, Puppet, OpenStack
Familiar with AWS Services: EC2, S3, IAM, RDS, ECS, ECR, EKS, KMS, Certificate Manager, VPC, Route53, CloudTrail and CloudWatch
Any AWS Certification is a plus
