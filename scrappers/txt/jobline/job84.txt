Present in the market under the Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions brand name, IT-Services Hungary (ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group and is the largest ICT employer in Hungary. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team. 
Cloud Architect in Azure Engineering - REF477J
You are the one we are looking for, if:You love to own a problem and are creative in resolving it in a scalable way, collaborating with a talented, really nice and honest team of cloud engineersYou enjoy creating functional and technical designs for cloud operations at scale, breaking them down for implementation by cloud engineers. This includes taking technological, architectural, and design decisions, often jointly with product owner, developers, and other stakeholdersYou see documentation of your designs as a useful method so engineers know what to implement and so we can refer to them laterWhen designing you always have the complete customer experience and set of services in mind (no customer-specific solutions)You are not afraid of starting from scratch, and you are also good at improving existing implementationsYou have state-of-the-art knowledge in the software engineering process and are committed to further developing the quality of software developmentYou can get the team behind your ideas while being open for others’ ideasYou proactively drive the topics of the day, organise, and facilitate meetingsYou see yourself at eye level with all team members and stakeholders, no matter who they are and where they come from, appreciating their respective expertise  You contribute to maintaining the product roadmap (customer-facing features as well as any technical debt)You support our revenue contribution and OKR business goals
You are a perfect match if:You love to dream big and take responsibility for making these dreams come trueYou have an ownership mindset and a getting-things-done attitudeSystems thinking is your go-to approachMicrosoft Azure and Azure DevOps is your home turfYou are really experienced in coding and scripting in PowerShell, PythonYou value inner clarity as a means to make your point with confidenceYou love to temporarily work directly with our customersYou are fluent in spoken and written English
