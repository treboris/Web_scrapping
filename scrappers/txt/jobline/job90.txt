Present in the market under the Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions brand name, IT-Services Hungary (ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group and is the largest ICT employer in Hungary. Kubernetes Specialist (AWS) - REF141L
Tasks
You’ll help drive, implement, support and maintain infrastructure services with a primary focus on Kubernetes (EKS), this will become the primary core component
Drive and support migration of services to K8S environments in AWS EKS
Develop and support consistent deployment/management practices across
Performing POC (Proof of Concept) technical evaluations for new container technologies for use in the Cloud
Hardening, securing the Kubernetes cluster with monitoring and auditing dashboards
Day-to-day Kubernetes Administration support to the customers
Work closely with application teams in ensuring best practices are followed and the infrastructure automation can be self-service
Produce documentation for technical implementations in AWS
Requirements
Implementation of containerisation workloads in AWS (ECS, EKS…)
Experience in implementing security and governance in Kubernetes clusters
Troubleshooting and optimising containerised workload deployments
Building and configuring Kubernetes-based infrastructure, Core-DNS, Kube Proxy, Ingress, etc
Proven experience working as a Kubernetes administrator in virtualised environment or public cloud
High-level understanding of AWS services across Security, Identity, Compliance, Management, Governance, and Networking
Excellent ability to collaborate and manage the expectations of stakeholders
Strong experience in defining standards, setting technical roadmaps, designs, strategies and mentoring on containerisation.
Development tools: GitHub, Python, Perl, Shell, JSON/YAML, Jenkins
Network: IPv4 CIDR, IPv6, BGP, DNS, VPN (IPsec)
Infrastructure as Code: CloudFormation, Terraform, Ansible, Chef, Puppet, OpenStack
Any AWS/Kubernetes administration Certification is a plus
Advantages:
Additional certification such as CKA (Certified Kubernetes Administrator), CKAD (Certified Kubernetes Application Developer) etc will be a bonus
Experience in containers, registries and microservices
