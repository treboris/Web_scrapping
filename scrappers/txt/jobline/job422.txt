Data Analytics Expert
Data Analytics Expert
Our multinational electricity management partner is looking for a seasoned professional for its data development team.
The new colleague will play an essential role in understanding and translating data correlation across procurement and sourcing processes, leading workshops and collaboration with procurement and sourcing professionals to further enhance our organization’s business intelligence capabilities.
Tasks:
Be accountable for building advanced and digitized data analytics.
Drive digital transformation aligned with our strategy.
Use processes, and tools to deliver enhanced efficiency in the organization.
Responsibilities:
Participates in global BI programs
Create and implement integrated data analytics
Drive data intelligence initiative
Seek and build tools/processes to support effectiveness
Design and create dashboards, management reporting, smart visuals, and predictive analytics
Engage with local data owners driving data quality and accuracy initiatives
Liaise with stakeholders to understand business needs
Design data frontend platforms
Participate in process improvement and transformation projects
Skills:
Microsoft Excel, SharePoint and PowerApps experiences
Knowledge and understanding of procurement data sources and processes
Strong presentation skills
Understands the role of BI in strategic planning
Able to connect with people and creates strong network of community
Has a strong understanding of operations and seeks the understanding of processes behind data
Knowledge of Tableau and Prism is an advantage
Spoken and written fluent communication in English
