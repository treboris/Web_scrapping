Our international client is looking for an experienced test automation engineer for their team based in Budapest. By joining the team, you'll be involved in the development and testing of one of their products, which optimizes the daily utilization of flights, increases user productivity and improves airline management when there are deviations from the regular schedule.
Medior/Senior Test Automation Engineer
Feladatok
Our new test engineer's focus will be:
Participating in implementation projects in a cross-functional team
Implementing test scenarios from component level to system tests including non-functional ones
Proactively improving the QA strategy
Engaging in all of the steps of the development lifecycle as a tester
Validating requirement / user stories from testing perspective
Követelmények
The requirements you should fulfill:
At least 3 years of experience in software development or test automation
Experience in Java and/or C#
Active English and Hungarian language skills (spoken and written)
Experience in CI/CD
Extensive knowledge of automation tools and software testing processes
Understanding of software development processes and methodologies (especially Agile and Scrum)
It's an advantage if you also have:
Familiarity with some of the following technologies: SQL/NoSQL databases, Git, Selenium, Azure, Linux/Unix
Bachelors Degree in Computer Science
Experience with containerized applications (Docker, Kubernetes)
Cloud experience

What our partner offers:
Flexible working hours, remote work opportunity
Training opportunities for professional and soft skills
Supportive, professional and multinational team
Discount on company flights
Agile (scrum) environment
