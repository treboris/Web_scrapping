Senior C++ Developer
Our Partner is a global leader in the field of machine vision. Their products are used worldwide for example in the fields of logistics, automotive/electronics industry and aircraft manufactures. One of their biggest development center is located in Budapest, where the colleagues are working in an agile environment. If you want to work with the latest technologies in an international team, please keep reading on!
About this position
tackling complex challenging problems in a software engineering team, where everybody has a passion for programming
designing/developing/testing the system software of our product
developing and maintaining applications in C/C++ (you might also have the chance to work in Python)
working with Linux kernel
traveling to Germany and to USA (it is occasional)
Requirements
at least 4+ years of experience in C/C++ experience in concurrent programming and multi-threading
team player personality and ability to handle more projects simultaneously confidence to work with large codebase (order of 1 million lines of code)
experience with unit testing/clean code/reducing code complexity
engineering degree in computer science/electrical engineering or related area
excellent written and verbal knowledge in English (you will be working in an international team!)
good presentation and interpersonal skills
keen to get experiences with the technologies like Python, Linux, Embedded Linux, Yocto
Offer
working with leading edge
machine vision technology
high energy, fast moving company with colleagues, who are passionate to work with the latest technologies
recognizes achievement with unique rewards
flexible, hybrid work
