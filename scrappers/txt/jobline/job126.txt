The largest ICT employerin Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH)is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sitesin Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
ServiceNow Business Analyst (REF189R)
The role has opened due to ever growing international business demands. As a Business Analyst your main responsibility is to analyze business and IT requirements, processes; understand the as-is situation and translate the inputs into solutions based on ServiceNow platform that fulfill the requirements. Your tasks will range from understanding the client’s requirements through applying industry best practices and detailed knowledge of ServiceNow in designing an optimal solution (processes, data models, workflows, etc.) to supporting the actual implementation with story writing and testing in Agile frameworks. 
Your tasks
Understanding client and business requirements, creating specifications and documentations (via meeting, document analysis, workshops, etc.)
Providing analysis and modeling for company-wide business processes, ability to deduce needs from business operation and realize IT development potentials
Providing process design to standardize organization’s workflows and business operation while utilizing industry best practices and ServiceNow OOB recommendation and features
Helping the collaboration between customers and IT development team. Conducting professional consultation with architects, project managers, team leaders, technical leads, developers and testers
Working with multiple complex systems on a wide range area, supporting integration designs 
Creating documentation, training materials and other supporting materials post-implementation to help transition and acceptance of the platform by customer’s end users 
Supporting proposal and concept creation in IT migration and helping with transition projects
Requirements:
1-2 years of experience in business analysis, consultancy, requirement gathering & specifications or similar roles
Experience with ServiceNow (at least as an end user)
Strong oral and written communication skills (both business English and Hungarian)
Ability to work in an international environment with local and international teams
Experience implementing systems using the Agile/Scrum methodology including client requirement analysis and system design
Experience designing and implementing IT and Non-IT related process workflows, procedures based on technical standards and best practices
Experience in test plan creation and execution as well as provide guidance for key users, preparation of corporate knowledge articles and conducting trainings
Personal qualities: strong analytical skills, creativity, innovation, passion, eagerness for self-development and a good sense of humor would come in handy working with us, IT geeks
Nice to have skills and capabilities
Hands-on experience with ServiceNow configuration and administration 
Working proficiency in German language
Great presentation skills
ServiceNow certification (CSA or others)
Presales experience, working with RfX proposals
 ITIL Foundation certification 
Experience with MS Visio and other BPM tools
Junior
* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
