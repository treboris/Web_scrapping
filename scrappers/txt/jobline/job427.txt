Scrum Master
Job Purpose:
Ensure development teams follow scrum framework values and agile practices, mentor and motivate team to improve processes, facilitate meetings and decision-making processes and eliminate team impediments.
Key Accountabilities / Responsibilities:
Working with the Program Manager and Engineering Manager to deliver key technical milestones that meet the needs of the business:
Facilitate Agile ceremonies (e.g. Daily Scrum, Sprint and Release Planning, Refining, Backlog Grooming, Sprint Retrospective)
Ensure team engagement
Teach and coach team practices, identify and eliminate impediments
Help the team meet its goals and coordinate activities with other teams
Work with Program Management to understand the business strategy to prioritize the backlog
Facilitate the team’s progress toward team goals
Seek evidence of completion and confirm quality
Identify and manage risks
Unearth the root-cause of any potential delays, then develop actionable countermeasures to recover the schedule
Improve velocity, quality and predictability
Continuously improve and optimise processes to develop high-quality products
Manage conflicts, facilitate a healthy work environment
Key Competencies:
Self-starter / self-directed
Able to assess gaps and propose fixes for approval
Adept at engaging with and influencing engineering teams
Outcome focussed
Adaptable and able to prioritise
Comfortable with complexity and multiple tasks
Resilient to work in a fast-paced evolving environment
Strong written / verbal communication skills in English
Experience / Knowledge / Technical Skills Required:
MSc or BSc degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or similar
Extensive experience in the Agile Framework with emphasis on Scrum
Strong interpersonal skills, flexibility and focus on value creation
Experience in working across the full lifecycle of software development
At least 5 years’ experience in Automotive Industry
Experience with Confluence & JIRA
Knowledge of MATLAB and Simulink is an advantage
