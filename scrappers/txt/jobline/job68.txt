The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Microsoft Application Administrator - Skype for Business, Teams (REF974Y)
Operate, maintain, and support international infrastructure based on Microsoft S4B application servers.
Regular tasks:
Operating and supporting Skype for Business and Teams
Maintenance, Incident Management, Problem Management, Change Management
Solving operating system and application related incidents, documenting and analyzing system outages
Handling, resolving and closing error events and incoming requests
Resolving requests according on defined processes and SLAs, eliminating error events
Detecting major incidents and reporting them based on well-defined procedures
Troubleshooting, and performing root cause analysis
Share information with coworkers, and other resolution teams
Learning and updating knowledge databases
Regular participation in meetings, telephone conferences, regular contact with foreign colleagues.
Non regular tasks:
Analysis of technical reports and risk assessments
Group level tasks:
Checking and improving existing processes, making recommendations, check for automation possibilities
Check documentations and keep them up-to-date
Requirements:
At least 2 years relevant experience with Skype for Business, Teams support
Fluent English language knowledge
Advantage:
Enterprise Voice / Sonus knowledge
MS certificates
Location
Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs, Szeged
