Be part of a more than 20 years old success story than was born in Budapest but got international with our client. We are proud that the dedication of our colleagues and the innovative knowledge made our Budapest development center truly successful.We have a place for you in our dynamically growing company if you value professional development and would like to be part of a cheerful international environment.
You can work on challenging developments in the automotive industry together with the leading car manufacturers all over the world.
Master Data Expert
Feladatok
Responsibilities:
You will be responsible for requirement gathering, analysis and implementation of changes surrounding our master data with a focus on material master data and BOM master data
Trouble shooting master data issues including interface monitoring and providing long term solutions will be part of your daily tasks
Collaborate with relevant business and IT responsibles to design and implement data quality measures
Optimize the transition period of our change to the S/4HANA template in the upcoming years is going to be part of your responsibilities
Benefits
A modern office in the 11th district is waiting for you to support your creativity with fun community places, cafe rooms, an own cinema and a skybar
You can be part of a successful and growing company in the field of technology
We support you with flexible working hours and home office opportunity (60 % of the working time in many of our positions)
Competitive salary and wide range of other benefits (cafeteria & bonus) are provided to you
You can learn new languages in small groups, selecting from 5 languages
We organize leisure time activities (board games, sport activities)
Követelmények
Experience in master data management with a focus on material master data as well as BOM data
Be able to understand and investigate topics related to multiple business areas
Thorough knowledge of at least one SAP module, preferably PP or MM
Strong written and communication skills in English are required
Advantage: SAP MDG or SAP PSS experience
