Our client is one of the market leaders in Hungary as an architecture design software company. We are looking for a C++ Software Developer to join their international team in Budapest.
C++ Software Developer
Feladatok
Development of an architectural modeling application in C++ in a Scrum team of 5-10 people
Design, implement, and test user functionalities with a high degree of autonomy
Continuous improvement of program structure on small and large scales 
Frequent coordination with other team members on architecture-related issues
Reviewing and commenting on code and technical designs written by other developers, and constructively addressing identified issues
Provide specialist consultation and teaching, comment on designs, and identify directions for further development
Mentoring junior colleagues
Követelmények
You are our ideal colleague if you:
have a minimum of 3-5 years of experience in C++ development 
are demanding of your own code
write expressive code that others can easily understand
put in a little extra effort for better quality
are open to renewing parts of code you have written in the past
are not afraid to work on large, complex systems because you know what the guarantees of bug-free performance are
are willing to work with others, you are happy to help your colleagues with ideas and you welcome and consider their suggestions and comments
are also interested in things you are not yet familiar with
have the courage to communicate in English, both orally and in writing, on professional issues 
Amit kínálunk
Competitive salary with a pretty wide benefits package
Refer-a-Friend – get a bonus in the employee referral program
Astonishing office environment in a green area
Every necessary tool and device in the office will be provided for you to be able to comfortably perform all project tasks: computers, meeting rooms, spacious modern kitchens with professional coffee machines and also full of various kinds of soft drinks
Wide range of leading-edge training (including both professional and also different language courses)
Employee assistance program (psychologist support, legal or financial advice)
Possibility to join a supportive and caring, great community
