Our partner is a global supplier of software engineering and IT outsourcing services, and they are looking for a Junior PHP Developer. You’ll be part of a team of professionals who are ready to find the best tailor-made IT solutions for their multinational clients in various industries and solve complex problems.
Junior PHP Developer
Feladatok
Tasks for the first three months:
Together with a mentor, a professional development plan will be designed directly for you
Hone your web development skills and use modern frameworks
Learn the subtleties of working in a team of responsive and professional colleagues
You will improve your English skills at an incredible pace
You will get a chance to try your skills in an actual project
Követelmények
What our client expects from you:
Basic knowledge of PHP 8 and OOP principles (at least 1 year of experience or 3-5 related projects)
Motivation to work as a web developer with the following technologies: Laravel, Symfony, or other frameworks
Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and a desire to improve
Strong knowledge of Git, and you already understand the difference between merge and rebase
Great communication skills in English are a must, because of the global environment
A desire to constantly develop your skills and to be a part of a great team long-term :)
Will be a plus:
Knowledge of Vue, React, Angular
