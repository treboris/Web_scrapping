Sustainability Business Analyst (Power BI)
Tasks
Establishing a framework and process for strategic intelligence (market research, competitive and market intelligence);
Evolve tools and reports to support the market forecasting process;
Identify relevant information and prepare implications based on key insights on market, competitive and customer analysis to team leadership;
Support with data analytics, data validation and creation of reports for top-management meetings;
Collection and analysis of market and financial KPIs and visualization in dashboards;
Outside-in challenging of market and component-level sales planning including comprehensive market/ competitor outlook;
Provide market data for SP/OP development (target setting, opportunity management, market size, and competitor market position/ share);
Our client is a global technology company supplying systems for passenger cars, commercial vehicles and industrial technology, enabling the next generation of mobility. They allow vehicles to see, think and act. In the four technology domains of Vehicle Motion Control, Integrated Safety, Automated Driving, and Electric Mobility, the company offers comprehensive product and software solutions for established vehicle manufacturers and newly emerging transport and mobility service providers. Our client electrifies a wide range of vehicle types. With its products, the company contributes to reducing emissions, protecting the climate and enhancing safe mobility.
Requirements
University /College degree;
1-3 or more years experience in relevant field preferred;
Excellent strategic and analytical skills (deriving product and solution trends, defining & structuring problems, driving analysis and “insights”, and making recommendations) and interpretation ability;
Strong business acumen as well as deep experience and understanding of e-mobility technologies and products, industry and markets;
IT affinity and experiences with digital solutions and drive to implement / manage those;
Excellent communication and presentation skills;
Committed team player willing to cooperate with multiple colleagues across office locations;
As we are an international team fluency in English is a requirement (spoken and written);
Advantages 
German language
Experience in Power-BI
Offer
Stable company background and long-term working possibility;
Full of challenges and high-level professional activities at an international company;
Participation in international projects and commission to foreign countries;
Career opportunities, continuous training program and language courses;
Competitive salary and performance-related bonus;
Cafeteria with optional elements;
School start support
