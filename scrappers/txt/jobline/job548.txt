SAP Consultant
Feladatok
Supporting onsite three manufacturing sites coordinating projects related to SAP developments
Requirements assessment related to SAP-PP and SAP-MM modules
Transferring the current status and needs to the developers, then discussing the feasibility of the plans
Preparation of specifications (business blueprint), coordinating the implementation and customization processes
Participating in the development of functional design and configuration
Stakeholder management, project tracking, scheduling and follow-up 
Examining occurring ad-hoc issues, proposing actions to solve them 
Completing the roll-out process, conducting go-live, integrating the finished product into the client's systems
Follow-up, support and incident management duties after the delivery phase
Taking part in global SAP projects, in alignment with the global IT strategies

1-3 years of SAP experience (SAP-PP, SAP-MM)
Experience in ABAP / ABAP-OO
Experience in customizing processes
Fluency in English

Industrial process knowledge
