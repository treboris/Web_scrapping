Our international client is looking for an experienced test automation lead for their team based in Budapest. By joining the team, you'll be involved in the development and testing of one of their products, which optimizes the daily utilization of flights, increases user productivity and improves airline management when there are deviations from the regular schedule.
Test Lead
Feladatok
Our new test lead's focus will be coaching and mentoring the developer and tester team, as well as taking part in the operative tasks.
Creating and driving a testing strategy for the product
Consolidating and enhancing test environments
Collaborating with supporting roles and stakeholders
Synchronizing the testing processes with customer needs
Participating in all phases of the software development lifecycle
Using your expertise to help create development tests
Követelmények
The requirements you should fulfill:
At least 5 years of relevant working experience
Selenium, Git, Java knowledge and experience
Active English and Hungarian language skills (spoken and written)
Excellent knowledge and in the field of software testing, as well as a comprehensive understanding of the processes of software development
Ability to take the "big picture" mindset
Root-cause analysis and ability to help others find solutions
It's an advantage if you also have:
Knowledge of: javascript, SQL, azure, linux, azure
Experience in development of UI testing system
Amit kínálunk
What our partner offers:
Flexible working hours, opportunity to spend 4 days a week in home office
Training opportunities for professional and soft skills
Supportive, professional and multinational team
Discount on company flights
Agile (scrum) environment
