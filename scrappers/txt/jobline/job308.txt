The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group and Germany-based Deutsche Telekom IT. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team. 
Software engineer(REF588C)
Our group VNI is responsible for the integration, validation and production of test results ("System Under Test") in accordance with the requirements of the Deutsche Telekom AG Platform. Daily tasks carried out in several virtual squads with German and International colleagues.You will be part of an innovative and agile work surrounding where you will be implementing future oriented software solutions to manage  one of the most focused Project within Telekom DeutschlandTasks:Writing well tested, scalable and performant code which flawlessly integrates in our existing software solutionYou conceptualize, design and implement monitoring solutions for our microservice infrastructure.You create visualizations of backend activities and define metrics to enable error analysis and handling.You are advocating for new and innovative solutions within your teamYou are acting on agile values and are constantly willing to improve the development cycle
You have a degree in informatics or have related experienceYou have multiple years of professional experience as either a Fullstack, Backend or Frontend EngineerYou are proficient in at least one OOP programming language, preferably C# ; TypeScriptYou have experience in containerization with Docker and KubernetesYou have experience in SPA-Frameworks, preferably Angular You have experience in configuring and developing monitoring tools like Grafana, Prometheus and DaprYou have experience in using existing design systems, preferably Tailwind CSSYou know about common best practices of software development like design patterns, clean code, TDD and git and are applying them naturally in your everyday workYou have proficient knowledge about microservice architectures, preferably with .NET and KubernetesYou are proficient in English
