IAM Engineer
Company Description 
Our Global Partner is an IT system integrator company that has become a significant player in the Hungarian market. Today, it covers the areas of high-end log management, network and IT security solutions with related expert services nation-wide. Our Global Partner has a dedicated team of experts with up-to-date knowledge, who can implement reliable and flexible solutions. 
You will… 
consult with clients
help to design identity and security systems based on the client needs
implement identity and security systems
document your work
support the implemented systems
participate in trainings and presales activities
learn new knowledge domains.
have experience with SailPoint product
 We’d love to hear from you, if you... 
are a team player and a responsible personality
have grit to learn new skills and technologies
care about the details
have experience in Identity Governance / Identity Management area
have experience with integrating, implementing or supporting security- or identity management solutions in productive, enterprise environment
have solid knowledge of Java (or at least C / C++ / C#)
are ready to work in shifts on longer term and potentially provide services on-call
can communicate in English.
Even better if you… 
have experience with SailPoint product
