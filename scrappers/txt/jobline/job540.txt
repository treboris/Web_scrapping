Team lead - Logistics
Tasks:
Managing daily tasks of logistics coordinators
Plan, organize and coordinate the daily international (export&import) operations of the shipments
Keep contact with customers and partners simultaneously
Book, track and control shipments along the entire transportation process
Manage and maintain all related records and documents
Liaise with peers and subcontractors to provide highest level of customer service at all time
Our partner is a logistics company with a global presence, employing more than 8000
professionals worldwide. In Europe they have 9 branches including the recently established
Hungarian office.
The main business lines of the Hungarian team:
 Sea (ocean) freight forwarding
 Rail freight : TCR, TSR
 Air freight forwarding
 Warehousing & Distribution
Now we are looking for a senior colleague who can manage the team of logistics coordinators.
Skills:
Min 5 years of experience in the field of logistics
Min 1 year of people management experience
Interest in the fast-paced and versatile freight industry
Self-motivated, proactive personality with a “can do” attitude
Excellent computer skills
Native Hungarian and a fluent English language skills
Offers:
Stable company background
Competitive salary
Career opportunity in the logistics field
Challenging work with diverse activities in an international environment
Downtown office location with excellent public transport connection
