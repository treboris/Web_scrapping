The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Rollout coordinator - automation - REF410H
Our Automation Team is looking for enthusiastic team members, with passion to participate in global development projects. We are continuously creating easy-to-use, fast and reliable software automation solutions for internal customers over the company group.
As a rollout coordinator you will
learn and support different operation automation solutions
deliver automation services with the help of experienced colleagues
utilize your skills to ensure that customer requirements of efficiency and quality are met.
As a member of our team, on this job you will build your expertise in the field of
project administration
international and remote collaboration
teaching end users
agile and scrum methodology
business process modeling, prototype visualization, software design
scripting and programming
software testing and implementation, version control and repository management
database maintenance and development
server virtualization and automation
As a candidate you have to
have any official IT qualification or have completed any coding bootcamp
be fluent in English
As an outstanding candidate, beyond the basics, you would
hold university degree in IT with system operator or software development specialization
share publicly available reference projects
have IT related work experience
have work experience in programming, testing or project coordination
be familiar with any IT operation automation framework
Location
Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs, Szeged
