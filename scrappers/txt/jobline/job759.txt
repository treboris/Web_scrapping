Senior Java Developer (English and German-Speaking)
About our Partner:
Our partner is looking for a new member to their development team.
For this particular role they need an experienced Java Developer.
Project is focused on adjustment of existing product in area of payment transactions (SCRUM development methodology).
Tasks:
Development of applications and application's modules based on previous technical specification
Creation of specifications and technical design
Configuration of related systems
Customization and development of system parts
Implementation of system parts and testing (if necessary)
Writing documentation related to the development
Requirements:
University education focused on IT
Previous experiences in software development (Java 8 and higher)
Ability to write a reliable Unit test and knowledge of Integration testing
Knowledge of automation build tools (Maven and Gradle)
Experiences with Spring are a huge advantage
Basic knowledge of Linux
Knowledge of cloud technologies (Docker and Kubernetes) are a huge advantage
Analytical and logical thinking
Team player and proactive approach toward assigned work task
Munkavégzés helye
