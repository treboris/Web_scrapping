Senior Java Developer
Our client offers full remote work opportunity for those who prefer.
Office location: Budapest
What you will do:
Senior Software Developer participates in the architecture, design, documentation, development, coding, and debugging of embedded MFP (multi-function printer) software solutions in either new software products or enhancements to existing software, under the general direction of his or her direct supervisor (team lead).
Conducting research, where appropriate and needed, to solve specific design problems
Work independently, or in a group under the direction of a team lead, creating modules, design documentation, subsystems or applications in Java, JavaScript, and HTML/CSS, but occasionally also using other development tools
Work with various operating platforms and third-party SDKs in the production of embedded systems meeting high performance, security, and scalability expectations.
Positive attitude and ability to work in a fast-paced environment
Support production users
Document his/her work in English
Participate regular team meetings and/or conference calls
Effective communication and relationships with other developers and the QA team
What you need to have:
3-5 years of professional experience as a Java Developer
Fluent language skills in both Hungarian and English
Demonstrated OOD experience
Working knowledge of version control systems (Git, SVN)
Firsthand experience with a combination of various other technologies and platforms, such as, TCP/IP, SOAP, XML, AJAX, JavaScript, AWT, Spring, WEB servers (Tomcat, Jetty), WEB browsers (Edge, Firefox, Chrome) is also required
Experience in multi-threaded and networked software development on embedded systems
Experience with Java for embedded environments, JUnit, Ant, Maven and Gradle is a definite asset
Ability to work both independently and in a team environment
Strong troubleshooting and debugging skills
Experience with virtualisation technologies, VMWare is preferred
Solid understanding of Azure and other technologies like Docker, Kubernetes and Kafka is a plus
Are you interested?
