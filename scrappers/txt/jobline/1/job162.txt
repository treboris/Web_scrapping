Do you want to work at a stable company presenting in several different industries? Would you participate in the work of a company that has a people-oriented attitude and where the employees love their workplace? 
Due to the continuous expansion of our client, which occupies a top position in automation, we are looking for an Embedded Firmware Developer professional.
Embedded Firmware Developer
Tasks
Development of embedded firmware for devices and sensors with focus on a communication for Industrial Ethernet
Definition of the product/firmware specification in the requirement management system
Coding, integration, testing and documentation of program code
Conceptual design of unit, integration and system tests, execution and evaluation of related tests
Collaboration with interdisciplinary, distributed product development teams
Requirements
Studies of electrical engineering, communications technology, computer science focus embedded systems or comparable qualification
Professional experience of at least 2-5 years
Very good knowledge in C, C++ and Ethernet bus systems
Very good experience in the development of embedded systems
English knowledge at communication level
Good knowledge of the corresponding tool chains (requirement management / development systems)
Experience with continuous integration and testing systems
Good analytical capabilities
Team player

Stable background, a continuously expanding company with a 30+-year history
Excellent work-life balance (based on employee satisfaction measurement)
Flexible working time, home office option
Professional trainings, opportunity for development and advancement
