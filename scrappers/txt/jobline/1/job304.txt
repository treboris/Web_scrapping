DevOps Engineer
To strengthen our team, we are looking for DevOps Engineer.
What to expect:
Work in an awesome team .
Conception, realization and support of future-oriented cloud infrastructure solutions as well as support of our software products in cooperation with developers and administrators .
Operation & monitoring of our digital touchpoints .
Develop and implement solutions in the area of operations, release management, security, multi-cloud computing .
Active technical support in 2nd and 3rd level .
Work with cutting-edge technologies.
Continuous learning.
Provide training on new technologies and improvements for other DevOps Engineers.
What convinces us:
Experience in working Microsoft Azure Cloud
Experience in the following tools: Git, Terraform, Docker, Kubernetes, Helm, npm,
Experience scripting in Bash, Python .
Experience in the DevOps environment as well as with containerization and server administration .
Experience in the application of agile methods in an agile team .
Good communication and teamwork skills.
High IT Affinity and willingness to learn new tools and technologies.
Fluency in English
