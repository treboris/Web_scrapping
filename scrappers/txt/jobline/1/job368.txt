eSignature Customer Support Representative- German-speaking
Our precious well-known client is looking for an eSignature Customer Support Representative. If you speak German and English fluently and would like to advance your career, apply today! 
Responsibilities:
Provide second-level support for our eSignature customer base
Acting as a liaison between customers and resources across our client company
Creating reports to a Technical Support Manager
Provide technical support for the company’s eSignature component 
Handling incoming support channels to troubleshoot customer inquiries
Escalating issues as needed promptly with clear and effective communication
Identifying improvements to the product, identifying bugs, and determining high-impact opportunities 
Promoting the products and services to customers and prospective customers via consultative discussion and evaluation of their service and support needs 
Requirements:
Fluent English and German knowledge
A passion for technology and helping customers solve their technical problems 
Experience in troubleshooting in a web-based environment  
Effective communication skills 
Bachelor of Science degree in a Computer Science, Engineering, or related technical discipline
What our client offers:
Training and continuous learning and certification opportunities 
Multilingual environment, native colleagues 
Pleasant and inspiring working atmosphere with multicultural community and state-of-the-art technologies 
Flexibility in working from home 
Easy to access location and modern office building
Free sport allowance (near the office buildings), All You Can Move SportPass (at a discounted price)
Medical benefits and other perks 
Language courses 
Team events and Company events 
High value awards and recognitions, annual bonus for top performers, and annual salary review
Advantages:
Understanding of web-based applications, web service APIs, and authentication technologies such as SAML and oAuth
