Present in the market under the Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions brand name, IT-Services Hungary (ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group and is the largest ICT employer in Hungary. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 4500 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Senior ServiceNow Business Analyst (REF525G)
The team If you’ll join our team you can be part of an experienced international ServiceNow team that has extensive industry and domain expertise with ServiceNow platform on consultancy and implementation projects in various sizes. You’ll find business analysts, developers, architects and scrum masters in the team, all of them with the same goal, to create value for our internal and external customers with ServiceNow solutions.The roleThe role has opened due to ever growing international business demands. As a Business Analyst your main responsibility is to analyze business and IT requirements, processes; understand the as-is situation and translate the inputs into solutions based on ServiceNow platform that fulfill the requirements. Your tasks will range from understanding the client’s requirements through applying industry best practices and detailed knowledge of ServiceNow in designing an optimal solution (processes, data models, workflows, etc.) to supporting the actual implementation with story writing and testing in Agile frameworks.The Senior ServiceNow Business Analyst will work closely with the Solution/Technical Architect to ensure the solution is technically feasible and provides real measurable value for the customer.Your tasksUnderstanding client and business requirements, creating specifications and documentations (via meeting, document analysis, workshops, etc.)Providing analysis and modeling for companywide business processes, ability to deduce needs from business operation and realize IT development potentials Providing process design to standardize organization’s workflows and business operation while utilizing industry best practices and ServiceNow OOB recommendations and featuresHelp collaboration between customers and IT development team. Conducting professional consultation with architects, project managers, team leaders, technical leads, developers and testersPreparing proposal and concept creation in IT migration and helping with transition projectsCreating documentation, training materials and other supporting materials post-implementation to help transition and acceptance of the platform by customer’s end usersWorking with multiple complex systems on a wide range area, taking responsibility for interface/integration designBuilding a trusting relationship with major project stakeholders and leading them to adoption of the best solutionSupporting the management at strategic planning and at the identification of the organization's business needs, acting as consultantCarrying extensive technical/economical knowledge about the business area he/she is working on
Your profile3-5 years of experience in business analysis/consultancy3+ years of experience supporting the configuration and development of ServiceNowStrong oral and written communication skills (both business English and Hungarian)Complex practical ITIL knowledge through the whole Service Management LifecycleComprehensive knowledge of ServiceNow platform key capabilities and workflow solutionsAbility to work in an international environment with local and international teamsExperience implementing systems using the Agile/Scrum methodology and in System Development Life Cycle (SDLC) processes including client requirement analysis and system designExperience designing and implementing IT and Non-IT related process workflows, procedures based on technical standards and best practicesExperience in test plan creation and execution as well as provide guidance for key users, preparation of corporate knowledge articles and conducting trainingsAdministration of a ServiceNow instances including the delegation of groups, modification of CMS, workflows, business rules, UI actions, UI policies, ACLs, dictionary, catalog itemsPersonal qualities: strong analytical skills, creativity, innovation, passion, eagerness for self-development and a good sense of humor would come in handy working with us, IT geeks Nice to have skills and capabilitiesWorking proficiency in German languageGreat presentation skillsServiceNow certification (CSA, CIS-ITSM, CAD or others)Presales experience, working with RfX proposalsITIL certifications (Foundation, Managing Professional)Experience with MS Visio and other BPM tools
