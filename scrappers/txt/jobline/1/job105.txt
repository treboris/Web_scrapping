The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Solution Engineer (REF1552J)
Tasks
You will develop and operate our own web portal built from scratch
You will develop applications to automate internal processes and systems (legacy MPLS VPN technologies)
You can prove your creativity! We build a system, and we give a free hand in designing, too. 
You can work in a young, dynamic team, where individual responsibilities are not limited
You will work in an agile work method
At Deutsche Telekom, we are committed to providing our customers with the best quality and most reliable service. As part of our commitment, it is important to be able to track and trace our processes in a transparent and up-to-date way. Our team in Germany is working on a web-based tool to administering maintenances on our network. In addition, this software will help our colleagues to record these maintenance tasks in a simple and collision-free way. We are looking for a new colleague to join our team to further develop our solutions together. If you are creative person, who like to see through complex problems and have a passion for problem solving, we are looking for you!
