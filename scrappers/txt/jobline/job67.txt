The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
COGNOS expert (REF1469X)
We are searching an COGNOS-Expert with a deep experience in designing, implementing and maintaining complex Reporting Solutions. Ideally you are not only familiar with COGNOS itself, but also TM1 and one or more State of the Art RDBMS-Products (e.g. DB2, ORACLE, etc.).
If you have proven your skills in some interessting projects and now want to develop yourself in an international challenging project environment this is your new job!
Your main task would be:
Development of new Cognos Reports and Dynamic Cubes.
Implement changes into existing Cognos Reports and Cognos Cubes.
Support the current data center migration by installing and configuring new Cognos and TM1 installations.
Maintenance of the DEV/TEST environments.
DB2 admin tasks; supporting the development team on DB2 specific tasks and problems.
Skills:
> 2 years experience with IBM Cognos Products (Cognos Analytics 11.2, Cognos Dynamic Cubes, TM1)
Good knowledge of SQL and RDBMS (DB2 preferred)
Solid Windows skills incl. scripting and task automation.
Good Data Warehousing knowledge
Good german or english communication skills
Customer- and quality-oriented mindset
Willingness to work with new technologies
Strong teamplayer
Additionaly beneficial Skills
Linux skills
Experience with Enterprise Architect
Experienced
* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
