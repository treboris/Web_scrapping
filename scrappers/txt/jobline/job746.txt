Our client is a global software engineering and IT outsourcing services supplier, looking for a Junior Golang Developer. You’ll be part of a team of professionals who are ready to find the best tailor-made IT solutions for their multinational clients in various industries and solve complex problems. You’ll create high-quality software products that move digital marketing business forward. Our client believes in agile methods, quick hypothesis testing, and shining eyes. They need you, a Golang developer, to help them make all of their plans come to fruition!
Junior Golang Developer

At least 1-year work experience in the IT sphere
Strong knowledge of Golang and understanding of the concurrency model of the language;

Good knowledge of SQL, experience with one of the relational databases;
skills in working with Git;
Strong technical English (conversational, written)
Will be a plus:
Experience with one of the frameworks Vue, React, Angular;
Experience with NoSQL;
Experience using Docker.
