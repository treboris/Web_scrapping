Company Description
The largest ICT employer in Hungary. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Job Description
The goal of the project is quality assurance of innovative technologies, the newly developed hardware and software being introduced by Deutsche Telekom (DT). Selected candidate will act as a developer in test for Functional and Integration Testing of DT network infrastructure and will support HW&SW testing processes on different levels in different network segments.
Responsibilities:
• In cooperation with internal customers (DT technical tribes) develop specific requirements for automated solutions based on existing components and frameworks
• Communicate with international distributed team
• Develop new program components, e.g. software agents, modules, test scripts and so on
• Develop in-house software tools
• Define software specifications for external companies and accompany software development until final acceptance
• Maintain existing automatization frameworks - bugfixing, release management etc.
• Contribute to innovative projects, e.g. provide and promote ideas, propose approaches, develop solutions etc.
Qualifications
• Good language skills - spoken and written English
• Experience in software architecture evaluating and development
• Deep expertise and practice in Java
• Deep knowledge in RDBMS (mySQL, Oracle, etc.)
• Expertise in usage of agile tools in devops environment (Jira, GITLAB, Jenkins …)
• Expertise in application and development of CI/CD frameworks
• Good communication skills
• Good presentation skills
• Self-motivated
• High level of personal responsibility
• Willing to learn
• Willing to be curious and helpful
• Customer oriented
Nice to have:
• University level or equivalent practice in general knowledge about networks and architecture, IP know-how
• Knowledge and experience in Python, JavaScript, PHP, Ruby etc.
• Expertise in Gherkin, Cucumber
• Expertise in Robot Framework
• German knowledge is considered as a benefit Additional Information
