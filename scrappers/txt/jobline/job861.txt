DevOps Engineer
Your new company
Your new role
We are looking for a DevOps Engineer with experience in Docker Swarm and K8s, building and automating CI/CD pipelines, managing and monitoring Infrastructure, planning, forecasting, and tracking operational costs and monitoring and supporting mission-critical solutions. 
Develop and maintain automated provisioning for development, testing, and production environments (Ansible)
Develop, test and maintain CI/CD pipelines to help deliver software of higher quality at greater speed
Build the necessary monitors, alerts, and self-healing capabilities for our solutions
Play a key role in advising on Architecture and available services along with the trade-offs between them
Participate in process improvement and setting engineering best practices with the team
Solve problems in ways that drive towards simplicity of architecture and operations
Produce and maintain documentation on installations, procedures and requirements for systems 
What you'll need to succeed
Strong experience with linux system administration
Strong experience with Bash or Python
Experience with Ansible, Puppet, or similar tools for configuration management
Working familiarity with Docker, virtualization, swarm and K8S
IT attitude, willingness to learn new systems
Are open to continuously extend your professional skills
Stress resistant, team player
Additional requirements:
Several years‘ relevant experience in IT application operation
Bachelor degree is an advantage
Strong English skills in written and spoken
Your personal strengths:
Careful work style and durability
High quality and responsibility in own work
What you'll get in return
Economically stable and growing enterprise in a high-tech environment
Excellent working atmosphere
Competitive remuneration package 
Non-conventional domain: lottery and sports games
Downtown office, in the heart of Budapest, at the foot of Buda castle Open company culture
