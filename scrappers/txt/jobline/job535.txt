Senior QA Tester
About our Partner:
Our partner is looking for a Senior QA Tester who will need to overlook all aspects of manual and automated testing in the BI team.
Tasks:
Develop and update test strategies
Validate that the business requirements are well described in dashboard requests
Understand the business needs and requirements for client dashboards
Draft, estimate and execute various types of test cases for the dashboards and databases, based on the business requirements
Investigate into the potential defects incl. validating the KPI setup, and exchange with BI Analysts and Project Teams
Create bug/gap reports, and follow up on those upon the closure of them
Report the results of the tests to the respective BI Analyst
Suggest and document general improvements for client dashboards
Plan, develop, maintain and enhance test automation for BI dashboards
Work together with other teams’ QA members for end-to-end testing
Requirements:
Bachelor’s degree in computer science or relevant field
ISTQB CTFL certification
Deep understanding of QA processes
Advanced SQL and/or Python knowledge
Familiarity with BI/data warehousing/ETL processes
Programming experience for test automation
High-level analytical skill, detail-oriented
Munkavégzés helye
Budapest, Pest
