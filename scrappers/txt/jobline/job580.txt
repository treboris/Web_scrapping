Azure Cloud Engineer
In your daily work, you will:
Leverage cutting edge technologies like Kubernetes, Terraform, Docker, Spring Boot to design and build new Identity and Access Management products for the Cloud;
Take end-to-end responsibility for your engineered results and automate everything to scale our Cloud service;
Work on all layers (infrastructure as code, automation monitoring) using open source components and open standards;
Integrate new components and technologies into existing software systems and architectures
Do on the job trainings;
Mentor junior colleagues (do code reviews, give support in technical design, etc.);
Use Scrum, TDD and modern software development methodologies
Our partner company is looking for their new Azure Cloud Engineer member to help fuel their rapid growth and expansion of their Cloud engineering team. If you are interested in cybersecurity and protection of customer identities, we are looking forward to your application.
For this challenging position, you must have:
Strong Cloud Provider and/or Kubernetes/Docker expertise;
You are a Clean Code evangelist and have experience in developing Java applications with Spring Boot;
Proven track record using Terraform, Ansible or similar to automate infrastructure in production settings;
Professional experience with cloud native development and 12-factor principles are a plus;
A university degree (MSc/BSc) in Computer Science or a related field - or similar
