The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Cloud Devops Engineer (REF104L)
Our group VNI is responsible for the integration, validation and production of test results ("System Under Test") in accordance with the requirements of the Deutsche Telekom AG Platform. Daily tasks carried out in several virtual squads with German and International colleagues.
As an engineer you are part of a team which is responsible for implementing future oriented Network solutions manage and run  the DT Labor Enviroment.
Tasks:
Create automation in Agile way, solutions that improve Testlab operations and efficiency.
Design, configure, implement and support an automated workflow for server, cloud and virtualization
Review and evaluate cloud (and virtual) server performance, capacity, networking and software
Comply with all audit, compliance and regulatory requirements and ensure policy, controls and procedures are maintained throughout virtualization and cloud environments
Perform server, cloud computing and virtualization environment build-outs, software installation, maintenance and support, documentation of operating procedures and processes
Requirements
In depth system administration experience and knowledge, including operating systems, hardware, storage and networking
Corporate hands-on experience with scripting and coding, such as Python, PowerShell, Terraform, shell script
Hands-on experience with OpenStack Cloud and Containers like Kubernetes and Docker
Knowledge and experience with canonical deployement tools, such as MaaS & Juju (JaaS)
In depth knowledge and extensive corporate hands-on experience in Vmware vSphere administration , maintenance, design, and architecture
In depth knowledge and experience with administration and configuration of services like DNS,NTP,Proxy,WSUS and KMS
Knowledge and expereince with automation, configuration management tools such as Puppet, Chef, Ansible, Gitlab, CI/CD pipelines
Profound knowledge about UNIX/ Linux distribution like Red Hat and Ubuntu, Win10, Win11 and Server 2019 is highly desired
Experience in Agile methodology
Fluent English language knowledge
German language knowledge is an advantage
Experience in Cisco, Juniper network are advantageous
Experienced
* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
