The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Automation Developer – Migration area
Our Automation Team is looking for enthusiastic team members, with passion to participate in global development projects. We are continuously creating easy-to-use, fast and reliable software automation solutions for customers over the world. Working within the Content Migration Services (CMS) Delivery Team to deliver data migration for a wide range of customers using our proprietary data migration software. The role involves working in a small, agile delivery team specialising in current Content Management System (CMS) technologies to deliver scalable and repeatable data migration solutions for our customers
Tasks:
Work in a fast moving and dynamic team with colleagues and customers in all regions
Engage in all phases of the delivery lifecycle with high-profile customers moving very large volumes of data
Assist in identifying customer requirements in analysis and design workshops and contribute to migration design
Use T-Systems proprietary software and methodology to deliver complex data migration projects
Execute full live migrations, working collaboratively, resolving exceptions, making refinements where necessary
Contribute to pre-sales activity including demos and presentations
Requirements:
Experience working with Web based Content Management systems
Applications development & test experience in line with Software Engineering methodologies within the industry
Experience or familiarity of working with agile processes
Knowledge of Python or similar programming/scripting languages and ideally some experience with Visual Studio and .NET
HTML, XML, JSON, knowledge of XPath and Regular Expressions
Understanding of Web Servers and Web Protocols
Understanding of Web Service API protocols such as REST and SOAP
Experience of working with relational or document databases
Apply, if You are:
Able to work flexibly and effectively under pressure and within tight deadlines and thrive on challenging problems
Committed to knowledge sharing and using knowledge management tools to share information in a pro-active and consistent manner
Someone with an understanding of the technical challenges of data migration projects
Able to manage your own priorities and seek support from the wider team when required
Someone experienced in working in technical teams to achieve results in line with customer’s requirements and timelines
Someone with a pragmatic, problem-solving mindset who can think critically and investigate issues then independently come up with a plan for resolution
Comfortable with coaching and being coached
A good communicator – both orally and in written forms within our own team and with customers
Experienced
* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
