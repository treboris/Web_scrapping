Citrix Server Engineer
About Our Partner:
They are a global information technology company that builds high-performance, security-centric solutions that can help change the world. Now, they are looking for a new colleague to join their Citrix team who support their international client.
Tasks:
Installing/Maintaining/Supporting XenApp and XenDesktop 7.15 & higher versions of infrastructure
Configuration and deployment of Store Front Servers
Installing, configuring and managing Citrix License Servers (Windows/VPX)
Image management: Managing Server and Desktop OS images in Citrix Provisioning services
Application/License life cycle management
Monitoring Citrix infrastructure through Citrix Edge Sight/Director
Day-to-day operational management and support of the company's enterprise-class, mission-critical Citrix infrastructure, including XenApp, XenDesktop, PVS and NetScaler environments
Installing, configuring and upgrading Netscaler VPX/MPX
Configuring Citrix policies Printing/Drive Mapping 
Management and support of Windows /2012/2016/2019 Server in an enterprise network environment, demonstrating extensive knowledge of Microsoft-based system principles and practices.
Participation in on-call rotation (flexible support hours between 6 AM and 6 PM)
Requirements:
Strong analytical and troubleshooting skills on Citrix XenApp/XenDesktop and Netscaler
Deep understanding of vSphere ESX and experience with deploying and managing a highly-virtualized infrastructure
Good exposure to ITIL processes like incident, change, problem and change management
Experience in the FSLogix Profile management system
Minimum 5+ years of experience in Windows and Citrix
Education: B.E / Any Graduate / Diploma
Good communication Skills with fluent English
Willingness to work in shifts (24 x 7 x 365 model)
Advantages:
Good Hands-on experience on Netscaler ADC and Access gateway 10.X & higher versions
Experience in configuring AAA, and SSL off-loading in Netscalers
Proficiency in PowerShell command lets, Putty, Wireshark, Netscaler Insight Center and scripting
Configuration of Netscaler HA/GSLB/Load Balancing L4-L7 traffic
Configure Netscaler ADC as a web proxy server
Configuring Rewrite, Respond, LDAP, Two-factor authentication
Install and maintain Access Gateway VPN/ICA Proxy, session profile
Certifications on Citrix Products
