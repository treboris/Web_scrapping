The largest ICT employer in Hungary, Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions (formerly IT-Services Hungary, ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey in 2021 and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team.
Architect (REF1102F)
Job Description :
The DevOps expert should further develop our container services based on Kubernetes and Rancher in project mode. Furthermore, activities in the Azure Cloud environment may arise in the context of automation for applications of a customer. Work will be done in an agile team for a major customer in the logistics environment.
Tasks :
Conversion of Kubernetes clusters from Docker-CE to ContainerD.
Integration of Azure AKS into the service
Development of a roadmap for the migration of existing clusters into the revised service
Presentation of the results to the customer and internally
Internal training for the team
Support cloud migrations of applications to the AZURE Cloud, if applicable.
Support automation of service deployments in the cloud context, if applicable.
Knowhow on the topics :
Kubernetes
Rancher
Containers
Microsoft AZURE Cloud
Terraform
Ansible
Gitlab
Github
AZURE Dev/OpS
Linux knowledge preferably Red Hat
Experience with building and deploying IaaS components
Experience with the deployment of PaaS components
Language skills: German and English
Independent work in an agile environment
Creation/presentation of own ideas for the further development of the services
Communicative and social skills
Experienced
* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
