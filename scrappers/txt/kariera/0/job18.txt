Your Role and Responsibilities:
The Application Architect will coordinate digital transformation solutions for our clients ensuring that the solution defined is carried out and executed to completion.
What would be your responsibilities?
Work directly with clients to understand their current state and environment and develop assessment material for decision-making purposes using appropriate frameworks and models.
Outline the implementation approach highlighting business benefit, value creation, key risks and mitigation strategies.
Propose high value solution design and architecture to the client using in-depth understanding of typical structures of business processes and architectures used by the respective industry.
Coordinate and manage team efforts for the delivery of the project. Relay project status, issues and challenges to the client and IBM representatives.
Establish deep relationships with existing IBM customers, understand the customers’ unique business challenge(s) and opportunities, create a vision and / or solution as well as presenting proposals, contracts and Statement of Work(s) with a unique and compelling business proposition.
Drive adoption of agile and iterative project approaches.
Required Professional and Technical Expertise:
Prior development and architecture experience in JEE, SOA or Microservice Java based systems is a must.
Effective leadership and project management skills.
Preferred Professional and Technical Expertise:
Cloud Native Application design experience with Spring Boot, MicroProfile or Quarkus is muchappreciated.
Hands-on experience with Docker and Kubernetes is appreciated.
Hands-on experience with any of the major public cloud providers (AWS, Google Cloud and/or Azure) is considered an advantage.
DDD (Domain Driven Design) -based architecture experience.
Fluent German (written and spoken).
