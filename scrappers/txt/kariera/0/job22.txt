Information Technologies - Software; IT Consultant, IT/Technical Support Specialist, ProgrammerJob description, information about job
As a consultant, you will use your consulting skills, business, and technical knowledge to effectively integrate comprehensive technology into the business environment of our clients and to achieve the best possible business results.
Don't know Adobe? Never mind, we'll introduce it to you!
Adobe has historically specialized in software for the creation and publication of a wide range of content, including graphics, photography, illustration, animation, multimedia/video, motion pictures, and print. Adobe offered a bundled solution of its products named Adobe Creative Suite, which evolved into a subscription software as a service (SaaS) offering named Adobe Creative Cloud. The company also expanded into digital marketing software and in 2021 was considered one of the top global leaders in Customer Experience Management (CXM). 
Our Adobe team at IBM is responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining complex application components and integrating software packages using a variety of tools. That is why we are looking for a new colleague who is ready to deal with various challenges and thus meet the requirements of our customers.
You can work from one of our offices - our center is located in Bratislava, Banská Bystrica, and Košice, so you can choose where it suits you best.
What would be your responsibilities?
•	Collection and analysis of business requirements
•	Design, development and implementation of business solutions in cooperation with developers
•	Coordination and documentation of project work
•	Regular communication with clients and building of relationships 
•	Active communication with customers, business analysts and other service teams to perform common operational tasks and troubleshoot applications
What do you need for your success?
•	Fluent English
•	Advanced knowledge of HTML / CSS
•	Communication and presentation skills (written and oral)
•	Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
•	IT related education
•	Experience in a similar job position is a plus (IT consultant)
•	Ability to work independently as well as a team player
•	Experience in Adobe AEM
