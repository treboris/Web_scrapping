Information Technologies - Software; IT ConsultantJob description, information about job
Your Role and Responsibilities :
Analyze client requirements and recommend solutions utilizing the ServiceNow platform
Provide expert guidance and best practices for the design, configuration, and implementation of ServiceNow modules, including but not limited to Incident Management, Problem Management, Change Management, and Configuration Management
Lead the development and implementation of custom solutions, workflows, and integrations with other systems
Facilitate the development of test plans and conduct testing of ServiceNow solutions to ensure quality and compliance with requirements
Manage project deliverables, timelines, and budgets to ensure successful project completion
Additionally you may be involved in:
Providing training and support to clients for the successful adoption and use of ServiceNow solutions
Develop and maintain strong client relationships, promoting customer satisfaction and further business opportunities
Mentor and lead junior consultants and team members to ensure their professional development and growth.
