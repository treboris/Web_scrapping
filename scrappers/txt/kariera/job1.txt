Core Responsibilities and Tasks:
Provide support to the client's ServiceNow platform.
Assist with ticket and enhancement requests for the ITSM module with CMDB
Collaborate with senior ServiceNow consultants on platform administration tasks
Participate in requirement gathering and analysis
Customize and configure ServiceNow according to client's needs
Test and debug customizations and configurations
Document processes, procedures and technical specifications
Support development and implementation of custom solutions, workflows, and integrations with other systems
Additionally you may be involved in:
Analyze client requirements and recommend solutions utilizing the ServiceNow platform
Lead the development and implementation of custom solutions, workflows, and integrations with other systems
Manage project deliverables, timelines, and budgets to ensure successful project completion
