Cégleírás / Organisation / DepartmentOur Client is one of the leading investment banks in the world, and for their growing professional team we are looking for an ECS (External Connectivity Security) Analyst.Elvárások / Requirements
1-3 years of IT Support or relevant experience;
Data transferring knowledge;
Networking knowledge is a plus, but not required;
Fluency in English;
 Amit kínálunk / Offer
Process Firewall requests and perform risk assessments to ensure that the connectivity is compliant with the company's Information Security Standards and IS Policies;
Liaise with multiple technology teams to facilitate cross-functional solutions as it relates to 3rd party connectivity aligning with the company's Information Security Standards;
Provide Information Security oversight and guidance to the company's businesses requiring 3rd party connectivity as it relates to the Information Security Standards and IS Policies;
Registering the rules and contacting businesses
 Kapcsolattartó / Information
Highly competitive compensation package;
Home office opportunity;
Long-term career path across geographies and business lines;
Flexible work arrangement;
Friendly work atmosphere;
