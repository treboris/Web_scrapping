At least 5 years of relevant working experience
Selenium, Git, Java knowledge and experience
Active English and Hungarian language skills (spoken and written)
Excellent knowledge and in the field of software testing, as well as a comprehensive understanding of the processes of software development
Ability to take the "big picture" mindset
Root-cause analysis and ability to help others find solutions
It's an advantage if you also have:
Knowledge of: javascript, SQL, azure, linux, azure
Experience in development of UI testing system
