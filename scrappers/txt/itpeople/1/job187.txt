Diákmunka - Project Management Intern állás Budapest XIII. kerület
Tisztelt álláskereső!Ez az álláshirdetés lejárt.Azonban oldalunk több ezer egyéb álláshirdetéssel rendelkezik! Találja meg az önnek megfelelő munkát erre a linkre kattintva!
											Work and grow your career at Schneider Electric!  Providing digital energy and automation solutions for efficiency and sustainability. We combine the world's leading energy technologies, real-time automation, software, and services into integrated solutions available in homes, buildings, data centers, infrastructures, and industries.  We are committed to our core values: #innovation #equality #sustainability. Did you know, for example, that rainwater is recycled in our office? For more information about us, click on https://www.se.com/ww/en/purpose/  Join us, be part of creating a sustainable future with Schneider Electric's energy and digital solutions! This internship is an excellent opportunity to learn and develop your career: if you are ready to face new challenges, feel free to apply, we will teach you the rest!  As a Project Management Intern, you will join the Logistics & Planning team where we transform and tailor our Supply Chain to meet our customer needs.  You will help animate our communications for Logistics and Planning function, while supporting on portfolio analytics and trainings. Your tasks will include: •Upkeep and improve Logistics and Planning Communication Plan (Sharepoint and Yammer)•Support and upkeep our document management system on Sharepoint•Quality validations of our Logistics and Planning Portfolio, adaption and adherence by our PM’s•Work with domain experts to identify new opportunities and needs•Support Global and out Regional PMO on certain activities •Interaction with business domain experts to understand their needs and requirements and identify data-driven opportunities•Develop training documentation and deliver training to users on systems developed.Experience and skills we are looking for:•Fluent English language knowledge, both written and spoken•Ongoing university studies in Logistics, Economics, or any equivalent field•You have at least 1 more active semesters left of your studies•Relevant work experience is an advantage•Working knowledge of Microsoft Office (especially Excel) and Tableau is a plus•You have good problem-solving and analytical skills•You are a fast-learner and have an open-minded attitude, looking for opportunities to improve your leadership skills •You are proactive and have excellent communicational skills•Gaining professional experience in project management sounds appealing to youWe are looking for you if you are not afraid of taking your career to the next level!•Flexible schedule, 30 hours/week •You can start as soon as possible: we are looking forward for you to join our team! •Working from home is possible, but please note that sometimes office presence can also be required (especially for training and specific projects)•Gross salary: 1800 Ft/hour •We provide a laptop for your work
