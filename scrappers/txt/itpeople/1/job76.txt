											Work and grow your career at Schneider Electric!Providing digital energy and automation solutions for efficiency and sustainability. We combine the world's leading energy technologies, real-time automation, software and services into integrated solutions available in homes, buildings, data centers, infrastructures and industries. We are committed to our core values #innovation #equality #sustainability. Did you know, for example, that rainwater is used to flush toilets in our office? You will be analyzing energy supply contract prices and terms and negotiating with energy suppliers to ensure optimal contracts are secured on behalf of Schneider Electric’s clients. •Manage client and supplier relationships through excellent formal and informal communication•Work closely with Sourcing Analysts and other internal teams to deliver an exceptional level of service to our clients•Manage pre-tender activities through collecting interval data, invoices, contracts, etc.•Manage the tendering, analysis and subsequent contract award and placement processes relating to complex energy supply contracts•Negotiate and utilise quantitative and qualitative techniques to deliver optimal energy supply contract terms and prices for our clients through regular interaction with the energy suppliers on the related marketsThis internship is an excellent opportunity to learn and develop your career: if you speak English and Slovakian, feel free to apply, we will teach you the rest! •Strong English skills•You speak one of these languages: Sebian, Croatian or Polish, Czech, Slovakian, Romanian.•Ongoing university studies.•Ideal internship would be at least 12 months with possibility of retention with a local contract after the internship.•Good knowledge of MS Excel is a must •Strong analytical and logical skills•A great communicator, capable of engaging people of varying levels of seniority and experience•Flexible schedule, minimum 30 hours /week  (+ Home Office opportunity)•You can start as soon as possible: we are looking forward for you to join our team!•Gross salary: 1800 HUF/hour
