Cyber Security Trainee állás Budapest
Gyakornok állások 
Elvárások ...are fluent in English (you will work with colleagues from other countries)  ...bring interest in cyber security and cloud environments …like to approach problems in a logical way …can ask for help if you feel stuck ...are a university student (Computer Science, Applied Informatics, or comparable field) ...have around 20 hours/week to work Feladatok Pick your favorite azure cloud security recommendation and work together with our colleagues on a concept how it can be resolved in an enterprise production environment.  Work together with our colleagues to enhance the capabilities of our automatic vulnerability management. Work on master data management and workflows in one of the biggest ITSM tools. Develop workflows together with our colleagues how to automatically investigate and resolve security incidents. Bring in or develop your understanding of cyber threats and use our cloud SIEM tool Microsoft Sentinel to perform actions without human interaction.  Előnyök Flexible working hours and conditions (you can schedule your working hours according to university) Opportunity to gain hands-on experience in several fields within different functions of a multinational company Long term career perspective, coaching Competitive student salary Company phone and notebook Training opportunities and continuous professional development (language trainings and professional trainings as well) Bilingual environment and international projects Opportunity to work in a modern office with coffee and drinks Home office opportunity Regularly organized team building events
