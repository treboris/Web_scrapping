Oracle EBS Reporting Applications, Senior Analyst
Howmet Aerospace, headquartered in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, is a leading global provider of advanced engineered solutions for the aerospace and transportation industries. The Company’s primary businesses focus on jet engine components, aerospace fastening systems and titanium structural parts necessary for mission-critical performance and efficiency in aerospace and defense applications, as well as forged wheels for commercial transportation. Howmet Aerospace is transforming the next phase of more fuel-efficient, quieter aerospace engines and sustainable ground transportation. For more information, visit www.howmet.com. Follow @howmet: Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn and YouTube.
Howmet Aerospace Inc. (“Howmet”) is currently in search of an Oracle EBS Reporting Applications, Senior Analyst to join the Global Information Systems (GIS) organization at the Howmet Corporate Center in Pittsburgh, PA or Székesfehérvár, Hungary.
In this role you will be the owner and application administrator of the Oracle EBS Reporting Applications: Orbit Analytics – a flexible business intelligence, reporting and analytics solution, with world-class dashboards and selfservice capability – and the Oracle Enterprise Command Center Framework (ECC) - enabling access a pre-selected subset of Oracle E-Business Suite information with enhanced navigation, information discovery along with visualization and exploration capabilities.
Major activities/Key challenges:
Assist in the formulation and implementation of strategies and policies around the maintenance, support, administration, and strategic usage of the reporting solution.Establish and maintain an effective working relationship across the organization, with analysists, leadership, customers, and 3rd party vendors.Help the business partners and customers with their reporting and analytics needs and translate them into functional requirements.Coordinate the design and development of data models that can be used across the business and lead the implementation of analytical solutions that advances the organization’s data strategy and promotes data integrity and quality.Participate in and support system upgrades, deployments, configuration, and technology expansions of the reporting software.Active role in maintaining and improving development practices, standards, procedures, and controls (e.g., documentation, development, and testing).Document release notes and develop training materials for business users.
Basic Qualifications:
Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution or 3 years of additional experience in Information Technology accepted in lieu of a degree.Minimum of 2 (two) years of experience in Information Technology.Employees must be legally authorized to work in the United States. Verification of employment eligibility will be required at the time of hire. Visa sponsorship is not available for this position.
Preferred Qualifications:
Prior experience in reporting and any BI tools.Experience with Orbit Analytics is an added advantage.Experience with Oracle R12 (12.2), Oracle 19c databases, PL/SQL, SQL.Exposure or working knowledge of Oracle ERP modules - Finance, Project Accounting, Procurement, Order-to-Cash, and Req-to-Pay is a plus.Strong verbal and written communications skills and ability to interact with customers, vendors, other IT professionals, and management.Excellent planning and organizational skills.
We will appreciate your services by providing:
A competitive remuneration package.Hybrid work schedule, promoting a collaborative work environment and flexibility.Friendly environment with a lot of company sponsored social activity.Open minded and welcoming environment, where your ideas are taken seriously.Challenging duties in truly International environment.
