Junior IT Security Manager
My multinational credit insurance client is looking for a new expert in its Global IT Team to join their Budapest based office as a Junior IT Security Manager.This is a regional role working with IT and business teams across 5 European markets, working in a close-knit team of 3-4 security engineers. Your overall role is about:
Task:
Defining and updating IT security requirements (policies, standards, baselines),Measuring, monitoring, and reporting on IT compliance with security requirements,Identification and assessment of IT risks as well as monitoring of IT risks treatment,Development of Cyber Security solutions and services,Supporting maintenance and development of cyber security controls and solutions for cloud solutions and platforms,Organizing security testing,Collaborate and represent Security Team in front of main local stakeholders,Work collaboratively with the IT teams in all countries.
Expectation:
~3 years of relevant work experience in the field of Information Technology and Security,Bsc/Msc degree (ideally Information Technology),Excellent Hungarian and English communication skills,Understanding of security technologies like SIEM, WAF, IDS/IPS, NDR, Endpoint Security, NAC and services like SOC, Vulnerability Scanning, Penetration testing,Problem solving approach and patience,Great work organizational skills,Experience with managing security projects.
Other:
Hands on experience with cloud computing,Industry recognised certifications: CISSP, CISM, CISA, CompTIA, Security+, GSEC; CCSK, CCSP/CSP,Experience with security managed services,Experience from financial field.
What we offer:
Base salary + performance based annual bonus + cafeteria + health insurance,Modern office located in South-Pest with an exceptional view to the Danube,Hybrid work: home office 3 times a week,Truly exciting Security projects in our unique space in the ever-evolving financial industry,You will be surrounded by a professional team, as part of a multinational, global team, working with modern technologies.
Sector(s):
IT Development
IT Consultant, Analyst, Auditor
IT, Development Manager
IT Operation, Telecommunication
IT Security
