Network Engineer
Here in Budapest
Here at the Nissan Regional Business Services (NRBS), we are a diverse and international team who actively contributing towards the development, growth and efficiency of the business across our region.
Mission and objectives:
Work in the position of a multi-technology Network Specialist, collaborating with remote members of the team, and supporting various site IT & IS representatives.  Required to administer diverse and inherently complex systems with high impact of failure.  Travel in Europe may be necessary.  Role encompasses:
Network Run and Level 3 support
Network Project Consultation, Design & Build
Tasks and responsibilities:
Run/Level 3 supportIncident Management & AnalysisCall Triage & PrioritisationProximity / Onsite Support with Short Notice MobilityRemote TroubleshootingCross-Team Collaboration: Telecoms, Servers, WAN, IT SecurityInfrastructure MonitoringSecurity Vulnerability Monitoring, SOC Reporting & Patch DeploymentCMDB, Life-cycle monitoring & Ownership Change/Projects/ActivitiesSingle entry point for NE Network Queries, e.g. Performance improvements, Hardware selectionDevelop deep understanding of NE network topology, infrastructure, processes, contacts & regional history to enhance quality of serviceDesign and Build Capability for New / Upgraded Office LAN & WiFi deploymentsSecure Vendor Connectivity RequestsFirewall Change Requests – Coordination of Business & 3rd Party RequirementsSite-to-Site VPN Change RequestsInfrastructure Access Management / TACACS+Vendor Liaison: Maintenance, WAN, Hybrid Cloud
Background & Experience:
At least 5 years’ experience directly providing and coordinating network support and consultancy for large multi-national, multi-cultural enterprises, preferably in the manufacturing sector.Proven track record facing and successfully managing / resolving large-scale or high impact network related incidents. Minimum Education & Qualifications:Valid certification mandatory for at least one, preferably two, of the following:CCNP Routing & SwitchingCCNP SecurityCCNP WirelessCCDP Valid certification desirable for any of the following:CCDE / CCIE Routing & Switching / CCIE Security / CCIE Wireless Specific Technical Skills / Software knowledge:Cisco ACSCisco ISE, 802.1xCisco Catalyst & NexusCisco WiFi & WLC with FlexConnectCisco PrimeJuniper LAN/Wi-Fi solutionHPE LAN/Wi-Fi solutionSolarWinds NCM/NPMNetFlowWintel Server AdministrationCisco AnyConnect VPNCisco ASA FirewallPalo Alto NGFW & PanoramaFortinet FortiGate/FortiManager/FortiAnalyzer NGFWF5 BIG-IP LTMEnterprise Dynamic Routing with EIGRP and/or OSPFAmazon AWSGoogle Cloud PlatformBasic awareness of Cisco ACI / Cisco TrustSec & SD-WAN solutions.
What we offer?
Competitive Benefits structure, including:Competitive salaryYearly bonusFlexible work scheduleHybrid working (home office & office presence)CafeteriaMedicover insurance packageTeam buildings, company events, recreational possibilities (beach volley, AYCM)Trainings & reimbursement of vocational training costs for existing degreesTravel & commuting allowance
What else ​can we offer:
Multinational experience at one of the leading automotive companies in the worldInternational & domestic career opportunitiesNewly refurbished office at Infopark with excellent accessibilityWorking in a team with highly qualified & open-armed colleaguesGet a good understanding of not only account but controlling, budgeting, forecasting, reporting
