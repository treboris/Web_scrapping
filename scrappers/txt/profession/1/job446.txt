AI Project Manager (f/m/div)*
Infineon’s work has a huge impact on people’s daily lives and on solutions for the future. Are you prepared to take the next step in your career and be part of our state-ofthe-art team in Cegléd? Then, join us as an AI Project Manager! As an AI Project Manager, you will have the chance, to be a part of a newly founded team in the field of data analytics and represent Cegléd in the AI community!
In your new role, you will:
Plan, execute, monitor, control and close pilot & rollout AI projects according to Infineon standardsDrive approval for all business gates releases (including documentation, presentation, justification, etc.)Manage and develop innovative data analysis and AI projects as part of the global community Artificial Intelligence Back End program (AI BE)Be responsible for the project schedule, resource planning, budget and achievement of content goalsImplement and report retrospectives and lessons learned from AI projects to management, the AI BEprogram, and internal customersPropose innovative ideas for the improvement of processes through idea workshops and direct contact with internal customers.
Profile
You have a positive posture toward change, you are data-driven, and have strong communication skills. Your analytical thinking helps you to work on technical and production-related issues and to advance agile projects. You use your communication skills to find your way in an international network and to advance complex data science topics with a broad group of colleagues. You are characterized by an independent and structured way of working. You are best equipped for this task if you have: A university degree (preferably Master/PhD) in the field of Data Science, Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics, Engineering or similar.Experience in managing teams and projects in the semiconductor industry or power electronics, preferably in the field of data analytics or digital transformationStrong project management background, preferably experience in a Strong project management background, preferably experience in a manufacturing environment.Know-how in handling agile projects and solving complex problems in the production environmentA comprehensive understanding of data, analytical challenges and solutions, as well as database systems and machine learning methods (e.g. Tensorflow, Keras, Pytorch, Elasticsearch, ...)Excellent communication skills and soft skills toward teams and internal customersFluency in English. Hungarian is a plus. Please send us your CV in English.
Benefits
Cegléd: Wide range of training offers & planning of career development; International assignments; Different career paths: Project Management, Technical Ladder, Management & Individual Contributor; Holiday child care; Onsite social counselling and works doctor; On-site canteen; Corporate pension benefits; Performance bonus; Reduced price for public transport
Why Us
Part of your life. Part of tomorrow. Infineon is a world leader in semiconductor solutions that make life easier, safer, and greener. Our solutions for efficient energy management, smart mobility, and secure, seamless communications link the real and the digital world.The backend segments enable the development of comprehensive innovation across the entire value chain: starting with chip manufacturing, to wafer testing, preassembly, package development and development of new materials. The backend cluster combines the competencies for planning, productivity, innovation and quality, assembly and testing.* The term gender in the sense of the General Equal Treatment Act (GETA) or other national legislation refers to the biological assignment to a gender group.
