YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES
You are the first point of contact for customers seeking technical assistance by using the self service portal, email or, if necessary, by phoneYour task is to qualify and document the problems raised by customers as well as Identify the types, categories, severity, and urgency of the ticketYou diagnose problems by asking follow-up questions and translating this into a technical escalation to second line supportYou are responsible for handling support of service requests which relate to all technology including; workstations, servers, printers, networks, and vendor specific hardware and softwareYou control and follow up customer tickets in order to comply with customer's Service Level Agreement.You act as a further escalation point for unresolved or escalated calls and maintain and/or update customer information in the Service Desk System.You participate and assist in driving knowledge management and transfer
WHAT YOU BRING
You have a deep knowledge of Microsoft products like Windows 10/11, MS Office 365 and MS 365 cloud apps and services.You gained experience in enduser support and have a solution-oriented mindsetYou keep calm in stressful situations, you are a real team player, and you always convince with your analytical skillsIn best case you have certifications like MD-100/MD-101 - Modern Desktop Administrator Associate or MS-900 Microsoft - Modern Desktop Administrator AssociateYour knowledge of Apple products (iPhones and iPads) and the use of Microsoft products on this platform is a benefitYour English is fluent and German skills are a benefit
