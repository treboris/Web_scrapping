FRENCH SPEAKING SERVICE DESK ANALYST – LEVEL 1
INTRODUCTION
Eager to see the benefits of your language skills? Are you ready to discover a position that combines IT with your interpersonal communication abilities? If yes, we can provide you an excellent opportunity to do so… In TATA Consultancy Services (TCS) you can implement your language knowledge with native speakers in a dynamic working environment.Learn and have fun while you explore our uniquely diverse company culture with more than 90 different nationalities in our Hungarian branch office.Give a boost to your career in a dynamically growing global enterprise that consists of 450.000+ employees around the world. As a Service Desk Analyst your main goal is: Provide solutions to IT related issues and handle customer requests in a pleasant and timely fashion, ensuring customer satisfaction.Impact by delivering excellent service to our clients. Your main focuses are to analyze their IT related issues, guide and educate them about the processes, and apply remote support in order to ensure the productive workflow of their business.Support our top customers using your specific language skills through different channels such as telephone, email or chat. Contract type: full time, indefiniteWorking hours: 40 hours/week in various shiftsLegal requirements: Candidate needs to be currently eligible to work in Hungary
REQUIREMENTS
You are the best fit if you have: a high school diplomaspeak English on a communication level (for interviews, training and internal communication)speak French on a business level (working/support language) … and advantages would be: Hungarian language knowledge on a business levelrelevant experience in a similar field (Service Desk, Help Desk or Customer Care)an extra language knowledge on a support leveladditional IT skills (Mac, Linux, PowerShell, and so on)IT-related studies, courses, among others
ORGANIZATION
About TCS Hungary’s IT IS UnitCome and work in our constantly growing Hungarian Global Delivery Center As one of the largest Business Units of TCS Hungary, the IT-IS Unit provides IT Service Desk solutions to top international clients in a long-term partnership, continuously helping them expand their business and develop new opportunities.
WE OFFER
Why working with us? … You will have the opportunity to: be part of a real multinational team and environmentdevelop your career path and grow domain wise (vertical and horizontal opportunities for growth and rotation among different units)work on a variety of tasks, implementing your own creativity for successful completionwork within a team, but also having the chance to perform independently, developing your own skills and ideas for the benefit the grouplearn and develop soft and hard skills (1000+ internal training) These are some of the benefits you will enjoy: competitive salary and cafeteria benefitswork in hybrid modeprivate health insurance with specialist’s care included (possibility to upgrade it and extend it to family members)free gym/sport sponsorships and internal competitionsHungarian language courses for non-Hungarian speakerscompany events (Winter Party, Summer Picnic, Family Day, among others)team building activitiesshuttle bus from the nearest Metro station
How to apply/ contact information
At TCS Hungary we are working with several different clients, be kindly informed that this job order describes our general criteria for all the Service Desk positions with French language skills. In order to launch your application please click on the apply button.
