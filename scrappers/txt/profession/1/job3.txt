IT Inhouse Consultant (m/f/d) Test Management
Since our beginnings at Dr. Oetker, we have been breaking new ground. With pizza, cake, and desserts, as well as digital platforms and services – quality and an innovative spirit set us apart.
What awaits you? It's up to you! At. Dr. Oetker, you can actively shape the future – both yours and ours. We want to be part of something bigger. Don't you? JOIN THE TASTE.
YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES
In your new role you can expect a highly motivated and qualified international team, which works independently and on its own responsibility. This framework offers a wide range of opportunities for intensive exchange and personal development in different subject areas.You will work conceptually and implementatively on the development of test concepts and test strategies within the scope of challenging projects.In addition to the planning of functional, integration and user acceptance tests, your tasks will also include organizing, coordinating, and monitoring the execution of technical and functional tests in the SAP environment.You create reports for monitoring and controlling the test processes and contribute actively to continuous process improvement and standardization.You work intensively on the development and setup of a test tool infrastructure.Your work is characterized by close cooperation with the departments and the project management.
WHAT YOU BRING
You have a degree in business informatics or a comparable field of study and relevant professional experience in the environment mentioned above.You have good knowledge of test design, test methods and test data management.Desirable would be a certification according to ISTQB.Furthermore, you have an analytical and solution-oriented mindset with an eye for pragmatic approaches.Assertiveness, persuasiveness and quality awareness characterize you.You speak English fluently, have a distinct intercultural competence as well as very good communication skills.In addition, you can work both as part of a team and independently in a solution-oriented manner.
