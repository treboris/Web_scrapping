Internal Control Assistant Manager
pozícióba!
Az Arriva a berlini központú Deutsche Bahn (DB) Csoport része, az európai mobilitási és logisztikai szektor egyik vezető szolgáltatója. Az ArrivaBus Kft. a 12 országban személyszállítási szolgáltatásokat nyújtó Arriva vállalatcsoport tagja, a hazai autóbuszos piac meghatározó szereplője. A 20 éves múlttal rendelkező társaságunk több, mint 1300 alkalmazottat foglalkoztat a stabil piaci hátteret biztosító tömegközlekedési szektorban.
PURPOSE OF THE POSITION:
As Internal Control AM you will contribute to the achievement of Arriva’s financial and operational goals through effective risk management by assisting in the development and sustainment of internal control policies, procedures and processes.
Position Accountabilities
Assist with the overall management of the DB and Arriva Group Control Environment (German GAAP, UK GAAP, BilMoG) programAssist with monitoring and reporting to management of the internal controls design and effectiveness;Help manage quarterly and year end evaluation of controls process by business process ownersPlan and perform internal controls testing to ensure completion of BilMoG annual program in an efficient and effective manner and in compliance with regulatory requirements;Work closely with external auditors, internal audit and business process owners to coordinate efficient and effective execution of testing plans including scheduling of testing and remediation of control deficiencies;Work with control owners to address control deficiencies and ensure that remediation plans are implemented by management on a timely basis;Assist in the preparation of reporting to senior management of control deficiencies and status of remediation;Take every opportunity to continuously improve internal control framework;Assist management in the maintenance of the IT risk and control library;Maintain and improve documentation of internal controls and management’s documentation of processes and controls in the Governance Reporting Tool (IKS Platform)Monitor processes and the regulatory environment (COSO) for changes that could impact internal control framework.Participation in process and compliance trainings, active contact with the relevant departments of the group, also organizing internal trainings to leverage the knowledge
Position Requirements
Master degree in a relevant field;Minimum of 3 years experience in auditing / IT auditing and/or controls design or assessment in a public company or public accounting firm;Professional Accounting Designation considered an asset;Strong knowledge of control requirements and testingDetailed working knowledge of COSOS requirements, as well as IT risk and control frameworksEffective relationship builder;Continuous improvement mindset, strong attention to detail and drive for results, strong communication skills (written and oral), self-starter;Experience in acquisition related integrations and/or system implementations considered an asset;Strong ability with Microsoft Office products specifically Word, Excel and Visio
What we offer
Getting to know the inner control environment as well as the newest trends of the public transportation sectorOpportunity to obtain unique and specific knowledgeResponsible and self-determinant positionWorking in a team, thats members are aspiring for dynamic developmentCompetitive salary and other benefitsOpen-ended employment contractWorking for a company that supports professional development – opportunity to manage your own projectsModern work environment and innovative atmosphereCrisis-tolerant economic sector
