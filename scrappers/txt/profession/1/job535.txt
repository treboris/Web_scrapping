KBC Bank, the parent company of the Hungarian K&H Bank, provides banking services in 5 European countries.The international IT solutions of the banking group, covering several European countries, takes place with Hungarian and Belgian colleagues in Hungary and Belgium, therefore not only our services are international, but also our teams.Our responsibilities range from day-to-day operation to application development and its operation.We are constantly expanding the Hungarian side of the team, which is why we are looking for enthusiastic, motivated new employees with junior, medior or possibly senior work experience.  The O365 Integration team will continuously integrates new technology from the O365 suite. We have a team of experienced developers, challenging functional analysts, a business analyst and a cell of application managers to support the products that we deliver. We work in a SAFe Agile framework with a Scrum Master and a Product Owner to facilitate the team and manage the backlog. Our team integrates most of the O365 stuff straight out of the box, but in a big company, you also have a broad range of demands, so sometimes customization is necessary.We have completely customized the main news site of the company, which is been read by more than 10.000 people each day.The general support site has been tweaked by us so that the search displays not only internal articles, but also Microsoft articles concerning the issue.We provide solutions in the Powerplatform (Apps/Automate) for example a seat reservation tool in times of COVID-19 So in short, our internal customer has needs, and we try to fulfill those needs within the O365 package, as broad
