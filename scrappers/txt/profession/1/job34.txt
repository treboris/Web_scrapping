We are a European discount chain. We attribute our success to our people and their commitment.
WMS SUPPORT MANAGER
Place of work: Gyál/hybrid
You will enjoy your work at PEPCO, because we offer you:
attractive salary packagedevelopment and promotion opportunitieswork in an international environmentan opportunity to participate on professional trainings and language coursesemployee discount up to 20%
Your duties include:
manage the WMS team and prioritise tasks accordinglybe the connection between business and ITsupport DC (Distribution Center) operations within efficient solutions implemented in WMS.improve DC processes by implementation of standardized and efficient WMS solutions.lead and support delivery of on time, in scope and in budget WMS and defined changes in WMS which supports DC operationsimplement and support WMS system in other Pepco warehouses
Join if you:
have 3-5 years’ experience working in logistics warehouse environment and have a broad awareness of how this operateshave particularly good knowledge of WMS (preferable JDA delivered by Blue Yonder)have good in team management and leadership skillshave excellent communication skillshave analytical skillshave a strong English knowledge written and spokenare willing to travel abroad
