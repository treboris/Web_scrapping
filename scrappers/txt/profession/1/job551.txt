Feladatok:
Main objective of the Regional Supply Planning (RSP) team is to lead the End2End DRP, forecast propagation to plants and performance for the Network. Trainee Main Tasks & Responsibilities:• Run weekly/monthly sanity checks to align material master discrepancies in SAP and Kinaxis.• Support with daily/weekly/monthly update of templates for RSP tasks Weekly templates: allocations e.g. actual production follow up of adapted products in allocationMonthly templates: coordination meetings, DRP.• Support RSP with additional spot tasks and/or projects e.g. availability checks for project, promotion, sales push, rebalances.• Work with planning tools: Kinaxis, SAP, Tableau & Internal Schneider tools
Elvárások:
• Fluent in English• Excellent skills in Excel required – knowledge of power query is a big plus.• SAP knowledge or experience would be considered as an advantage• Ability to adapt to an international team and to work under pressure• Practical-mathematical mindset & problem-solving approach• Engineering and/or Business Administration studies preferred
