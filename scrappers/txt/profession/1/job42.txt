WernerCo is a privately owned, fully integrated, global manufacturer and distributor of access products, storage systems, fall protection and light duty construction equipment. Our core values of teamwork, integrity, customer focus, continuous improvement and winning are at the forefront of everything we do, which is why our people are our most valued resource. We provide competitive salaries and benefits, ongoing training and new opportunities to grow.Our two sites in Kecskemét have been employing our colleagues for more than 20 years in a family atmosphere, where we manufacture special ladders and scaffolds by machining aluminium for professional and household use, but we can also serve individual customer needs.
