Hello there! We’re Vodafone Intelligent Solutions (also known as _VOIS) and we’re looking for a
Demand Management Specialist (IT project coordinator)
Your role in a nutshell:
To provide overall governance of IT demand management, to ensure the quality of end to end demand management process. To liaise between customers and the solution groups. To act as the single point of contact until the end of the demand lifecycle. To track and push the approval of the demands and the solution of the customer requests. To investigate standard solutions and potential external solutions. To manage customer demand projects fully or partially and make status reports to the Steering Committee.
What you will love in your role:
Drives the IT demand management process and ensures that demands are captured, assessed, costed and are implementable/ scalable with agreements on delivery commitments made by the implementation and operations teams.May coordinate IT transition and solution implementation projects as well.Highlights any arising risks, provides mitigation plan, also brings any issues up and applies problem solving techniques to resolve them.Chairs the IT Demand Board meetings.Examines local standard solutions, and also looks for solutions externally. Makes proposal for 3-4 alternative IT technical solutions.Engages with 3rd parties and (internal and external) IT teams.End to end IT solution delivery.Identifies and communicates any deviation from the demand management process, defines and implements corrective actions.
What will you bring to the team:
experience of an IT methodology or process model (e.g. ITIL)IT demand management experienceexperience in project/ process/ change managementtelecom or SSC experienceSAP system/ application maintenance / development and infrastucture knowledge (desired)fluent English language knowledgestrong communication/ negotiation skills
In return we provide you with:
A dynamic environment where innovative ideas are always welcome;A collaborative community where your professional goals and work are supported by a diverse team;Access to internal trainings through Vodafone University and external trainings via other providers;A supportive internal coaching and mentoring culture;Opportunities to participate in company activities and Vodafone Foundation events;Corporate assets, including a laptop and mobile phone with Vodafone RED subscription (unlimited EU talk + 20GB data);Vodafone HOME discounted fleet services for employees and their friends & familyCafeteria package;Private Medical Services;Employee Assistant Program to support your wellbeing and mental health;To ensure our colleagues’ health & safety we are working from home until the end of the pandemic. In the long run we will be meeting each other max 2 times/week in our fully refurbished, digital office. What you need to know about usWe are the engine that powers Vodafone around the world: our work impacts millions of people around the globe every day. If you want to experiment and learn fast, integrated into an international, multicultural and inclusive team, then _VOIS is the place for you.
