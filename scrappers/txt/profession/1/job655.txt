Technical Project Leader for Driver Assistance and Automotive Steering
Do you want beneficial technologies being shaped by your ideas? Whether in the areas of mobility solutions, consumer goods, industrial technology or energy and building technology – with us, you will have the chance to improve quality of life all across the globe.Welcome to Bosch.The Bosch Engineering Centre of Budapest has been established in 2001. Today, it is a major research and development hub within the Bosch world focusing on the big mobility trends: automated, electrified and connected mobility. More than 2100 engineers are working together on complex automotive products within various fields of engineering from software to mechanical and hardware product development. Your ideas will be part of products improving the life across the globe.
Task:
 The divisions Cross-Domain Computing Solutions (XC) and Automotive Steering (AS) develop innovative components, systems and functions in the field of vehicle safety, vehicle dynamics and driver assistance. Through the combination of systems based on cameras, radar and ultrasonic sensors, electric power steering and active or passive safety systems we not only make driving safer and more comfortable today but we are also building the basis for the autonomous driving of tomorrow.Your contribution to something big►Responsible for planning, tracking and execution of development projects for our customers in the automotive industry, from quotation to start of production, ensuring quality, timing and budget targets►Technical coordination of international, interdisciplinary project teams including systems, HW, SW, electronics, mechanics, application, verification, etc.►Cooperation with international, cross-functional teams including sales, manufacturing, quality, technical purchasing, controlling, etc.►Agreement of project targets with customer and regular reporting of project status to customer and management►Apply project management tools like quality management, change management, risk   management to ensure project success
Expectation:
What distinguishes you►BSc/MSc in electrical or mechanical engineering, or comparable►Proven experience in managing technical projects for automotive industry or similar►Good technical skills and motivation to solve technical challenges►Flexibility and ability to adapt to new environments►Good communication and presentation skills►Willingness to take responsibility and to make decisions►Target, process and quality orientation►Fluent EnglishWhat gives you further advantages►Project management certification ►Experience in the automotive industry►Experience working with SCRUM►German language knowledge
