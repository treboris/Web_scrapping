IT Business Analyst
As our IT Business Analyst you will be responsible for providing local support to GSK Vaccines Gödöllő site users (mainly in Enterpise Systems), assist the deployment of global business process changes/roll-outs and facilitate new application/system implementation to optimize and digitalize business processes in the site.
Responsibilities:
Manage local and global IT projects and software development, including continuous improvement of the functionality and availability of business applications, combined with increased efficiency within business processes.Provide SAP support for local users and key users in processes locally deployed.Analyze local user requests/needs and translate them into generic business requirements, manage IT tickets, planning/scheduling and/or change requests accordinglyAnalyze the IT tickets, suggest solution or forward them to the global competency center, follow-up the resolution and facilitate the communication between local/global teamsKeep contact with local business to ensure the appropriate Enterprise IT System usage and continuously conduct usage assessments to improve the SAP usage maturity of the colleagues.Represent local IT in Gödöllő’s Enterprise IT System & global business process roll-out incl. process optimization projects through all phases, including fit/gap analysis, customizing, testing and user training, as well as providing technical and user documentationEnsure global procedures are deployed and well used locally (change requests, access requests, my-learning)Responsibility is to ensure that all IT activities are carried out in compliance with IT standards and proceduresDrive & Support the site in the digitalization journeySupport IT related Risk & Compliance topicsBe an SME for SAP related access control and topics (represents these on audits/inspections)
Basic Qualifications:
University or College degree in engineering or technical area2-3 years of experience with informatics areaExperience with SAP and Office 365 is mandatoryFluency in both Hungarian and English languages
Preferred Qualifications:
Experience working in an international environmentSome key user experience or time spent in a large multinational environment would be valuableProject management experienceVeeva and knowledge of other enterprise Systems is nice to haveGreat communication and collaboration skills
What we offer:
Possibility for Home Office 2-3 days / weekAnnual bonusCafeteriaInsurance packageVoluntary pension fund contribution Please apply with an English language CV!
