SAP Technical Architect
Our Company
Nissan creates vehicles and technologies that enrich people’s lives. It’s in our DNA and value.
We are a leading global automotive company we can be found 191 countries, with over 160.000 employees worldwide, creating and sharing innovative products and services since 1933.
Nissan has been always passionate about creating innovative technologies and products in the spirit of "do what others don’t dare to do." We always think about creating new value with unprecedented technologies that go beyond mobility to transform the way we drive and live.
Leveraging our core strengths, we are prioritizing our research and development to increase value for our customers and deliver human-centric, cleaner and smarter technologies.
With a focus on Mobility, Operational Excellence, Value to our Customers and the Electrification of vehicles, you can expect to be part of an exciting employee journey. Our people are the one driving the business forward.
Here in Budapest
Here at the Nissan Regional Business Services (NRBS), we are a diverse and international team who actively contributing towards the development, growth and efficiency of the business across our region.
Our team members enjoy on a daily basis, working on exciting international projects and we are growing, and we welcome your application, Join us.
Tasks and Responsibilities:
Develop and maintain the SAP Technical Architecture approach, including the development of Technical Architecture Road Maps and Standards, Sizing Standards, Change Control Standards.Work with business and project representatives to understand Technical Architecture performance, availability, and recovery requirements and translate those in agreed SLAs and KPIsWorks with enterprise architecture, information security, application & infrastructure teams to create optimal, high level, conceptual, and cost effective designs.Responsible for Release an Upgrade Management Strategy, act as a key SAP Contact to maintain/develop the roadmap/process of applying notes/patches.Designs solutions that must be aligned with business and IT strategies and comply with the organization’s architectural standards.Analyses the current SAP architecture to identify weaknesses and develop opportunities for improvement such as enhancing business processes, expanding capacity, reducing time & cost, and increasing overall effectiveness.Analyses technology industry, market trends and impact of SaaS/Cloud and determines the potential impact on the enterprise. Maintains relationship with SAP ecosystem vendors and maintains vendor roadmaps. Facilitates the evaluation and selection of software product standards.
Skills and experience
Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or other related field, or equivalent work experience,5 to 7 years of relevant experience in a similar role.Should be able to demonstrate and show reference examples of deep technical understanding of SAP Platform technologies such as the SAP Suite:  SAP ERP/ECC, SAP HANA, SAP NetWeaver with specific emphasis on PI, and UI technologies across a variety of database types.Demonstrable experience within SAP in Memory platform technology such as HANA.Be able to demonstrate experience and/or working knowledge within Amazon Web Service with Cloud Transformation experienceAble to assess and manage technical risk and issuesProven ability to work creatively and forward thinking in a problem-solving environmentAbility to communicate needs and schedules to IS and clients, as well as ensure that plans are in place and standards are adhered toGood oral and written communication skills, as well as ability to present and explain functional information in a way that establishes rapport, persuades others and gains understandingStrong customer focus and ability to manage client expectations

Competitive Benefits structure including bonus, Medicover, Cafeteria & travel supportFlexible Working Learning opportunities: our culture of life-long learning up-skilling
