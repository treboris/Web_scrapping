Task:
Position SummaryThe Web Developer (HubSpot + Webflow Specialist) will help with building out TCWGlobal landing pages, templates, and design modules with our CRM, HubSpot and WebFlow. Expectation:
Key Responsibilities Help with building landing pages, building email templates, building design modules, and other website pages, and development-related tasks both within Webflow and HubSpotDevelop design modules within HubSpot marketing, utilizing landing pages, SEO, and moreMaintenance and updates to live pages and site organizationUpload blogs, buildout blog pages and tags as well as implement an interlinking strategy to blogs and websitesMake website changes as requestedAssist in the brand messaging and how to convey it cohesively across our multiple Web properties.Create site layout/user interface by using standard HTML/CSSConceptualize original website design ideas that bring simplicity and user-friendliness to complex roadblocks.Help take our online efforts to a new level

Qualifications & Skills Must have Webflow, Javascript, HTML & CSS, HubSpot Landing Pages/Marketing experienceExperience with design and landing page creationExperience with Figma, Canva, or Adobe Design SuiteExperience with creating HubSpot Landing PagesAttentive to detail, problem solver, and complete deliverables promptlyExcellent verbal and written communication skillsA good mix of design and development skills
