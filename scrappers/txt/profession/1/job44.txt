Team Leader for Web Analysis
We are looking for our new colleague as Team Leader for Web Analysis.
Introduction
Are you interested in leading an international team of sales people and supporting Heureka Group to become the biggest cross-border marketplace in the CEE region? If your answer is yes, we may have the perfect job for you! We are looking for the new Team Leader for Web analysis. Our business is connected with websites and mobile apps but there isn't a leader who will create a strategy for web/app measurement. There is a huge challenge in front of us this and next year (implementation of Google Analytics 4) but there isn't a person who is in detail on this topic. We need to create a strong web inhouse analytical team.We are Heureka! Group, the largest price comparator and shopping advisor in Europe. We help millions of users shop online conveniently, quickly and easily. We connect our business partners with customers not only in Hungary and the Czech Republic but also in many other countries in the Adriatic area.
What you will do
In this role, the main purpose of your work will be to implement Google Analytics 4 in the whole Heureka Group and create a strong inhouse analytical team.As our new TL for Web analysis your responsibilities will be:Create strategy and concept of the measurement in Google analytics;management of the web analytical team;monitor news in Google analytics and implement them into our measurement concept;communication (regular meetings) with other departments about their needs and requirements around measurement;assigning task connected to web/app measurement among team members (internal/external);leading project of new measurement Google analytics 4 implementation.
Requirements
Expected to have:Advanced knowledge of:Google analytics implementation;Google Tag Manager;Javascript, BigQuery and also good knowledge of SQL, HTML.Basic knowledge of:SEO;Digital marketing.Leadership and communication skills;project management experience;at least professional working proficiency knowledge (level C1-C2) in English.Nice to have:Native knowledge of Hungarian language.
You can count on us with…
Working remotely and from the office from either Prague, Brno or Budapest;having a beneficial remuneration package, cafeteria, modern and professional working environment among others;supporting you with 1 day in a month dedicated for your personal improvement;being part of One Marketplace vision;having a supportive team of colleagues.
Further informations
Our office if you work onsite:in Hungary…:1074 Budapest, Rákóczi út 70-72., R70 Office Complex.…or in Prague:Karolinská 706/3, 186 000 Karlín, Prague…or in Brno:Nováčkova 401/53, 614 00 Brno-sever-Husovice
