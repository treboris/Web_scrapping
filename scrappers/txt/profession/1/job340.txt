Company DescriptionPresent in the market under the Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions brand name, IT-Services Hungary (ITSH) is a subsidiary of the Deutsche Telekom Group and is the largest ICT employer in Hungary. Established in 2006, the company provides a wide portfolio of IT and telecommunications services with more than 5000 employees. ITSH was awarded with the Best in Educational Cooperation prize by HIPA in 2019, acknowledged as one of the most attractive workplaces by PwC Hungary’s independent survey and rewarded with the title of the Most Ethical Multinational Company in 2019. The company continuously develops its four sites in Budapest, Debrecen, Pécs and Szeged and is looking for skilled IT professionals to join its team. 
Tasks:
Job DescriptionOur group VNI is responsible for the integration, validation and production of test results ("System Under Test") in accordance with the requirements of the Deutsche Telekom AG Platform, as measured by the diversity of the group. The work is carried out in several virtual squads within the group, mainly with german but also international colleagues. In one of these squads, called EDG_BNG, we are looking for an experienced DevOps Engineer colleague. The squad EDG_BNG is responsible for engineering, basic testing, product compliance (solution), SSt test (composite test with IT), PV, rollout and the active operation of the BNG. Tasks include:Lifecycle Management (E4X) for the Components and System Software of the BNG, drive further development of the BNGTasks: Description/Creation/Revision of test cases Automation of test cases Specification of product features environment
Expectation:
QualificationsCisco CCNP exam or equivalent knowledge and relevant experienceStrong intermediate knowledge of GermanOpenness to new network technologies, like BNGPhython and JavaScript knowhowBasic knowhow about Test Automation Framework ( TAF)Accurate and thorough work, inclination to learn, teamwork Advantage: Knowledge of Cisco CCNP-SP / Juniper JNCIAEnglish intermediate levelExperience in agile methodology Additional Information* Please be informed that our remote working possibility is only available within Hungary due to European taxation regulation.
