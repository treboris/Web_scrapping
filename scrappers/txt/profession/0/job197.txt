Tech People is a European provider of technical skills permanent recruitment and temporary contracting services for multiple industries.
Currently we are looking for
CLOUD ENGINEER WITH DB SKILLSET/GCP KNOWLEDGE (JD)
Location: Budapest, IX district (partly home office)
Salary: competitive
Type of employment: employee
Tasks:
Perform various migrations between Clouds or from premises to Cloud based on customers’ requirements and needs, focusing on GCPCreate, define and improve processes and plans related to migrationOptimize all onboarding scenarios for customers on public cloud together with 3rd party products for migrationWork in highly dynamic, international environment and with customer representatives in the team, while serving as the technology expertCollaborate with T-systems external and internal partners Participate in the roadmap implementation and feedback to the product teamDirectly communicate with end customers to discuss possibilities of migration, transformation and their optimal designsAnalysis of current setup and following that, definition of requirementsOracle DB design/configuration on GCP (VM) incl. HA and DR setupsOracle DB migration strategies and approaches - Oracle migration solution design on GCP (VM)Migration support and troubleshooting additionally, but not must-have transformation Oracle DB to GCP PaaS services
QUALIFICATIONS:
We are looking for an individual with strong background in public cloud technologies who is ready to help customers on their cloud journey to public cloud. In addition, you ideally bring the following with you:2+ years of relevant work experience with public cloud technologies (GCP), with some professional/advance certificationEnglish Language (Fluent)Understanding of migration concepts and migration toolingUnderstanding of Infrastructure as Code and automation possibilities (Terraform, ARM templates, Ansible, SaltStack)Some experience with Agile tooling and processes (Jira, Kanban, Scrum methodology)Very high motivation and the ability to thrive in a dynamic environmentGeneral ICT skills (Network, OS, Storage, Database etc.)Basic Scripting & Coding knowledge (PowerShell, Bash, Python); Oracle"
