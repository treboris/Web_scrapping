Egészségbiztosítás
Hello there! We’re Vodafone Intelligent Solutions (also known as _VOIS) and we’re looking for a
Strategic Operations Programmes Lead
Role purpose:
Within Device Operations Supply Chain function, the Lead for Strategic Operations Programmes is accountable for the set-up, delivery and performance measurement of cross-functional, complex and strategic to SLT level projects in the area of Device Operations, projects with financial impact on the company (such as: working capital improvement, efficiency gains, automation and process rationalisation). Moreover, the role holder will play a key role in implementation and monitoring of Commercial Ops & Enablement governance and act as vertical facilitator of Agile Ways of Working across Operations teams in VPC, VOIS and Local Markets. What you’ll do:Successfully engage in multiple cross-functional Commercial Ops & Enablement projects simultaneously (such as Working Capital improvement initiative or roll out self-billing initiative across suppliers)Ensure proper project setup, carefully involves necessary stakeholders (internal and external) and ensure compliance with the Kanban & Agile methodology Ensure proper and regular usage of tools, like project charter, meeting minutes, risk management tool, project timing, workshops, etc.Drive and challenge project team on their assumptions of how the project will be successfully executedDeep understanding of business need, business case (both benefits, expenses, investments) operational requirements and technical solutionsEnsure project transparency via visible leadership and proactive communicationMake decision regarding issue categorization and necessary escalationProvide e2e project reporting (documentation according to internal rules, progress reports and status updates, project status senior management team)Support Operations Team governance and internal communication (team meetings, team presentations, regular management reporting, etc.)Support other functions in project management
What you will bring to the team:
Fluent English knowledge Experienced in methods and concepts of complex transformation programmes, as well as in programme and project management Acting in a flexible, agile way, with changing priorities and multiple relevant topics at the same timeCapable to define roadmaps, express complex circumstances in simple languageConfident to discussions with senior leaders, with good communication skills and proficient ways of expressingAble to work independently, quickly understand the core of the subject and driving the necessary actions / resolution
In return we provide you with:
A dynamic environment where innovative ideas are always welcome;A collaborative community where your professional goals and work are supported by a diverse team;Access to internal trainings through Vodafone University and external trainings via other providers;A supportive internal coaching and mentoring culture;Opportunities to participate in company activities and Vodafone Foundation events;Corporate assets including a laptop and mobile phone with Vodafone RED subscription (unlimited talk, text message, and data within the EU)Vodafone HOME discounted fleet services for employees and their friends & familyCafeteria package;Private Medical Services;Employee Assistant Program to support your wellbeing and mental health;Currently, we are trialling a flexible and hybrid way of working to ensure our colleagues’ wellbeing and health & safety; we combine working from home with meeting max 2 times/week in our fully refurbished, digital office in Budapest.
What you need to know about us
We are the engine that powers Vodafone around the world: our work impacts millions of people around the globe every day. If you want to experiment and learn fast, integrated into an international, multicultural and inclusive team, then _VOIS is the place for you. #movewithus #_VOIS #VOISHU #LI-Hybrid
