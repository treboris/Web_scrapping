Software Tester
Our aim is to improve process efficiency within internal Tax functions, as well as to create software-based solutions that our customers love in the world of taxation. Client-facing portals, automated risk assessment, online reporting, system integration, and calculation tools are just a few examples of our Hungarian Tax initiatives, and many more will follow – as taxation and business are ever-evolving.
Who we are looking for
2+ years of Web/Mobile/Desktop application testing experienceexperience in working with databases and APIsknowledge of testing methods and technologiesexperience with test management and bug tracking systems (we use Jira)be a good team player, manage your tasks independently, communicate effectivelygood communication skills both in Hungarian and in English
Your future role:
you will test our in house developed web based softwareanalyze and help resolving issuescreate test cases, test cycles and plansyou will be trained in test automation with robotics technology – UIPathwork in a well refined scrum team, is a structured way
What we offer:
you can benefit from the company’s training program in your preferred tech areasyou will belong to a team where we help each other to reach the company’s and your personal goalsmentoring and technical coaching from senior staffthe most recent technologies and integrated development componentshybrid working environment – home office days are allowedcompetitive salary including a wide range of benefits, and performance bonuswe organize regular team events, including wakeboard weekends, rafting, hiking, skiing, drinks, festivals, and more…challenging multinational environment, where personal development and growth are encouragedfresh fruit every day and quality coffee are provided for healthy and energized working daysattractive and modern working environment with great location in the heart of Budapest
