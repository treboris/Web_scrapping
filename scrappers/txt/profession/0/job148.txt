CLOUD
KNORR-BREMSE Hungária Kft.
We are looking for a driven and experienced Embedded Software Architect to setup together with lead architects the Design Elements and Building Blocks of an IoT Design Kit Environment, containing of Software Artefacts, Concepts, Blueprints and HW Reference Designs. You will focus on high performance IoT Devices from smart sensors up to multicore embedded Linux systems as onboard computing units for rolling stock. The kit will enable all subsystem suppliers within Knorr-Bremse to select the best fitting elements to design the subsystems as digital ready.
You will be part of a new team that will focus on the development of new Digital Products and Services which will have a huge impact on the future of rail freight in Europe. Take a look at this article: THE FUTURE OF RAIL FREIGHT ()
Embedded Software Architect for IoT Systems
MAIN TASKS:
Define scalable architecture elements with capsulated software artifacts, elements on cloud and edge sideWorking out concepts and definitions in strong cooperation and alignment with the lead architects.Coordinate the implementation, handle technical problems, make technical decisionsBe responsible for the generic approach of kit elements for scalability and flexibilityHave ownership for selected kit elements over the whole life cycle, including documentationWork together with teams in Europe and IndiaTravel occasionally
REQUIREMENTS:
Degree in Software Engineering, Electrical Engineering or Computer ScienceStrong experience in embedded systems and software architectureExperience in IoT technologies and embedded container solutionsExcellent verbal and written communicationProficient English language skills
BENEFITS:
