Data Engineering Manager
Driven to be the best Together!The purpose of the job is to manage a team of professionals who will take care of platform development and operations support within a new centralized global data architecture, engineering and analytics framework. The job is both technical and managerial, focusing on platform development, operations and support aspect of the IT solutions delivered within the data realm but also coaching and growing a team of experts.
Task:
Ensuring that standards and procedures for the effective integration of systems into operational services are developed, maintained and adhered to.Reviewing new or amended standards, procedures and other processes, checking for compliance against the quality system, and external standards where appropriateTranslates logical designs into physical designs and delivers technical prototypes of proposed components for approval by customer and execution by technicians.Working with data management colleagues to ensure that modelling is carried out consistently, and that corporate standards for repository administration and data dictionaries are observedApplying appropriate safeguards to handling the data and any analysis resultsInvestigating available information sources (including, for example, from sensors & measurement systems), to establish what information is availableWithin a project environment, assists in the investigation of application data and process requirements, documenting them according to the required standards utilising the prescribed methods and toolsTaking a leading technical role in systems development projects, both within systems design and in interfaces with other stages of development. Evaluates and undertakes impact analysis on major design options.Ensuring that the risks associated with the installation or decommissioning are understood and that measures to reduce them are adopted where appropriate.Setting goals and assess performanceConducting periodic performance reviewsAssessing and establishing training plan for each team memberAssign tasks, roles and responsibilities to each team member within the framework and workstreams of the larger IT organization
Expectation:
University / College degree in preferably IT domain, or Business/Economics6-7+ years of relevant work experience in data platform engineering and/or business intelligenceAdvanced expertise in many areas of data platform engineering (infrastructure resources: storage, computation, networking, data management, transfer related to complex data solutions) and data engineering (data modelling, ETL design and development, data and metadata managements., query design/development/tuning, database optimization, data governance, data quality, best practices)Competent level of infrastructure, cloud platforms (preferably AWS) and design patterns around those and the “devops” conceptExpert level understanding of databases and data technologies including: traditional RDMBS, NoSQL, data stream solutionsThe role requires to have eagerness and ability to learn and adapt technologies, industry practices, tools, and trendsFluent level of English, additional language knowledge is a plusGood verbal and written communication skillsExemplary teamwork skills as well as excellent interpersonal skillsConsistently uses time effectively and efficientlySelf-starter: able to initiate, prioritize, and complete assignments with little to no supervision
What we offer:
Flexible way of working according to our remote policy in effectPossibility to work from abroad (according to remote policy in affect)Cafeteria with wide range of elements, e.g. SZÉP Card (Széchenyi Recreation Card), mutual aid fund, targeted health fund, pension fund, language courseEmployee discounts program at various contracted partnersWe are proud of working at the greenest of Budapest – Green House Office BuildingWe support your career development with internal learning portfolio and talent programsWe are never bored - our Fun Factory team takes care of event organisation (well-being & mental health program, team building, social events, CSR - Charity activities)Recognition program – we appreciate your achievementsAdditional holiday for your birthdayYoung, dynamic, and multinational environment – the average age alongside the BSC is 31years, we represent over 30 nationalities
