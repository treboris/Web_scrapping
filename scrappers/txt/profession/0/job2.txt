Group Data Protection Officer
Purpose of the position:
The Group Data Protection Officer will be responsible for directing and overseeing all data protection activities for the Group (currently includes Wizz Air Hungary, Wizz Air UK, Wizz Air Malta and Wizz Air Abu Dhabi). The role is appointed to fulfil a statutory obligation for a Data Protection Officer under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) as well as local requirements. The Group Data Protection Officer will report to the Head of Legal, with dotted line reporting to the Corporate Officer.
What we offer:
Base salary and employee bonus schemeUnlimited number of significantly discounted and confirmed seating tickets, giving access to a continuously growing network of destinationsRelocation package (if applicable) – expense budget, Temporary accommodation, Administration supportLife insuranceAYCM sport pass contracting possibilityInstant impact, from any level of the organisation, exposure to C-suite managementFast career progression, internal mobility and talent management supported by training opportunities (Wizz Academy and bespoke trainings)True international and multi-cultural work environment, passionate, driven and diverse teams
Key responsibilities:
Managing the assignment of responsibilities to ensure privacy by design at all levels and to deliver compliance with data protection laws and policies of the GroupInforming and advising on data protection laws and policies of the GroupMaintaining appropriate data protection policies and procedures and ensuring their implementationMonitoring compliance with data protection laws and policies of the Group: collection of information to identify processing activities, analysis and review of compliance of processing activitiesOverseeing the maintenance of records required to demonstrate data protection complianceSupervising and advising on data protection impact assessmentsManaging a program of awareness-raising and training to deliver compliance and to foster a data privacy culture within the GroupEnsuring that the Group addresses all queries from data subjects within legal timeframesLiaising with service providers and other contracted partners who participate in the data processing activities of the GroupActing as a point of contact with EU residents, supervisory authorities and internal teamsFollow up with changes in data protection law and issue recommendations to ensure complianceContinuous review to simplify and streamline processes to provide scalability for growth of the business 
Requirements:
Master's degreeLegal background and at least 5 years of experience in a consulting company or in a law firm and/or in the legal or compliance department of a multinational company with proven experience in Data Protection and IT LawSolid knowledge of the European data protection legal frameworkIn-depth understanding of the GDPR (the candidate should ideally hold a CIPP/E and/or CIPM as well as CISM or CISSP)Experience with developing, documenting, communicating and rolling out a global privacy programExperience in tools to automate privacy, and to report on privacy complianceKnowledge of cybersecurity risks, PCI DSS and other information security standardsUnderstanding of global privacy law in relation to all business functions (sales, marketing, HR, R&D)Strong leadership skills and ability to manage cross-functional projectsAbility to make good judgements regarding data privacy risks and to prioritize resources and activity around managing those risksGood analytical and problem solving skillsEffective communication capabilities, both verbal and written and comfortable working with diverse culturesAbility to work with peers and senior management, inspiring trust and confidenceGood organizing and coordinating skillsFluent professional English, both spoken and writtenHigh degree of independence, with attention to detail Unfortunately, due to the volume of applications we receive we are unable to provide feedback to you at this stage, should your application not meet the requirements. Thank you for your understanding.
