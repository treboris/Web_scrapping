CRM Business Analyst
We are currently looking for a CRM Business Analyst to join our newly created Digital Customer Experience team, with the focus on Salesforce projects. You will become part of the Global Salesforce practice and you will be working on a project basis in a multinational team engaged in a wide range of Salesforce projects.
Responsibilities:
Participate in Salesforce implementation projects from start to finish.Performs “As-Is” and Fit/Gap analyses in to map our offerings and capabilities to the Client business needs.Works closely with technical architects to make the bridge between the business team and IT team.Presents and leads client sessions and requirement workshopsProvides knowledge of the Salesforce platform to make adequate design decisions & propose solutions to requirements based on industry & technology best practices.Design and deliver customer journeys by wireframing, prototyping and user interface designs in collaboration with UI designers.Defines test cases and executes testing to confirm that solution performs as per the business requirements.Coordinates releases and post Go-Live materials (e.g. training documentation, training sessions).
You need to have:
Bachelor’s DegreeGood communication skills (written and oral)Business analyst capabilities (req. gathering, documentation, testing, workshops)UX design experience in a successfully launched web, mobile applicationMust speak Hungarian
Advantage:
Salesforce. com Administrator CertificationExperience in Salesforce. com platformExperience in business consultancy in the financial sector
We offer:
Capgemini Global in-house training and continuous learning opportunitiesCompetitive salary packageMedical benefit packageHome office Applicants must have knowledge of Hungarian language and hold valid Hungarian working visa in case of foreign nationality!
