DEKRA Akademie Kft. - IT Nearshore Center
IT Global Licensing Expert
Since its foundation almost hundred years ago, the goal of DEKRA has been to ensure the safety of human interaction with technology and the environment. The company currently employs around 44,000 people all around the world.
Right now we are looking for IT professionals with dedication and commitment to innovation in our newly built DEKRA IT Nearshore Center. Want to be part of a constantly growing, innovative and multinational team?
An exciting opportunity has arisen in our Global IT Division. Come and work as an IT Global License Expert at DEKRA where we offer great benefits with opportunities to advance and develop in a team full of professionals.
What you will be doing:
You define and manage organizational framework for enterprise-wide SAMYou document licenses and agreements in the DEKRA License Management ToolYou monitor the license compliance situation for the entire/global company on regular basis and recognize shortfalls and over coverage situationsYou conduct internal license audits and support with external auditsYou support DEKRA Group companies in calculating licenses requirements based on metricYou advise and support Procurement in software acquisitions and renewalsYou optimize usage of existing licensesYou monitor market for licensing issues and changes in licensing metrics
What makes you the best fitting candidate:
Experience in the area of software licensing/software asset management and software auditsExperience in working with large software providers would be preferred.Good German and English skills (B2)
What we offer:
Long term career perspectiveCompetitive salary and benefitsCafeteria and annual performance orianted bonusHealth insuranceCompany phone and notebookTraining oppurtunities and continuous professional development (language trainings and professional trainings as well)Travel allowanceBilingual environment and international projectsOppurtinity to work in a modern office with coffee and drinks, under flexible working conditionsHome office opportunity (2 times per week)Regularly organized team events and team Place of Work: Budapest One Business Park (Budapest, XI.)Start date: Immediately
