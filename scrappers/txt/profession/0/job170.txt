Associate Product Manager
Our mission is to help people achieve their dream of starting a business and help them be successful.You will be part of our Hungarian market team represented by our Country Manager Ivan Redey.In 2021, we doubled the company to 200 people in Czech Rebublic and celebrated the fair employer of the year award. We have a healthy company culture, a meaningful product and the ambition to become an e-commerce leader in CEE. Do you want to be there with us and move Shoptet forward? In the role of Associate Product Manager, you will have the opportunity to join our company and use your potential to improve our product together with us.Your missionMission of Associate Product Manager is contributing to initiatives for achieving product / market fit for our solution in Hungarian market.
Task:
What will be your agenda?Interviewing merchants and partner agencies to know their biggest pain points.Gathering product requirements towards product localisation, improvements and legal compliance.Collecting quantitative data and evaluating which initiatives works and what don't.Collaboration with various teams from customer support, through marketing and other product teams for identifying opportunities and solutions.Reporting to Country Lead, collaborating with CPO, local team and product teams.Helping network for the position: Product Managers of other teams, that will help you deliver value to customers.Product Marketing Manager, that will help you promote new improvements and products.Data team, that will help you analyse large scale datasets.External partners, that cooperates with Shoptet and are tied to our success.
Expectation:
Native Hungarian with good English.Having 1-3 years experience in Product Management.Excellent prioritisation and commmunication skills.Proactive, self-driven, flexible, open-minded and able to work independently.Based in Budapest or nearby with possibility to visit office.E-commerce background, local knowledge of the webshop market is a big plus. Having experience with SaaS business model is advantage.
What we offer:
We create inspiring and challenging environment, and support people to be successful. Our focus is to provide the organisation with a sustained competitive advantage to full-fill our great mission and vision.Great colleagues with experience from industry leading companies.A fast but human and friendly environment where teamwork really matters.Cool office in a very premium location (Millenáris Park).Growth-enhancing environment.Home office (maximum 2 days per week) is available after onboarding.Great community events - B-day, Teambuilding.Enterprise tariff for your own e-shop for free.Hiring process:Intro call with HR1 on 1 with CPO1 on 1 with Country LeadCase study presentation with audienceFinal call + offerWe look forward to welcome you on-board!
